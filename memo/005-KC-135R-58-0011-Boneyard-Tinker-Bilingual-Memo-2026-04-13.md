## 逐段双语对照

🔹 **一架注册号为 58-0011 的美国空军 KC-135R 同温层加油机，被发现从亚利桑那州图森市的第 309 航空维护与再生大队"飞机坟场"飞往俄克拉荷马城的廷克空军基地。**  
🔸 A U.S. Air Force **KC-135R Stratotanker with registration number 58-0011** was found to have flown from the "aircraft boneyard" of the **309th Aerospace Maintenance and Regeneration Group** in Tucson, Arizona to Tinker Air Force Base in Oklahoma City.

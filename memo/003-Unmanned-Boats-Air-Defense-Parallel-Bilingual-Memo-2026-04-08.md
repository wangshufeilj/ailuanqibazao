🔹 **【真没想到，它居然真的有效】**  
🔸 **【It’s surprising, but it actually works】**

🔹 关于打击无人快艇以及与防空系统的相似之处  
🔸 On combating unmanned motorboats and the parallels with air defense systems

🔹 最近我们解释了，为什么乌克兰的半潜式无人快艇效率显著下降。而且时不时会出现一些直观的例子。  
🔸 Recently, we explained why the efficiency of Ukrainian semi-submersible maritime drones has **declined significantly**. Visual examples of this surface from time to time.

🔹 在这段视频中——在远离海岸线的黑海水域，两艘乌方配备机枪的无人快艇被击毁。  
🔸 In this video, two Ukrainian unmanned boats equipped with machine guns were destroyed in the waters of the Black Sea, **far from the coastline**.

🔹 起初它们是由俄军的ZALA侦察无人机发现的，随后“柳叶刀”无人机对目标发动了打击。  
🔸 They were initially detected by Russian **ZALA reconnaissance UAVs**, after which **"Lancet" loitering munitions** carried out strikes on the targets.

🔹 一年前我们曾提出疑问：为什么不使用在前线已被证明有效的无人机来对付它们？  
🔸 A year ago, we raised a question: why not use drones that have already proven effective on the front lines to counter them?

🔹 这显然比派遣苏-30或别-12去投掷自由落体炸弹更高效、更安全。  
🔸 This is clearly **more efficient and safer** than dispatching Su-30s or Be-12s to drop free-fall bombs.

🔹 而如今，这已经成为一种常规做法。甚至不需要发明什么全新的东西——问题在于协同、指挥以及对现有资源的运用。  
🔸 Today, this has become a **standard practice**. There was no need to invent anything entirely new—the issue lay in **coordination, command**, and the effective utilization of existing resources.

🔹 ❗️而这在很大程度上也适用于防空保障的问题。  
🔸 ❗️To a large extent, this also applies to the challenges of **air defense provision**.

🔹 是的，在那里不能简单地把防空导弹系统的弹药量翻倍，但有一些方案本可以尝试并加以推广。  
🔸 Granted, one cannot simply double the ammunition capacity of surface-to-air missile systems, but there are solutions that could have been tested and scaled up.

🔹 从打击无人快艇的例子可以看出，这种方法是有效的。  
🔸 As demonstrated by the example of combating unmanned motorboats, this approach is **indeed effective**.

## 逐句对照

<!-- 双语组：🔹英 / 🔸中；第一行行尾为 Markdown 硬换行（两个空格）。 -->

🔹 Iran will move all schools to virtual learning beginning April 21, according to the country's Ministry of Education.  
🔸 伊朗教育部宣布，自**4月21日**起全国所有学校将**转为线上教学**。

---

🔹 A ministry spokesperson said the shift applies to all educational levels across the country and will remain in effect until further notice, as reported by Tasnim News Agency.  
🔸 据**塔斯尼姆通讯社**报道，该部发言人表示，这一调整**适用于全国所有教育阶段**，并将**持续至另行通知**。

---

🔹 The decision covers primary, secondary, and higher education institutions, marking a full transition to remote instruction.  
🔸 此项决定**涵盖小学、中学及高等教育机构**，标志着**全面转向远程授课**。

以下为**英中双语对照**摘录：一侧概括美方公开通报中对封锁初期态势的描述，另一侧为对**同一时段**内公开船舶追踪信息的**对照性表述**（含船名、呼号/识别与港口）。**原文链接未随文提供**；若需核对具体通报原文与航迹截图，请以美国中央司令部、海事开源数据库及实时 AIS 为准。

---

🔹 美国中央司令部发布的首张执行封锁任务以来的态势图，声称在最初的 24 小时内，**没有船只能够突破美国封锁线**，**6 艘商船遵从美军指令掉头重新进入阿曼湾的伊朗港口**。封锁任务动用了 **12+ 艘军舰、100+ 架战斗机和监视飞机**。  
🔸 The U.S. Central Command released its first situational map since the blockade operation began, claiming that **no vessels were able to breach the U.S. blockade line during the initial 24 hours**, with **6 merchant ships complying with U.S. military orders and turning back to re-enter Iranian ports in the Gulf of Oman**. The blockade operation mobilized **over 12 naval vessels and over 100 fighter jets and surveillance aircraft**.

---

🔹 **这不对**，我们在过去 24 小时内**至少追踪到四艘不同的船只通过**：Roshak(EPCA2) 来自伊朗阿巴斯港，Azargoun(EPBZ7) 来自伊朗阿巴斯港，Shabdis(EPBQ7) 来自伊朗阿巴斯港，TAVA 4 (D6A4061) 来自伊朗马赫夏赫尔港。  
🔸 **That is not correct**. We have **tracked at least four different vessels passing through** in the past 24 hours: Roshak (EPCA2) from Iran's Abbas Port, Azargoun (EPBZ7) from Iran's Abbas Port, Shabdis (EPBQ7) from Iran's Abbas Port, and TAVA 4 (D6A4061) from Iran's Mahshahr Port.

---

🔹 其中，**TAVA 4 已经穿越了伊朗、阿曼两国领海及所谓的封锁线**，目的地是**印度孟买 INNSA**，**目前仍然在继续航行**。  
🔸 Among these, **TAVA 4 has already traversed the territorial waters of both Iran and Oman as well as the so-called blockade line**, with its destination being **Mumbai, India (INNSA)**, and **it is currently continuing its voyage**.

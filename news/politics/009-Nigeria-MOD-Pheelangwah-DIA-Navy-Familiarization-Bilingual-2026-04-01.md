### 背景说明

以下为尼日利亚国防部（MOD）相关通报的**英中逐句对照**汇编，涉及常务秘书理查德·菲兰瓦（Richard Pheelangwa / Pheelangwah）代表国防部长出席**国家雷患行动利益相关方研讨会**开幕式，以及其对国防情报局（DIA）、国防情报局长（CDI）、海军参谋长（CNS）等的**熟悉性视察**。研讨会举办日期为 **2026-03-24**；对 CDI 的熟悉访问为 **2026-03-18**。

---

### 英中对照正文

🔹 **The Honourable Minister of Defence, Gen. Christopher Gwabin Musa**, represented by the Permanent Secretary, **Mr. Richard Pheelangwa**, at the opening of the **National Mine Action Stakeholders Workshop**.  
🔸 **国防部长克里斯托弗·格瓦宾·穆萨将军**由国防部常务秘书**理查德·菲兰瓦先生**代表，出席了**国家雷患行动利益相关方研讨会**的开幕式。

🔹 The workshop was held on Tuesday, **March 24th, 2026**, at **Reiz Continental Hotel, Abuja**.  
🔸 研讨会于**2026年3月24日**星期二在**阿布贾雷兹大陆酒店**举行。

🔹 The workshop is for delegates and stakeholders who will discuss **national strategies** and collaborative efforts aimed at strengthening **mine action coordination**, safety, and **humanitarian response** across Nigeria.  
🔸 此次研讨会面向各方代表及利益相关者，讨论**国家战略**与协作努力，以加强全尼日利亚**雷患行动协调**、安全及**人道主义救援**。

🔹 Permanent Secretary, Ministry of Defence, **Mr. Richard Pheelangwah**, conducted a familiarization tour to the **Defence Intelligence Agency (DIA)**.  
🔸 国防部常务秘书**理查德·菲兰瓦先生**对**国防情报局 (DIA)** 进行了熟悉访问。

🔹 During the visit, he encouraged the use of **cutting-edge technologies** as part of his tour to agencies under the **Ministry of Defence**.  
🔸 作为对**国防部**下属机构巡视的一部分，他在访问期间鼓励使用**前沿尖端技术**。

🔹 On Wednesday, **March 18, 2026**, the Permanent Secretary paid a familiarization visit to the **Chief of Defence Intelligence (CDI)**.  
🔸 **2026年3月18日**星期三，常务秘书对**国防情报局局长 (CDI)** 进行了熟悉访问。

🔹 The Permanent Secretary also paid a familiarization visit to the **Chief of the Naval Staff (CNS)**, Vice Admiral **Idi Abbas**.  
🔸 常务秘书还对**海军参谋长 (CNS)**、海军中将**伊迪·阿巴斯**进行了熟悉访问。

### 补充（通报常见全称表述）

🔹 **THE PERMANENT SECRETARY, MINISTRY OF DEFENCE, RICHARD P. PHEELANGWAH, PAID A FAMILIARIZATION VISIT TO THE CHIEF OF DEFENCE INTELLIGENCE (CDI), LIEUTENANT GENERAL EAP UNDIANDEYE, AT THE DEFENCE INTELLIGENCE HEADQUARTERS IN ABUJA.**  
🔸 国防部常务秘书理查德·P·菲兰格瓦在位于阿布贾的国防情报总部，会见了国防情报局长伊安·昂迪安德耶（EAP Undiandeye）中将，开展了熟悉性视察访问。

🔹 **THE VISIT FORMS PART OF ONGOING ENGAGEMENTS BY MOD AIMED AT STRENGTHENING COLLABORATION AND ENHANCING INTELLIGENCE-DRIVEN OPERATIONS WITHIN THE NATION’S DEFENCE ARCHITECTURE.**  
🔸 此类访问是国防部近期一系列接触活动的一部分，旨在加强部门协作，并提升国家国防体系中情报驱动型行动的效能。

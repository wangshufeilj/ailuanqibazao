# 模块四：素材与语料库积累

## 4A. 重点词汇解析

---

### W — 写作高频词

---

**1. quagmire** /ˈkwæɡmaɪr/ *n.*

- **英文释义（LDOCE）**：a difficult, complicated, or unpleasant situation that is hard to escape from; also, an area of soft wet muddy ground
- **中文释义**：（比喻）泥潭、困境；（字面）沼泽地
- **语域**：书面语 / 新闻 / 政治评论
- **同义词**：morass, mire, predicament, impasse
- **反义词**：resolution, clarity
- **拓展**：
  - 可数名词，常用单数
  - 常见搭配：*get bogged down in a quagmire* / *diplomatic quagmire* / *legal quagmire* / *drawn into a quagmire*
  - 本义（沼泽）现已少用，比喻义（难以脱身的困境）在政治、法律、外交语境中极为常见
  - "Iraq quagmire"是美国政治写作的经典搭配，可直接用于写作引用

- **例句**：
  > Years of mismanaged foreign policy had plunged the country into a **quagmire** from which no easy exit remained.
  > 多年的外交政策失误已将这个国家拖入一个难以脱身的泥潭。

---

**2. vacillate** /ˈvæsɪleɪt/ *v.*

- **英文释义（Cambridge）**：to be uncertain what to do, or to change often between two opinions or decisions
- **中文释义**：动摇；摇摆不定；犹豫不决
- **语域**：书面语 / 正式 / 新闻评论
- **同义词**：waver, oscillate, dither, fluctuate, hedge
- **反义词**：commit, resolve, stand firm
- **拓展**：
  - 不及物动词；常见搭配：*vacillate between A and B* / *vacillate on an issue*
  - 名词形式：**vacillation** /ˌvæsɪˈleɪʃn/（常含贬义，指领导力不足）
  - 形容词：**vacillating**（动摇的）
  - 区分：*vacillate*（反复摇摆）vs. *hesitate*（一次性迟疑）vs. *oscillate*（物理摆动，或更机械式的来回变化）

- **例句**：
  > A leader who **vacillates** on national security sends a dangerous signal of weakness to both allies and adversaries.
  > 一位在国家安全问题上摇摆不定的领导人，向盟友和对手发出了软弱的危险信号。

---

**3. reiterate** /riˈɪtəreɪt/ *v.*

- **英文释义**：to say or do something again, especially to emphasize it
- **中文释义**：重申；反复强调
- **语域**：正式 / 书面 / 新闻
- **同义词**：repeat, restate, emphasize, underscore, stress
- **拓展**：
  - 及物动词；常接 that 从句：*reiterate that...*
  - 名词：**reiteration** /riˌɪtəˈreɪʃn/
  - 写作技巧：比 "repeat" 更正式、更强调有意为之的重复

- **例句**：
  > At the press conference, the secretary of state **reiterated** that no ground troops would be deployed.
  > 在新闻发布会上，国务卿再次重申不会派遣地面部队。

---

**4. stewardship** /ˈstuːərdʃɪp/ *n.*

- **英文释义**：the job of supervising or taking care of something, such as an organization or property
- **中文释义**：管理职责；托管；监管权
- **语域**：正式 / 书面 / 政治 / 商业
- **同义词**：management, governance, custodianship, oversight
- **拓展**：
  - 不可数名词
  - 常见搭配：*under sb's stewardship* / *economic stewardship* / *stewardship of the economy*
  - 带有"受托照管"的隐含义，比 "management" 更强调责任与信托关系

- **例句**：
  > Voters will judge the president's **stewardship** of the economy in the upcoming midterm elections.
  > 选民将在即将到来的中期选举中评判总统对经济的治理表现。

---

**5. retrospectively** /ˌretrəˈspektɪvli/ *adv.*

- **英文释义**：looking back on past events; with hindsight
- **中文释义**：回顾地；事后地；追溯地
- **语域**：正式 / 学术 / 书面
- **同义词**：in hindsight, with hindsight, looking back
- **拓展**：
  - 形容词：**retrospective**（adj./n.）
  - 常见搭配：*retrospectively cast as* / *retrospective analysis* / *apply a law retrospectively*
  - 法律语境中常指"溯及既往"

- **例句**：
  > The policy was **retrospectively** seen as a strategic blunder that alienated key regional allies.
  > 这项政策事后被视为一个战略失误，疏远了关键的地区盟友。

---

**6. rebuttal** /rɪˈbʌtl/ *n.*

- **英文释义**：a statement that says that a claim or accusation is wrong or untrue; refutation
- **中文释义**：反驳；驳斥
- **语域**：正式 / 法律 / 新闻 / 政治
- **同义词**：refutation, counterargument, riposte, rejoinder
- **拓展**：
  - 可数名词；常见搭配：*offer a rebuttal* / *a powerful rebuttal* / *rebuttal to calls for...*
  - 动词：**rebut** /rɪˈbʌt/（他+双写+ed = **rebutted**）
  - 法律写作中常见：*rebuttal evidence* / *rebuttal witness*

- **例句**：
  > His landslide victory was seen as a **rebuttal** to those who had called for mandatory retirement ages for politicians.
  > 他的压倒性胜利被视为对那些呼吁强制议员退休年龄限制者的有力回击。

---

**7. riff** /rɪf/ *v./n.*

- **英文释义（v.）**：to speak or perform in a spontaneous, improvisational way, especially in a long, loose manner; to improvise
- **中文释义**：（口语）即兴发挥；漫谈；随性表达
- **语域**：非正式 / 口语 / 新闻评论（带轻微贬义色彩）
- **同义词**：improvise, ramble, ad-lib
- **拓展**：
  - 本义为爵士/摇滚乐中的重复乐句段落（*guitar riff*）；引申至口语中指即兴、漫无边际的说话
  - 常见搭配：*just riffing* / *riff on a topic*
  - S类（熟词僻义）：熟知音乐含义者易忽略其政治评论语境中"即兴乱说"的引申用法

- **例句**：
  > The senator appeared to be **riffing** rather than delivering prepared remarks, leaving reporters scrambling to verify his claims.
  > 这位参议员似乎是在即兴发挥，而非宣读准备好的讲稿，让记者们纷纷忙于核实他的说法。

---

**8. outweigh** /ˌaʊtˈweɪ/ *v.*

- **英文释义**：to be greater or more important than something else
- **中文释义**：超过；比……更重要；压过
- **语域**：正式 / 书面 / 新闻 / 学术
- **同义词**：surpass, override, overshadow, eclipse
- **拓展**：
  - 及物动词；常见搭配：*the benefits outweigh the risks* / *concerns outweigh hopes*
  - 常用于权衡利弊的表述，是写作论证的高频工具词

- **例句**：
  > Analysts argued that the long-term geopolitical risks of the intervention far **outweighed** any short-term strategic gains.
  > 分析人士认为，此次干预的长期地理政治风险远远超过任何短期战略收益。

---

**9. shedding light on** *phrase*

- **英文释义**：to make something clearer or easier to understand; to reveal information about something
- **中文释义**：阐明；揭示；使……更清晰
- **语域**：正式 / 新闻 / 学术
- **同义词**：illuminate, reveal, clarify, elucidate
- **拓展**：
  - 固定搭配：*shed light on sth* / *potentially shedding light on...*
  - 名词短语：*cast new light on* / *throw light on*（同义变体）

- **例句**：
  > The newly declassified documents are expected to **shed light on** the covert financial networks that sustained the operation.
  > 这批新解密文件预计将揭示维持该行动运转的秘密金融网络。

---

**10. self-contradictory** /ˌselfˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəri/ *adj.*

- **英文释义**：containing elements that are inconsistent with or oppose one another
- **中文释义**：自相矛盾的
- **语域**：书面 / 学术 / 正式评论
- **同义词**：inconsistent, paradoxical, incoherent, contradictory
- **拓展**：
  - 常见搭配：*a self-contradictory statement / position / argument*
  - 近义区分：*self-contradictory*（逻辑层面互相矛盾）vs. *inconsistent*（前后不一致，程度稍弱）

- **例句**：
  > The minister's **self-contradictory** remarks on the peace negotiations led commentators to question whether any coherent strategy existed.
  > 这位部长在和平谈判上自相矛盾的表态让评论人士质疑是否存在任何连贯的战略。

---

**11. fixation** /fɪkˈseɪʃn/ *n.*

- **英文释义**：an extreme and often unhealthy interest in or preoccupation with something
- **中文释义**：执念；过度关注；痴迷
- **语域**：正式 / 新闻 / 心理学
- **同义词**：obsession, preoccupation, compulsion
- **拓展**：
  - 可数/不可数均可；常见搭配：*sb's fixation on/with sth*

**38. cast as** /kæst æz/ *phr. v.*

**39. pop off** /pɑːp ɔːf/ *phr. v.*（熟词僻义）

- **同义词（义②）**：sound off, mouth off, blurt out, rant

## 4D. 金句积累

**金句一**

> **"Victory has a thousand fathers, but defeat is an orphan."**
> — Matthew Yglesias（引自肯尼迪总统典故变体）

**中文翻译**：
> 胜利有千个父亲，失败是孤儿。

**语境与用法**：
这是英语政治写作中最经典的格言之一，源自意大利谚语，由美国总统肯尼迪在1961年猪湾事件后引用而广为流传。适用于任何涉及"责任归属""政策失败后无人担责"的写作场景。可直接引用，亦可仿写：*"In the current political climate, policy success claims a thousand architects, while its failures become — inevitably — orphans."*

---

**金句二**

> **"Most Americans are too busy for social media, too normal for politics, too rational to tweet."**
> — Axios

**中文翻译**：
> 大多数美国人太忙碌，无暇顾及社交媒体；太普通，无意卷入政治漩涡；太理性，不屑于在推特上发声。

**语境与用法**：
这句话以三重排比（*too... for / too... for / too... to*）的句式，以反讽的方式揭示社交媒体舆论的失真性。该句式本身即是高分写作的示范结构，可仿写为：*"Most citizens are too pragmatic for ideology, too hardworking for outrage, too experienced for empty slogans."*

---

**金句三**

> **"This isn't just about the price of oil. It's about everything oil becomes — fertilizer, AI chips, plastic — and the cost of snapping the Achilles heel of the global shipping economy."**
> — Derek Thompson

**中文翻译**：
> 这不仅仅关乎石油价格本身。它关乎石油所转化的一切——化肥、AI芯片、塑料——以及切断全球航运经济致命命脉的代价。

**语境与用法**：
此句是议论文"层层递进、扩大论证范围"的典范写法。以 *"This isn't just about X. It's about Y — a, b, c — and the cost of Z"* 的句式呈现因果链，将局部议题升华至全球系统性风险。*"Achilles heel"* 与 *"snapping"* 的比喻连用，使抽象经济概念具象化，是高级学术写作的有效技巧。

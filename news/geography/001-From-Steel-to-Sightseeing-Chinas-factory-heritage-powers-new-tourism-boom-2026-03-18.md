# From Steel to Sightseeing: China's factory heritage powers new tourism boom

**Date:** 15:37, 07-Nov-2025  
**Source:** CGTN  
**Reporter:** Zheng Chunying (Beijing)

## Transcript

China's once-bustling factories and steel plants are finding a new lease on life – not through production, but through tourism. From Beijing's Shougang Industrial Park to former industrial hubs nationwide, sites once dominated by smoke and steel are being reborn as popular destinations. Zheng Chunying has more.

Once symbols of China's heavy industry, places like Beijing's Shougang Industrial Park are now drawing crowds of curious visitors. Across the country, industrial tourism is breathing new life into old factories and steel mills – transforming production sites into destinations of innovation and pride.

**ZHENG CHUNYING Beijing**  
"This used to be all about steel – smoke, fire, and furnaces. Now, Shougang Industrial Park stands as one of Beijing's most striking makeovers, where heavy industry has turned into a hub of creativity and green innovation."

Relocated ahead of the 2008 Olympics, Shougang has reinvented itself as a vibrant space for culture and technology. Its towering blast furnaces now house art exhibitions, cafes and creative studios – and even served as the stage for the Big Air event during the 2022 Winter Olympics.

**Visitor Interviews:**
- "I used to live here when the plant was still operating. It's amazing to see how the whole area has changed."
- "I think it's fascinating to blend art and culture into the old furnaces. It's also a great place for taking photos."

And Shougang Park is not alone.

According to Trip.com's 2025 National Day tourism data, high-tech and heavy-industry study tours have surged in popularity. Aerospace and automobile factory visits now account for over 20 per cent of travel searches.

Tourists say the boom reflects a growing interest in culture and history.

From repurposed steel plants to high-tech workshops, China's industrial tourism is redefining travel – blending education, innovation, and national pride. ZCY, CGTN, BJ.

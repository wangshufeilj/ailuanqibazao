# 甲骨文凌晨6点突发裁员3万人

**事件日期**：2026-03-31（当地时间）  
**摘要**：甲骨文向全球员工群发邮件宣布新一轮大规模裁员，媒体报道称波及约 3 万人（约 18% 全球员工），印度市场据传约 1.2 万岗位。

---

🔹 **甲骨文凌晨6点突发裁员3万人**  
🔸 **Oracle Unexpectedly Lays Off 30,000 Employees at 6 AM**

🔹 全球软件巨头**甲骨文（Oracle）**于当地时间3月31日凌晨向员工群发邮件，宣布启动**新一轮大规模裁员**。  
🔸 Global software giant **Oracle** sent a mass email to employees in the early hours of March 31 local time, announcing the commencement of a **new round of large-scale layoffs**.

🔹 多名收到通知的员工在社交媒体上披露，邮件措辞**极其直白**：“在认真评估甲骨文当前的业务需求后，我们决定**取消您的岗位**。今天是您的最后一个工作日。”  
🔸 Several employees who received the notice revealed on social media that the email was **startlingly blunt**: "After carefully evaluating Oracle's current business needs, we have decided to **eliminate your position**. Today is your last working day."

🔹 据多家媒体报道，此次裁员覆盖美国、印度、欧洲等多个市场，波及工程、云服务、技术支持等部门，受影响岗位可能达**3万人**，约占其全球员工总数的**18%**。  
🔸 According to multiple media reports, this layoff spans several markets including the U.S., India, and Europe, affecting departments such as engineering, cloud services, and technical support. The number of affected positions could reach **30,000**, accounting for approximately **18%** of its total global workforce.

🔹 其中，**印度市场**据传有约**1.2万个岗位**被裁撤。  
🔸 Among them, the **Indian market** is rumored to have about **12,000 positions** cut.

🔹 有网友评价，“甲骨文根本不是一家经营困难的公司，他们的**收入比以往任何时候都高**，但仍因为支出问题裁掉了3万人”。  
🔸 One netizen commented: "Oracle is by no means a company in financial distress; their **revenue is higher than ever**, yet they still eliminated 30,000 jobs due to spending concerns."

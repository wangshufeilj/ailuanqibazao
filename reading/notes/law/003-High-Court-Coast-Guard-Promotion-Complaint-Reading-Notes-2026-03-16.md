This article covers a High Court ruling in Trinidad and Tobago concerning military promotion grievance procedures and administrative accountability. - **翻译难点**：*instructed by* 在法律文书中意为"由……律师事务所委托"（solicitor委托barrister出庭），非"被……指示"
- **拓展**：
  - 本文"instructed by attorneys Mariah Ramrattan..."表示出庭大律师（counsel）是经由事务律师（attorney/solicitor）委托而出庭
  - 英联邦两级律师制度：*solicitor/attorney*（事务律师，直接接案）→ *barrister/counsel*（大律师，由事务律师指示出庭）
- **例句**：
  > Senior counsel, **instructed by** the firm of Chambers & Chambers, argued that the statutory provisions had been misapplied at first instance.
  > 由钱伯斯律师事务所委托出庭的资深大律师提出，一审法院对相关法定条款的适用存在错误。

**33. terminal** /ˈtɜːrmɪnl/ *adj.*

**34. advance** /ədˈvæns/ *v. / n. / adj.*

**35. maintain** /meɪnˈteɪn/ *v.*

- **英文释义（僻义）**：In legal/formal contexts, to assert or uphold a position persistently, often against opposing arguments. ≠ "to keep in good condition"
- **中文释义（僻义）**：坚持主张；坚称（某一立场）
- **语域**：法律 / 正式 / 书面
- **例句**：

**36. find** /faɪnd/ *v.*

- **英文释义（僻义）**：In legal usage, to determine as a fact or conclusion after consideration of evidence; to rule or hold. ≠ "to discover a lost object"
- **中文释义（僻义）**：（法院）认定；裁定；判定
- **语域**：法律 / 正式
- **拓展**：
  - 名词：*finding*（认定；调查结论）：*findings of fact*（事实认定）/ *findings of law*（法律认定）
  - 区分：*find*（认定事实或得出结论）vs. *hold*（就法律问题作出裁定）vs. *rule*（宣布裁决）—三者在法律文书中语义微妙不同
- **例句**：
  > The tribunal **found** that the employer had failed to follow its own disciplinary procedures, rendering the dismissal procedurally unfair.
  > 仲裁庭认定雇主未遵循其自身的纪律程序，致使解雇在程序上不公正。

**37. hold** /hoʊld/ *v.*

- **英文释义（僻义）**：In judicial contexts, to decide a point of law; to declare as a legal ruling. ≠ "to physically grasp"
- **中文释义（僻义）**：（法院就法律问题）裁定；判定
- **语域**：法律 / 正式
- **常见搭配**：the court held that... 法院裁定……/ held, that the...（判决书正文）
- **拓展**：
  - 区分：*hold*（就法律问题作权威裁定）vs. *find*（认定事实）vs. *conclude*（得出结论）
  - *holding*（名词）= the court's legal ruling on a point of law，区别于 *dictum/obiter*（法律旁述，无约束力）
- **例句**：
  > The Court of Appeal **held** that a public authority cannot rely on self-induced delay to justify a failure to comply with statutory deadlines.
  > 上诉法院裁定，公共机构不得以自身导致的延误为由，为其未能遵守法定期限的行为开脱。

**38. reject** /rɪˈdʒekt/ *v.*

- **拓展**：

**39. prestige** /preˈstiːʒ/ *n.*

**40. note** /noʊt/ *v.*

**42. approach** /əˈproʊtʃ/ *v.*

**45. lodge a complaint / lodge a formal request**

- **语域**：法律 / 正式 / 英联邦书面

**46. in favour of** /ɪn ˈfeɪvər əv/ *prep. phrase*

**48. over the following months** *adv. phrase*

**49. seek judicial review** *v. phrase*

- **英文释义**：To formally apply to a court to examine the lawfulness of a public body's decision or inaction; a standard procedural phrase in administrative law.
- **中文释义**：寻求/申请司法审查
- **语域**：法律 / 正式
- **同义词**：apply for judicial review, bring a judicial review claim, challenge by way of judicial review
- **拓展**：
  - 标准搭配链：*seek → obtain leave/permission → bring → succeed in judicial review*（申请→获许可→提起→胜诉司法审查）
  - 美国对应语境：*seek review under the Administrative Procedure Act (APA)* 或 *petition for writ of mandamus*[ref:22,23]
  - 注意：司法审查不审查决定是否"正确"，而只审查是否"合法"——这是最重要的考点
- **例句**：
  > The residents' association decided to **seek judicial review** of the planning authority's decision to approve the industrial development without an environmental impact assessment.
  > 居民协会决定就规划当局在未进行环境影响评估的情况下批准工业开发项目一事申请司法审查。

**50. on the basis of** *prep. phrase*

- **英文释义**：Using a particular fact, condition, or principle as the foundation or justification for an action or conclusion.
- **中文释义**：基于；以……为依据；依照
- **语域**：正式 / 法律 / 学术 / 书面
- **同义词**：based on, founded on, by virtue of, in light of, pursuant to (更正式)
- **拓展**：
  - 在法律论证中高频出现：*on the basis of seniority and competence*（基于资历与能力）
  - 书面写作中可替换：*on the grounds that*（以……为由）/ *by reason of*（由于）/ *in view of*（鉴于）
  - 辨析：*on the basis of*（较中性，描述依据）vs. *on the grounds that*（带有理由/借口含义，多用于争议性论述）
- **例句**：
  > The promotion committee evaluated all candidates strictly **on the basis of** their annual performance appraisals and operational experience records.
  > 晋升委员会严格依照年度绩效考核结果和实战经历记录对所有候选人进行评估。

**51. no decision was ever communicated to him** *v. phrase (pattern)*

- **英文释义**：A legal reporting pattern emphasizing that an official determination was never formally conveyed to the party concerned, thereby establishing procedural failure.
- **中文释义**：始终未有任何决定被告知于他（强调行政不作为的核心事实模式）
- **语域**：法律报道 / 正式叙事
- **拓展**：
  - *communicate a decision*（正式通知某项决定）是行政法中的重要概念——决定作出与决定送达是两个独立的法律步骤；未送达即等同于程序未完成
  - 类似模式：*no response was forthcoming*（始终未获回应）/ *the matter was left unresolved*（事项悬而未决）/ *the complaint went unanswered*（投诉石沉大海）
- **例句**：
  > Despite submitting three written requests over an eight-month period, **no decision was ever communicated to** the applicant regarding her reclassification request.
  > 尽管申请人在八个月内先后提交了三次书面申请，始终未收到任何关于其重新分类申请的正式答复。

**52. in granting relief** *participial phrase*

- **英文释义**：A standard legal writing phrase introducing the remedies issued by the court following its findings; ≈ "in awarding the remedies"
- **中文释义**：在给予救济/裁定补救措施时（引出法院判决结果的标准套语）
- **语域**：法律 / 正式 / 判决书写作
- **拓展**：
  - 类似导入结构：*In allowing the application...* / *In upholding the claim...* / *In dismissing the appeal...*
  - 该结构后通常跟随 *the court declared that... / ordered that... / held that...*，构成完整的判决描述句式
- **例句**：
  > **In granting relief**, the tribunal made clear that the respondent's pattern of delay had caused measurable harm to the claimant's professional standing and pension entitlement.
  > 在作出救济裁定时，仲裁庭明确指出，被申请人的一贯拖延行为对申请人的职业声誉及退休金权益造成了可量化的损害。

**53. a delay of approximately [X] months** *n. phrase (pattern)*

- **英文释义**：A factual legal phrase quantifying the duration of administrative inaction as a basis for establishing unreasonableness.
- **中文释义**：约[X]个月的延迟（用于量化行政不作为期间的标准表达）
- **语域**：法律 / 正式
- **拓展**：
  - 在司法审查判决书中，法官对延迟时长的精确量化是裁定"不合理"的关键步骤；美国APA框架下，法院采用"TRAC因素"（Telecommunications Research & Action Center v. FCC）测试延迟合理性[ref:23,26]
  - 写作应用：论述行政效率或程序正义时，量化时间维度能显著增强论证说服力
- **例句**：
  > **A delay of approximately 18 months** in processing the permit application was held to be unreasonable given that the applicable regulations prescribed a 60-day determination period.
  > 考虑到相关法规规定了60天的裁决期，约18个月的许可申请处理延迟被裁定为不合理。

**54. ordering [party] to pay costs** *v. phrase*

- **英文释义**：A standard judicial order requiring the losing party to pay the legal costs incurred by the winning party.
- **中文释义**：命令（某方）承担诉讼费用（败诉方付费原则）
- **语域**：法律 / 正式
- **拓展**：
  - 英联邦诉讼成本规则："costs follow the event"（费用随结果）——通常败诉方须支付胜诉方合理律师费
  - 美国规则（American Rule）：各方通常自付律师费，除非有法规明确规定或法院行使裁量权
  - 相关术语：*taxed costs*（经核定的诉讼费用）/ *indemnity costs*（全额补偿费用，惩罚性）/ *party-and-party costs*（标准对方费用）
- **例句**：
  > Having succeeded on all grounds of judicial review, the claimant was **ordered** by the court **to pay** nothing; instead, the defendant authority was **ordered to pay** the claimant's costs on a standard basis.
  > 申请人在司法审查的所有事由上均获胜，无需支付任何费用；反之，被告机关被命令按标准基础承担申请人的诉讼费用。

**55. constitute a valid justification** *v. phrase*

- **英文释义**：To amount to or serve as a legally or logically acceptable reason for an action or omission.
- **中文释义**：构成正当理由；足以成为有效辩解
- **语域**：法律 / 正式 / 学术
- **同义词**：amount to adequate grounds, serve as sufficient justification, provide legal excuse
- **反义词**：fail to justify, provide no excuse for
- **拓展**：
  - 高频法律写作模式：*X does/does not constitute a valid justification for Y*（X是否构成Y的正当理由）
  - 类似表达：*afford a defence*（构成抗辩理由）/ *provide lawful authority*（提供合法授权）
- **例句**：
  > The court found that budgetary constraints alone did not **constitute a valid justification** for the authority's failure to provide the claimant with a timely response.
  > 法院认定，仅凭预算限制不足以构成当局未能及时答复申请人的正当理由。

**56. bring to a close / draw to a close** *v. phrase*

- **英文释义**：To end or conclude a process, proceeding, or matter formally.
- **中文释义**：正式结束；告一段落
- **语域**：书面 / 正式 / 演讲
- **拓展**：
  - 在法律/行政语境中，*bring [proceedings] to a close* 或 *draw matters to a resolution* 均为地道书面表达
  - 写作亮点：比 *end/finish* 更具正式感和文学性，适用于报告结尾或论文收官段落

## 4D. 金句积累

---

**金句一**

> **"The issue to be decided was not complex and could have been resolved once the seniority report and Cabinet Minute were available by November 2023."**

**中文翻译：**
> "待裁决的事项并不复杂，一旦资历报告与内阁决议文件于2023年11月备齐，本应即可作出决定。"

**写作价值：**
这句话揭示了行政延误的本质——不是客观困难，而是主观怠惰。适用于论述行政效率、程序正义或官僚惰性时引用，具有强烈的批判力度。

---

**金句二**

> **"A delay of approximately 13 months, particularly when Lightbourne was approaching his compulsory retirement date, was unreasonable."**

**中文翻译：**
> "约13个月的延迟，尤其是在莱特本正临近其强制退役日的情况下，属于不合理的拖延。"

**写作价值：**
该句示范了如何将抽象的"不合理"标准落地为具体事实论证——以时间长度（13个月）叠加时间节点敏感性（退役临近）构建双重论证，是法律事实分析的经典范式，也可迁移至任何涉及"时间紧迫性"的论述场景。

---

**金句三**

> **"Lightbourne contended that he had a legitimate expectation of promotion based on his seniority and competence."**

**中文翻译：**
> "莱特本主张，基于其资历与能力，他对晋升具有合法期望。"

**写作价值：**
此句将两个核心法律概念（*legitimate expectation* 和 *seniority/competence*）凝练为一个论点句，是行政法论文写作中主张段落的标准句式。*contend that + [legal principle] + based on + [factual basis]* 这一论证结构可直接套用于各类权利主张场景。

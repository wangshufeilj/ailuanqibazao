### 文章基本信息
**来源**：RT (Russia Today)
**题目**：Kenyan police rescue foreign nationals from trafficking ring (肯尼亚警方从贩卖集团手中营救外国公民)
**日期**：2026年3月6日
**作者背景**：本文为RT非洲新闻编辑部（RT Africa）发布的实事报道。RT（前身为Russia Today）是一家总部位于俄罗斯的国际新闻网络，其非洲频道关注中非、东非等地的社会安全、主权治理及国际犯罪打击等议题。
**背景注释**：
*   **Ruai area**: 位于肯尼亚首都内罗毕（Nairobi）东部的一个快速发展的半城市化地区，常作为各类仓库或中转站。
*   **Ethiopia & Eritrea**: 埃塞俄比亚与厄立特里亚，两国均位于非洲之角。由于长期存在的冲突、干旱及经济不稳定，这两国公民常通过肯尼亚的中转路径前往南非或欧洲。
*   **Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA)**: 乔莫·肯雅塔国际机场，东非最大的航空枢纽。
*   **William Ruto**: 威廉·鲁托，肯尼亚第五任总统，2022年就职。其政府致力于打击跨国犯罪和提升区域安全。

>     *   注意：在本文语境下特指"警务行动"。
>     *   例句：The search and rescue **operation** continued throughout the night despite the challenging weather conditions. (尽管天气条件恶劣，搜救**行动**仍持续了整个晚上。)

🔹 **A month earlier, / around 60 individuals, / both foreigners and Ghanaians, / who had been exploited / in sexual and cybercrime schemes, / were rescued.**
🔸 **一个月前，/ 大约60名个人（/ 既有外国人也有加纳人），/ 他们曾在性犯罪和网络犯罪阴谋中 / 遭到剥削，/ 最终获救。**

> *   **Individual** [N-COUNT] (W) /ˌɪndə'vɪdʒuəl/ : a person, considered separately from the rest of the group or society.
>     *   W：写作中常用来替换person。
>     *   例句：Each **individual** in the society has a responsibility to contribute to the common good. (社会中的每个**个人**都有责任为公共利益做出贡献。)
> *   **Exploit** [V-T] (R/W) /ɪk'splɔɪt/ : to treat someone unfairly by asking them to do things for you, but giving them very little in return.
>     *   R/W高频：剥削，开发利用（正负面皆可，此处为负面）。
>     *   例句：Strict labor laws are necessary to prevent corporations from **exploiting** vulnerable workers. (为了防止公司**剥削**弱势劳工，严格的劳动法是必要的。)
> *   **Cybercrime** [N-UNCOUNT] (R) /'saɪbəkraɪm/ : crime that is committed using the internet or computers.
>     *   例句：As more services move online, the threat of **cybercrime** continues to grow in scale and complexity. (随着更多服务转向线上，**网络犯罪**的威胁在规模和复杂性上持续增长。)

> *   **Similarly** [ADV] (W) /'sɪmələli/ : in a similar way.
>     *   W：雅思写作常用连接词，表示类比。
>     *   例句：High-quality education boosts economic growth; **similarly**, investment in healthcare improves national productivity. (高质量的教育能促进经济增长；**同样地**，在医疗保健方面的投资能提高国家的生产力。)
> *   **Border** [N-COUNT] (R) /'bɔːdə/ : the official line that separates two countries or regions.
>     *   例句：The agreement aims to streamline the process for transporting goods across the international **border**. (该协议旨在简化跨境运输货物的流程。)
> *   **Refugee camp** [N-COUNT] (R) : a place where people who have been forced to leave their country live in temporary buildings.
>     *   背景：国际关系与社会安全话题。
>     *   例句：International aid agencies are providing essential food and medical supplies to the **refugee camps** in the region. (国际援助机构正向该地区的**难民营**提供必要的食品和医疗用品。)

> *   **Question** [V-T] (R) /'kwestʃən/ : to ask someone questions in order to get information about something, especially a crime.
>     *   熟词僻义：此处为动词"审问、讯问"。
>     *   例句：Witnesses were **questioned** by the legal team to gather more evidence for the upcoming trial. (法律团队对证人进行了**询问**，以为即将到来的审判搜集更多证据。)
> *   **Urge** [V-T] (W) /ɜːdʒ/ : to strongly suggest that someone does something.
>     *   搭配：**urge** somebody **to do** something.
>     *   例句：Environmentalists **urge** governments **to** adopt more ambitious targets for reducing greenhouse gas emissions. (环保主义者**敦促**各国政府采取更宏大的减排目标。)
> *   **Ensure** [V-T] (W) /ɪn'ʃʊə/ : to make certain that something will happen properly.
>     *   例句：Quality control measures are implemented to **ensure** that every product meets the safety standards. (实施质量控制措施是为了**确保**每一件产品都符合安全标准。)
> *   **Stranger** [N-COUNT] (R) /'streɪndʒə/ : someone that you do not know.
>     *   例句：Children are often taught from a young age not to accept gifts from **strangers**. (孩子们从小就被教育不要接受**陌生人**的礼物。)

# 精读笔记：教育部办公厅关于开展中小学阳光招生专项行动（2026年）的通知

## 文章基本信息

- **文章来源**：中华人民共和国教育部官网 (Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China)
- **文章题目**：教育部办公厅关于开展中小学阳光招生专项行动（2026年）的通知 (Notice of the General Office of the Ministry of Education on Carrying Out the Special Action for "Sunshine Enrollment" in Primary and Secondary Schools (2026))
- **发布机构**：教育部办公厅 (General Office of the Ministry of Education)
- **作者背景**：教育部办公厅是教育部的综合办事机构。其主要职能包括负责文电、会务、机密电报、档案等机关日常运转工作；承担信息、建议提案办理、信访、安全保密、政务公开等工作。在政策发布中，办公厅负责统筹协调并向下级教育行政部门传达中央及部的决策部署。

## 逐句精读解析

**🔹Notice / of the General Office / of the Ministry of Education / on Carrying Out / the Special Action / for "Sunshine Enrollment" / in Primary and Secondary Schools / (2026).**  
**🔸教育部办公厅 / 关于开展 / 中小学 / 阳光招生 / 专项行动 / （2026年）/ 的通知。**

> **重点词汇/词组解析**
> 1. **Carry out / 开展；执行**
> [v. phr.] To perform a task or put a planned course of action into practice. (执行、开展、实施)
> **语域**：正式/政务。
> **拓展内容**：近义词包括 **implement**, **execute**, **conduct**。常见搭配：**carry out an investigation** (开展调查), **carry out a policy** (执行政策)。在考研/雅思写作中，表达“政府实施了某项措施”时，`carry out` 是极佳的替代 `do` 的正式短语。
> 2. **Enrollment / 招生；入学人数**
> [n. U/C] The act of officially joining a course, school, or university; the number of people enrolled. (招生，注册人数)
> **语域**：教育。
> **拓展内容**：动词形式为 **enroll** (美) / **enrol** (英)。常见搭配：**enrollment rate** (入学率), **open enrollment** (开放式招生)。易混辨析：**Admission** 侧重于“准许进入、录取”的过程，而 **enrollment** 侧重于“注册、正式加入”的动作。

**🔹The education departments / of all provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities / directly under the Central Government, / and the Education Bureau / of the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps:**  
**🔸各省、自治区、直辖市 / 教育厅（教委），/ 新疆生产建设兵团 / 教育局：**

> **背景注释**
> **Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps (XPCC)**: 新疆生产建设兵团，是中国的一个特殊组织，承担着国家赋予的屯垦戍边职责，实行党、政、军、企合一的体制，受中央政府和新疆维吾尔自治区双重领导。

> **重点词汇/词组解析**
> 1. **Municipality / 直辖市；自治市**
> [n. C] A city or town that has corporate status and local government. (直辖市，地方自治市)
> **语域**：行政/法律。
> **拓展内容**：在中国的行政语境下，专门指 **municipality directly under the Central Government**。复数形式为 **municipalities**。派生词：**municipal** [adj.] (市政的，市级的)，如 **municipal government** (市政府)。
> 2. **Autonomous / 自治的**
> [adj.] (of a country or region) having the freedom to govern itself or control its own affairs. (自治的，有自主权的)
> **语域**：政治/正式。
> **拓展内容**：名词为 **autonomy** (自治权)。在教育语境中，**learner autonomy** 指“学习自主性”。同义词：**self-governing**, **independent**。

**🔹To continuously improve / the level / of standardization and scientific management / in primary and secondary school / admissions, / effectively safeguard / educational equity, / and protect the equal rights / of school-age children and adolescents / to receive education, / the Ministry of Education / has decided / to launch / the Special Action / for "Sunshine Enrollment" / (2026).**  
**🔸为持续提升 / 中小学招生入学工作 / 的规范化与科学化水平，/ 切实维护教育公平，/ 保障适龄儿童少年 / 平等受教育权利，/ 教育部决定 / 开展 / 中小学阳光招生 / 专项行动 / （2026年）。**

> **重点词汇/词组解析**
> 1. **Standardization / 规范化；标准化**
> [n. U] The process of making something conform to a standard. (规范化，标准化)
> **语域**：正式/工业/管理。
> **拓展内容**：动词为 **standardize**。在考试中，常用于讨论教育改革、生产流程。反义词：**diversification** (多样化)。
> 2. **Safeguard / 维护；保护**
> [v. T] To protect something from harm or damage with an appropriate measure. (保护，卫护)
> **语域**：正式/法律/政治。
> **拓展内容**：既可作动词也可作名词。常见搭配：**safeguard national security** (维护国家安全), **safeguard the interests of...** (维护……的利益)。近义词：**protect**, **preserve**, **uphold** (尤指维护法律或原则)。
> 3. **Equity / 公平；公正**
> [n. U] The situation in which everyone is treated fairly according to their needs. (公平，公正)
> **语域**：法律/社科。
> **拓展内容**：**Equity** 不同于 **Equality**。**Equality** 强调“平均、相同”，而 **Equity** 强调“因地制宜的公平”。考研英语常考词。同义词：**fairness**, **impartiality**。
> 4. **School-age / 适龄的**
> [adj.] Relating to the age at which children are required by law to attend school. (学龄的，适龄的)
> **语域**：教育。
> **拓展内容**：通常用作定语。如 **school-age population** (学龄人口)。与之相关的词有 **preschooler** (学前儿童), **adolescent** (青少年)。

**🔹Building on the achievements / of consolidating / the special action / for "Sunshine Enrollment" / in compulsory education, / the specific requirements / are notified / as follows.**  
**🔸在巩固 / 义务教育 / 阳光招生 / 专项行动成果 / 的基础上，/ 现将有关工作要求 / 通知如下。**

> **背景注释**
> **Compulsory Education**: 义务教育。在中国通常指九年制义务教育，包括小学（6年）和初中（3年）。

> **重点词汇/词组解析**
> 1. **Consolidate / 巩固；加强**
> [v. T/I] To make a position of power or success stronger so that it is more likely to continue. (巩固，使加强)
> **语域**：正式/政治/商业。
> **拓展内容**：名词为 **consolidation**。常见搭配：**consolidate one's lead** (巩固领先地位), **consolidate assets** (合并资产)。在学术写作中，常用其表示“强化研究成果”或“合并数据”。
> 2. **Notify / 通知；告知**
> [v. T] To inform someone or give notice to them of something officially. (官方通知，告知)
> **语域**：正式/法律。
> **拓展内容**：用法为 **notify somebody of something** 或 **notify somebody that...**。名词为 **notification**。易混词：**Inform** (通用语，较随意), **Apprise** (极其正式)。

**🔹I. Work Objectives**  
**🔸一、工作目标**

**🔹Consolidate the results / of the special action, / broaden / its coverage, / and fully implement / policies / such as / entrance without exams / for schools near one's residence / in compulsory education, / localized enrollment / for regular senior high schools, / and "synchronized enrollment / of public and private schools."**  
**🔸巩固 / 专项行动成果，/ 拓宽 / 专项行动覆盖范围，/ 全面落实 / 义务教育 / 免试就近入学、/ 普通高中 / 属地招生 / 和“公民同招”等政策。**

> **背景注释**
> 1. **Entrance without exams for schools near one's residence**: “免试就近入学”，中国义务教育阶段的核心政策，旨在减轻学生负担。
> 2. **Localized enrollment**: “属地招生”，指高中学校主要在学校所在行政区域内招生，防止跨区域抢夺生源。
> 3. **Synchronized enrollment of public and private schools**: “公民同招”，指公办学校和民办学校同时报名、同时录取，防止民办学校提前“掐尖”。

> **重点词汇/词组解析**
> 1. **Broaden / 拓宽；扩大**
> [v. T/I] To become wider or make something wider; to increase the range of something. (变宽，扩大范围)
> **语域**：通用/正式。
> **拓展内容**：常见搭配：**broaden one's horizons** (开阔眼界), **broaden the scope** (扩大范围)。类似构词法：**deepen** (加深), **strengthen** (加强), **lengthen** (拉长)。
> 2. **Implement / 实施；贯彻**
> [v. T] To start using a plan or system. (实施，履行)
> **语域**：正式/行政。
> **拓展内容**：名词形式相同（但重音不同，作名词时指“工具”）。常见搭配：**implement a strategy** (执行策略), **implement a ban** (执行禁令)。雅思写作高频词。
> 3. **Localized / 属地的；地方化的**
> [adj.] Confined or restricted to a particular location. (地方性的，局部的)
> **语域**：通用/科学/政治。
> **拓展内容**：动词为 **localize**。在 IT 行业指“本地化”，在医疗指“局部病变”。此处对应中文“属地”。

**🔹Further improve / the balanced class assignment system / for compulsory education, / strictly regulate / specific types of enrollment / in primary and secondary schools, / and continuously strengthen / the long-term mechanism / characterized by / fairness, openness, / standardization, transparency, / and strong supervision.**  
**🔸进一步完善 / 义务教育 / 均衡编班制度，/ 从严规范 / 中小学特定类型招生，/ 不断健全 / 公平公开、/ 规范透明、/ 监管有力 / 的长效机制。**

> **重点词汇/词组解析**
> 1. **Regulate / 规范；管理**
> [v. T] To control an activity or process by means of rules and principles. (管理，规范，调节)
> **语域**：正式/法律/商业。
> **拓展内容**：名词 **regulation** (规章制度)。常用词组：**highly regulated industry** (高度监管的行业)。辨析：**Control** 侧重掌控力，而 **Regulate** 侧重依据规则进行的秩序化管理。
> 2. **Mechanism / 机制**
> [n. C] A system of parts working together in a machine; a natural or established process by which something takes place. (机械装置，机制)
> **语域**：正式/科学/政治。
> **拓展内容**：在政策文章中，常指“制度化的运作方式”。常见搭配：**market mechanism** (市场机制), **coping mechanism** (应对机制)。
> 3. **Transparency / 透明度**
> [n. U] The characteristic of being easy to see through; the quality of being done in an open way without secrets. (透明，透明度)
> **语域**：通用/政治/商业。
> **拓展内容**：形容词为 **transparent**。在政府治理中，**transparency** 是核心价值之一。反义词：**opacity** (不透明)。

**🔹II. Key Tasks**  
**🔸二、重点任务**

**🔹(1) Strengthening / the forward-looking layout / of resources.**  
**🔸（一）强化 / 资源前瞻布局。**

> **重点词汇/词组解析**
> 1. **Forward-looking / 前瞻性的**
> [adj.] Planning for the future; innovative. (向前看的，有远见的)
> **语域**：正式/商务。
> **拓展内容**：同义词：**proactive**, **visionary**, **prescient**。常用于经济预测或政策规划。
> 2. **Layout / 布局；规划**
> [n. C] The way in which text or pictures are set out on a page; the way in which the parts of something are arranged. (布局，设计)
> **语域**：通用/设计/建筑。
> **拓展内容**：动词短语为 **lay out**。在教育政策中，**resource layout** 指的是学校、教师、设备在空间和时间上的分配。

**🔹Localities / should coordinate / factors / such as / birth rates, / urbanization rates, / population mobility, / industrial layout, / and household registration system reform / to build a predictive database / for the school-age population.**  
**🔸各地 / 要统筹考虑 / 人口出生率、/ 城镇化率、/ 人口流动、/ 产业布局、/ 户籍制度改革等因素，/ 建好学龄人口预测数据库。**

> **背景注释**
> **Household Registration System (Hukou)**: 户籍制度。中国特有的人口管理制度，与教育、医疗、社会保障等公共资源的分配紧密挂钩。

> **重点词汇/词组解析**
> 1. **Coordinate / 统筹；协调**
> [v. T/I] To make many different things work effectively as a whole. (协调，配合)
> **语域**：通用/正式。
> **拓展内容**：名词为 **coordination**，形容词为 **coordinated**。常用搭配：**coordinate efforts** (协同努力)。在政府文件中，常对应“统筹”。
> 2. **Mobility / 流动性**
> [n. U] The ability to move or be moved freely and easily. (流动性，灵活性)
> **语域**：社科/正式。
> **拓展内容**：形容词为 **mobile**。常见短语：**social mobility** (社会流动性), **labor mobility** (劳动力流动)。在人口学中指人口迁移。
> 3. **Predictive / 预测性的**
> [adj.] Relating to the ability to predict what will happen or be needed in the future. (预测的，预兆的)
> **语域**：学术/正式。
> **拓展内容**：动词为 **predict**，名词为 **prediction**。常见短语：**predictive analytics** (预测分析)。

**🔹Conduct / regular investigations / of basic school conditions / such as / school buildings and assets, / teacher staffing, / and equipment configuration / at the county level, / accurately analyze and assess / the shortage or surplus / of basic education seats / in various stages and years, / and issue / seat prediction warnings.**  
**🔸定期 / 以县域为单位 / 开展 / 校舍资产、/ 教师配备、/ 装备配置等 / 基本办学条件摸排，/ 精准分析研判 / 各学段各年度 / 基础教育学位余缺状况，/ 进行 / 学位预测预警。**

> **重点词汇/词组解析**
> 1. **Investigation / 调查；摸排**
> [n. C/U] A formal or systematic examination or research. (调查，审查)
> **语域**：通用/法律/科学。
> **拓展内容**：动词为 **investigate**。在政策语境下，“摸排”常翻译为 **thorough investigation** 或 **screening**。
> 2. **Staffing / 人员配备**
> [n. U] The providing of people to work in an organization. (职员配备)
> **语域**：商业/行政。
> **拓展内容**：动词为 **staff**。如：**The office is staffed by volunteers.** (办公室由志愿者负责)。
> 3. **Configuration / 配置；布局**
> [n. C] An arrangement of elements in a particular form, figure, or combination. (结构，配置)
> **语域**：技术/正式。
> **拓展内容**：常用于计算机硬件或软件设置。在教育中指教学设备的配比。
> 4. **Surplus / 过剩；余缺**
> [n. C/U] An amount that is extra or more than you need. (剩余，过剩)
> **语域**：经济/正式。
> **拓展内容**：反义词为 **deficit** (赤字，逆差) 或 **shortage** (短缺)。常见搭配：**budget surplus** (预算盈余)。

**🔹Actively adapt / to changes / in the school-age population, / properly handle / adjustments / to school district zoning / and school layout, / perform / scientific and comprehensive evaluations, / solicit opinions / extensively, / announce to the public / in advance, / and do a good job / in policy interpretation and publicity.**  
**🔸积极适应 / 学龄人口变化，/ 妥善处理好 / 学校片区划分调整、/ 学校布局调整等，/ 科学全面评估，/ 广泛征求意见，/ 提前向社会公布，/ 做好 / 政策解读和宣传。**

> **重点词汇/词组解析**
> 1. **Solicit / 征求；索求**
> [v. T] To ask someone for money, information, or help. (恳求，征求)
> **语域**：正式。
> **拓展内容**：常见搭配：**solicit opinions/advice** (征求意见)。注意：在某些语境下（如街头）该词可能指拉客，但在政府/商业文件中是“正式征求”的标准用词。
> 2. **Zoning / 划分；分区**
> [n. U] The process of dividing an area into zones. (分区制)
> **语域**：城市规划/法律。
> **拓展内容**：此处指学校所属的地理区域划分。动词为 **zone**。
> 3. **Extensively / 广泛地**
> [adv.] In a way that covers or affects a large area. (广泛地，大规模地)
> **语域**：通用/正式。
> **拓展内容**：形容词为 **extensive**。近义词：**widely**, **broadly**。

**🔹(2) Strictly prohibiting / various types / of illegal recruitment.**  
**🔸（二）严禁 / 各类 / 违规招生。**

> **重点词汇/词组解析**
> 1. **Prohibit / 禁止**
> [v. T] To officially forbid something. (禁止，阻止)
> **语域**：正式/法律。
> **拓展内容**：名词为 **prohibition**。用法：**prohibit someone from doing something**。近义词：**ban**, **forbid**。**Prohibit** 语气最重，且带有法律效力。

**🔹Compulsory education schools / must fully implement / the policy / of entrance without exams / for schools near one's residence, / and are strictly forbidden / from selecting students / through interviews, / evaluations, / or receiving resumes.**  
**🔸义务教育学校 / 全面实施 / 免试就近入学，/ 严禁 / 以面试、/ 评测、/ 接收简历等方式 / 选拔学生。**

> **重点词汇/词组解析**
> 1. **Interview / 面试**
> [n. C / v. T] A formal meeting in which one or more persons question, consult, or evaluate another person. (面试，采访)
> **语域**：通用。

## 模块四：素材与语料库积累

### 4A. 重点词汇解析

---

#### **W — 写作高频词**

---

**W1. equitable**
- **音标** /ˈekwɪtəbl/ *adj.*
- **英文释义** Fair and reasonable in a way that gives equal treatment to everyone involved. *(Longman)*
- **中文释义** 公平合理的；公正的
- **语域** 正式 / 书面 / 学术 / 法律
- **同义词** fair, just, impartial, unbiased **反义词** inequitable, unfair, biased
- **常见词组** equitable distribution / equitable access / equitable treatment / equitable solution
- **拓展**
  - *equitably* (adv.) — 公平地；*inequitable* (adj.) — 不公平的；*equity* (n., 不可数) — 公平；衡平法（法律语境）
  - 区分：**equitable**（程序或结果公平合理）vs. **equal**（数量或待遇完全相同）。法律中"equitable relief"指衡平救济，区别于普通法救济（legal remedy）。
  - 中文政策文本中"公平公正"常译为 *equitable and just* 或 *fair and equitable*。
- **例句**
  - *The government must ensure an **equitable allocation** of educational resources across urban and rural schools.*
    政府必须确保城乡学校之间对教育资源进行公平分配。

---

**W2. compulsory**
- **音标** /kəmˈpʌlsəri/ *adj.*
- **英文释义** That must be done because of a rule, law, etc.; required by law or a rule; obligatory. *(Cambridge)*
- **中文释义** 强制性的；义务的；必须履行的
- **语域** 正式 / 法律 / 新闻
- **同义词** mandatory, obligatory, required **反义词** voluntary, optional, elective
- **常见词组** compulsory education / compulsory schooling / compulsory attendance / compulsory military service
- **拓展**
  - 仅作定语形容词，不作表语（不说 *Education is compulsory to attend*，应说 *Attendance is compulsory*）。
  - 区分：**compulsory**（法律强制）vs. **mandatory**（规定要求，语义略宽）vs. **obligatory**（偏道义强制）
  - "义务教育" 正式英译为 *compulsory education*，法律文本亦见 *free and compulsory education*。
- **例句**
  - ***Compulsory education** in China spans nine years, covering six years of primary school and three years of junior secondary school.*
    中国义务教育为期九年，涵盖六年小学和三年初中阶段。

---

**W3. transparent**
- **音标** /trænsˈpærənt/ *adj.*
- **英文释义** Open and honest; not keeping anything secret; easy for people to know about and understand. *(Longman)*
- **中文释义** 透明的；公开的；不隐瞒的
- **语域** 正式 / 政务 / 新闻 / 学术
- **同义词** open, clear, candid, accountable **反义词** opaque, secretive
- **常见词组** transparent process / transparent governance / transparent mechanism / transparent criteria
- **拓展**
  - *transparency* (n., 不可数) — 透明度；*transparently* (adv.)
  - 政策语境中"公开透明"固定译法：*open and transparent*
  - 中文"阳光招生"中的"阳光"意象与英语的 *transparent / sunshine policy* 相呼应。
- **例句**
  - *A **transparent** admissions process allows parents and students to verify that selection criteria are applied consistently.*
    公开透明的招生程序使家长和学生能够核实录取标准是否得到一致执行。

---

**W4. standardize**
- **音标** /ˈstændərdaɪz/ *v.*
- **英文释义** To make things of the same type all have the same basic features; to cause something to conform to a standard. *(Cambridge)*
- **中文释义** 使规范化；使标准化；统一
- **语域** 正式 / 行政 / 学术
- **同义词** normalize, regulate, systematize, codify **反义词** diversify, customize
- **常见词组** standardize procedures / standardize enrollment / standardize testing / standardized management
- **拓展**
  - *standardization* (n., 不可数) — 标准化；*standardized* (adj.) — 标准化的
  - 不规则变化：无（规则动词）
  - 区分：**standardize**（统一为同一标准）vs. **regulate**（通过法规管控）
- **例句**
  - *Authorities have moved to **standardize** the class assignment process to prevent schools from forming elite tracks.*
    当局已着手规范编班程序，以防止学校形成精英分层班。

---

**W5. accountability**
- **音标** /əˌkaʊntəˈbɪlɪti/ *n. (不可数)*
- **英文释义** A situation in which people or organizations are responsible for their actions and have to explain them when they are asked. *(Longman)*
- **中文释义** 问责制；问责性；责任担当
- **语域** 正式 / 政务 / 学术 / 新闻
- **同义词** responsibility, answerability, liability **反义词** impunity, unaccountability
- **常见词组** accountability mechanism / accountability system / political accountability / hold sb. accountable
- **拓展**
  - *accountable* (adj.) — 应负责任的；*account for* — 对……负责；解释
  - 搭配：**be held accountable for** sth. 为某事被追责
  - 中文"严肃追究相关责任"常译为 *hold relevant parties strictly accountable*
- **例句**
  - *Without a robust **accountability** mechanism, violations of enrollment regulations will go unpunished.*
    若缺乏健全的问责机制，违规招生行为将难逃惩处。

---

**W6. mitigate**
- **音标** /ˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/ *v.*
- **英文释义** To make a situation or the effects of something less severe, serious, or painful. *(Cambridge)*
- **中文释义** 减轻；缓解；降低（风险/损害）
- **语域** 正式 / 法律 / 学术 / 政策
- **同义词** alleviate, reduce, lessen, minimize **反义词** aggravate, exacerbate, worsen
- **常见词组** mitigate risk / mitigate harm / mitigate the effects of / mitigating circumstances
- **拓展**
  - *mitigation* (n.) — 减轻；（法律）酌情减刑；*mitigating factors/circumstances* 为法律常用搭配
  - 区分：**mitigate**（主动减轻）vs. **alleviate**（常用于痛苦/压力，略口语化）
- **例句**
  - *Proactive contingency planning can **mitigate** the disruption caused by sudden school closures.*
    提前制定应急预案有助于减轻学校突然关停带来的冲击。

---

**W7. streamline**
- **音标** /ˈstriːmlaɪn/ *v.*
- **英文释义** To improve a system, organization, or process by removing unnecessary parts of it and making it more efficient. *(Longman)*
- **中文释义** 精简；优化（流程）；使更高效
- **语域** 正式 / 行政 / 商业
- **同义词** simplify, rationalize, optimize, reorganize **反义词** complicate, bureaucratize
- **常见词组** streamline procedures / streamline the application process / streamline administration
- **拓展**
  - 本义为"使流线型"（空气动力学），引申为"精简流程"。
  - *streamlined* (adj.) — 精简的；流线型的
  - 对应通知中"简化表格填写和证明材料提交"，英文可表达为 *streamline document submission requirements*。
- **例句**
  - *The new digital platform is designed to **streamline** the school enrollment process by integrating data from multiple government agencies.*
    新数字平台旨在整合多部门数据，优化学校招生办理流程。

---

**W8. circumvent**
- **音标** /ˌsɜːrkəmˈvent/ *v.*
- **英文释义** To avoid obeying a rule or law, or to find a way around something that would otherwise prevent you from doing what you want. *(Cambridge)*
- **中文释义** 规避；绕过（规定/障碍）
- **语域** 正式 / 法律 / 书面
- **同义词** bypass, evade, sidestep, skirt **反义词** comply with, adhere to, observe
- **常见词组** circumvent the rules / circumvent regulations / circumvent the law / circumvent oversight
- **拓展**
  - *circumvention* (n.) — 规避；绕过；区分：**circumvent**（主动绕过规则，带负面含义）vs. **bypass**（较中性，也可指技术性绕过）
  - 通知中"变相提前招生"对应的英文逻辑即 *circumvent the ban on early enrollment*。
- **例句**
  - *Some schools attempted to **circumvent** the prohibition on early admissions by issuing unofficial "intent registration" notices.*
    一些学校试图通过发放非正式"意向登记"通知来规避提前招生禁令。

- **例句**

**R4. disparity**

#### **T — 翻译重要词**

---

**T1. 免试就近入学 → school enrollment without examination based on school district proximity**
- **音标** / *phrase*
- **英文释义** A policy under which school-age children are automatically assigned to a nearby school without any entrance examination.
- **中文释义** 免试就近入学政策（义务教育阶段核心原则）
- **语域** 政策 / 行政 / 教育法律
- **拓展**
  - 官方译法亦见：*nearest-school enrollment without examination*；外媒常译为 *district-based school assignment*
  - 对应法律依据：《义务教育法》第十二条
- **例句**
  - *China's policy of **enrollment without examination based on school district proximity** aims to eliminate competition-driven stress at the primary school level.*
    中国免试就近入学政策旨在消除小学阶段由竞争驱动的学业压力。

---

**T2. 掐尖招生 → selective cherry-picking enrollment / cream-skimming admissions**
- **音标** / *phrase*
- **英文释义** The practice by which elite schools poach top-performing students from other districts, undermining local enrollment fairness.
- **中文释义** 跨区掐尖招生：重点学校跨区域抢招优质生源的行为
- **语域** 新闻 / 政策 / 教育
- **拓展**
  - 可译作 *siphoning off top students*；学术语境中称 *cream-skimming* 或 *selective poaching*
  - 英语中"cream-skimming"原指金融机构只服务优质客户，引申至教育领域
- **例句**
  - ***Cherry-picking enrollment** by prestigious high schools has been widely criticized for exacerbating educational inequalities between districts.*
    名校的掐尖招生行为因加剧区域间教育不公平而受到广泛批评。

---

**T3. 公民同招 → simultaneous enrollment for both public and private schools**
- **音标** / *phrase*
- **英文释义** A policy requiring that public (公办) and private (民办) schools conduct student recruitment simultaneously, on the same timeline, during the same enrollment cycle.
- **中文释义** 公办学校与民办学校同步招生政策
- **语域** 政策 / 行政
- **拓展**
  - 直译为 *simultaneous enrollment for public and private institutions*，英文政策讨论中亦见 *unified enrollment calendar*
  - 背景：该政策出台前，民办学校常提前以各种名义锁定优质生源。
- **例句**
  - *The **simultaneous public-private enrollment policy** prevents private schools from gaining an unfair advantage by recruiting students before the official admissions window opens.*

### 4D. 金句积累

---

**金句一**

> 原文：切实维护教育公平，保障适龄儿童少年平等受教育权利。

**English rendering:**
*To genuinely safeguard educational equity and ensure equal access to education for all school-age children and adolescents.*
真正维护教育公平，保障所有适龄儿童和青少年平等享有受教育的权利。

**写作点评：** "genuinely safeguard" + "equal access" 是国际教育政策写作中的核心搭配，适合用于议论文立场句或政策分析引言。

---

**金句二**

> 原文：不断健全公平公开、规范透明、监管有力的长效机制。

**English rendering:**
*To continuously refine a long-term mechanism characterized by fairness, openness, procedural integrity, and robust oversight.*
持续完善一套以公平、公开、程序规范和有力监管为核心特征的长效机制。

**写作点评：** 四组并列形容词形成排比结构，英文转化为 *characterized by* 引出的名词短语，节奏工整，适合政策类写作结尾升华。

---

**金句三**

> 原文：对不实招生信息要主动发声、及时辟谣、释疑解惑。

**English rendering:**
*Authorities must proactively speak out against false enrollment information, debunk rumors in a timely manner, and address public doubts and misconceptions.*
主管部门必须主动就虚假招生信息发声，及时辟谣，并回应公众疑虑与误解。

**写作点评：** 三个并列动词短语（*speak out / debunk / address*）构成递进式行动框架，*debunk rumors* 是新闻与政策写作中极具力度的地道表达，可广泛迁移至舆论治理、信息核查等议题。

## 全文精读笔记 | Intensive Reading Notes

🔹 **US-Israeli strike / hits / gas tanks / in southern Iran, / halts / output / at 2 refineries: / Iranian media**  
🔸 **美以联合空袭 / 击中 / 伊朗南部储气罐，/ 致使 / 两座炼油厂 / 停产：/ 伊朗媒体报道**

> 1. **Strike** [straɪk]  
>    - **n. [C]** A sudden military attack. (突然的**军事打击/袭击**)  
>    - **Usage:** Often used in terms like *airstrike* or *preemptive strike*. (常用于“空袭”或“先发制人打击”。)  
>    - **Collocation:** Launch a strike against... (发动针对...的打击)  
> 2. **Halt** [hɔːlt]  
>    - **v. [T/I]** To stop; to make something stop moving or happening. (**停止/使停下/停摆**)  
>    - **Synonym:** Cease, terminate. (同义词：停止、终结。)  
>    - **Note:** Often used in news for production or progress stopping. (新闻常用于描述生产或进展中断。)  
> 3. **Refinery** [rɪˈfaɪnəri]  
>    - **n. [C]** A factory where a substance such as oil or sugar is made pure. (**炼油厂/加工厂**)  
>    - **Verb form:** Refine (精炼/提纯).  
>    - **Plural:** Refineries. (复数形式。)

🔹 **A US-Israeli airstrike / hit / gas storage tanks / at Iran's South Pars field / in the southern city of Asaluyeh / on Wednesday, / halting / production / at two refineries / with a combined capacity / of about 100 million cubic meters / per day, / Iranian media reported.**  
🔸 **据伊朗媒体报道，/ 周三 / 一次美以联合空袭 / 击中了 / 位于南部城市阿萨卢耶的 / 伊朗南帕尔斯气田的 / 储气罐，/ 导致 / 两座 / 日总产能 / 约 1 亿立方米的 / 炼油厂 / 停产。**

> **[注释]**  
> - **South Pars (南帕尔斯气田):** 位于波斯湾，是世界上已知最大的天然气田，由伊朗和卡塔尔共有。  
> - **Asaluyeh (阿萨卢耶):** 伊朗布什尔省的一个港口城市，是南帕尔斯气田开发的重要能源枢纽和工业基地。

> 1. **Airstrike** [ˈeəstraɪk]  
>    - **n. [C]** An attack by military aircraft on a surface target. (**空袭**)  
>    - **Expansion:** A compound word from "air" and "strike". (由“空中”和“打击”组成的合成词。)  
> 2. **Combined** [kəmˈbaɪnd]  
>    - **adj.** Done or produced by two or more people or groups working together; total. (**联合的/总计的**)  
>    - **Collocation:** Combined efforts (共同努力); a combined total of (总计达...).  
> 3. **Capacity** [kəˈpæsəti]  
>    - **n. [U/C]** The total amount that can be contained or produced. (**容量/产能/生产能力**)  
>    - **IELTS Point:** Often used in discussions about economy or resources. (雅思考点：常用于讨论经济或资源生产。)  
>    - **Antonym:** Incapacity (无能力).

🔹 **According to the semi-official Fars News Agency, / loud explosions / were heard / across multiple refinery sites / in Asaluyeh / following the attack.**  
🔸 **据半官方的法尔斯通讯社报道，/ 袭击发生后，/ 阿萨卢耶 / 多个炼油厂区 / 都听到了 / 剧烈的爆炸声。**

> **[注释]**  
> - **Fars News Agency (法尔斯通讯社):** 伊朗的一家半官方新闻机构，通常被认为与伊斯兰革命卫队（IRGC）有紧密联系。

> 1. **Semi-official** [ˌsemi əˈfɪʃl]  
>    - **adj.** Coming from someone who has some connection with the government. (**半官方的**)  
>    - **Etymology:** Prefix "semi-" means "half". (词源：前缀 "semi-" 意为“半”。)  
> 2. **Explosion** [ɪkˈspləʊʒn]  
>    - **n. [C/U]** A violent burst, as of a bomb. (**爆炸/爆发**)  
>    - **Verb:** Explode (不规则变化：exploded, exploded). (动词：爆炸。)  
>    - **Synonym:** Blast, detonation. (同义词：爆炸、引爆。)

🔹 **The outlet / said / that / several gas storage tanks / and parts of the refinery infrastructure / were targeted / across different operational phases, / including units three, four, five, and six.**  
🔸 **该机构 / 表示，/ 多个储气罐 / 以及部分炼油厂基础设施 / 在不同的运营阶段（期次）/ 遭到攻击，/ 包括第 3、4、5 和 6 单元。**

> 1. **Outlet** [ˈaʊtlet]  
>    - **n. [C]** In this context, a news organization. (**新闻机构/媒体渠道**)  
>    - **熟词僻义:** Usually means a store or a power point (socket), but in media, it refers to a platform for publishing. (通常指“折扣店”或“插座”，但在媒体背景下指“媒体平台/机构”。)  
> 2. **Infrastructure** [ˈɪnfrəstrʌktʃə(r)]  
>    - **n. [C/U]** The basic systems and services that are necessary for a country or an organization. (**基础设施/基建**)  
>    - **Examples:** Road, water, power systems. (例如：道路、供水、电力系统。)  
> 3. **Target** [ˈtɑːɡɪt]  
>    - **v. [T]** To direct an attack or criticism at someone or something. (**把...作为目标/瞄准**)  
>    - **n. [C]** A goal or a result that you try to achieve. (作为名词时指“目标”。)  
> 4. **Phase** [feɪz]  
>    - **n. [C]** A particular period of time during the development of something. (**阶段/期次**)  
>    - **Collocation:** In phases (分阶段); phase in/out (逐步引入/逐步废除).

🔹 **The agency / said / employees / were evacuated / to safe areas, / while / firefighting and rescue teams / were working / to contain / blazes / triggered by the strike.**  
🔸 **该通讯社 / 称，/ 员工 / 已被疏散 / 至安全地带，/ 与此同时，/ 消防和救援队 / 正在努力 / 控制 / 由袭击引发的 / 火灾。**

> 1. **Evacuate** [ɪˈvækjueɪt]  
>    - **v. [T]** To move people from a dangerous place to a safe place. (**疏散/撤离**)  
>    - **Noun:** Evacuation (疏散).  
> 2. **Contain** [kənˈteɪn]  
>    - **v. [T]** To keep something harmful within limits and not allow it to spread. (**控制/抑制/遏制**)  
>    - **熟词僻义:** Beyond "to hold" (包含), in military or emergency contexts, it means to stop something from spreading. (除了“包含”，在军事或紧急情况下意为“防止扩散”。)  
> 3. **Blaze** [bleɪz]  
>    - **n. [C]** A large and often dangerous fire. (**大火/烈焰**)  
>    - **Synonym:** Conflagration, fire. (同义词：大火。)  
> 4. **Trigger** [ˈtrɪɡə(r)]  
>    - **v. [T]** To cause something to start. (**引发/导致**)  
>    - **n. [C]** The part of a gun you pull to fire. (作为名词指“扳机”。)  
>    - **Writing Tip:** Useful for describing cause and effect in IELTS Task 2. (写作建议：适用于雅思写作大作文描述因果关系。)

> 1. **Casualty** [ˈkæʒuəlti]  
>    - **n. [C]** A person who is killed or injured in a war or in an accident. (**伤亡人员/受害者**)  
>    - **Note:** Usually plural in news: *casualties*. (在新闻中通常用复数。)  
>    - **Distinction:** Difference between *death* and *casualty* (casualty includes both killed and injured). (注意：casualty 包含死亡和受伤人员。)  
> 2. **Extent** [ɪkˈstent]  
>    - **n. [U/S]** The size or importance of something; the degree to which something happens. (**程度/范围/限度**)  
>    - **Phrase:** To some extent (在某种程度上); to the extent that... (到...的程度)。

>    - **n. [C]** A planned military attack. (**攻势/攻击**)

> 3. **Supreme** [suːˈpriːm]  
>    - **adj.** Highest in rank or authority. (**最高的/至高无上的**)

🔹 **Iran / has retaliated / with drone and missile strikes / targeting Israel, Jordan, Iraq, and Gulf countries / hosting US military assets, / causing casualties / and damage to infrastructure / while / disrupting global markets and aviation.**  
🔸 **伊朗 / 已通过无人机和导弹袭击 / 进行了反击，/ 目标瞄准了以色列、约旦、伊拉克以及 / 驻有美国军事资产的 / 海湾国家，/ 造成了人员伤亡 / 和基础设施损坏，/ 同时也 / 扰乱了全球市场和航空业。**

> 1. **Retaliate** [rɪˈtælieɪt]  
>    - **v. [I]** To do something bad to someone because they have done something bad to you. (**报复/反击**)  
>    - **Noun:** Retaliation.  
>    - **Preposition:** Retaliate *against* someone *with* something. (对某人以某手段进行报复。)  
> 2. **Asset** [ˈæset]  
>    - **n. [C]** A useful or valuable thing, person, or quality; military equipment. (**资产/财产/军事装备/有用的东西**)  
>    - **Context:** Here refers to military equipment and personnel. (此处指军事装备和人员。)  
> 3. **Disrupt** [dɪsˈrʌpt]  
>    - **v. [T]** To prevent something, especially a system, process, or event, from continuing as usual or as expected. (**扰乱/中断/使混乱**)  
>    - **Noun:** Disruption.  
>    - **IELTS Writing:** Highly useful for discussing the impact of technology or social changes. (雅思写作：在讨论科技或社会变革的影响时非常有用。)  
> 4. **Aviation** [ˌeɪviˈeɪʃn]  
>    - **n. [U]** The designing, building, and flying of aircraft. (**航空/飞行**)  
>    - **Context:** Civil and commercial flight operations. (此处指民用和商业飞行运营。)

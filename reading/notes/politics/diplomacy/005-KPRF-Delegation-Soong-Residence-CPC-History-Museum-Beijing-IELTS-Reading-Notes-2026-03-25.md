# 模块一：翻译与全文概要

## 英文翻译

**KPRF — Official Website | Party Life | News of the Central Committee of the KPRF**

**KPRF Delegation in Beijing Visits the Song Qingling Memorial Residence and the Museum of the History of the CPC**

On March 24, the Central Committee delegation of the Communist Party of the Russian Federation (KPRF), as part of an official working visit to China, visited the Song Qingling Memorial Residence in Beijing and the Museum of the History of the Communist Party of China. The activities were conducted in the format of familiarization visits and working meetings with the Chinese side.

At the Song Qingling Memorial Residence, the delegation was received by Chen Hongqiu, Secretary-General of the Song Qingling Foundation. The guests were presented with the museum's exhibition dedicated to the life and activities of Song Qingling — a prominent Chinese state and public figure who played a significant role in shaping the modern Chinese state.

Song Qingling was one of the few women to have held high state offices in China's history. She served in the country's top leadership as Vice-Chairwoman of the People's Republic of China and played a significant role in the political and public life of the state. Her activities combined participation in state governance with active work in the areas of international cooperation, the protection of women's rights, and support for youth. During the tour, the KPRF representatives noted that her example demonstrates the contribution of women to the development of Chinese statehood and public institutions.

Maria Drobot noted the significance of China as one of the centers of the emerging multipolar world. She emphasized that China's achievements in the economy, science, and social sphere exert a substantial influence on international processes, and that China's policy is oriented toward developing equal-footing cooperation and respecting the sovereignty of states.

Special attention during the visit was paid to the issues of working with youth and the younger generation — matters to which Song Qingling attached great importance in her public and state activities. The head of the Russian delegation noted that in the Soviet state, an analogous role was played by Nadezhda Konstantinovna Krupskaya — the wife of the founder of the USSR, who stood at the origins of the Pioneer organization and the development of the Komsomol.

It was emphasized that this work remains relevant today. With the support of the KPRF, Pioneer and Komsomol organizations continue to develop in Russia, and work with children and youth is one of the priority areas of the party's activities.

On the same day, the KPRF delegation visited the Museum of the History of the Communist Party of China — one of the key memorial complexes dedicated to the formation and development of the party. The museum's exhibition covers the main stages of the CPC's history, from its founding in 1921 to the present period, including the revolutionary struggle, the building of the socialist state, and the era of reforms and modernization.

The KPRF representatives familiarized themselves with documents, archival materials, and multimedia exhibitions reflecting the party's role in China's socioeconomic development. It was noted that the museum fulfills an important function of ideological and historical education, and is also used as a venue for the training of party cadres and the reception of foreign delegations.

The visit to these memorial sites was part of the delegation's program aimed at studying the historical experience of the CPC, exchanging party work practices, and strengthening inter-party interaction between the KPRF and the CPC.

A particular impression on the delegation members was made by the museum's multimedia exhibitions, including the screening of 3D films in a cinema hall. The Russian communists were able to take virtual tours of China's largest provinces, witness the country's natural diversity — from tropical coastlines to snow-capped mountain peaks and vast agricultural territories. A film was also presented dedicated to China's achievements in industry, science, and space, which provided a vivid sense of the scale of the country's modern development under the red banner.

### 👤 人物

**1. 宋庆龄（Song Qingling / Soong Ching-ling，1893–1981）**
中国政治家，孙中山之妻，曾任中华人民共和国副主席（1954–1975年）及名誉主席（1981年）。1951年获斯大林和平奖。她长期从事妇女权益保护、青少年教育及国际和平运动，是中国历史上担任国家最高职位的极少数女性之一。中国宋庆龄基金会以其名义创立，致力于儿童福利和对外文化交流。

**2. 孙中山（Sun Yat-sen / Sun Zhongshan，1866–1925）**
中国革命先行者，中华民国开国元勋，中国国民党创始人，被尊称为"中国国父"。1911年领导辛亥革命推翻清朝，建立共和制度；提出"三民主义"（民族、民权、民生）。孙中山同时受到中国共产党和国民党的共同尊崇。

**3. 玛利亚·德罗博特（Мария Дробот，生于1982年）**
俄罗斯政党活动家及国家政治人物，俄罗斯联邦共产党（КПРФ）中央委员会委员、主席团成员、书记，第八届俄罗斯国家杜马代表。毕业于罗斯托夫国立大学历史学系（历史政治学方向，2005年）。长期负责俄共妇女、儿童及青年组织工作，现任国家杜马家庭、父职、母职与童年保护委员会副主席。本次访华代表团团长。

**4. 娜杰日达·克鲁普斯卡娅（Надежда Константиновна Крупская，1869–1939）**
苏联教育家和政治活动家，列宁之妻。在苏联早期国家建设中参与创立共青团（Komsomol）和少先队（Pioneer Organization），主导苏联公共图书馆体系建设和工农群众教育改革，著有大量教育学著作，曾任苏联教育人民委员部副委员。

**5. 陈红秋（Chén Hóngqiū）**
中国宋庆龄基金会秘书长，在本次俄共代表团访问宋庆龄故居纪念馆期间出面接待。（公开资料有限，仅为本文涉及人物。）

# 模块四：素材与语料库积累

## 4A. 重点词汇解析

---

### W — 写作高频词

---

**1. delegation** /ˌdeləˈɡeɪʃən/ *n.*

- **英文释义：** A group of people chosen to represent a company, organization, or government, especially at a meeting or during an official visit; also, the act of giving authority or responsibility to someone else.
- **中文释义：** 代表团；（权力或任务的）委托、授权
- **语域：** 正式 / 新闻 / 外交
- **同义词：** delegation ↔ envoy（特使，个人）; mission（使团）; contingent（一支队伍）
- **词性变化：** delegate (n./v.)；delegatory (adj.)
- **搭配：** a high-level delegation（高级别代表团）；lead/head a delegation（率领代表团）；a delegation to the UN（出席联合国的代表团）；trade delegation（贸易代表团）；dispatch/send a delegation（派遣代表团）
- **拓展：** delegate 作名词意为"代表、委派人员"；作动词意为"委派、授权"（delegate authority to sb.）。注意 delegation 在外交文书中极为常见，常与 receive, host, welcome, dispatch, lead 等动词搭配。
- **例句：**
  - A **high-level delegation** from the African Union arrived in Geneva to attend the **multilateral** climate negotiations.
  - 非洲联盟一个**高级别代表团**抵达日内瓦，出席**多边**气候谈判。

---

**2. commemorate** /kəˈmeməreɪt/ *v.*

- **英文释义：** To do something special in order to remember and honor an important person or event in the past. (Cambridge: to remember officially and give respect to a great person or event)
- **中文释义：** 纪念；举行纪念活动以铭记某人或某事
- **语域：** 正式 / 书面 / 新闻
- **同义词：** honor, memorialize, mark, celebrate (区别：celebrate 侧重庆祝，commemorate 侧重庄重追念)
- **词性变化：** commemoration (n. 不可数/可数)；commemorative (adj., e.g., commemorative stamp 纪念邮票)
- **搭配：** commemorate the founding of...（纪念……的创立）；a ceremony to commemorate...（纪念……的仪式）；in commemoration of（以纪念……）
- **拓展：** commemoration 作名词时：*in commemoration of those who died*（以纪念阵亡者）。commemorative 作形容词常修饰 plaque（铭牌）、stamp（邮票）、ceremony（仪式）。
- **例句：**
  - A series of **commemorative** events were held across the country to **commemorate** the 80th anniversary of the end of World War II.
  - 全国各地举办了一系列**纪念**活动，以**纪念**二战结束80周年。

---

**3. pivotal** /ˈpɪvətəl/ *adj.*

- **英文释义：** More important than anything else in a situation or system; central and crucial. (Longman: more important than anything else — SYN key)
- **中文释义：** 关键性的；举足轻重的；核心的
- **语域：** 正式 / 学术 / 书面
- **同义词：** crucial, central, key, cardinal, decisive; 反义词：peripheral, marginal
- **搭配：** play a pivotal role in...（在……中发挥关键作用）；pivotal to the success of...（对……的成功至关重要）；a pivotal moment/figure（关键时刻/关键人物）
- **拓展：** 比 important 语气更强，强调某事物处于整个系统或局势的"轴心"位置。写作中可替换 key/crucial，使表达更具深度。pivot (n.) 原意为"枢轴"，由此引申。
- **例句：**
  - The city of Shanghai played a **pivotal role** in China's economic transformation, serving as the country's commercial and financial hub for over a century.
  - 上海在中国经济转型中发挥了**关键性作用**，一个多世纪以来一直是中国的商业和金融中心。

---

**4. sovereignty** /ˈsɑːvrənti/ *n.* [不可数]

- **英文释义：** Complete freedom and power to govern; the power that an independent country has to govern itself. (Longman: complete freedom and power to govern)
- **中文释义：** 主权；最高统治权；国家自主权
- **语域：** 正式 / 法律 / 外交 / 学术
- **同义词：** autonomy（自治权）, independence（独立性）, self-determination（自决权）
- **词形变化：** sovereign (adj./n.)——sovereign state（主权国家）；sovereign (n.)——君主

# 视频丨长城和故宫哪个好？斯里兰卡总理：“真的很难挑！”

**🔸 Source: CCTV News | Published: April 4, 2026**

**🔹 近日，斯里兰卡总理哈丽妮·阿马拉苏里亚接受总台《高端访谈》专访。**  
**🔸 Recently, Sri Lankan Prime Minister Harini Amarasuriya sat down for an exclusive interview with CMG's "Leaders Talk."**

**🔹 阿马拉苏里亚表示，参观故宫和长城后，对中国的悠久历史与灿烂文化留下了深刻印象。**  
**🔸 Amarasuriya remarked that after visiting the Forbidden City and the Great Wall, she was deeply impressed by China's long-standing history and splendid culture.**

**🔹 难选出某一个最难忘的方面，因为整个行程都令人享受。**  
**🔸 She found it difficult to single out one most memorable aspect because the entire journey was thoroughly enjoyable.**

**🔹 阿马拉苏里亚说，故宫让她感到震撼，展现了中国人民的智慧与创新精神，希望时间更充裕以便细看。**  
**🔸 Amarasuriya said the Forbidden City left her in awe, showcasing the wisdom and innovative spirit of the Chinese people, and she expressed a wish for more time to explore it in greater detail.**

**🔹 长城之旅则是一次独特而充满乐趣的体验，虽然考验体力，但沿途风景美丽，让人遥想当年的历史氛围。**  
**🔸 The Great Wall trip, on the other hand, was a unique and fun-filled experience; despite the physical challenge, the stunning scenery along the way evoked the historical atmosphere of centuries past.**

**🔹 阿马拉苏里亚说，能瞻仰人民英雄纪念碑也让她深感荣幸，认为这些地方都具有深厚的历史意义。**  
**🔸 Amarasuriya also stated that she felt deeply honored to pay tribute at the Monument to the People's Heroes, noting that these sites all hold profound historical significance.**

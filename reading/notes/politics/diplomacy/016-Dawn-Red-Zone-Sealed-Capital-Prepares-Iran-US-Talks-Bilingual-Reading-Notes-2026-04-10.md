# 首都为伊美高层会谈做准备，红区已封锁

## 一、背景信息

**文章来源：** 《黎明报》（Dawn）——巴基斯坦历史最悠久、影响力最大的英文报纸，由巴基斯坦国父穆罕默德·阿里·真纳创办，被视为该国的“记录报”。  
**文章标题：** 首都为伊美高层会谈做准备，红区已封锁 (Red Zone sealed as capital prepares for high-stakes Iran-US talks)  
**作者背景：** Munawer Azeem，《黎明报》资深记者，长期关注伊斯兰堡的城市治理、治安及安全事务，擅长报道政府行政指令与安全部署。  
**事件背景：** 本文报道了2026年4月间，巴基斯坦首都伊斯兰堡作为东道主，为伊朗与美国之间举行的具有重大地缘政治意义的和平谈判所采取的极端安全措施。

## 三、逐句精读

**🔹 Red Zone / sealed / as capital / prepares / for high-stakes Iran-US talks**  
**🔸 随着首都为伊美高层会谈做准备，“红区”已被封锁。**
> **注释：** **Red Zone**（红区）是指巴基斯坦伊斯兰堡的一个行政区域，集中了议会大厦、总理府、最高法院及多国使馆，是该国的政治核心地带。
>
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **high-stakes** /ˌhaɪˈsteɪks/ (adj.) **高风险的；高回报的；关键性的**
> - 英文释义：Involving serious risks if there is no success, or high rewards if there is.
> - 语域：新闻、政治、商业。
> - 拓展：常用于形容谈判 (negotiations)、比赛 (game) 或决定 (decision)。近义词：pivotal, critical。
> [2] **seal** /siːl/ (v.) **封锁；密封**
> - 英文释义：To close an entrance or a container so that nothing can enter or leave it.
> - 拓展：**seal off** 也是常用搭配，指用警戒线或障碍物封锁某区域。

**🔹 Two holidays / declared / in Rawalpindi, Islamabad**  
**🔸 拉瓦尔品第和伊斯兰堡宣布放假两天。**
> **注释：** **Rawalpindi**（拉瓦尔品第）是伊斯兰堡的姊妹城市，巴基斯坦陆军总部所在地。

**🔹 Hotel / vacated, / reserved / for delegations**  
**🔸 酒店已清空，预留给代表团使用。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **vacate** /vəˈkeɪt/ (v.) **腾出（房间等）；空出**
> - 英文释义：To leave a building, seat, etc., especially so that it is available for someone else to use.
> - 语域：正式（法律、酒店、行政）。
> - 变形：名词形式为 **vacation**（假期/腾出），但在美式英语中常用作假期。
> [2] **delegation** /ˌdelɪˈɡeɪʃn/ (n.) **代表团**
> - 英文释义：A group of people who are chosen to represent a particular organization or country.
> - 拓展：动词为 **delegate**（委派/授权）。

**🔹 Entry points / to Islamabad / likely to face closures / Excise, Taxation offices / will remain open**  
**🔸 进出伊斯兰堡的通道可能会面临关闭，但消费税和税务办公室将保持开放。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **Excise** /ˈeksaɪz/ (n.) **消费税；货物税**
> - 英文释义：A tax that is paid on particular goods and services that are sold in a country.
> - 拓展：**Excise and Taxation Department** 是巴基斯坦负责车辆登记和税收的重要政府部门。

**🔹 ISLAMABAD: / The authorities / have declared / two local holidays / in Islamabad and Rawalpindi / on Thursday and Friday / to ensure security / for scheduled peace talks / between Iran and the United States.**  
**🔸 伊斯兰堡：当局已宣布本周四和周五在伊斯兰堡和拉瓦尔品第实行两天局部假期，以确保计划中的伊朗与美国和平谈判的安全。**

**🔹 Due to the sensitivity and importance / of the talks, / comprehensive security measures / have been put in place, / involving all law enforcement agencies / and security forces.**  
**🔸 鉴于此次会谈的敏感性和重要性，已部署了全面的安全措施，涉及所有执法机构和安全部队。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **comprehensive** /ˌkɒmprɪˈhensɪv/ (adj.) **全面的；综合的**
> - 英文释义：Including all, or almost all, the items, details, facts, etc., that may be involved.
> - 搭配：**comprehensive insurance**（综合保险）；**comprehensive study**（全面研究）。
> [2] **put in place** (phrase) **部署；落实；实施**
> - 英文释义：To implement or establish something (a system, rule, or plan).
> - 拓展：在政府公文中，常用于描述政策或措施的生效。

**🔹 A hotel / located within the Red Zone / has been reserved / for the delegations / and vacated / on government instructions, / with heightened security / deployed / in and around the premises.**  
**🔸 位于红区内的一家酒店已根据政府指令清空并预留给代表团，酒店内部及周边部署了加强的安保力量。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **heightened** /ˈhaɪtnd/ (adj.) **强化的；加剧的**
> - 英文释义：Increased in degree or intensity.
> - 搭配：**heightened tension**（紧张局势加剧）；**heightened awareness**（警惕性提高）。
> [2] **premises** /ˈpremɪsɪz/ (n.) **（尤指公司或机构使用的）房屋及土地；经营场所**
> - 词性：名词，通常用复数形式。
> - 辨析：**premise**（单数）意为“前提”。

**🔹 Sources / told Dawn / that under the federal government’s directives, / the hotel management / had asked the guests / to vacate the premises.**  
**🔸 消息人士告诉《黎明报》，根据联邦政府的指令，酒店管理层已要求住客搬离。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **directive** /dəˈrektɪv/ (n.) **指令；指示**
> - 英文释义：An official instruction.
> - 语域：正式（政府或官方机构）。

**🔹 The management / through a letter / has informed the guests / that the government / had requisitioned the hotel / for an important event / till Sunday evening.**  
**🔸 管理层通过信函通知住客，政府已征用该酒店用于一项重要活动，直至周日晚上。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **requisition** /ˌrekwɪˈzɪʃn/ (v.) **征用**
> - 英文释义：To officially demand the use of a building, vehicle, etc., especially during a war or an emergency.
> - 词性：既可作动词也可作名词。
> - 拓展：常用于军事或紧急状态下的行政行为。

**🔹 According to a notification / issued by the Office of the Deputy Commissioner Islamabad, / April 9 and 10 / (Thursday and Friday) / have been declared / local holidays / across the Islamabad Capital Territory, / except for essential services.**  
**🔸 根据伊斯兰堡副专员办公室发布的通知，4月9日和10日（周四和周五）被宣布为整个伊斯兰堡首都辖区的局部假期，基本服务部门除外。**
> **注释：** **Deputy Commissioner** (DC) 是巴基斯坦行政体系中负责地区行政和治安的重要官员。**Islamabad Capital Territory** (ICT) 指伊斯兰堡首都直辖区。

**🔹 These services / include / the Municipal Corporation Islamabad, / the Capital Development Authority, / the Islamabad Capital Territory administration, / Islamabad Police, / Islamabad Electric Supply Corporation, / Sui Northern Gas Pipelines Limited, / and hospitals.**  
**🔸 这些服务部门包括伊斯兰堡市政公司、首都发展局、伊斯兰堡首都辖区行政当局、伊斯兰堡警察局、伊斯兰堡供电公司、苏伊北方天然气管道有限公司以及各医院。**
> **注释：** **Sui Northern Gas Pipelines Limited** (SNGPL) 是巴基斯坦最大的天然气公用事业公司。

**🔹 However, / the police facilitation centres / meant to issue driving licences / and other services / would remain closed / for the public.**  
**🔸 然而，旨在办理驾驶执照等服务的警察服务中心将对公众关闭。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **facilitation** /fəˌsɪlɪˈteɪʃn/ (n.) **便利；促进**
> - 英文释义：The act of making something possible or easier.
> - 搭配：**trade facilitation**（贸易便利化）。

**🔹 Meanwhile, / all state-run rescue departments / and hospitals / have been put / on high alert.**  
**🔸 与此同时，所有国营救援部门和医院已进入高度戒备状态。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **state-run** /ˈsteɪt rʌn/ (adj.) **国营的；政府经营的**
> - 英文释义：Operated or managed by the government.
> - 反义词：**privately-owned**。

**🔹 They / have been asked / to remain on standby / and ensure the availability / of staff / and essential supplies, / the sources added.**  
**🔸 消息人士补充说，这些部门已被要求保持待命，并确保人员和必需物资的供应。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **standby** /ˈstændbaɪ/ (n./adj.) **待命；备用**
> - 英文释义：A person or thing that is available to be used if necessary.
> - 搭配：**on standby**（待命状态）。

**🔹 A series of meetings / has also been held / in the twin cities / on Wednesday / to review security arrangements / and devise strategies / for the coming days, / while more meetings / are scheduled / late at night.**  
**🔸 周三，这对“双子城”举行了一系列会议，以审查安保安排并制定未来几天的策略，同时深夜还安排了更多会议。**
> **注释：** **Twin cities**（双子城）在巴基斯坦语境下特指伊斯兰堡和拉瓦尔品第，两城相邻且功能互补。
>
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **devise** /dɪˈvaɪz/ (v.) **设计；制定；发明**
> - 英文释义：To invent a new way of doing something.
> - 近义词：concoct, formulate。
> [2] **strategy** /ˈstrætədʒi/ (n.) **策略；战略**
> - 词性：可数名词。不规则复数形式为 **strategies**。

**🔹 The sources further said / that the Red Zone / would remain completely sealed / during the stay / of the delegates / in the capital.**  
**🔸 消息人士进一步表示，代表团在首都逗留期间，红区将保持完全封锁。**

**🔹 In addition, / the high-security zone / starting from Faisal Avenue / would also be sealed, / depending on the status / of the visiting delegations.**  
**🔸 此外，从费萨尔大道开始的高安保区域也将根据访问代表团的情况进行封锁。**
> **注释：** **Faisal Avenue** 是伊斯兰堡的一条主要南北干道。

**🔹 Some entry points / to the capital / will also be closed, / said the sources, / adding that / the security arrangements / for the talks / would be similar to / or more stringent than / those put in place / during the 23rd Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) summit / held in Islamabad / in October 2024.**  
**🔸 消息人士称，首都的一些入口也将关闭，并补充说，此次会谈的安保安排将与2024年10月在伊斯兰堡举行的第23届上海合作组织（SCO）峰会的安保强度相似，甚至更加严格。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **stringent** /ˈstrɪndʒənt/ (adj.) **严格的；严厉的**
> - 英文释义：(of a law, rule, etc.) Very strict and that must be obeyed.
> - 语域：正式（法律、安保、财务）。
> - 搭配：**stringent safety regulations**（严格的安全规定）。

**🔹 According to another notification, / Trail 2, 3, 4, 5 / and the trail behind the Saidpur village / shall remain closed / for public / on April 9 and 10.**  
**🔸 根据另一份通知，2号、3号、4号、5号徒步路径以及赛义德布尔村后的路径将在4月9日和10日对公众关闭。**
> **注释：** **Trails** 指伊斯兰堡马加拉山（Margalla Hills）的国家公园徒步路线，是当地居民主要休闲场所。

**🔹 Hours after Islamabad’s notification, / the Rawalpindi administration / also announced / local holidays / in the garrison city.**  
**🔸 在伊斯兰堡发布通知数小时后，拉瓦尔品第当局也宣布在这座卫戍城市实行局部假期。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **garrison city** (n.) **卫戍城市；驻军城镇**
> - 英文释义：A city that has a large number of soldiers living in it to defend it.
> - 背景：拉瓦尔品第是巴基斯坦军方总部（GHQ）所在地，因此被称为 garrison city。

**🔹 Although / Pakistan Secretariat, Islamabad, / and all Federal Government establishments / shall remain closed / on Thursday (April 9), / all officers and secretarial staff / shall work from home / and shall not leave station.**  
**🔸 虽然位于伊斯兰堡的巴基斯坦秘书处及所有联邦政府机构将在周四（4月9日）关闭，但所有官员和秘书人员应居家办公，且不得离开驻地。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **establishment** /ɪˈstæblɪʃmənt/ (n.) **机构；企业；驻地**
> - 英文释义：An organization, a large institution, or a hotel.
> [2] **leave station** (phrase) **离开驻地；离开工作城市**
> - 语域：行政/军事术语。指在待命期间未经许可不得离开所在的城市。

**🔹 Furthermore, / Friday (April 10) / shall be a closed day / under the current four-day workweek arrangements.**  
**🔸 此外，根据现行的四天工作制安排，周五（4月10日）将是不办公日。**

**🔹 VVIP protocol / under the Blue Book / will be extended / to the delegations / during their movement, stay and meetings.**  
**🔸 代表团在移动、停留和会议期间，将享受《蓝皮书》规定的VVIP礼遇和安保协议。**
> **注释：** **Blue Book**（蓝皮书）在巴基斯坦是指一份机密的安保手册，详细规定了对国家元首、政府首脑及外国贵宾（VVIPs）的保护程序。
>
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **protocol** /ˈprəʊtəkɒl/ (n.) **礼仪；协议；方案**
> - 英文释义：The system of fixed rules and formal behaviour used at official meetings.
> [2] **extend** /ɪkˈstend/ (v.) **提供；给予（礼遇等）；延伸**
> - 英文释义：To offer or give something to somebody.
> - 搭配：**extend a warm welcome**（表示热烈欢迎）。

**🔹 Police, paramilitary troops / and other security forces / will perform duties / under strict guidelines / outlined in the Blue Book / and other applicable SOPs.**  
**🔸 警察、准军事部队和其他安全力量将根据《蓝皮书》及其他适用标准作业程序（SOPs）中概述的严格指南履行职责。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **paramilitary** /ˌpærəˈmɪlɪtri/ (adj.) **准军事的**
> - 英文释义：(of a group) Organized like an army but not part of the official armed forces.
> - 例子：巴基斯坦的 Rangers 或 Frontier Corps 属于准军事部队。
> [2] **SOP** (abbreviation) **标准作业程序**
> - 全称：Standard Operating Procedure。

**🔹 Separate routes / are also being prepared / for the movement / of the delegations.**  
**🔸 还为代表团的移动准备了专用路线。**

**🔹 Although / the capital police / are regularly conducting search operations / in Islamabad, / additional intelligence-based surveillance / has been enhanced.**  
**🔸 尽管首都警察定期在伊斯兰堡进行搜查行动，但基于情报的额外监控已得到加强。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **surveillance** /sɜːˈveɪləns/ (n.) **监视；监控**
> - 英文释义：The act of carefully watching a person suspected of a crime or a place.
> - 搭配：**surveillance camera**（监控摄像头）。
> - 易混词：**survey**（调查/测量）。

**🔹 Security / has also been tightened / at bus terminals, / including Faizabad and Pirwadhai, / Chongi No 26, / as well as in slums / and wooded areas / around the high-security zone, / which also houses the Red Zone.**  
**🔸 巴士总站（包括法伊扎巴德、皮尔瓦多、26号关卡）以及高安保区域（红区所在地）周边的贫民窟和林区的安保也已加强。**

**🔹 The capital administration, / however, / announced / that in order to further facilitate / the process of motorcycle transfer, / the offices of the Excise and Taxation Department / will remain open / for the next four days, / including holidays.**  
**🔸 然而，首都行政当局宣布，为了进一步便利摩托车过户手续，消费税和税务局的办公室将在未来四天（包括假期）保持开放。**

**🔹 This facility / will be available / from April 9 to April 12, / during which / citizens will be able / to complete the transfer / of their motorcycles / with ease, / even on public holidays.**  
**🔸 这一便利服务将在4月9日至4月12日期间提供，在此期间，市民即使在公共假期也能轻松完成摩托车过户。**

**🔹 “The purpose of this initiative / is to provide maximum convenience / to citizens / and to make the motorcycle transfer process / faster and more efficient.”**  
**🔸 “这一举措的目的是为市民提供最大的便利，并使摩托车过户过程更加快捷高效。”**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **initiative** /ɪˈnɪʃətɪv/ (n.) **倡议；新方案；主动性**
> - 英文释义：A new plan for dealing with a particular problem or for achieving a particular purpose.
> - 搭配：**take the initiative**（采取主动）。

**🔹 “All citizens / are requested / to take full advantage / of this opportunity / and ensure the timely transfer / of their motorcycles,” / says the announcement.**  
**🔸 公告称：“请广大市民充分利用这一机会，确保及时办理摩托车过户。”**

**🔹 The government / has announced / this facility of motorcycle transfer / to allow the people / to avail the subsidy / on petrol / through a mobile app.**  
**🔸 政府宣布这项摩托车过户便利措施，是为了让民众能够通过手机应用程序领取汽油补贴。**
> **重点词汇解析：**
> [1] **avail** /əˈveɪl/ (v.) **利用；有帮助**

**中文释义：** 根据...的指示；按...的要求

**地道用法：** 
- "On government instructions"
- "Under official directives"
- "Following government orders"

**例句：**

**拓展：** "instruction" 可指：①指导；教学 ②指示；命令（正式用法）

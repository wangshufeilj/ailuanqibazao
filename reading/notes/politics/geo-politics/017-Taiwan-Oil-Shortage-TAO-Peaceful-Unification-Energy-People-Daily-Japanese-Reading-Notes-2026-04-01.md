# 来源与概要 | Source & Summary

**来源：** 人民网日语版 (People's Daily Japanese Edition)  
**题目：** Oil Shortages in Taiwan: TAO Says "After Peaceful Unification, Concerns Over Energy and Resource Scarcity Will Be Eliminated" / 台湾出现石油短缺 国台办：“和平统一后，无需再为能源和物资短缺担忧”  
**发布：** 2026年4月1日（人民网日语版所载）

**概要：** 国务院台湾事务办公室例行新闻发布会上，就台湾媒体所称中东局势致石油供应紧张、波及塑料原料并可能出现药品短缺等提问，发言人回应批评民进党当局“油气供应没问题”系自我安慰与欺瞒；并阐述和平统一后两岸互联互通、大陆产业体系可保障台湾能源与物资供应。

🔹 **Oil Shortages in Taiwan: TAO Says "After Peaceful Unification, Concerns Over Energy and Resource Scarcity Will Be Eliminated"**  
🔸 **台湾出现石油短缺 国台办：“和平统一后，无需再为能源和物资短缺担忧”**

🔹 At a regular press conference of the **Taiwan Affairs Office (TAO)** of the State Council on the 1st, a reporter asked: "According to Taiwan media reports, the **tightening of oil supplies** due to the Middle East situation has spilled over into the plastic raw materials market, leading to a **'plastic scramble'** in the Taiwan region, with potential future shortages of **pharmaceuticals**."  
🔸 在国务院台湾事务办公室（国台办）1日的例行新闻发布会上，有记者提问：“据台湾媒体报道，受中东局势影响，**石油供应紧张**已波及塑料原料市场，台湾地区内出现了**‘塑料争夺战’**，未来还可能面临**药品短缺**。”

🔹 "Public opinion in Taiwan points out that even in the face of such difficulties, the **DPP authorities** merely claim 'there is no problem with oil and gas supply,' and their **crisis response** is always lagging. What is your comment on this?"  
🔸 “台湾舆论指出，面对这些困难，**民进党当局**只会说‘油气供应没问题’，**危机应对**始终慢半拍。对此有何评论？”

🔹 In response, spokesperson **Zhang Han** stated: "The DPP authorities' claim that 'there is no problem with oil and gas supply' is actually just **self-consolation** for their **lack of supply capacity**, and more than that, an act of **deceiving the public**."  
🔸 对此，发言人**张晗**表示：“民进党当局所谓‘油气供应没问题’的主张，实际上只是面对**供应能力缺失**的**自我安慰**，更是**欺骗民众**的行为。”

🔹 "The DPP authorities have neither the intention nor the ability to address the **livelihood crises** within the Taiwan region; they are simply **evading responsibility**, which has long caused strong dissatisfaction across various sectors of Taiwan society."  
🔸 “民进党当局既无心也无力应对台湾地区的**民生危机**，一味**逃避推脱**，台湾社会各界早已深感不满。”

🔹 "After **peaceful unification**, the comprehensive **interconnection** between the two sides will be realized. The mainland's **complete industrial system** and stable market supply capacity will become the most **reliable support** for Taiwan compatriots."  
🔸 “在实现**和平统一**后，两岸将实现全面**互联互通**，大陆**完善的产业体系**和稳定的市场供应能力将成为台湾同胞最**坚实的后盾**。”

🔹 "This can fully compensate for Taiwan's deficiencies in areas such as **crude oil, natural gas, and industrial raw materials**."  
🔸 “这能够完全弥补台湾在**原油、天然气、工业原料**等领域的缺口。”

🔹 "No matter how unstable the external situation may be, we can promptly ensure Taiwan's **energy security, industrial production, and material supply**."  
🔸 “无论外部形势如何动荡，我们都能及时保障台湾的**能源安全、工业生产和物资供应**。”

🔹 "Taiwan compatriots will no longer need to feel **anxious** about various **energy and resource shortages**."  
🔸 “台湾同胞将无需再为各类**能源和物资短缺**而感到**不安**。”

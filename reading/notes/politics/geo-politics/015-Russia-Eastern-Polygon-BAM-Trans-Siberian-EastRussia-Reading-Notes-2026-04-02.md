### 📝 英语词汇与句法 | English Lexis & Grammar

---

🔹 **180 миллионов тонн не приехали: почему Восточный полигон не дотягивает до планов.**  
🔸 **180 million tons / did not arrive / : / why / the Eastern Polygon / falls short of / plans.**  
🔻 **1.8亿吨货物未能运抵：为何东方铁路网未能达到预期计划。**

> **Eastern Polygon**: 专有名词；文中指 BAM + Trans-Siberian 构成的远东铁路网。写作中首次出现可写全称再缩写。  
> **fall short of**: 固定动词短语，后接 *plans / expectations / targets*，表示“未达标”。同义：*fail to meet, miss*；更正式：*fall short of projections*。  
> **did not arrive**: 此处拟人化（tons *arrive*），英语里 cargo 可搭配 *arrive, move, flow*。勿说 *the tons did not come true* 之类中式表达。  

---

🔹 **В 2026 году / финансирование программы модернизации / БАМа и Транссиба / на фоне общего снижения / инвестпрограммы ОАО «РЖД» / может сократиться / более чем втрое.**  
🔸 **In 2026, / the funding / for the modernization program / of the BAM and Trans-Siberian / against the background of a general decrease / in the investment program of JSC "RZD" / may be reduced / by more than three times.**  
🔻 **2026年，/ 在俄罗斯铁路公司（RZD）整体投资计划缩减的背景下，/ 贝阿铁路和西伯利亚大铁路的 / 现代化计划融资 / 可能会 / 缩减三分之二以上。**

> **against the background of**: 书面语，引出宏观背景；同义 *amid, in the context of, against a backdrop of*。  
> **funding**: 不可数名词，指“资金拨付”；动词搭配 *allocate / secure / cut funding for*。  
> **modernization**: 与 *upgrade, overhaul* 近义；*modernization program* 为常见搭配。  
> **JSC "RZD"**: *Joint Stock Company*；报道体中常保留缩写。  
> **decrease / reduction in**: 注意介词 *in*（*a decrease in the investment program*）。  
> **may be reduced by more than three times**: 英语里 “减少几倍” 易歧义；本句实为俄译英习惯（约减至 1/3）。写作中更稳妥：*cut to less than a third of the previous level* 或 *slashed by roughly two-thirds*。  

---

🔹 **Как пойдет / дальнейшая реализация этого проекта / сказать сложно – / даже в более благоприятных финансовых условиях / предыдущие этапы развития / Восточного полигона / неоднократно переносились.**  
🔸 **How / the further implementation of this project / will proceed / is difficult to say – / even in more favorable financial conditions, / previous stages of development / of the Eastern Polygon / were repeatedly postponed.**  
🔻 **该项目的 / 进一步实施 / 将如何进行 / 很难预料—— / 即使在更优越的财务条件下，/ 东方铁路网的 / 早期开发阶段 / 也曾多次被推迟。**

> **implementation**: 正式用语，*the implementation of a project*；动词 *implement*。勿与 *installation*（安装）混淆。  
> **further**: 此处作形容词 *further stages* = “后续阶段”；副词 *furthermore* 用于递进。  
> **favorable conditions**: 搭配固定；*under favorable financial conditions*。近义：*conducive to, supportive of*。  
> **repeatedly**: 频度副词，位置多在主要动词前或句首；同义 *time and again*。  
> **were postponed**: 被动语态，主语为 *stages*；同义 *were pushed back, were rescheduled*。  

---

🔹 **При этом / и грузоотправители / и заинтересованные регионы / указывают на необходимость / располагать информацией, / какой эффект / получит инфраструктура / от реконструкции / не только в перспективе, / но и в текущем моменте.**  
🔸 **At the same time, / both shippers / and interested regions / point to the need / to have information / on what effect / the infrastructure / will receive / from reconstruction / not only in the long term, / but also at the current moment.**  
🔻 **与此同时，/ 发货方 / 和相关地区 / 都指出有必要 / 掌握相关信息，/ 即基础设施 / 从改建中 / 获得的效益 / 不仅应体现在长远前景中，/ 也应体现在当前阶段。**

> **shipper**: 物流常用词，= 托运方；与 *carrier*（承运方）、*consignee*（收货方）相对。  
> **infrastructure**: 常作不可数名词；*infrastructure will receive an effect* 为译文痕迹，更自然：*what benefits infrastructure will get* / *the impact on infrastructure*。  
> **in the long term**: 与 *in the short term, at present* 对举；亦作 *over the long term*。  
> **point to the need to**: 正式结构，后接不定式；同义 *highlight the need for*。  
> **reconstruction**: 与 *renovation, upgrade* 近义；铁路语境常指 *track / station reconstruction*。  

---

🔹 **Хотя второй этап развития / Восточного полигона / в РЖД объявили завершенным, / работы по нему / активно продолжались / в течение всего 2025 года.**  
🔸 **Although / the second stage of development / of the Eastern Polygon / was declared completed by RZD, / work on it / actively continued / throughout the whole of 2025.**  
🔻 **尽管 / 俄铁公司宣布 / 东方铁路网的第二阶段开发 / 已经结束，/ 但相关工作 / 在整个2025年期间 / 仍在积极进行。**

> **was declared completed**: 被动 + 补语 *completed*；亦作 *was declared to be complete*。  
> **throughout**: 介词，*throughout 2025* = 全年；勿写成 *through all 2025 year*。  
> **stage of development**: 固定搭配；*early/late stage*。  
> **actively continued**: 副词修饰动词；同义 *continued to be carried out actively*（更冗）。  

---

🔹 **Только на БАМе / было открыто движение / более чем на 30 новых объектах, / сообщили в пресс-центре / АО «Бамстроймеханизация».**  
🔸 **On the BAM alone, / traffic was opened / on more than 30 new objects, / reported the press center / of JSC "Bamstroymekhanizatsiya".**  
🔻 **仅在贝阿铁路（BAM）上，/ 就有30多个新项目 / 建成通车，/ “贝阿建筑机械化”股份公司 / 新闻中心报道称。**

> **on the BAM alone**: *alone* 后置强调“仅贝阿线就……”；同义 *on the BAM by itself*。  
> **traffic was opened**: 新闻英语里常见“通车”表述；更口语 *services began / trains started running*。  
> **objects**: 此处指工程“标段/设施”，非哲学“客体”；可换 *sites, facilities*。  
> **reported the press center of…**: 倒装语体（叙事体），正常语序 *the press center of … reported that*。  

---

🔹 **Строились / вторые пути / и реконструировались станции, / что увеличивало / пропускную способность / наиболее загруженных участков магистрали.**  
🔸 **Second tracks / were built / and stations were reconstructed, / which increased / the traffic capacity / of the most congested sections of the highway.**  
🔻 **通过建设 / 复线（第二条轨道） / 和改建车站，/ 提高了 / 干线最繁忙路段的 / 通行能力。**

> **traffic capacity**: 与下文的 *carrying capacity* 常成对出现；前者偏“通过能力/对数”，后者偏“货运吨数”。  
> **congested**: *congested sections* 比 *busy* 更技术化；名词 *congestion*。  
> **second tracks**: 即“复线”；英式也可用 *double track* 作定语。  
> **sections of the highway**: 此处 *highway* 指铁路干线（美式用法），英式多用 *main line*。  

---

🔹 **В числе крупных объектов – / узловая станция Комсомольск-Сортировочный, / где заработал транзитный парк «Д», / рассчитанный на обработку / длинносоставных поездов.**  
🔸 **Among the large objects is / the junction station Komsomolsk-Sortirovochny, / where transit yard "D" began operating, / designed for processing / long-haul trains.**  
🔻 **重大项目包括 / 共青城编组站（枢纽站），/ 其中的“D”中转车场已投入运营，/ 专门用于处理 / 长编组列车。**

> **junction station**: *junction* = 枢纽节点；勿与 *connection*（普通连接）混用。  
> **transit yard**: 美铁用语 *yard*；此处为编组/中转场。  
> **designed for processing**: *processing* 指“办理、处理（车列）”而非工业加工。  
> **long-haul trains**: 强调“长交路/重载”；若指“长编组”可换 *long consists*（更口语）。  

---

🔹 **Общая протяженность / построенных путей / составила 15,6 км.**  
🔸 **The total length / of the constructed tracks / amounted to 15.6 km.**  
🔻 **新建线路的 / 总长度 / 达到了15.6公里。**

> **amounted to**: 数据总结常用，*The length amounted to 15.6 km*；同义 *came to, totaled*。  
> **constructed tracks**: 过去分词作定语；*newly laid track* 亦常见。  

---

🔹 **На станции Тында / первые поезда / принял новый транзитный парк «Т».**  
🔸 **At Tynda station, / the first trains / were received by the new transit yard "T".**  
🔻 **在腾达站，/ 新建的“T”中转车场 / 接发了 / 首批列车。**

> **were received by**: 被动语态，强调车场作主语接纳列车；主动 *the yard received the first trains*。  
> **yard**: 铁路义为“车场/编组场”，非 *garden*；易读错重音。  

---

🔹 **Он повысил / переработку этого железнодорожного узла / с 2,4 тыс. до 3 тыс. вагонов в сутки.**  
🔸 **It increased / the processing capacity of this railway junction / from 2.4 thousand to 3 thousand wagons per day.**  
🔻 **该车场将该铁路枢纽的 / 处理能力 / 从每天2400辆车皮 / 提高到了3000辆。**

> **processing capacity**: 此处指车站“办理能力”，非工厂加工。  
> **railway junction**: 可数名词；*at the junction of two lines*。  
> **wagons per day**: 英式 *wagons*，美式货运车厢也可用 *freight cars*。  

---

🔹 **Из значительных искусственных сооружений / был открыт / 392-метровый мост / через реку Нюкжа / на перегоне Талума – Дюгабуль (линия Тында – Хани).**  
🔸 **Among the significant engineering structures, / a 392-meter bridge / across the Nyukzha River / on the Taluma – Dyugabul section (Tynda – Khani line) / was opened.**  
🔻 **在重大的工程建筑中，/ 位于塔卢马至久加布尔区间（腾达—哈尼线）的 / 跨纽克扎河 / 392米长的大桥 / 已建成通车。**

> **significant engineering structures**: 正式语体；*major structures* 更短。  
> **section**: 两站间 *section*；与 *segment*（线路分段）略有差别。  
> **across the river**: 介词 *across*；勿用 *cross the river* 作介词短语。  

---

🔹 **Он стал / самым протяженным / из мостовых переходов, / возведенных в ходе второго этапа.**  
🔸 **It became / the longest / of the bridge crossings / erected during the second stage.**  
🔻 **它成为了 / 第二阶段建设中 / 所建桥梁中 / 最长的一座。**

> **the longest of the bridge crossings**: *crossing* 指“跨越结构”；*bridge span* 强调单跨。  
> **erected during**: 过去分词作后置定语；同义 *built during*。  

---

🔹 **В настоящее время / к завершающей стадии / приближается электрификация / линии Волочаевка-2 – Комсомольск-на-Амуре – Советская Гавань / протяженностью 870 км.**  
🔸 **Currently, / the electrification / of the Volochayevka-2 – Komsomolsk-on-Amur – Sovetskaya Gavan line / with a length of 870 km / is approaching / the final stage.**  
🔻 **目前，/ 全长870公里的 / 沃洛恰耶夫卡2站—共青城—苏维埃港线的 / 电气化工程 / 正接近 / 尾声。**

> **electrification**: 不可数名词；动词 *electrify*。行业报道常用 *electrification of the line*。  
> **is approaching the final stage**: *approach* = 接近（阶段尾声）；同义 *nears completion, is nearing completion*。  
> **with a length of 870 km**: 长度作后置修饰；亦可 *870 km long* 作补语。  
> **line**: 铁路语境 = 线路，勿与 *queue* 混淆。  

---

🔹 **Это один из наиболее капиталоемких объектов / Восточного полигона / (первоначально проект оценивался свыше 200 млрд руб.).**  
🔸 **This is one of the most capital-intensive objects / of the Eastern Polygon / (initially the project was valued at over 200 billion rubles).**  
🔻 **这是东方铁路网中 / 资本密集度最高的项目之一 / （最初该项目估值超过2000亿卢布）。**

> **capital-intensive**: 与 *labor-intensive* 对举；可作定语 *capital-intensive projects*。  
> **initially**: 句首或句中；同义 *at first, originally*。  
> **was valued at**: 被动；*The project was valued at over 200 billion rubles*。  

---

🔹 **Сначала / электрифицировать этот участок БАМа / планировалось в рамках второго этапа программы, / то есть до 2024 года.**  
🔸 **Initially, / it was planned / to electrify this section of the BAM / within the second stage of the program, / that is, until 2024.**  
🔻 **最初，/ 该段贝阿铁路的电气化 / 计划在计划的第二阶段内完成，/ 即2024年之前。**

> **electrify**: 及物动词；*electrify this section*。  
> **within the second stage of the program**: *within* 表时间/阶段范围；同义 *under the program*。  
> **that is, until 2024**: 同位解释；书面语亦用 *i.e.*（后常加逗号）。  

---

🔹 **Затем / было объявлено / о переносе срока работ.**  
🔸 **Then / it was announced / that the deadline for the work would be postponed.**  
🔻 **随后，/ 官方宣布 / 该工程期限将被推迟。**

> **it was announced that**: 形式主语结构；同义 *officials announced that*。  
> **deadline**: 可数；*miss a deadline, extend a deadline*。  
> **would be postponed**: 过去将来被动，报道中常见。  

---

🔹 **В ходе стройки / строители уже установили / более 24 тыс. опор контактной сети, / уложили свыше 1,2 тыс. км кабельных линий.**  
🔸 **During the construction, / builders have already installed / more than 24 thousand catenary supports / and laid over 1.2 thousand km of cable lines.**  
🔻 **在施工期间，/ 建设者已安装了 / 2.4万多个接触网支柱，/ 铺设了超过1200公里的电缆线路。**

> **installed / laid**: *lay* 不规则动词 *lay – laid – laid*；易与 *lie* 混淆。  
> **catenary supports**: 接触网支柱；*catenary* 为电力铁路术语。  
> **laid … km of cable**: 量词结构；*over 1.2 thousand km* 口语可 *more than 1,200 km*。  

---

🔹 **Ведутся работы / по строительству и реконструкции / 17 тяговых подстанций.**  
🔸 **Work is underway / on the construction and reconstruction / of 17 traction substations.**  
🔻 **17座牵引变电站的 / 建设和改建工作 / 正在进行中。**

> **work is underway**: 固定搭配；*underway* 常作表语形容词。  
> **traction substation**: *traction* = 牵引；与汽车 *traction control* 同源不同域。  

---

🔹 **В марте / к системе внешнего электроснабжения / подключили подстанцию Сельгон.**  
🔸 **In March, / the Selgon substation / was connected / to the external power supply system.**  
🔻 **今年3月，/ 塞尔贡变电站 / 已接入 / 外部供电系统。**

> **was connected to**: 被动；技术英语常用 *hook up to, tie into*（更口语）。  
> **external power supply system**: *external* 与站内设备相对。  

---

🔹 **Как проинформировали в ДВЖД, / она стала четвертым объектом, / на который подали напряжение, / наряду с подстанциями Эльбан, Алькан (Разъезд №21), Джелюмкен.**  
🔸 **As informed by the Far Eastern Railway (DVRD), / it became the fourth object / to which voltage was supplied, / along with the substations Elban, Alkan (Siding No. 21), and Dzhelyumken.**

> **the idea has been voiced**: *voice* 动词 = 表达；被动 *be voiced*。

🔹 **В свою очередь / вице-премьер РФ – полпред в Дальневосточном федеральном округе / Юрий Трутнев заявил о том, / что задержек в строительстве стратегических объектов инфраструктуры нет.**

> **delays in the construction**: 介词 *in*；*delay* 可数/不可数皆可。  
> **strategic infrastructure**: 形容词修饰名词；*strategic* ≠ *strategical*（后者罕用）。

🔹 **«Никаких сигналов о том, / что РЖД каким-то образом / не справляются с обязательствами, / нет.»**

> **signals**: 此处 = 迹象 *signs*，非电讯信号。  
> **in some way**: 模糊语，避免断言；同义 *to some extent*。

🔹 **«Они помогают нам / выполнять очень важную работу / по расширению Восточного полигона», – сказал он.**

🔻 **“他们正在协助我们 / 完成扩建东方铁路网的 / 这项极其重要的工作，”他说道。**

> **perform work**: *perform* + 抽象名词；口语 *do work*。  
> **expansion**: 与 *extension* 近义；*expansion* 偏规模扩大。  

---

🔹 **Безусловно, / постепенное увеличение провозной способности / имеет место быть, / но возможности БАМа все же не закрывают потребности отправителей, / свидетельствует рынок.**  
🔸 **Undoubtedly, / a gradual increase in carrying capacity / is taking place, / but the capabilities of the BAM still do not cover the needs of shippers, / the market testifies.**  
🔻 **毫无疑问，/ 货运能力确实在逐步提升，/ 但市场反馈显示，/ 贝阿铁路（BAM）的能力仍无法满足发货人的需求。**

> **undoubtedly**: 句首或动词前；语气强于 *probably*。  
> **gradual increase**: 图表描述高频；副词 *gradually*。  
> **do not cover the needs**: *cover* = 满足（需求）；非“覆盖”本义。  
> **the market testifies**: 拟人；更中性 *the data suggest*。  

---

🔹 **Так, одним из ключевых факторов, / определивших деятельность Железных дорог Якутии в прошлом году, / явилось сокращение отправок угля / со станций Чульбасс и Денисовский / вследствие недостаточной провозной способности инфраструктуры РЖД.**  
🔸 **Thus, one of the key factors / that determined the activity of the Yakutian Railways last year / was the reduction in coal shipments / from the Chulbass and Denisovsky stations / due to the insufficient carrying capacity of RZD infrastructure.**  
🔻 **例如，去年决定“雅库特铁路公司”业绩的关键因素之一，/ 便是楚尔巴斯站和杰尼索夫斯基站的煤炭发运量缩减，/ 究其原因，正是俄铁基础设施的货运能力不足。**

> **one of the key factors that determined**: 关系从句；*determined* 过去时。  
> **reduction in coal shipments**: *reduction in* + 名词；*shipment* 可数。  
> **due to insufficient carrying capacity**: *due to* 后接名词；勿接从句（口语除外）。  

---

🔹 **К тому же / реальное освоение перевозок / по контрольным сечениям / и отдельным направлениям Восточного полигона / продолжает вызывать вопросы в профессиональном сообществе.**  
🔸 **In addition, / the actual realization of shipments / through control cross-sections / and individual directions of the Eastern Polygon / continues to raise questions in the professional community.**  
🔻 **此外，/ 东方铁路网在关键断面 / 和特定方向上的 / 实际货运完成情况，/ 依然引发了专业界的质疑。**

> **actual realization of shipments**: *actual* 易与 *current* 混：*actual* = 实际发生。  
> **control cross-sections**: 行业术语；可意译 *monitoring points*。  
> **raise questions**: = *prompt questions*；比 *ask questions* 更客观。  
> **professional community**: = *industry* 更短。  

---

🔹 **По словам члена совета директоров АО «Морцентр» Дениса Илатовского, / сегодня крайне важно добиваться исполнения целевых показателей / по вывозу грузов через Дальний Восток, / которые закреплены в правительственных постановлениях.**  
🔸 **According to Denis Ilatovsky, a member of the board of directors of JSC "Morcenter", / today it is extremely important to achieve the fulfillment of target indicators / for the export of goods through the Far East, / which are fixed in government decrees.**  
🔻 **“海运中心”股份公司董事会成员丹尼斯·伊拉托夫斯基表示，/ 如今达成政府法令中确定的 / 远东货物外运目标指标 / 至关重要。**

> **a member of the board of directors**: 头衔语序；*JSC* = joint-stock company。  
> **it is extremely important to achieve**: 形式主语 *it*；*achieve fulfillment* 可接 *of*。  
> **target indicators**: 政策英语；*KPIs* 口语化。  
> **fixed in government decrees**: *fixed* = 明文规定；被动 *be fixed in*。  

---

🔹 **«О чем говорят эти уважаемые документы? Они говорят, что на Восточный полигон уже с 2024 года должно ехать 180 млн т по сечению.»**  
🔸 **"What do these respected documents say? They say that 180 million tons should already be traveling through the cross-section of the Eastern Polygon starting from 2024."**  
🔻 **“这些权威文件说了什么？它们规定，从2024年起，东方铁路网断面的货运量应达到1.8亿吨。”**

> **respected documents**: *respected* 带礼貌或讽刺；视语境。  
> **cross-section**: 物流/工程统计用语；勿译作“横截面”脱离语境。  

---

🔹 **«И сейчас их не 180 млн: в прошлом году перевезено 159 млн т.»**  
🔸 **"And currently it's not 180 million: last year 159 million tons were transported."**  
🔻 **“而现在并没有达到1.8亿吨：去年仅运输了1.59亿吨。”**

> **were transported**: 被动；*tons were transported* 主谓一致。  

---

🔹 **«То есть у нас имеется недозавоз на Восточный полигон относительно постановления правительства.»**  
🔸 **"That is, we have an under-delivery to the Eastern Polygon relative to the government decree."**  
🔻 **“也就是说，相对于政府法令，我们对东方铁路网的运量未能达标。”**

> **under-delivery**: 前缀 *under-*（未达标准）；类似 *underperformance*。  
> **relative to the decree**: 介词短语；*relative* 形容词前置。  

---

🔹 **«При этом монополия разработала методику, которая доказывает, что 159 млн – это 180 млн», – подчеркивает он.**  
🔸 **"At the same time, the monopoly has developed a methodology that proves that 159 million is 180 million," – he emphasizes.**  
🔻 **“与此同时，这家垄断企业（俄铁）还开发了一套方法论，试图证明1.59亿吨就等同于1.8亿吨，”他强调道。**

> **monopoly**: 可数名词；此处指 RZD。  
> **methodology**: 正式；口语 *method* 更短。  
> **proves that 159 million is 180 million**: 讽刺性并列；引号内为引语。  
> **emphasizes**: 动词；*stress, underline* 近义。  

---

🔹 **Выход на целевые показатели провозной способности / будет не только означать дальнейшее освоение грузовой базы... / но и загрузку вагонного парка операторских компаний.**  
🔸 **Reaching the target indicators for carrying capacity / will not only mean the further development of the freight base... / but also the utilization of the wagon fleet of operator companies.**  
🔻 **达到货运能力目标指标，/ 不仅意味着能进一步开发货源基地，/ 还能提高各运营商车队的使用率。**

> **reaching the target indicators**: *reach* 及物；*indicators* 常用复数。  
> **freight base**: 货源地；*development of the freight base*。  
> **utilization of the wagon fleet**: *utilization* 使用率；*fleet* 集合名词。  
> **operator companies**: 铁路货运里“运营商”；与 *owner* 角色区分。  

---

🔹 **...которая в целом просела из-за сокращения перевозок на сети РЖД, / увеличение заказов на постройку новых вагонов / (объем вагоностроения в стране в 2025 году упал на 28%).**  
🔸 **...which in general has sagged due to the reduction of shipments on the RZD network, / and an increase in orders for the construction of new wagons / (the volume of wagon building in the country in 2025 fell by 28%).**  
🔻 **由于俄铁线路运量减少，车队使用率整体下滑，/ 达标后也将带动新车订单的增加 / （2025年该国的车厢制造业产量下降了28%）。**

> **has sagged**: 非正式经济报道用语 = 疲软；正式 *has declined*。  
> **orders for the construction of**: *order* 可数；介词链 *for*。  
> **the volume of … fell by 28%**: 图表句型；*by* 表示幅度。  

---

🔹 **В рамках третьего этапа модернизации БАМа и Транссиба / к 2031 году / на восток железнодорожным транспортом / должно вывозиться уже 210 млн т, / продолжает эксперт.**  
🔸 **Within the framework of the third stage of modernization of the BAM and Trans-Siberian, / by 2031, / 210 million tons / should already be exported to the east by rail, / the expert continues.**  
🔻 **这位专家继续说道，在贝阿铁路和西伯利亚大铁路现代化的第三阶段框架下，/ 到2031年，/ 铁路向东部出口的运量应达到2.1亿吨。**

> **by 2031**: 截止时间；*by* ≠ *until*（语义不同）。  
> **exported to the east by rail**: 被动；*by rail* 方式状语。  

---

🔹 **В дальнейшем / этот объем возрастет до 270 млн т.**  
🔸 **In the future, / this volume will increase to 270 million tons.**  
🔻 **未来，/ 这一数值还将增加到2.7亿吨。**

> **in the future**: 将来时标记；与 *going forward* 近义（口语）。  
> **will increase to**: 表预测；*rise to, grow to* 可换。  

---

🔹 **«Если просто требовать этих сакральных цифр – 180, 210 и 270, / то в принципе вся экономическая цепочка будет в позитиве или в балансе.»**  
🔸 **"If we simply demand these sacred figures – 180, 210, and 270, / then in principle the entire economic chain will be in the positive or in balance."**  
🔻 **“如果仅仅是要求达成这些‘神圣’的数字——1.8亿、2.1亿和2.7亿，/ 那么原则上整个经济链条将处于增长或平衡状态。”**

> **sacred figures**: 隐喻；讽刺“数字不可质疑”。  
> **in principle**: 与 *in practice* 常对举。  
> **economic chain**: = *supply chain* 部分重叠；此处偏宏观。  
> **in the positive or in balance**: 俄式英译痕迹；更自然 *in positive territory or in balance*。  

---

🔹 **«Будет спрос на вагоностроение, / будет спрос на перевозку тяжелых грузов», – рассуждает Денис Илатовский.**  
🔸 **"There will be demand for wagon building, / there will be demand for the transportation of heavy goods," – Denis Ilatovsky reasons.**  
🔻 **“届时将会有对车厢制造的需求，/ 也会有对重载货物运输的需求，”丹尼斯·伊拉托夫斯基论述道。**

> **demand for**: 名词 *demand* 搭配 *for*；动词 *demand* 搭配不同。  
> **reason** (v.): 此处作动词，义近 *argue / contend*（逻辑推演），非名词“原因”；口语可换 *says, argues*。  

---

🔹 **В РЖД, / объясняя разницу / между расчетными показателями и текущей картиной перевозок, / ссылались на ряд факторов.**  
🔸 **In RZD, / explaining the difference / between the calculated indicators and the current picture of shipments, / they referred to a number of factors.**  
🔻 **俄铁公司在解释 / 理论计算指标与当前运输现状之间的差距时，/ 列举了一系列因素。**

> **the difference between A and B**: *between … and …* 平行结构。  
> **calculated indicators**: 与 *actual* 对举。  
> **referred to a number of factors**: *refer to* = 援引；过去时 *referred*。  

---

🔹 **При активном строительстве / целевой параметр не достижим / из-за большого числа технологических окон, / требующих перерыва в движении.**  
🔸 **During active construction, / the target parameter is not achievable / due to a large number of technological windows / requiring a break in traffic.**  
🔻 **在施工活跃期，/ 由于存在大量的“技术天窗”（停运检修时间），/ 目标参数是无法实现的。**

> **not achievable**: *achievable* 可前置；或 *cannot be achieved*。  
> **due to**: 正式；口语 *because of*。  
> **technological window**: 铁路借词；英语亦可用 *maintenance window*。  
> **a break in traffic**: *break* = 中断；*traffic* 含列车运行。  

---

🔹 **В качестве других причин / приводилась необходимость отводить часть грузопотока / на другие направления.**  
🔸 **As other reasons, / the need to divert part of the freight flow / to other directions was cited.**  
🔻 **其他原因还包括 / 需要将部分货流 / 分流到其他方向。**

> **divert … freight flow**: *divert* + 宾语 + *to*；正式用语。  
> **was cited**: 被动；*reasons were cited*。  

---

🔹 **...в частности, в адрес железнодорожного погранперехода с КНР Забайкальск – Маньчжурия, / где также идет реконструкция / и имеется повышенный спрос на перевозку со стороны российских компаний.**  
🔸 **...in particular, towards the railway border crossing with the PRC, Zabaykalsk – Manzhouli, / where reconstruction is also underway / and there is increased demand for transportation from Russian companies.**  
🔻 **……特别是流向中俄边境的后贝加尔斯克—满洲里铁路口岸，/ 该口岸目前也在进行改建，/ 且俄罗斯公司对其运输需求激增。**

> **railway border crossing**: 固定搭配；*with* 引出邻国。  
> **PRC**: 正式文书缩写；口语 *China*。  
> **reconstruction is underway**: *underway* 表进行；同义 *in progress*。  
> **increased demand for transportation**: *demand for* + 名词。  

---

🔹 **...увеличение в графике количества контейнерных поездов: / масса таких составов гораздо меньше, чем у маршрутов с углем, нефтепродуктами и т.п., / а следовательно, это размывает общий эффект.**  
🔸 **...an increase in the number of container trains in the schedule: / the mass of such compositions is much smaller than that of routes with coal, petroleum products, etc., / and consequently, this blurs the overall effect.**  
🔻 **……运行图中集装箱列车数量的增加：/ 这类列车的重量远低于煤炭、石油产品等专列，/ 因此摊薄了总运量数据。**

> **in the schedule**: *schedule* = 运行图/时刻表；美式 *timetable* 亦用。  
> **container trains**: 与散装煤列对比；*composition* 此处 = 编组列车。  
> **the mass of such compositions**: *mass* 物理/运输量；与 *volume, tonnage* 相关。  
> **consequently**: 正式连接词；同义 *therefore, as a result*。  
> **blur the overall effect**: 隐喻“冲淡效果”；非“视觉模糊”本义。  

---

🔹 **В зимний период / в недозагрузе РЖД обвиняли получателей угля – / из-за смерзаемости груза плохо шла выгрузка в портах.**  
🔸 **In the winter period, / RZD blamed coal recipients for the under-loading – / due to the freezing of the cargo, unloading in ports went poorly.**  
🔻 **在冬季，/ 俄铁公司将装载不足归咎于煤炭接收方—— / 由于货物易冻结，港口的卸货工作进展缓慢。**

> **blame A for B**: 结构固定；被动 *A was blamed for B*。  
> **freezing of the cargo**: *freezing* 动名词；专业语境“冻结粘结”。  
> **unloading went poorly**: *go* + 副词，口语叙事；正式 *unloading was delayed*。  

---

🔹 **...на подходах к ним скапливался подвижной состав, / и монополии приходилось объявлять конвенцию / на погрузку в адрес припортовых станций.**  
🔸 **...rolling stock accumulated on the approaches to them, / and the monopoly had to declare a convention / on loading towards port stations.**  
🔻 **……导致大量列车在港口入口处积压，/ 俄铁公司不得不宣布“装车禁令（公约）”，/ 限制向港口车站发运货物。**

> **rolling stock**: 集合名词，机车车辆总称；无复数形式。  
> **accumulated on the approaches**: *approaches* = 进站引线段；动词 *accumulate*。  
> **declare a convention**: 行业俚语借 *convention*；一般读者易误解为“国际公约”，宜靠上下文。  

---

🔹 **Несмотря на объяснения перевозчика, / грузовладельцы хотели бы понимать / реальные достижения показателей по участкам Восточного полигона / и фактическим объемам перевозимых грузов.**  
🔸 **Despite the explanations of the carrier, / cargo owners would like to understand / the real achievements of indicators by sections of the Eastern Polygon / and the actual volumes of transported goods.**  
🔻 **尽管承运方给出了种种解释，/ 货主们仍希望了解 / 东方铁路网各路段指标的真实完成情况 / 以及实际运送的货物量。**

> **carrier**: 承运方；与 *shipper* 区分。  
> **cargo owners**: 货主；亦作 *cargo interest*（法律文体）。  
> **actual**: 易混词——≠ *current*（当前）；*actual volumes* = 实际发生量。  

---

🔹 **Стоит сказать, / в Совете Федерации предлагали РЖД и Минтрансу / ежеквартально раскрывать информацию / по фактическому исполнению работ.**  
🔸 **It is worth saying / that in the Federation Council, it was proposed to RZD and the Ministry of Transport / to disclose information quarterly / on the actual execution of work.**  
🔻 **值得一提的是，/ 俄罗斯联邦委员会（上议院）曾建议俄铁和交通部 / 每季度披露一次 / 工程实际进度的信息。**

> **it was proposed to … to disclose**: 不定式作宾补；*propose that* 从句用虚拟（英式）。  
> **quarterly**: 副词由形容词转化；*disclose information quarterly*。  
> **disclose**: 正式“披露”；*reveal* 更中性。  

---

🔹 **«Например, сколько тонн грузов, и каких именно, / не вывезено в связи с новым строительством и отдельно с ремонтом [пути], / какие реальные параметры пропускных способностей с учетом ремонтных окон», – пояснял ранее Андрей Кутепов.**  
🔸 **"For example, how many tons of cargo, and which exactly, / were not exported due to new construction and separately due to road repairs, / what are the real parameters of traffic capacities taking into account repair windows," – Andrei Kutepov explained earlier.**  
🔻 **“例如，有多少吨、什么种类的货物 / 因为新工程或道路维修而未能运出，/ 考虑到维修天窗后的实际通行参数是多少，”安德烈·库捷波夫此前解释道。**

> **due to … and separately due to**: 并列原因；*separately* 强调分项。  
> **road repairs**: 此处指“线路/路基维修”；勿死译成“公路”。  
> **taking into account**: 介词短语；同义 *given, allowing for*。  
> **explained earlier**: 插入语；时态过去 *explained*。  

---

🔹 **Судя по всему, / это так и осталось пожеланием.**  
🔸 **Apparently, / this remained just a wish.**  
🔻 **显然，/ 这仅仅停留在愿望阶段。**

> **apparently**: 推断语气，非100%肯定；同义 *seemingly, it would seem*。  
> **remained just a wish**: *remain* + 名词补语；*wish* 与 *mandate* 语气对比。  

---

🔹 **Тем временем / высказываются предпочтения / относительно реализации альтернативных транспортных проектов.**  
🔸 **Meanwhile, / preferences are being expressed / regarding the implementation of alternative transport projects.**  
🔻 **与此同时，/ 有人开始倾向于 / 实施其他的替代性运输项目。**

> **meanwhile**: 段落衔接；句首常加逗号。  
> **preferences are being expressed**: 被动进行，书面委婉（不愿点名主语）。  
> **regarding**: 介词，= *on, about*；勿与 *regardless* 混淆。  
> **alternative transport projects**: *alternative* + 名词；与 *main* 对举。  

---

🔹 **«Восточный полигон, на мой взгляд, / объемом вывозимой продукции конечен, / и расширять [дальше], увеличивать на десятки миллионов тонн я бы, например, не стал.»**  
🔸 **"The Eastern Polygon, in my view, / is finite in terms of the volume of exported products, / and I, for example, would not expand [further] or increase it by tens of millions of tons."**  
🔻 **“在我看来，东方铁路网能承载的出口产品量是有限的，/ 换做是我，就不会再继续扩建或追求增加数千万吨的运量。”**

> **in my view**: 观点标记语；同义 *in my opinion*（稍随意）。  
> **finite in terms of volume**: *in terms of* 界定维度；*finite* 与 *infinite* 对举。  
> **tens of millions of tons**: 数量级表达；注意 *million* 单复数与 *of*。  

---

🔹 **«Я бы эти деньги / переориентировал на другие проекты, / о которых заявляет наш президент.»**  
🔸 **"I would / reorient this money / to other projects / stated by our president."**  
🔻 **“我会将这些资金 / 重新投向 / 总统所提到的其他项目中。”**

> **reorient … to**: *re-* 前缀“重新”；搭配 *reorient funding to other projects*。  
> **stated by our president**: 被动 *stated*；过去分词短语作后置定语。  

---

# 模块一：翻译与全文概要

## 英文翻译

**A Polygon of Uncertainty: Why the Eastern Polygon Falls Short of Its Targets**
*(EastRussia, March 17, 2026 | Author: Pavel Usov)*

In 2026, funding for the BAM and Trans-Siberian modernization program may be cut by more than two-thirds against the backdrop of a general reduction in Russian Railways' (RZhD) capital investment program. How the project will proceed is difficult to say — even under more favorable financial conditions, previous phases of the Eastern Polygon's development were repeatedly delayed. Meanwhile, both freight shippers and concerned regions are calling for clearer information about the infrastructure gains from reconstruction — not only in the long term, but right now.

**Rails and Wires**

Although RZhD announced the completion of the second phase of Eastern Polygon development, work on it continued actively throughout 2025. On the BAM alone, train operations were opened on more than 30 new facilities, according to the Bamstroymekhanizatsiya press center. Second tracks were being laid and stations reconstructed, expanding the carrying capacity of the most congested sections of the mainline.

Among major projects: the junction station Komsomolsk-Sortirovochny, where the transit yard "D" — designed for long-train handling — entered operation. Total track length built: 15.6 km. At Tynda station, the new transit yard "T" received its first trains, boosting throughput from 2,400 to 3,000 wagons per day. Among significant engineering structures, a 392-meter bridge across the Nyukzha River was opened on the Taluma–Dyugabul section (Tynda–Khani line) — the longest bridge built during the second phase.

At present, electrification of the 870-km Volochayevka-2 – Komsomolsk-on-Amur – Sovetskaya Gavan line is approaching completion. This is one of the most capital-intensive projects in the Eastern Polygon (originally estimated at over 200 billion rubles). It was initially planned for completion within Phase 2, i.e., by 2024, but was subsequently postponed. During construction, workers have already installed more than 24,000 overhead line poles and laid over 1,200 km of cable. Work is underway on 17 traction substations. In March, the Selgon substation was connected to the external power supply system — the fourth facility to receive power, alongside Elban, Alkan (Siding No. 21), and Dzelyumken.

At the same time, ports of the Vanino cluster are preparing for electrification. The Osterra specialized terminal (formerly Daltranusgol) plans to electrify over 45 km of its own railway tracks, allowing electric locomotives to operate directly on port infrastructure, accelerating wagon placement and removal.

Passenger train operations on electric traction from Volochayevka-2 to Komsomolsk are planned for this year; from Komsomolsk to Sovetskaya Gavan — next year. Combined with ongoing second-track construction, this will increase infrastructure throughput — including for heavy freight — and reduce environmental impact. The carrying capacity of the Komsomolsk–Vanino section is expected to reach 81.8 million tonnes per year, with a line capacity of 53 train pairs per day.

Electrification could also benefit passenger services — in particular, launching electric trains would improve connectivity between Khabarovsk, Komsomolsk, and other settlements in Khabarovsk Krai.

The program is entering its third phase, which requires building over 2,000 km of second tracks and more than 1,500 engineering structures. The goal is to transform the BAM into a full-fledged double-track mainline.

On electric traction, there have been proposals at the state level for full BAM electrification, though RZhD has argued for retaining diesel locomotives on certain sections for energy security reasons and to stimulate domestic locomotive manufacturing. The electrification project is also costly, and prevailing macroeconomic conditions are constraining the infrastructure owner's investment resources. In 2025, Eastern Polygon funding was 116.9 billion rubles; in 2026, approximately 34 billion. Nevertheless, even in a reduced investment program, BAM/Trans-Sib modernization still accounts for more than half of RZhD's expenditure on mainline infrastructure development projects — excluding the Moscow–St. Petersburg high-speed rail line.

**Infrastructure Grows, But Can't Keep Up**

Assessments of the Eastern Polygon's modernization pace vary. Eastern railways remain a bottleneck, says Pavel Fedyayev, First Deputy Chairman of the State Duma Committee on Transport and Transport Infrastructure Development. Even with some easing of freight flows in 2025, they are operating at the limit of their capacity.

"I was one of the co-authors of the infrastructure development bill that allowed simultaneous design and preparatory work, which had a positive effect on construction. Unfortunately, that law expired in 2025 without any initiative to extend it. Overall, Eastern Polygon development is the primary rail challenge. We just need to get on with building — unfortunately, the projects there are seriously behind schedule," said the legislator.

Russian Deputy Prime Minister and Presidential Plenipotentiary in the Far Eastern Federal District Yuri Trutnev, on the other hand, stated that there are no delays in the construction of strategic infrastructure. "There are no signals that RZhD is in any way failing to meet its obligations. They are helping us carry out the very important work of expanding the Eastern Polygon," he said.

Gradual increases in carrying capacity are indeed taking place — but BAM's capacity still falls short of shippers' needs, the market indicates. One key factor weighing on Yakutian Railways' performance last year was a reduction in coal dispatches from Chulbass and Denisovsky stations, attributable to insufficient RZhD infrastructure capacity.

Moreover, actual cargo throughput at control cross-sections (specifically the Novy Urgal–Komsomolsk and Izvestkovaya–Volochayevka segments) and along individual Eastern Polygon corridors continues to raise questions in the professional community.

**Sacred Numbers**

According to Denis Ilatovsky, member of the board of directors of JSC Morcenter, it is now critically important to achieve the freight export target figures for the Far East as enshrined in government resolutions.

"What do those respected documents say? They say that 180 million tonnes should already be moving through the Eastern Polygon cross-section as of 2024. But the figure isn't 180 million today — last year it was 159 million. So we have a 21-million-tonne shortfall relative to the government's resolution. Meanwhile, the monopoly has developed a methodology that purports to show that 159 million equals 180 million," he emphasized.

Meeting the target throughput figures will not only mean further unlocking of the cargo base and satisfying logistics needs across multiple economic sectors, but also loading the wagon fleet of operating companies (overall utilization has slumped due to reduced traffic on the RZhD network), and boosting orders for new wagon construction (wagon production in Russia fell 28% in 2025).

Under Phase 3 of the BAM and Trans-Sib modernization, 210 million tonnes should be moving east by rail by 2031, Ilatovsky continued. That figure is ultimately projected to rise to 270 million tonnes. "If we simply insist on these sacred numbers — 180, 210, 270 — then in principle the entire economic chain will be in positive territory or at least in balance. There will be demand for wagon manufacturing, demand for heavy-haul transport," he reasoned.

RZhD, in explaining the gap between target figures and actual freight flows, cited several factors: active construction requires frequent maintenance windows that interrupt traffic; part of the freight flow must be diverted to other corridors — notably the Zabaikalsk–Manzhouli rail border crossing with China, which is also undergoing reconstruction and faces high demand from Russian shippers; an increase in the number of container trains, whose tonnage is far lower than coal or petroleum product trains, diluting the overall tonnage effect. In winter, RZhD blamed coal recipients for poor unloading at ports (due to frozen cargo), leading to rolling stock accumulation on port approaches and the need to declare a moratorium on loading to port-side stations.

Despite the carrier's explanations, freight owners want to understand the actual achievement of performance indicators across Eastern Polygon sections and actual cargo volumes.

It is worth noting that the Federation Council proposed that RZhD and the Ministry of Transport publish quarterly reports on actual progress and the achievement of carrying and line capacity targets for each corridor specified in the investment project passport — including, for instance, how many tonnes of what cargo were not transported due to new construction versus routine track maintenance, and what the actual capacity parameters are with maintenance windows factored in. This apparently remained a wish rather than a mandate.

Meanwhile, calls are growing for alternative transport projects. "The Eastern Polygon, in my view, has a finite cargo export ceiling, and I personally would not keep expanding it by tens of millions of tonnes. I would redirect that money toward other projects announced by our president — though it clearly requires careful prioritization," said Sergei Burago, Deputy Director General of RustaLogistik LLC.

These projects include the Northern Latitudinal Railway (Obskaya–Salekhard–Nadym–Novy Urengoy–Korotchayevo), the North Siberian Railway, and a meridional route connecting the Trans-Siberian to China's rail network via the Kyzyl–Kuragino corridor in Tuva.

Russia's ambitious Eastern Polygon — the collective upgrade of the Baikal-Amur Mainline (BAM) and Trans-Siberian Railway — stands at a critical crossroads in 2026.
俄罗斯雄心勃勃的"东部多边形"计划——即贝阿铁路（BAM）与西伯利亚大铁路（Trans-Sib）的联合升级工程——在2026年面临重要十字路口。

本年度经费已缩减至去年水平的约三分之一（从1169亿卢布降至约340亿），但第三阶段建设已正式启动。

实体工程进展真实但参差不齐：第二阶段完成了关键枢纽站改造、一座里程碑式的跨河大桥，并即将完成一段870公里支线的电气化改造，这些都将提升货运和客运能力。

然而核心差距触目惊心：政府规定2024年东部多边形截面运量须达1.8亿吨，2025年实际运量仅为1.59亿吨——缺口达12%——而俄铁却以统计重构代替透明问责。

A broader debate is emerging: whether to keep pouring capital into the Eastern Polygon or pivot to alternative mega-corridors — the Northern Latitudinal Railway, the North Siberian line, and a potential Trans-Sib–China meridional link through Tuva.
更大范围的讨论正在涌现：是否应继续向东部多边形注资，抑或转向其他重大走廊——北纬铁路、西伯利亚北部干线，以及经图瓦的跨西伯利亚—中国经向连接线。

The article's central tension — between official optimism and market frustration — underscores a structural challenge in large-scale state-led infrastructure: the distance between plan and performance is not merely logistical, but political.
文章的核心张力——官方乐观与市场不满之间的落差——揭示了国家主导大型基础设施建设中的结构性挑战：计划与实效之间的距离，不仅是物流问题，更是政治问题。

- **同义词**：chokepoint, constraint, impediment, congestion point
- **反义词**：smooth flow, throughput efficiency
- **常见搭配**：create/cause a bottleneck（造成瓶颈）；remove/eliminate a bottleneck（消除瓶颈）；a bottleneck in supply chains（供应链瓶颈）；remain a bottleneck（仍是瓶颈）
- **拓展**：
  - 可数名词（countable）。原意为瓶颈部，后引申为任何制约整体效率的关键节点。
  - 在经济学与管理学中常见于"Theory of Constraints"（约束理论）讨论。
  - 与 *chokepoint* 区分：后者多用于军事、地缘政治场景（如霍尔木兹海峡），*bottleneck* 更泛用于工程、物流、生产流程。
- **例句**：
  > **Underfunding and outdated rolling stock** have long made the eastern rail corridor a chronic **bottleneck** for Russia's commodity exporters.
  > 资金不足和陈旧的机车车辆长期以来使东部铁路走廊成为俄罗斯大宗商品出口商长期面临的瓶颈。

---

**② earmark (sth for sth)**
- **音标**：/ˈɪrˌmɑːrk/（动词/名词）
- **英文释义**（Cambridge）：To decide officially that money will be used for a particular purpose. (*be earmarked for sth*)
- **中文释义**：专项拨付；划定（资金）用途；指定用于……
- **语域**：正式 / 财政 / 新闻 / 政策文件
- **同义词**：designate, allocate, set aside, reserve, apportion
- **反义词**：reallocate, divert, freeze
- **常见搭配**：be earmarked for investment（被专项划拨用于投资）；funds earmarked for infrastructure（专用基础设施资金）；earmark a budget（划定预算用途）
- **拓展**：
  - 动词规则变化；常以被动语态出现：*funds are earmarked for...*
  - 词源：字面义为在牲畜耳朵上做标记以标注归属，后引申为"标记特定用途"。
  - 名词用法：an *earmark* 也指财政拨款中被专项指定的部分（美国国会预算语境）。
- **例句**：
  > More than half of the holding's **earmarked** development budget will go **toward** expanding **mainline rail infrastructure** in the east.
  > 该控股集团超过一半的专项发展预算将用于扩建东部干线铁路基础设施。

**③ overhaul**
- **音标**：/ˈoʊvərˌhɔːl/（名词）；/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːl/（动词）
- **英文释义**（LDOCE）：(n.) Necessary changes or repairs made to a machine or system; (v.) to repair or improve something thoroughly.
- **中文释义**：（名）彻底检修；大修；全面改革；（动）对…进行彻底检修/改造
- **语域**：书面 / 新闻 / 工程 / 政策
- **同义词**：renovation, revamp, restructuring, rehabilitation（基建语境）
- **反义词**：neglect, deterioration
- **常见搭配**：a major/complete overhaul（大规模改造）；undergo an overhaul（接受检修改造）；overhaul the system（对系统进行全面改革）
- **拓展**：
  - 名词可数（a major overhaul；undergo several overhauls）。
  - 动词规则变化：overhaul → overhauled → overhauled。
  - 与 *renovation* 区分：*renovation* 偏指建筑翻新；*overhaul* 偏指机械、系统或制度的彻底检修。
  - *Restructuring* 侧重组织架构变革；*overhaul* 更强调系统性修复与改进。
- **例句**：
  > The **complete overhaul** of the signaling and traction systems is expected to **slash transit times** along the corridor by 30 percent.
  > 对信号与牵引系统的彻底改造预计将使该走廊的运输时间缩短30%。

**④ throughput**
- **音标**：/ˈθruːˌpʊt/（名词）
- **英文释义**（LDOCE）：The amount of work, goods, or people that are dealt with in a particular period of time.
- **中文释义**：吞吐量；产量；处理量；通过量（铁路语境中指"通过能力/运输能力"）
- **语域**：技术 / 商业 / 工程 / 新闻
- **同义词**：output, volume, flow rate, handling capacity
- **常见搭配**：increase/boost throughput（提升吞吐量）；high/low throughput（高/低吞吐量）；throughput capacity（通过能力）；freight throughput（货物吞吐量）
- **拓展**：
  - **不可数名词（uncountable）**，但可加修饰语：*a weekly throughput of 100,000 tonnes*。
  - 与 *capacity* 区分：*capacity* 是静态的最大可能量，*throughput* 是实际流动/处理的动态量。与 *output* 区分：*output* 多指生产产出，*throughput* 强调流经系统的量。
  - 在铁路语境中，俄文 "провозная способность"（carrying capacity）和"пропускная способность"（line capacity / throughput capacity）均可以 *throughput* 对应，但严格区分时前者译 *freight-carrying capacity*，后者译 *traffic throughput capacity*。
- **例句**：
  > The completion of the **second-track** expansion is projected to raise the line's annual **freight throughput** from 50 million to over 80 million tonnes.
  > 双线扩建完成后，预计该线路的年货运吞吐量将从5000万吨提升至8000万吨以上。

**⑤ fall short of**
- **音标**：/fɔːl ʃɔːrt əv/（短语动词）
- **英文释义**：To fail to reach a required level or standard; to be less than what is needed or expected.
- **中文释义**：未达到（标准/目标）；不足；差距；有缺口
- **语域**：新闻 / 正式 / 书面
- **同义词**：fail to meet, underperform, come up short, miss the target
- **反义词**：meet, achieve, exceed, surpass
- **常见搭配**：fall short of the target/goal/plan（未达目标）；fall short of expectations（未达预期）；fall far short of（远未达到）
- **拓展**：
  - 注意介词 *of*，不可省略；不规则过去式：fall → fell → fallen short of。
  - 可用于多种主语：*results fell short of*, *actual figures fell short of*, *the project fell short of its original timeline*。
  - 在经济报告和政策分析类写作中极为常用，可替换 *fail to reach* 使文章更地道。
- **例句**：
  > Actual coal tonnage exported through the eastern corridor **fell far short of** the **government-mandated target**, exposing a systemic gap between planning ambition and operational reality.
  > 经东部走廊实际出口的煤炭吨数远未达到政府规定目标，揭示了规划雄心与运营现实之间的系统性落差。

### R — 阅读高频词

---

**⑥ corridor**
- **音标**：/ˈkɔːrɪdər/（名词）
- **英文释义**（LDOCE）：A passage between two rows of rooms; also, a narrow strip of land connecting two places, or a route used for transport.
- **中文释义**：走廊；（运输/政治）通道；（地理）狭长地带
- **语域**：地缘政治 / 交通 / 新闻 / 地理
- **常见搭配**：transport corridor（运输走廊）；trade corridor（贸易走廊）；rail corridor（铁路走廊）；the Eastern corridor（东部通道）
- **拓展**：
  - 可数名词。在地缘政治语境中，"corridor"常指连接两地或提供战略通道的狭长区域（如"波兰走廊"），不等同于 *route*（更强调具体路径）。

- **例句**：
  > The **Northern Sea Route** and the eastern rail **corridor** are increasingly viewed as complementary pillars of Russia's **export diversification** strategy.
  > 北海航线与东部铁路走廊日益被视为俄罗斯出口多元化战略相辅相成的两大支柱。

**⑦ electrification**
- **音标**：/ɪˌlektrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/（名词）
- **英文释义**：The process of converting a railway, region, or system to use electricity as its power source.
- **中文释义**：电气化；电力化（铁路线路改造的核心工程术语）
- **语域**：工程 / 新闻 / 政策
- **同义词**：conversion to electric power, power conversion
- **常见搭配**：railway/rail electrification（铁路电气化）；electrification project（电气化项目）；complete/full electrification（全线电气化）；electrification of the line（线路电气化）
- **拓展**：
  - 不可数名词（uncountable）。其动词形式为 *electrify*（过去式 *electrified*）。
  - 与 *energization*（通电）区分：*electrification* 强调系统性改造工程，*energization* 特指将某节点接入电网/投入运行。
  - 与政策词汇"carbon neutrality"（碳中和）联动使用频率高，因铁路电气化是减少柴油机车碳排放的重要手段。
- **例句**：
  > **Railway electrification** not only reduces operational costs for the carrier but also **cuts carbon emissions** substantially, making it a key plank of green transport policy.
  > 铁路电气化不仅能降低运营成本，还能大幅削减碳排放，是绿色交通政策的重要支柱。

**⑧ moratorium**
- **音标**：/ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːriəm/（名词）
- **英文释义**（LDOCE）：An official agreement to stop an activity for a period of time.
- **中文释义**：（官方）暂停令；临时禁令；延期偿付令（金融语境）
- **语域**：法律 / 金融 / 新闻 / 正式书面
- **同义词**：suspension, freeze, halt, standstill（非正式）
- **反义词**：resumption, reinstatement
- **常见搭配**：declare a moratorium（宣布暂停令）；a moratorium on loading（禁止装车令）；impose/lift a moratorium（实施/解除暂停令）；debt moratorium（债务延期偿付）
- **拓展**：
  - 可数名词，复数为 *moratoria* 或 *moratoriums*。
  - 词源：拉丁语 *moratorius*（造成延迟的），在金融语境中特指"延期偿债协议"；在行政/行业管理中指"临时性限制措施"。
  - 文中俄语原文"конвенция на погрузку"（装载公约/限装令）的准确英文对应即为 *moratorium on loading* 或 *loading embargo*。
- **例句**：
  > Faced with **port congestion** and **frozen cargo** backlogs in winter, the railway operator was forced to **declare a moratorium** on freight loading to port-bound destinations.
  > 面对冬季港口拥堵和货物冻结积压，铁路运营商被迫对驶往港口方向的货物装载发布暂停令。

**⑨ mandate** (n. & v.)
- **音标**：/ˈmændeɪt/（名词/动词）
- **英文释义**（LDOCE）：(n.) An official instruction or authority given to an organization to carry out a policy; (v.) to give official authority for something to happen.
- **中文释义**：（名）授权；指令；使命；（动）授权；强制规定；委托
- **语域**：正式 / 法律 / 政策 / 新闻
- **同义词**（n.）：directive, order, instruction, authorization, remit
- **常见搭配**：government mandate（政府指令）；a mandate to do sth（授权做某事）；mandated target（法规规定的目标）；government-mandated（政府授权/规定的）
- **拓展**：
  - 名词可数；动词 *mandate* 后常接不定式：*be mandated to publish quarterly reports*（被规定每季度公布报告）。
  - 注意：*mandate* 在美式英语中作动词使用更为常见（法律/政策语境）；英式英语中较多以名词形式出现。
  - 与 *directive* 区分：*directive* 更常见于EU/机构政策；*mandate* 含更强的授权与责任含义。
- **例句**：
  > The **government-mandated** freight volume of 180 million tonnes per year was enshrined in an official resolution, yet no enforcement mechanism was put in place to ensure compliance.
  > 政府规定的每年1.8亿吨货运量目标已写入官方决议，但始终未建立任何执行机制来确保合规。

**⑩ divert**
- **音标**：/daɪˈvɜːrt/（动词）
- **英文释义**（LDOCE）：To change the direction of something, especially traffic or money, so that it goes somewhere different.
- **中文释义**：转移（资金/货物/注意力）；改变方向；分流
- **语域**：新闻 / 物流 / 政策 / 通用
- **同义词**：redirect, reroute, channel, shift
- **常见搭配**：divert freight（转移货流）；divert funds from sth to sth（将资金从…转向…）；divert traffic（分流交通）；divert attention（转移注意力）
- **拓展**：

### T — 翻译重要词

---

**⑪ carrying capacity / freight-carrying capacity**
- **音标**：/ˈkæriɪŋ kəˈpæsəti/
- **英文释义**：The maximum volume of freight (usually measured in tonnes per year) that a railway line or section can transport within a given period.
- **中文释义**：（铁路）运输能力；载运量；（区别于"通过能力"的）货物运量上限
- **语域**：交通工程 / 铁路政策 / 技术文献
- **拓展**：
  - 对应俄文："провозная способность"（运输能力/载运量）≠"пропускная способность"（通过能力，即单位时间可通过的列车对数）。翻译时需区分：
    - **провозная способность** → *freight-carrying capacity*（以吨位计量）
    - **пропускная способность** → *line capacity / throughput capacity*（以列车对数计量）
  - 两者在中文中均可译为"运能"，但在专业语境中须加以区分。
- **例句**：
  > The distinction between **line capacity** — measured in train pairs per day — and **freight-carrying capacity** — measured in annual tonnage — is often obscured in public reporting, leading to confusion over whether targets have been met.
  > "通过能力"（以每日列车对数计量）与"运输能力"（以年度吨位计量）之间的区别，在公开报道中常被模糊，导致外界对目标是否实现产生混淆。

---

**⑫ traction substation / traction power supply**
- **音标**：/ˈtrækʃən ˌsʌbsteɪʃən/
- **英文释义**：An electrical installation that converts and distributes electricity to railway overhead lines for locomotive traction purposes.
- **中文释义**：牵引变电站；牵引供电系统
- **语域**：铁路工程 / 技术文献
- **拓展**：
  - 对应俄文："тяговая подстанция"
  - 在铁路电气化报道中的标准术语，不可用 *power station*（发电站）替代，后者指发电设施而非变电配电设施。
  - 延伸词汇：*overhead line / catenary*（接触网）；*pantograph*（受电弓）；*electric locomotive*（电力机车）
- **例句**：
  > The commissioning of **traction substations** at regular intervals along the newly electrified segment is a prerequisite for authorizing **electric locomotive operations** on the line.
  > 在新建电气化区段沿线定期投入牵引变电站运行，是授权电力机车上线运营的先决条件。

---

**⑬ capital-intensive**
- **音标**：/ˌkæpɪtl ɪnˈtensɪv/（形容词）
- **英文释义**（LDOCE）：Requiring a large amount of money in relation to the number of workers or the amount produced.
- **中文释义**：资本密集型的；耗资巨大的
- **语域**：经济学 / 商业 / 新闻 / 正式书面
- **同义词**：high-cost, investment-heavy, resource-intensive（广义）
- **反义词**：labor-intensive（劳动密集型的）；asset-light（轻资产的）
- **常见搭配**：capital-intensive project（资本密集型项目）；capital-intensive industry（资本密集型产业）；highly capital-intensive（极度资本密集的）
- **拓展**：
  - 对应俄文："капиталоёмкий"（如：один из наиболее капиталоемких объектов）
  - 注意词组结构：形容词前置修饰名词，或用 *be capital-intensive* 作表语。
  - 与 *labor-intensive*（劳动密集型）、*technology-intensive*（技术密集型）并列为重要的产业分类形容词。
- **例句**：
  > Full electrification of the BAM is among the most **capital-intensive** undertakings in Russia's infrastructure pipeline, with cost estimates running well above 200 billion rubles.
  > 贝阿铁路全线电气化是俄罗斯基础设施项目中最为资本密集的工程之一，成本估算远超2000亿卢布。

---

**⑭ maintenance window (technical window)**
- **音标**：/ˈmeɪntənəns ˈwɪndoʊ/
- **英文释义**：A scheduled period during which railway traffic is halted to allow track inspection, repair, or construction work.
- **中文释义**：维修窗口；（铁路）施工天窗；作业间隙
- **语域**：铁路工程 / 运营管理 / 技术文献
- **同义词**：engineering possession, track possession, scheduled maintenance slot
- **拓展**：
  - 对应俄文："технологическое окно"（技术窗口）。
  - 在铁路运营中，"天窗"时间是开展大量施工和维护工作的核心机制，但也是制约实际通过能力的关键因素——文中RZhD即以此解释运量缺口。
  - 英文中也可用 *possession* 或 *track occupation period* 等表达。
- **例句**：
  > During periods of **active construction**, frequent **maintenance windows** inevitably reduce the number of train paths available, temporarily **depressing** actual freight throughput below theoretical capacity.
  > 在大规模施工期间，频繁的维修天窗不可避免地减少了可用的列车运行路径，使实际货运量暂时低于理论通过能力。

---

**⑮ investment program / capital expenditure program**
- **音标**：/ɪnˈvestmənt ˈproʊɡræm/
- **英文释义**：A formally approved plan outlining the allocation of capital to specific infrastructure or business development projects over a defined period.
- **中文释义**：投资计划；资本支出计划；（国有企业）投资项目纲要
- **语域**：财务 / 政策 / 新闻 / 企业管理
- **拓展**：
  - 对应俄文："инвестиционная программа"（投资纲要）。
  - 在中国语境下对应"投资计划"或"资本性支出预算（capex budget）"。
  - 与 *budget*（预算）区分：*investment program* 特指资本性长期投入，不包括运营开支。
- **例句**：
  > A sharp contraction in RZhD's **capital expenditure program** means that only the highest-priority segments of the Eastern Polygon expansion will receive funding in 2026.
  > 俄铁资本支出计划的大幅压缩意味着，2026年只有东部多边形扩建中优先级最高的路段才能获得资金支持。

### S — 熟词僻义 / 引申义

---

**⑯ capacity** *(in a ... capacity)*
- **音标**：/kəˈpæsəti/（名词）
- **常见义**：容量；能力
- **僻义/引申义**（LDOCE释义3）：**Someone's job, position, or duty** — used to describe in what official role someone is acting.
- **中文释义（引申义）**：以……身份；作为……（职务/角色）
- **语域**：正式 / 法律 / 书面
- **典型用法**：*in an advisory capacity*（以顾问身份）；*in his/her capacity as chairman*（以主席身份）；*acting in an official capacity*（以官方身份行事）
- **拓展**：此用法在法律文书和正式报告中极为常见，是中文"以……身份/资格"的标准英文表达，切勿混同于"能力/容量"义。
- **例句**：
  > Denis Ilatovsky contributed to the discussion **in his capacity as** a board member of a major port analytics firm, bringing both industry data and sector-level advocacy to the table.
  > 杰尼斯·伊拉托夫斯基以某大型港口分析公司董事会成员的身份参与讨论，为会议带来了行业数据和行业层面的倡议主张。

---

**⑰ window** *(maintenance/technical window)*
- **音标**：/ˈwɪndoʊ/（名词）
- **常见义**：窗户；（计算机）窗口
- **僻义/引申义**：A limited period of time during which something is possible — esp. in rail/aerospace contexts, a scheduled interruption in operations for maintenance.
- **中文释义（引申义）**：（铁路/航天/医学）窗口期；作业间隙；施工天窗
- **语域**：铁路技术 / 航天 / 医学 / 金融（*window of opportunity*）
- **拓展**：
  - *a window of opportunity*：机会窗口（通用惯用语）
  - *launch window*：（火箭/卫星）发射窗口
  - *maintenance window*：维修窗口（IT / 铁路）
  - 文中俄文"технологическое окно"直译即 *technical/maintenance window*，是铁路行业专业术语。
- **例句**：
  > The **window of opportunity** to commission the new substation before winter traffic peaks is narrow, and any delay will force further reliance on diesel traction.
  > 在冬季运量高峰前完成新变电站投运的窗口期极为有限，任何延误都将迫使继续依赖柴油牵引。

**⑱ convention** *(конвенция / loading moratorium)*
- **音标**：/kənˈvenʃən/（名词）
- **常见义**：惯例；公约；大会
- **僻义（铁路/俄语语境）**：In Russian railway industry usage (*конвенция на погрузку*), a **temporary administrative embargo on loading freight** to a designated destination when congestion or capacity limits are exceeded.
- **中文释义（引申义）**：（铁路行业）限装令；临时禁运令；装载公约
- **语域**：俄罗斯铁路行业专业语境
- **拓展**：该"Convention"义项源自俄语行业惯例，英文中须翻译为 *loading moratorium*、*freight embargo* 或 *loading suspension order*，直译"convention"会导致误解。注意与 *treaty/convention*（国际条约/公约）明确区分。
- **例句**：
  > When frozen cargo delayed unloading at Pacific terminals, the carrier **imposed a loading embargo** — colloquially referred to in Russian rail parlance as a *конвенция* — on **coal shipments** bound for port-side stations.
  > 当冻结货物导致太平洋港口卸车迟缓时，承运方对驶往港站的煤炭货物实施了限装令——在俄铁行业术语中通称"konventsiya"（公约）。

**⑲ sacred** *(сакральные цифры / sacred numbers)*
- **音标**：/ˈseɪkrɪd/（形容词）
- **常见义**：神圣的；宗教的
- **僻义/引申义**（讽刺修辞用法）：In journalistic/rhetorical usage, used **ironically** to describe figures or targets that are treated as inviolable or beyond questioning, often by those in power — implying they may be arbitrary or politically motivated.
- **中文释义（引申义）**：（讽刺义）神圣不可侵犯的；被奉若神明的（数字/目标）
- **语域**：新闻写作 / 讽刺修辞 / 政策评论
- **拓展**：此为文章标题精华所在。原文"сакральные цифры"（神圣数字）是文中受访专家用来讽刺"180、210、270万吨"这一串目标数字被当局奉为教条般神圣不可动摇、却又未能落实的现象，是高级新闻写作中常见的讽刺性修辞手法。英文中以 *sacred* 搭配 *numbers/targets* 即可传达这一讽刺色彩。
- **例句**：
  > The **sacred targets** of 180 and 210 million tonnes function less as operational benchmarks than as **political totems** — invoked to justify budget claims, yet rarely subjected to rigorous public accountability.
  > 1.8亿和2.1亿吨这些"神圣目标"与其说是运营基准，不如说是政治图腾——被援引以支撑预算申请，却鲜少接受严格的公开问责。

**⑳ account** *(be on special account / hold sb/sth in high account)*

- **音标**：/əˈkaʊnt/（名词/动词）
- **英文释义**（LDOCE）：
  - (n.) A description or report of an event; a record of money received and spent; (phrase) *on (special) account* — held in particular regard or priority.
  - (v.) *account for* — to form a particular amount or part of something; to give an explanation for something.
- **中文释义**：
  - 叙述；账目；报告；（短语）受到特别重视
  - 动词短语：*account for* = 占（比例）；解释；说明原因
- **语域**：正式 / 书面 / 财务 / 新闻
- **同义词（"解释"义）**：explain, justify, give reasons for
- **常见搭配**：
  - *on no account*（绝对不；在任何情况下都不）
  - *on account of*（因为；由于）
  - *take into account / take account of*（考虑到；把…纳入考量）
  - *hold sb accountable for sth*（要求某人对…负责）→ 名词：*accountability*（不可数）
  - *remain on special account*（仍受到特别重视）——文中俄语原义"на особом счету"
  - *call sb to account*（追究某人责任）
- **拓展**：
  - *account for* 作"占比"时不可用被动：✓ *Eastern Polygon accounts for over half of the investment* ✗ *is accounted for over half*
  - 形容词 *accountable*（负责任的；须承担责任的）：*be accountable to sb for sth*（对某人就某事负责）；*held accountable*（被追责）
  - 名词 *accountability*（不可数）是新闻、政策类写作核心词，如：*lack of accountability*（缺乏问责）；*public accountability*（公众问责）
- **例句（"占比"义）**：
  > BAM and Trans-Siberian upgrades **account for** more than half of RZhD's entire **capital development** expenditure in 2026, underscoring the project's continued political priority.
  > 2026年，贝阿铁路和西伯利亚大铁路的升级改造占俄铁全部资本发展支出的一半以上，凸显出该项目持续受到的政治重视。

- **例句（"问责"义）**：
  > Without a binding mechanism to **hold the operator accountable**, mandated freight targets risk becoming little more than aspirational figures on paper.
  > 若缺乏具有约束力的问责机制，法定货运目标就有可能沦为纸面上的美好愿景。

**㉑ narrow/widen the gap**

- **音标**：/ˈnæroʊ ðə ɡæp/ / /ˈwaɪdən ðə ɡæp/（短语）
- **英文释义**：To reduce / increase the difference between two levels, quantities, or positions.
- **中文释义**：缩小/扩大差距
- **语域**：新闻 / 政策 / 商业 / 通用书面
- **同义词（缩小差距）**：close the gap, bridge the divide, reduce the disparity
- **常见搭配**：
  - *narrow the gap between supply and demand*（缩小供需缺口）
  - *widen the gap between plan and performance*（扩大计划与执行之间的差距）
  - *bridge the gap*（弥合差距，含"过渡/衔接"语义）
  - *close the gap*（消除差距，程度更强，接近"清零"）
- **拓展**：
  - *gap* 在此类语境中强调两个数值/状态之间的落差，是新闻和政策写作中极高频的比喻性表达，远比 *difference* 更有力。
  - *bridge the gap*（v.）含"主动搭建"的意象，语气比 *narrow* 更积极；*close the gap* 表示差距几乎消除。
- **例句**：
  > Analysts warn that **narrowing the gap** between government-mandated targets and actual throughput will require not just more investment, but **structural reforms** in how performance data are reported and verified.
  > 分析人士警告，要缩小政府规定目标与实际吞吐量之间的差距，不仅需要更多投入，更需要在绩效数据的报告和核实方式上进行结构性改革。

**㉒ on paper vs. in practice**

- **音标**：/ɒn ˈpeɪpər/ vs. /ɪn ˈpræktɪs/（短语对）
- **英文释义**（Cambridge）：
  - *on paper*: existing in written form, but not in reality; in theory.
  - *in practice*: what actually happens as opposed to what is supposed to happen.
- **中文释义**：
  - *on paper*：纸面上；从理论上看；书面上
  - *in practice*：实际上；在实践中；事实上
- **语域**：书面 / 新闻 / 法律 / 政策分析
- **同义词（on paper）**：theoretically, nominally, in theory, formally
- **同义词（in practice）**：in reality, in effect, de facto, as it actually works
- **常见搭配**：
  - *on paper, the target looks achievable*（从纸面看，目标似乎可达）
  - *but in practice, results fall far short*（但在实践中，结果远未达标）
  - *paper compliance vs. real compliance*（形式合规 vs. 实质合规）
- **拓展**：这一对表达是新闻写作和法律论证中构建对比论点的利器。在批评计划与现实脱节时，*on paper…but in practice…* 结构句式极具冲击力，也是GRE、GMAT写作和雅思、托福大作文的高分表达。
- **例句**：
  > **On paper**, Phase 2 of the Eastern Polygon was declared complete; **in practice**, construction continued throughout 2025, with multiple facilities still awaiting commissioning.
  > 从纸面上看，东部多边形第二阶段已宣告竣工；但在实践中，整个2025年施工仍在持续，多处设施尚未投入运营。

**㉓ at the limit of one's capacity / at full stretch**

- **音标**：/æt ðə ˈlɪmɪt əv wʌnz kəˈpæsəti/（短语）
- **英文释义**：Operating at or beyond the maximum level of what is possible; unable to handle any more demand.
- **中文释义**：在能力极限上运转；满负荷运行；超负荷工作
- **语域**：新闻 / 工程 / 经济分析 / 书面
- **同义词**：at full capacity, at breaking point, at full stretch, maxed out（非正式）, operating at the limit
- **常见搭配**：
  - *run/operate at full capacity*（满负荷运行）
  - *stretch the infrastructure to its limits*（使基础设施达到极限）
  - *at breaking point*（处于崩溃边缘，语气更强）
- **拓展**：文中俄语原文"работают на пределе возможностей"（在能力极限上运转）是这一地道英语表达的准确对应。在基础设施、制造业和供应链报道中，这一短语是描述产能不足状况的标配表达。
- **例句**：
  > With coal and container freight both surging, the eastern rail network has been **operating at the limit of its capacity** for months, leaving exporters with no reliable alternative routing.
  > 随着煤炭和集装箱货运双双猛增，东部铁路网络已连续数月在能力极限上运转，令出口商无可靠的替代路线可用。

**㉔ reframe the numbers / statistical reframing**

- **音标**：/riːˈfreɪm/（动词）
- **英文释义**（Cambridge）：To consider or present something in a different way; to change the frame of reference for interpreting data or an argument.
- **中文释义**：重新定框（将同一事实以不同叙事方式呈现）；重新包装数据；改变诠释视角
- **语域**：新闻 / 政策分析 / 修辞学 / 认知语言学
- **同义词**：reinterpret, recast, spin（贬义/非正式）, redefine the metrics
- **拓展**：
  - *Reframing* 是新闻批评和政治传播研究中的核心术语（源自认知语言学 *frame theory*，George Lakoff等学者的研究）。
  - 文章中RZhD"将159百万吨诠释为达成180百万吨目标"即为典型的 *statistical reframing*，在英文政策批评中常用 *spin*（政治包装）、*redefine the goalposts*（改变目标定义）、*move the goalposts*（临时改变规则/标准）等更为尖锐的表达。
  - *Move the goalposts*（移动球门柱）= 在游戏进行中更改规则以达到原本无法达成的目标，是高级英语写作中极为地道的批评性表达。
- **例句**：
  > Rather than acknowledging the **shortfall**, the state monopoly chose to **reframe the metrics**, effectively **moving the goalposts** to make underperformance appear as achievement.
  > 国家垄断企业非但没有承认差距，反而选择重新定义指标，实质上是在临时改变规则，将不达标的表现包装成成就。

**㉕ a wish rather than a mandate**

- **音标**：（短语）
- **英文释义**：A desire or suggestion that lacks binding legal force; something expressed as desirable but not enforceable.
- **中文释义**：一厢情愿的期望，而非具有法律约束力的指令；停留于愿景层面的建议
- **语域**：新闻评论 / 政策分析 / 法律
- **同义词**：aspirational rather than enforceable; a recommendation, not a requirement; advisory, not binding
- **拓展**：
  - 原文结尾"это так и осталось пожеланием"（这终究只是一个愿望）是这一表达的俄语来源。
  - 在法律和政策写作中，*wish* 与 *mandate* 的对立精准区分了"软法"（soft law）与"硬法"（hard law）的边界——*mandatory requirement*（强制性要求）vs. *advisory guideline*（建议性指引）。
  - 写作中可延伸为：*the proposal remained aspirational rather than binding*（该提案停留于愿景，而非具有约束力）
- **例句**：
  > The Federation Council's call for **quarterly disclosure** of capacity data remained, in the end, **a wish rather than a mandate** — the monopoly faced no legal obligation to comply.
  > 联邦委员会要求按季度公开运力数据的呼吁，最终不过是一个一厢情愿的愿望，而非具有约束力的指令——垄断企业在法律上并无合规义务。

## 4C. 相关法律问题

**① 中华人民共和国铁路法（2015修正）货物运输 承运人赔偿责任**

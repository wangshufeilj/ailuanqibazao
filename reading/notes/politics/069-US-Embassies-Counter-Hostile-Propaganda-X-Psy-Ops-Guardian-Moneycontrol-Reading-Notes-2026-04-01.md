> **重点词汇解析：**
>
> 1. **counter** [ˈkaʊntə(r)] (v.): To do something in order to prevent something bad from happening or to reduce its bad effects. **打击；反击；对抗**。
> 2. **hostile** [ˈhɒstaɪl] (adj.): Showing strong dislike; unfriendly. **敌对的；不友好的**。
> 3. **propaganda** [ˌprɒpəˈɡændə] (n.): Ideas or statements that may be false or exaggerated and that are used in order to gain support for a political leader, party, etc. **宣传（通常指有偏见的或误导性的）**。
> 4. **tap** [tæp] (v.): To use or take what is available from a particular source. **开发；利用；挖掘**。
> 5. **psy-ops** [ˈsaɪ ɒps] (n. abbreviation): Short for "Psychological Operations"; operations to convey selected information to audiences to influence their emotions and behavior. **心理战**。
>
> **【背景注释】**
>
> - **Elon Musk’s X**：原名 Twitter（推特），在 2022 年被埃隆·马斯克收购并改名为 X。
> - **Embassies（大使馆）**：一个国家派驻在另一个国家首都的官方代表机构。

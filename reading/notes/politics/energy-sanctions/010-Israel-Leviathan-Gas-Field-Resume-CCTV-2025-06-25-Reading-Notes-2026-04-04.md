### 正文对照 | Bilingual Text

🔹 **以色列最大天然气田将恢复运营**  
🔸 **Israel's Largest Natural Gas Field to Resume Operations**

🔹 2025-06-25 | 来源：**央视新闻**  
🔸 June 25, 2025 | Source: **CCTV News**

🔹 以色列能源部当地时间25日宣布，已允许**天然气田恢复运营**。  
🔸 The Israeli Ministry of Energy announced on the 25th, local time, that it has authorized the **resumption of natural gas field operations**.

🔹 据以色列能源公司 NewMed Energy 透露，以色列最大海上天然气田**利维坦气田（Leviathan）**将在未来几个小时内恢复运营。  
🔸 According to the Israeli energy company NewMed Energy, the **Leviathan gas field**, Israel's largest offshore natural gas field, will resume operations within the next few hours.

🔹 此前，以色列能源部在6月13日宣布，出于**安全因素**考量，下令暂时关闭该国最大的天然气田利维坦气田。  
🔸 Previously, on June 13, the Israeli Ministry of Energy ordered the temporary shutdown of the Leviathan field, the country's largest, citing **security considerations**.

🔹 利维坦气田自2019年投入运营以来，一直是以色列及周边国家**天然气供应的重要来源**。  
🔸 Since its commissioning in 2019, the Leviathan gas field has been a **critical source of natural gas supply** for Israel and its neighboring countries.

🔹 它不仅满足了以色列国内的需求，还向**埃及和约旦**等邻国提供天然气。  
🔸 It not only meets Israel's domestic demand but also provides natural gas to neighboring countries such as **Egypt and Jordan**.

### 要点提要 | Key Takeaways

1. **政策节点**：2025-06-13 因安全因素临时关停 → 2025-06-25 能源部批准恢复运营；NewMed Energy 称利维坦气田将在数小时内复产。
2. **地缘与供应**：利维坦为以色列最大海上气田（2019 年投产），对以色列国内及邻国（埃及、约旦等）天然气供应具有重要性。

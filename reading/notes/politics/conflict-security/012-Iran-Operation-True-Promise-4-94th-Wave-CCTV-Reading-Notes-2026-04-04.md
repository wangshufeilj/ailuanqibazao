🔹 **视频丨伊朗发起“真实承诺-4”行动第94波攻势**  
🔸 **Video | Iran Launches the 94th Wave of "Operation True Promise-4"**

🔹 当地时间**4月4日**，**伊朗伊斯兰革命卫队**公共关系部发布消息称，当天上午，在**“真实承诺-4”第94波**打击行动中，一些位于**以色列南部、中部、北部以及特拉维夫地区**的以色列**工业和军事设施**、以军**指挥官和部队所在地**遭到摧毁。  
🔸 On **April 4** local time, the Public Relations Department of the **Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps (IRGC)** announced that during the **94th wave** of **"Operation True Promise-4"** earlier that morning, several Israeli **industrial and military facilities**, as well as locations of **commanders and troops** across **southern, central, and northern Israel and the Tel Aviv area**, were destroyed.

🔹 打击行动使用了**弹道导弹**、**固液双燃料重型导弹**、**多弹头导弹**和**无人机**等。  
🔸 The strikes involved the use of **ballistic missiles**, **solid-liquid dual-fuel heavy missiles**, **multi-warhead missiles**, and **drones**.

🔹 同时，消息还称，**迪莫纳、内盖夫、贝尔谢巴和拉马特甘**等地成为**重点打击目标**。  
🔸 Meanwhile, the report stated that areas including **Dimona, Negev, Beersheba, and Ramat Gan** were designated as **primary targets**.

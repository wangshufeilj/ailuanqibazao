**背景注释**

🔹 Such a position demanded heavy allocation of budget and resources for the police who are the first line of action against any attack.  
🔸 这种地位要求为警察分配大量预算和资源，因为他们是应对任何袭击的第一道防线。

🔸 然而在实践中，该省总体上以及警察部门的预算分配似乎并不足。

🔹 Annual spending on salaries, training, capacity building, operations, infrastructure, gadgets, accessories, weapons, bunkers, digital and communication equipment, etc. is not enough.  
🔸 在薪资、培训、能力建设、行动、基础设施、小器械、附件、武器、掩体、数字和通信设备等方面的年度支出是不够的。

### 重点词汇解析

**Infrastructure** [ˈɪnfrəstrʌktʃər] *n.* 基础设施  
语域：通用。

**Gadget** [ˈɡædʒɪt] *n.* 小装置；小器具  
语域：通用。

The existing rates / of poverty, / hunger, / the dilapidated conditions / of the health sector, / poor infrastructure / are some of the reasons / why police / did not get / the desired budgetary allocation / (Hughes, 2002) / and Suddle (2015) / also highlights / the issue / of budgetary allocation / for police / and documents / that successive governments / have failed / to increase the police budget.  
🔹 The existing rates of poverty, hunger, the dilapidated conditions of the health sector, poor infrastructure are some of the reasons why police did not get the desired budgetary allocation (Hughes, 2002) and Suddle (2015) also highlights the issue of budgetary allocation for police and documents that successive governments have failed to increase the police budget.  
🔸 现有的贫困率、饥饿状况、医疗部门的破败不堪以及基础设施的薄弱，是警察未能获得理想预算分配的部分原因；Suddle (2015) 也强调了警察预算分配问题，并记录了历届政府都未能增加警察预算。

### 重点词汇解析

**Dilapidated** [dɪˈlæpɪdeɪtɪd] *adj.* 破旧的；荒废的  
语域：正式。  
拓展：常修饰建筑物或基础设施。

**Successive** [səkˈsesɪv] *adj.* 连续的；接连的  
语域：通用。  
拓展：successive governments（历届政府）。

Failure / to increase budget / leaves / adverse effects / on the performance / of the police.  
🔹 Failure to increase budget leaves adverse effects on the performance of the police.  
🔸 未能增加预算对警察的履职表现产生了不利影响。

“For certain cases, / we have to travel, / even to other provinces, / for arrests / of certain criminals, / it is / not possible / to do it / without a good budget.”  
🔹 “For certain cases, we have to travel, even to other provinces, for arrests of certain criminals, it is not possible to do it without a good budget.”  
🔸 “对于某些案件，我们不得不前往其他省份抓捕特定罪犯，如果没有充足的预算，这是不可能实现的。”

“Besides / other work-related stress, / the stress / of lack of budget / is a huge obstacle / in the way of / effective policing.”  
🔹 “Besides other work-related stress, the stress of lack of budget is a huge obstacle in the way of effective policing.”  
🔸 “除了其他与工作相关的压力外，预算匮乏带来的压力也是实现有效警务的巨大障碍。”

“We need money / to commute, / we need resources’’.”  
🔹 “We need money to commute, we need resources.”  
🔸 “我们需要钱来通勤，我们需要资源。”

### 重点词汇解析

**Commute** [kəˈmjuːt] *v.* 通勤；（在此语境下指因公出差/往返）  
语域：通用。

Most of the criminals / fled the scene / and go far away / to other areas.  
🔹 Most of the criminals fled the scene and go far away to other areas.  
🔸 大多数罪犯逃离现场并远走他乡。

Previously / they used to / take shelter / in the tribal areas, / or to some other province.  
🔹 Previously they used to take shelter in the tribal areas, or to some other province.  
🔸 以前，他们通常在部落地区或其他省份寻求庇护。

**背景注释**  
Tribal areas：指巴基斯坦以前的联邦直辖部落地区（FATA），曾因法律管辖薄弱而成为犯罪分子的避难所。

It is / due to / financial constraints / that timely arrests / cannot be made / in many cases.  
🔹 It is due to financial constraints that timely arrests cannot be made in many cases.  
🔸 正是由于财务限制，许多案件无法及时实施抓捕。

The (CIO) / further remarked:  
🔹 The (CIO) further remarked:  
🔸 首席调查官进一步评论道：

“Although, / in certain cases, / we got information / from our sources / that the criminal / is living in Lahore / or any other city, / due to lack of finances / or economic resources / police / can't ensure / the arrest / of the criminal / within due time”.  
🔹 “Although, in certain cases, we got information from our sources that the criminal is living in Lahore or any other city, due to lack of finances or economic resources police can't ensure the arrest of the criminal within due time.”  
🔸 “虽然在某些案件中，我们从线人那里得到消息称罪犯住在拉合尔或其他城市，但由于缺乏资金或经济资源，警察无法确保在规定时间内逮捕罪犯。”

Mobility / is one of the main duties / the police / should perform, / but a shortage of logistics / including vehicles / and fuel / hampers the mobility / (Jackson, Asif, Bradford, & Zakria Zakar, 2014) / of the police officials.  
🔹 Mobility is one of the main duties the police should perform, but a shortage of logistics including vehicles and fuel hampers the mobility (Jackson, Asif, Bradford, & Zakria Zakar, 2014) of the police officials.  
🔸 机动性是警察应履行的主要职责之一，但包括车辆和燃料在内的后勤短缺阻碍了警务人员的机动性。

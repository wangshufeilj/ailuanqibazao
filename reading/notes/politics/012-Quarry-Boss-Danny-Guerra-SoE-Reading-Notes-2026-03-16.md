# 模块四：素材与语料库积累

## 4A. 重点词汇解析

---

### W — 写作高频词

---

**1. detain**
- 音标：/dɪˈteɪn/ *v.*
- 英文释义：To keep someone in an official place, such as a police station or prison, and prevent them from leaving.
- 中文释义：拘押，扣押（官方行为）
- 语域：新闻 / 法律 / 正式书面
- 同义词：hold, confine, imprison, incarcerate；反义词：release, free, discharge
- 拓展：名词形式 **detention**（不可数/可数均可）；常见搭配：*detain sb for questioning*（因讯问扣押某人）、*detain sb without charge*（未起诉拘押）；与 **arrest** 区别：arrest 强调依法正式逮捕，detain 强调限制人身自由的状态，范围更广。
- 例句：The authorities **detained** three suspects for 48 hours pending further investigation.
  *当局以待进一步调查为由将三名嫌疑人拘押了48小时。*

---

**2. preventive detention** *(也作 preventative detention)*
- 音标：/prɪˈventɪv dɪˈtenʃən/ *n. phr.*
- 英文释义：The imprisonment of a person without trial in order to prevent them from committing a crime or posing a threat to public safety.
- 中文释义：预防性拘留（无需正式起诉即可执行的拘押手段）
- 语域：法律 / 新闻 / 正式
- 同义词：administrative detention, pre-trial detention；反义词：unconditional release
- 拓展：常出现于宪法法律、安全法及紧急状态法律语境中；与 *remand in custody*（候审羁押）区分——后者需经司法程序。
- 例句：Critics argued that the use of **preventive detention** orders during the emergency circumvented normal due process protections.
  *批评者认为，紧急状态期间使用预防性拘留令绕开了正常的正当程序保障。*

---

**3. allege**
- 音标：/əˈledʒ/ *v.*
- 英文释义：To state that someone has done something wrong or illegal, without giving proof.
- 中文释义：指控，声称（尤指尚未经证实的）
- 语域：新闻 / 法律 / 正式
- 同义词：claim, assert, charge, contend；反义词：prove, demonstrate
- 拓展：**alleged**（adj.）表"据称的"，新闻报道中大量使用以规避法律风险；**allegation**（n.）指控，指称；常见搭配：*allege that...*, *face allegations of...*
- 例句：The opposition party **alleged** that the detention was politically motivated rather than grounded in genuine security concerns.
  *反对党声称，此次拘押是出于政治目的，而非真正的安全顾虑。*

---

**4. bail**
- 音标：/beɪl/ *n./v.*
- 英文释义：*(n.)* Money left with a court to allow someone accused of a crime to be freed until their trial; *(v.)* to release or obtain the release of a prisoner by providing bail.
- 中文释义：保释金；保释
- 语域：法律 / 新闻
- 同义词：bond（美）；常见搭配：*grant/deny bail*, *out on bail*, *post bail*, *bail conditions*
- 拓展：动词 *bail out* 另有"帮助脱困"的引申义；**bailee**（法律）受托保管人；注意区分 *bail*（保释）与 *parole*（假释，针对已服刑者）。
- 例句：The judge granted him **bail** of $100,000 on condition that he surrender his passport and report to police weekly.
  *法官以10万美元保释金批准保释，条件是上缴护照并每周向警方报到。*

---

**5. custody**
- 音标：/ˈkʌstədi/ *n.*（不可数）
- 英文释义：The state of being kept in prison or under guard, especially while awaiting trial; also, the legal right to care for a child.
- 中文释义：（警方）羁押；（孩子的）监护权
- 语域：法律 / 新闻 / 正式
- 同义词：detention, confinement；常见搭配：*remain in custody*, *take into custody*, *police custody*, *remand in custody*
- 拓展：*in custody* = 被羁押中；*take sb into custody* = 将某人逮捕羁押；本文两义均无需混淆，重点掌握警务义。
- 例句：Following the verdict, the defendant was immediately taken **into custody** to begin serving his sentence.
  *判决宣布后，被告立即被羁押，开始服刑。*

---

**6. allegation**
- 音标：/ˌæləˈɡeɪʃən/ *n.*（可数）
- 英文释义：A statement that someone has done something wrong or illegal, especially one that has not been proved.
- 中文释义：指控，指称，说法（尤指未经证实的）
- 语域：新闻 / 法律 / 正式
- 同义词：accusation, charge, claim；常见搭配：*face allegations of*, *make/level allegations against*, *allegations of misconduct/corruption*
- 拓展：复数 *allegations* 在新闻报道中极为常见；**unsubstantiated allegation**（无根据指控）是重要搭配。
- 例句：The minister denied all **allegations** of financial impropriety, calling them a smear campaign.
  *部长否认了所有关于财务不当行为的指控，称之为污名化攻势。*

---

**7. national security**
- 音标：/ˈnæʃənl sɪˈkjʊərɪti/ *n. phr.*（不可数）
- 英文释义：The protection of a country and its citizens against threats from internal or external enemies or forces.
- 中文释义：国家安全
- 语域：政治 / 法律 / 新闻 / 正式
- 常见搭配：*national security concerns/threats/interests*, *on grounds of national security*, *national security legislation*

### T — 翻译重要词

---

**25. state of emergency**
- 翻译难点：中文对应可为"紧急状态""非常状态""战时状态"，须根据语境选择。中国大陆法律体系中对应"紧急状态"，但其制度内涵与普通法国家的 SoE 有别，翻译时不宜简单对等。
- 惯用译法：**紧急状态**（正式法律文本）；**非常状态**（学术讨论）

---

**26. preventive / preventative**
- 音标：/prɪˈventɪv/, /prɪˈventətɪv/ *adj./n.*
- 英文释义：Designed to prevent something from happening; especially used in medical or legal contexts.
- 中文释义：预防性的；预防措施
- 翻译难点：法律语境中 *preventive detention* 应译为"预防性拘留"，不宜译为"预防拘留"；医学语境中 *preventive medicine* 译为"预防医学"。
- 例句：The new legislation introduced a series of **preventive** measures targeting money laundering in the real estate sector.
  *新立法引入了一系列针对房地产领域洗钱行为的预防性措施。*

---

**27. processing plant**
- 音标：/ˈprɒsesɪŋ plɑːnt/ *n. phr.*（可数）
- 英文释义：A large industrial facility where raw materials are treated or transformed for commercial use.
- 中文释义：加工厂，加工设备
- 翻译难点：工业语境下可译"加工装置"或"处理厂"；采矿报道中常见；注意 *plant* 既可指"工厂"也可指"设备/机器"，需根据语境判断。

**40. most likely**
- 常见义：最有可能

- 例句：The analysts agreed that the merger would **most likely** face regulatory scrutiny before receiving final approval.
  *分析人士一致认为，此次并购在获得最终批准前极有可能面临监管审查。*

**41. grounds**

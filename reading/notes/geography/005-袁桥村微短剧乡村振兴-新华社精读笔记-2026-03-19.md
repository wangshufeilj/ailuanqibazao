### W — 写作高频词

---

**1. surge** /sɜːrdʒ/ *n. / v.*

- **英文释义**：(n.) a sudden large increase in something; (v.) to increase suddenly and greatly
- **中文释义**：（名）骤增，浪涌；（动）急剧上涨，涌现
- **语域**：新闻、经济、学术写作
- **同义词**：spike, boom, upsurge, escalation｜**反义词**：decline, slump, plummet
- **常见词组**：a surge in demand / a surge of excitement / surge forward
- **拓展**：
  - 名词可数；作动词时规则变化
  - 朗文：常与"in/of"搭配，如 *a surge in prices / a surge of anger*
  - 与 spike 区别：spike 更强调短暂峰值后回落；surge 可持续

**写作用途**：以具体细节佐证论点，展示某事物的实用价值，叙事生动，逻辑自然。

**金句三**
> "An has since developed into a confident and eloquent storyteller, regaling tourists with details of the history of the ancient buildings."

> 1. **Heritage** [ˈherɪtɪdʒ] `n. [U]`（不可数名词）  
>    - **释义**：property that is or may be inherited; an inheritance; valued objects and qualities such as cultural traditions.（文化遗产；传统）  
>    - **拓展**：cultural heritage（文化遗产），world heritage site（世界遗产地）。  
>    - **考点**：常用于雅思/考研写作关于“传统文化保护”话题。  
> 2. **Power** [ˈpaʊə(r)] `v. [T]`（及物动词）  
>    - **释义**：to supply a device with mechanical or electrical energy; to move or drive something.（为……提供动力；推动）  
>    - **熟词僻义**：此处不是名词“权力”，而是动词“驱动/助力”。  
>    - **搭配**：power the economic growth（驱动经济增长）。  
> 3. **Boom** [buːm] `n. [C]`（可数名词）  
>    - **释义**：a period of great prosperity or rapid economic growth.（繁荣；激增）  
>    - **考点**：tourism boom（旅游热）。同义词：surge, explosion。反义词：slump, recession。

**概要（中文）**

本文聚焦中国工业旅游的兴起，以北京首钢工业园为核心案例，展现中国工业遗址如何从钢铁重镇蜕变为文旅融合的新兴目的地。文章指出，随着国家产业转型与城市更新的推进，废弃厂区与高炉正以文化展览、创意园区、极限运动场馆等形式焕发新生，并与奥运遗产深度结合，形成独特的文化记忆空间。Trip.com平台2025年国庆旅游数据进一步印证了工业旅游的热度——航天与汽车工厂参观已占旅行搜索量的20%以上。这一现象折射出中国消费者旅游偏好的结构性转变：从传统观光走向兼具教育意义与国家认同感的深度体验式旅游。

**Summary (English)**

This report highlights the rise of industrial tourism in China, centering on Beijing's Shougang Industrial Park as a flagship example of how former heavy-industry sites are being reimagined as cultural and technological destinations. Once synonymous with smoke-belching blast furnaces, these relics of China's industrial past are now attracting visitors through art exhibitions, creative studios, and even Olympic venues. Backed by Trip.com's 2025 National Day data showing aerospace and automobile factory tours claiming over 20% of travel searches, the piece illustrates a structural shift in Chinese domestic tourism — from passive sightseeing toward immersive, education-driven experiences that also nurture a sense of national identity.

# 模块四：素材与语料库积累

## 4A. 重点词汇解析

---

### W — 写作高频词

---

**1. bustling** /ˈbʌs.lɪŋ/ *adj.*

- **英文释义**：Full of busy activity; lively and energetic (Longman)
- **中文释义**：熙熙攘攘的，热闹忙碌的
- **语域**：书面语 / 新闻 / 描述性写作
- **同义词**：lively, thriving, vibrant, teeming
- **反义词**：deserted, quiet, desolate
- **拓展**：常修饰 city, street, market, port, hub；来自动词 bustle（忙碌奔走）；名词形式 bustle
- **例句**：The once-**bustling** port city had fallen silent after the collapse of its fishing industry.
  > 这座昔日热闹非凡的港口城市，在渔业崩溃后陷入了沉寂。

---

**2. reinvent** /ˌriː.ɪnˈvent/ *v.*

- **英文释义**：To change something so much that it appears to be entirely new; or to reinvent oneself/itself — to change one's image or role significantly (Cambridge)
- **中文释义**：重新打造，彻底革新（尤指形象或定位）
- **语域**：新闻 / 商业 / 书面语
- **同义词**：transform, reimagine, rebrand, overhaul
- **常见词组**：reinvent itself / reinvent oneself / reinvent the wheel（重复他人已做过的工作，含贬义）
- **拓展**：名词 reinvention；注意 "reinvent the wheel" 是固定习语，意为"做无用功"，与本文语境不同。
- **例句**：After years of declining sales, the automobile brand successfully **reinvented** itself as a leader in electric mobility.
  > 经历多年销量下滑后，该汽车品牌成功将自身重塑为电动出行领域的领军者。

---

**3. transform** /trænsˈfɔːrm/ *v.*

- **英文释义**：To change something completely, especially improving it (Longman)
- **中文释义**：使彻底改变，使转变（尤指改善）
- **语域**：正式 / 学术 / 新闻
- **同义词**：convert, metamorphose, overhaul, revamp
- **反义词**：preserve, maintain, stagnate
- **拓展**：名词 transformation；形容词 transformative（具有变革性的，学术写作高频词）；常见搭配：transform A into B；transform the landscape of...
- **例句**：Digital technology has **transformed** the way researchers access and share academic data.
  > 数字技术彻底改变了研究人员获取和共享学术数据的方式。

---

**4. surge** /sɜːrdʒ/ *n. / v.*

- **英文释义**：A sudden large increase in something (n.); to increase suddenly and greatly (v.) (Cambridge)
- **中文释义**：激增，涌现（名词/动词）
- **语域**：新闻 / 经济 / 学术
- **同义词**：spike, upsurge, boom, leap (n.); soar, skyrocket (v.)
- **反义词**：plunge, slump, decline
- **常见搭配**：a surge in demand/prices/interest；surge in popularity；surge ahead
- **拓展**：可数名词；surge 强调突然性，与 growth（持续增长）有别。
- **例句**：The data showed a dramatic **surge** in online retail transactions during the national holiday period.
  > 数据显示，在法定假日期间，网络零售交易量出现了大幅激增。

---

**5. redefine** /ˌriː.dɪˈfaɪn/ *v.*

- **英文释义**：To change the nature or limits of something; to make people think about something in a new way (Longman)
- **中文释义**：重新定义，赋予新的内涵
- **语域**：正式 / 学术 / 新闻
- **同义词**：reconceptualize, reshape, reimagine
- **拓展**：名词 redefinition；在新闻写作中常用于描述行业颠覆或范式转移，具有强烈的论点推进功能。
- **例句**：The rise of remote work has fundamentally **redefined** the relationship between employees and physical office space.
  > 远程办公的兴起从根本上重新定义了员工与实体办公场所之间的关系。

---

**6. vibrant** /ˈvaɪ.brənt/ *adj.*

- **英文释义**：Full of activity and energy in a way that is exciting and attractive (Cambridge)
- **中文释义**：充满活力的，生机勃勃的
- **语域**：书面语 / 新闻 / 旅游文案
- **同义词**：dynamic, lively, thriving, energetic
- **反义词**：stagnant, lifeless, dull
- **常见搭配**：vibrant community / vibrant culture / vibrant economy / vibrant hub
- **例句**：Once neglected, the waterfront district has become a **vibrant** arts quarter attracting young entrepreneurs.
  > 这片曾被忽视的滨水区，如今已成为吸引年轻创业者的充满活力的艺术街区。

---

**7. heritage** /ˈher.ɪ.tɪdʒ/ *n.*

- **英文释义**：The traditions, achievements, and qualities of a country that have existed for a long time and have great historical importance (Longman)
- **中文释义**：遗产，文化传承（尤指有历史价值的）
- **语域**：正式 / 新闻 / 学术 / 文化政策
- **不可数名词**（一般情况），但在具体语境中可作可数名词：heritages
- **常见搭配**：cultural heritage / industrial heritage / heritage site / heritage tourism / world heritage
- **同义词**：legacy, tradition, inheritance
- **拓展**：注意 heritage 与 inheritance（法律上的财产继承）在用法上的区分：heritage 侧重文化/历史维度。
- **例句**：The city has launched an initiative to document its **industrial heritage** before the last remaining factories are demolished.
  > 该市已发起一项举措，在最后几座工厂被拆除之前对其工业遗产进行记录存档。

---

**8. innovation** /ˌɪn.əˈveɪ.ʃən/ *n.*

- **英文释义**：A new idea, method, or invention; the introduction of new things or methods (Cambridge)
- **中文释义**：创新，革新
- **语域**：正式 / 学术 / 商业 / 政策文件
- **可数/不可数**：两者皆可；an innovation（一项创新）/ innovation as a process（作为过程的创新）
- **同义词**：invention, advancement, breakthrough, novelty
- **常见搭配**：hub of innovation / drive innovation / foster innovation / technological innovation
- **拓展**：形容词 innovative；副词 innovatively；名词 innovator
- **例句**：The government's science park was established to foster **innovation** in biotechnology and advanced manufacturing.
  > 政府建立该科技园旨在促进生物技术和先进制造领域的创新。

---

**9. breathe new life into** *phrase*

- **英文释义**：To make something more active, successful, or interesting again after a period of decline (Longman)
- **中文释义**：为……注入新的活力，使重焕生机
- **语域**：新闻 / 书面语 / 演讲
- **同义词**：revitalize, rejuvenate, reinvigorate, give a new lease on life
- **拓展**：固定短语，属于新闻写作中常见的生命力隐喻（vitality metaphor）
- **例句**：The injection of private capital has **breathed new life into** the region's once-moribund textile sector.
  > 私人资本的注入为该地区一度死气沉沉的纺织业注入了新的活力。

---

**10. blend** /blɛnd/ *v. / n.*

- **英文释义**：To combine things in a way that produces an effective or harmonious result (Cambridge)
- **中文释义**：融合，混合（强调有机结合）
- **语域**：中性 / 新闻 / 写作描述
- **同义词**：merge, integrate, fuse, combine
- **常见搭配**：blend education with entertainment / a blend of tradition and modernity / blend art and culture
- **拓展**：名词 blend（混合体）；注意与 mix 的区别：blend 更强调浑然一体，mix 更中性。
- **例句**：The exhibition **blended** archival photographs with interactive digital installations to create an immersive historical experience.
  > 该展览将历史档案照片与互动数字装置融为一体，打造了一种沉浸式的历史体验。

---

**11. striking** /ˈstraɪ.kɪŋ/ *adj.*

- **英文释义**：Unusual or interesting enough to attract attention; very noticeable (Longman)
- **中文释义**：引人注目的，显著的，令人印象深刻的
- **语域**：书面语 / 新闻 / 学术
- **同义词**：remarkable, notable, prominent, conspicuous, arresting
- **反义词**：unremarkable, inconspicuous, ordinary
- **拓展**：来自动词 strike（打击，给人留下印象）；副词 strikingly；区分 striking（外观上的显著）与 significant（重要性上的显著）
- **例句**：The contrast between the rusted iron framework and the modern glass facade created a **striking** visual dialogue.
  > 锈迹斑斑的铁架与现代玻璃幕墙之间的对比，形成了引人注目的视觉对话。

---

**12. account for** *phrasal v.*

- **英文释义**：To form a particular amount or part of something; to constitute (Cambridge)
- **中文释义**：占（某比例/份额）；对……作出解释
- **语域**：正式 / 学术 / 新闻 / 统计写作
- **同义词**：constitute, comprise, make up, represent
- **拓展**：此为熟词僻义，切勿与"负责/解释"混淆。在数据分析、经济报道中极为高频。
- **例句**：Renewable energy sources now **account for** nearly 40% of the country's total electricity generation.
  > 可再生能源现在占该国总发电量的近40%。

**13. lease on life / new lease on life** *phrase*

- **英文释义**：An opportunity to live or continue longer, or to become more healthy, active, or energetic than before (Longman)
- **中文释义**：重获生机；（获得）新的活力与前景
- **语域**：新闻 / 书面语（相当普遍的惯用语）
- **同义词**：revival, rejuvenation, second wind
- **拓展**：lease 本义为"租约/租期"，引申为"获得一段新生命的机会"；美式英语中也说 "a new lease on life"，英式英语说 "a new lease of life"。
- **例句**：The historic cinema was given a **new lease on life** after a local arts foundation undertook its restoration.

**26. national pride** *n. phrase*

**27. showcase** /ˈʃoʊ.keɪs/ *v. / n.*

- **英文释义**：(v.) To show the best qualities or features of something; to present something in an advantageous way. (n.) An event or occasion that presents something favorably (Cambridge)
- **中文释义**：（动）展示，展现（最佳面貌）；（名）展示窗口，展览平台
- **语域**：新闻 / 商业 / 正式 / 营销
- **同义词**：display, highlight, feature, exhibit, spotlight
- **拓展**：showcase 作动词时含有"以最佳方式呈现"的含义，比 show 更具积极色彩；名词为可数名词。
- **翻译要点**：翻译时避免简单译为"展示"，可根据语境译为"展示窗口""彰显""精心呈现"。

**28. repurpose** /ˌriːˈpɜːr.pəs/ *v.*

- **英文释义**：To use something for a different purpose than it was originally intended for (Longman)

- **语域**：建筑 / 可持续发展 / 新闻 / 正式
- **同义词**：convert, adapt, recycle, reallocate
- **拓展**：名词形式 repurposing；在可持续发展领域极为高频；注意与 renovate（翻新）的区别：repurpose 强调功能转变，renovate 强调外观修缮。
- **翻译要点**：可译为"转型利用""改作他用""功能再生"；在工业遗址语境下译为"功能置换"（建筑学专业术语）更为精准。
- **例句**：The disused railway terminus was **repurposed** as a contemporary art gallery without altering its original steel and glass structure.

**30. formerly** /ˈfɔːr.mər.li/ *adv.*

- **英文释义**：In the past; before the present time (Longman)

- **语域**：正式 / 书面 / 新闻
- **同义词**：previously, once, in the past, at one time
- **拓展**：注意 formerly（以前）与 formally（正式地）的拼写混淆，是高频考点；former（形容词）指"前者"或"前任的"。

**32. house** /haʊz/ *v.*（熟词僻义）

**33. stage** /steɪdʒ/ *v. / n.*（熟词僻义）

**47. reborn as** *phrase*

**48. towering** /ˈtaʊər.ɪŋ/ *adj.*（地道描述词）

**50. blending education, innovation, and national pride** *phrase*

- **英文释义**：Combining multiple values or purposes into a unified experience
- **中文释义**：将教育、创新与民族自豪感融为一体
- **语域**：新闻 / 演讲 / 正式写作
- **用法**：这类"blending X, Y, and Z"句式是英语新闻写作中常见的三元并列结构（tricolon），节奏感强，适合写作结尾句或论点总结句。
- **例句**：The exhibition was praised for **blending** historical documentation, scientific inquiry, and civic storytelling into a coherent and engaging narrative.
  > 这场展览因将历史文献记录、科学探究与公民叙事融合为一个连贯而引人入胜的叙事而广受好誉。

---

**51. account for over 20 per cent** *phrase*

- **英文释义**：To constitute or represent more than a fifth of the total (数据新闻常见表达)
- **中文释义**：占20%以上
- **语域**：新闻 / 数据分析 / 商业报告
- **用法**：account for + 百分比/数量 是学术写作与新闻报道中最地道的"占比"表达，远比 "take up" 更正式精准。
- **例句**：Small and medium-sized enterprises **account for over** 60 per cent of the country's total employment.
  > 中小企业占该国就业总量的60%以上。

---

**52. redefining travel** *phrase*

- **英文释义**：Changing the fundamental nature or understanding of what travel means
- **中文释义**：重新定义旅行的本质与内涵
- **语域**：新闻 / 商业 / 品牌传播
- **用法**：redefine + 行业/概念 是科技、商业报道中极具穿透力的写作模式，暗示颠覆性转变；适合用于议论文结论句。
- **例句**：Low-cost carriers fundamentally **redefined** air travel by making it accessible to millions who had never flown before.
  > 廉价航空公司从根本上重新定义了航空旅行，使数百万从未乘过飞机的人得以翱翔云端。

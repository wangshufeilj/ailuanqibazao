### 🗺️ 注释与拓展 | Annotations & Context

1. **王海大队 (Wang Hai Brigade)**:
   - 该部队正式名称为**空军航空兵某旅飞行一大队**。在抗美援朝战争中，由王海率领的一大队与美国空军激战，击落击伤29架敌机。2020年，该大队被授予“时代楷模”称号。
   - **Note**: "Brigade" corresponds to **旅**, while "Group" or "Squadron" corresponds to **大队** depending on the military structure translation.
2. **歼-20 (Chengdu J-20)**:
   - 中国自主研制的第五代隐身战斗机，代号“威龙”(Mighty Dragon)。
3. **金头盔 (Golden Helmet) & 金飞镖 (Golden Dart)**:
   - **金头盔**: PLAAF's highest honor for individual air-to-air combat skills (空军对抗空战考核最高奖).
   - **金飞镖**: PLAAF's highest honor for precision strike and ground attack (空军突防突击考核最高奖).
4. **东海防空识别区 (East China Sea ADIZ)**:
   - An Air Defense Identification Zone established by China in 2013 to identify, monitor, and control aircraft for national security.
5. **中央军委 (Central Military Commission - CMC)**:
   - The highest national defense organization in the People's Republic of China, which commands the entire People's Liberation Army (PLA).

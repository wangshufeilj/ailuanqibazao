# Preparing for Victory（节选精读）

英文篇名：*Preparing for Victory. The U.S. Office of War Information Overseas Branch’s illustrated magazines in the Netherlands and the foundations for the American Century, 1944-1945*  

## 一、预处理信息

- **来源网站**：OpenEdition / European Journal of American Studies（欧洲美国研究杂志）
- **文章标题**：同上
- **作者背景**：**Marja Roholl**，荷兰鹿特丹伊拉斯姆斯大学（Erasmus University Rotterdam）历史系副教授（已退休）。主要研究领域为美国文化外交、美荷关系以及二战期间及战后的跨国文化传播。
- **发表时间**：2012 年 3 月 29 日（在线发布）

---

## 二、前情提要（结构导航）

### 引言（Introduction）

- 二战期间美国政府的双重目标：赢得战争 + 建立以美国为首的世界新秩序。
- 罗斯福政府的宣传策略：通过展示「美国生活方式」来增强可信度。
- OWI（战争信息局）的使命：消除误解，为战后美国的影响力铺平道路。

### 文献综述与文章主旨（Literature Gap & Thesis）

- 反思现有研究：大多数研究关注战后，本文强调战末宣传的延续性。
- 核心论点：OWI 在荷兰发行的画报（KIJK、Victory 等）是「美国世纪」构想的先行实践。

### 第一部分：战争信息局（1. The Office of War Information）

- 机构成立：1942 年 6 月成立，旨在整合国内外的宣传与信息活动。
- 国内分支（Domestic Branch）：面临共和党的政治猜疑，被视为「新政」宣传工具，最终预算被大幅削减。
- 海外分支（Overseas Branch）：
  - 领导层构成：罗伯特·舍伍德等亲罗斯福精英。
  - 任务：维护盟友关系，反击敌方宣传，建立美国作为世界领导者的长期形象。
  - 内部博弈：埃尔默·戴维斯的专业性与舍伍德/巴雷特之间的风格差异（画报 vs. 纯文字）。
- 伦敦办事处（London Outpost）：
  - 地理与政治核心：服务于欧洲流亡政府，作为美军解放欧洲的「耳目」。
  - 人员构成：由代表美国各州的复合型人才组成，旨在传达整体美国价值观。

## 三、逐句精读

**🔹Winning the war / against the Axis countries / was not the only goal / the American government had in mind / during the Second World War.**  
**🔸赢得针对轴心国的战争并非美国政府在二战期间唯一的初衷。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Axis countries**（名词短语）**轴心国**。指二战期间以德国、日本、意大利为首的军事同盟。  
> **have in mind**（动词短语）**心中打算；考虑到**。语域：通用。常见搭配：keep/bear in mind（记住）；What do you have in mind?（你有什么主意？）。  
> 解析：此句揭示了文章的宏大背景，即战争不仅是军事博弈，更是政治蓝图的构建。

**🔹It also worked towards / creating a new world order / in which the United States, / as the new world leader, / would play a major role.**  
**🔸它还致力于建立一种新的世界秩序，在这种秩序中，美国将作为新的世界领导者发挥重要作用。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **work towards**（动词短语）**致力于；为……而努力**。语域：正式/学术。towards 之后接动名词或名词，表示奋斗的目标。近义词：strive for, endeavor to。  
> **world order**（名词短语）**世界秩序**。语域：政治/外交。  
> **play a major role**（动词短语）**扮演重要角色；发挥主要作用**。role 也可用 part 替换。

**🔹To make its performance more credible, / the Roosevelt administration felt it necessary / to inform the rest of the world / of America’s ideas and ideals, / and to explain the successes / of the American way of life.**  
**🔸为了使其表现更具可信度，罗斯福政府认为有必要向世界其他地区传达美国的思想和理想，并阐释美国生活方式的成功。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **credible**（adj.）**可信的；可靠的**。派生词：credibility（可信度）, incredibly（难以置信地）。常见搭配：credible evidence（可靠证据）, credible threat（可信的威胁）。  
> **inform... of...**（动词短语）**通知/告知某人某事**。语法：inform sb. of/about sth.，不能直接加双宾语。  
> 背景：**Roosevelt administration**（罗斯福政府）指富兰克林·德拉诺·罗斯福任内的美国政府，其「新政」（New Deal）深刻影响了 OWI 的意识形态。

**🔹Although the Americans were confident / that the role they played in the Second World War / was highly appreciated, / they were wary of the effects / of German and Japanese propaganda, / and aware of some general ‘misunderstandings’ / regarding the nation / dating back to the pre-war era.**  
**🔸尽管美国人确信他们在第二次世界大战中所扮演的角色受到了高度赞赏，但他们仍对德日宣传的影响保持警惕，并意识到一些关于该国的普遍「误解」可以追溯到战前时代。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **wary of**（形容词短语）**对……警惕的；提防的**。语域：正式/书面。近义词：cautious about, suspicious of, leery of。  
> **propaganda**（n. [U]）**宣传**。语域：政治/社会；常带贬义。  
> **date back to**（动词短语）**追溯到**。  
> **pre-war era**（名词短语）**战前时代**。构词：pre-（在……之前）。

**🔹Therefore / the Overseas Branch of the US Office of War Information (OWI) / was instructed to develop propaganda programs / for their allies, / for instance England and Australia, / as well as for countries to be liberated by the Americans, / such as France and the Netherlands.**  
**🔸因此，美国战争信息局（OWI）海外分支机构奉命为盟国（例如英国和澳大利亚）以及将由美国解放的国家（如法国和荷兰）制定宣传计划。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **be instructed to**（动词短语）**奉命……；被指示做某事**。语域：行政/官方。  
> **ally**（n. [C]）**盟友；盟国**。复数 allies。  
> **liberate**（v.）**解放；释放**。派生词：liberation。

**🔹By telling the story of ‘how America lives,’ / the OWI Overseas Branch aimed / to persuade people in these nations / of “both the attractiveness of American principles / and the weight of American power, / [so that they] will, accordingly, / be prepared for a strong American influence / on the future peace.”1**  
**🔸通过讲述「美国人的生活方式」，OWI 海外分支旨在使这些国家的人民相信「美国准则的吸引力和美国力量的分量，[从而使他们]为美国在未来和平中的强大影响力做好准备」。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **persuade... of...**（动词短语）**使某人信服……**。  
> **principle**（n. [C]）**原则；准则；信条**。易混：principal（主要的；校长）。  
> **weight**（n. [U]）**分量；重要性；影响力**（此处为比喻）。  
> **accordingly**（adv.）**因此；相应地**。语域：正式。

**🔹Most studies of US post-war cultural diplomacy / (mine included) / start their story after WWII, / with the official beginning of peacetime programs.**  
**🔸大多数关于美国战后文化外交的研究（包括我自己的在内）都从二战后开始，即从和平时期计划的正式启动讲起。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **cultural diplomacy**（名词短语）**文化外交**。  
> **peacetime**（n. [U]）**和平时期**。反义词：wartime。  
> **included**（adj.）**包括在内的**；常置于名词/代词之后，如 "all of us included"。

**🔹This essay, however, / shows that especially during the last phase of the war, / the content and approach of the activities / undertaken by the OWI Overseas Branch / anticipated the programs / produced / by the State Department and the United States Information Agency (USIA), / this despite very different political and international circumstances.**  
**🔸然而，本文指出，特别是在战争的最后阶段，OWI 海外分支所开展活动的内容和方式，已经预示了随后由国务院和美国新闻署（USIA）制定的计划，尽管当时的政治和国际环境大不相同。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **undertake**（v.）**承担；从事；进行**。不规则：undertook, undertaken。常见搭配：undertake a task/project/study。  
> **anticipate**（v.）**预见；预示；先于……而做**。  
> **despite**（prep.）**尽管；虽然**；后接名词或代词。  
> 背景：**State Department**（美国国务院）；**United States Information Agency (USIA)**（美国新闻署）成立于 1953 年，冷战时期美国主要的海外宣传机构。

**🔹Incorporating this story more fully / into the history of cultural diplomacy / may help to illustrate / that there is more continuity / than previously assumed, / whether through asserting America’s leadership / of the post-war world, / explaining the essence of the American way of life, / promoting democracy, / exporting the ideology of economic growth, / or simply through promising abundance / in people’s personal lives, / especially in and around the kitchen.**  
**🔸将这段往事更完整地纳入文化外交史，可能有助于说明这种连续性比此前假设的要强，无论这种连续性是体现在维护美国在战后世界的领导地位、解释美国生活方式的本质、推广民主、输出经济增长的意识形态，还是仅仅体现在承诺人们个人生活的丰足（尤其是围绕厨房的生活）。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **incorporate... into...**（动词短语）**将……并入；将……纳入**。  
> **continuity**（n. [U]）**连续性；连贯性**。  
> **assert**（v.）**断言；主张；维护**。常见搭配：assert leadership。  
> **ideology**（n.）**意识形态**。  
> **abundance**（n. [U]）**丰富；充裕；丰足**。文中 kitchen 象征美国消费主义与物质生活的叙事。

**🔹I’ll make the case / for this continuity / by discussing the OWI’s program for the Netherlands, / focusing on the illustrated magazines it produced - / KIJK, (LOOK), De Amerikaansche Vrouw (American Women) and Victory - / which would play an important role / in informing the general Dutch public about America.2**  
**🔸我将通过讨论 OWI 为荷兰制定的计划来论证这种连续性，重点关注它制作的画报——《看》(KIJK)、《美国女性》(De Amerikaansche Vrouw) 和《胜利》(Victory)——这些杂志在向荷兰普通大众介绍美国方面发挥了重要作用。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **make the case for**（动词短语）**提出支持……的论据；论证**。  
> **illustrated magazine**（名词短语）**画报；插图杂志**。

**🔹Although these publications / were to a certain extent tailored to this specific audience, / they also reveal the messages / the US government tried to get across / in post-war Europe more generally.**  
**🔸尽管这些出版物在一定程度上是针对这一特定受众定制的，但它们也揭示了美国政府试图在更广泛的战后欧洲传递的信息。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **to a certain extent**（介词短语）**在一定程度上**。近义词：to some degree。  
> **get across**（动词短语）**解释清楚；传达（信息）**。  
> **tailored to** **量身定制的；适应……的**。近义词：customized, adapted。

**🔹2 In the Dutch context, / the only official goal / of the Office of War Information / was to inform the people of the Netherlands / about “the world news in word and image” / until such time as the Dutch media / could perform the task themselves.3**  
**🔸2 在荷兰的背景下，战争信息局唯一的官方目标是在荷兰媒体能够独立承担任务之前，通过「文字和图像」向荷兰人民通报世界新闻。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **context**（n.）**背景；语境**。  
> **perform the task**（动词短语）**执行任务；完成工作**。  
> **in word and image**（介词短语）**以文字和图像的形式**。

**🔹But the content of these magazines / suggests that the Americans / had different functions in mind as well.**  
**🔸但这些杂志的内容表明，美国人心中还有其他的职能考量。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **suggest**（v.）**暗示；表明**（学术写作中常表「据证据推论」）。  
> **function**（n. [C]）**功能；职能；作用**。

**🔹Positioned at the transition / between war and peace, / these publications raise interesting questions / regarding policy and politics, / production and distribution, / audiences and the preferred self-image of America.**  
**🔸这些出版物位于战争向和平转型的交接点，就政策与政治、生产与发行、受众以及美国所偏好的自我形象等方面提出了引人深思的问题。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **transition**（n.）**过渡；转变；转型**。常见搭配：transition period。  
> **regarding**（prep.）**关于；至于**。近义词：concerning, as to。  
> **distribution**（n. [U]）**分发；发行；分配**。  
> **preferred self-image**（名词短语）**偏好的自我形象**。

**🔹3 In the following pages, / I will locate the illustrated magazines / KIJK, De Amerikaansche Vrouw and Victory / within the broader context and goals of the OWI, / and the circumstances of the Netherlands more generally, / before turning to some of their dominant themes.**  
**🔸3 在接下来的篇幅中，我将把《看》、《美国女性》和《胜利》这些画报置于 OWI 更广泛的背景和目标以及荷兰当时的普遍环境下进行考察，然后再转向探讨它们的一些主导主题。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **locate... within...**（动词短语）**将……置于（背景/框架）中考察**。  
> **dominant theme**（名词短语）**主导主题；核心主题**。  
> **turn to**（动词短语）**转向；开始关注**。

**🔹Next to short-term war-related goals, / they reveal a longer-term strategy / that can be read / as paving the way / for what Henry Luce famously called the ‘American Century’ / in a Life magazine editorial (February 17, 1941), / addressing the expansion / of the nation’s political, economic, moral and cultural influence / in the midst of a world at war.**  
**🔸除了短期的战争相关目标外，它们还揭示了一种长远战略，这种战略可以被视为在为亨利·卢斯在 1941 年 2 月 17 日《生活》杂志社论中所著名的「美国世纪」铺平道路，即在世界大战之中扩张美国在政治、经济、道德和文化方面的影响力。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **pave the way for**（动词短语）**为……铺平道路；为……扫清障碍**。  
> **address**（v.）**探讨；处理；应对**（此处非「地址」）。  
> **in the midst of**（介词短语）**在……之中**。  
> 背景：**Henry Luce**；《**American Century**》。

**🔹The aftermath of World War II / was carefully planned / even as battles were still being fought; / and some of those plans / were ‘communicated’ with the Dutch / through these OWI magazines.**  
**🔸即便在战斗仍在进行时，二战后的格局就已被精心规划；其中一些计划通过这些 OWI 杂志与荷兰人民进行了「沟通」。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **aftermath**（n. [usually sing.]）**后果；（战争或灾难后的）余波/时期**。常见搭配：in the aftermath of the war。  
> **communicate with**（动词短语）**与……交流/沟通**；文中引号暗示导向性宣传。

**🔹1. The Office of War Information**  
**🔸1. 战争信息局**

**🔹4 In June 1942, / as one of the responses to ‘Pearl Harbor’ / and America’s subsequent total involvement in the war, / President Roosevelt created the US Office of War Information, / consolidating a number of earlier created organizations / charged with propaganda and information activities / at home and overseas.4**  
**🔸4 1942 年 6 月，作为对「珍珠港事件」以及美国随后全面卷入战争的回应之一，罗斯福总统创建了美国战争信息局，整合了此前成立的一系列负责国内外宣传和信息活动的组织。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **subsequent**（adj.）**随后的；接下来的**。  
> **consolidate**（v.）**合并；巩固；强化**。近义词：merge, combine。  
> **be charged with**（动词短语）**负责；承担……的任务**（亦可表「被指控」，依语境而定）。

**🔹The OWI consisted of two branches, / the Domestic branch and the Overseas Branch.**  
**🔸OWI 由两个分支组成：国内分支和海外分支。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **consist of**（动词短语）**由……组成/构成**（不用于进行时）。

**🔹5 The goals of the Domestic Branch / were short-term and war-related.**  
**🔸5 国内分支的目标是短期的且与战争相关的。**

**🔹Although the necessity of informing the American people / about war-related rules and regulations, / mobilizing women for the defense industry, etc., / was generally acknowledged, / many Republicans, / but also many Southern Democrats, / were suspicious of putting this kind of power / into the hands of the Roosevelt administration; / it would, they feared, / be ‘more New Deal propaganda.’5**  
**🔸虽然向美国人民通报战时规章制度、动员女性加入国防工业等必要性得到了普遍认可，但许多共和党人以及许多南方民主党人对将这种权力交到罗斯福政府手中深感疑虑；他们担心这会变成「更多的『新政』宣传」。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **suspicious of**（形容词短语）**对……怀疑/疑虑的**。  
> **mobilize**（v.）**动员；调动**。派生词：mobilization。  
> **New Deal**（名词短语）**新政**。

**🔹And they had a point.**  
**🔸而且，他们的担忧并非没有道理。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **have a point**（动词短语）**有道理；说得对**。

**🔹Many of the people working for the OWI / and its predecessors / were active supporters of the New Deal.**  
**🔸许多在 OWI 及其前身机构工作的人都是「新政」的积极支持者。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **predecessor**（n. [C]）**前任；前身；被取代的事物**。反义词：successor。

**🔹For instance Archibald MacLeish, / Pulitzer prize winning poet, / head of the Library of Congress / and staunch supporter (and close friend) of Roosevelt, / became assistant director of the OWI, / and Milton Eisenhower, / who used to work for one of the New Deal agencies / became OWI’s associate director.**  
**🔸例如，普利策奖获奖诗人、国会图书馆馆长、罗斯福的坚定支持者（兼密友）阿奇博尔德·麦克利什出任 OWI 助理局长；而曾在新政机构工作的米尔顿·艾森豪威尔则出任 OWI 副局长。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **staunch**（adj.）**坚定的；忠诚的**。常见搭配：staunch supporter。  
> **associate director**（名词短语）**副局长；副主任**。  
> 背景：**Milton Eisenhower** 为德怀特·艾森豪威尔之弟。

**🔹Robert Sherwood, / playwright, film critic for Life / and one of Roosevelt’s confidants, / became the director of the Overseas Branch.**  
**🔸罗伯特·舍伍德，剧作家、《生活》杂志影评人、罗斯福的亲信之一，出任海外分支局长。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **confidant**（n. [C]）**心腹；知己；亲信**。  
> **playwright**（n. [C]）**剧作家**（注意结尾 -wright）。

**🔹However, Elmer Davis, director of the OWI, / although a Democrat, / was not part of the clique.**  
**🔸然而，OWI 局长埃尔默·戴维斯虽然也是民主党人，却并不属于那个小圈子。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **clique**（n. [C]）**派系；小集团；私党**（常带贬义）。  
> **nonpartisan**（adj.）**无党派的；中立的**。

**🔹His appointment was based / in the first place / on his reputation as a journalist / for the New York Times and CBS, / and Democrats and Republicans alike / considered him a nonpartisan, trusted and trustworthy voice.6**  
**🔸他被任命首先是基于他作为《纽约时报》和 CBS 记者的声誉，民主党和共和党一致认为他是一个不偏不倚、值得信赖且可靠的发声者。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **in the first place**（介词短语）**首先；第一**。  
> **reputation**（n.）**名声；声望**。  
> **alike**（adv.）**同样地；一致地**；常置于 A and B 之后。  
> **trustworthy**（adj.）**值得信赖的**。辨析：trusting（易相信别人的）。

**🔹The Office of Strategic Services (OSS), / in charge of ‘black propaganda’ abroad, / by contrast, / was seen as a stronghold of the Republicans.7**  
**🔸相比之下，负责海外「黑色宣传」的战略情报局（OSS）则被视为共和党的据点。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **black propaganda**（名词短语）**黑色宣传**（伪造来源、针对敌方的宣传）。  
> **stronghold**（n. [C]）**大本营；据点；堡垒**。  
> **OSS**：CIA 前身。

**🔹6 The short-term goals / for the Overseas Branch / included stimulating a bond among the Allies, / counteracting Axis characterizations of America, / and helping to prevent frictions / between American armies / and the liberated countries’ civilian populations, / whether these were enemies or allies.**  
**🔸6 海外分支的短期目标包括：增进盟国间的纽带，反击轴心国对美国的定性描绘，并协助防止美国军队与被解放国家的平民（无论这些国家是敌是友）之间产生摩擦。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **stimulate**（v.）**刺激；促进；激发**。  
> **bond**（n. [C]）**纽带；联系；结合**。  
> **counteract**（v.）**抵消；中和；反击**。  
> **friction**（n.）**摩擦；冲突**。  
> **civilian population**（名词短语）**平民人口**。

**🔹The long-term strategy / was to create a new and robust image of America, / and to prepare audiences abroad / for America’s role as the new world leader.8**  
**🔸长期战略则是塑造一个崭新且强有力的美国形象，并让海外受众为美国扮演世界新领导者的角色做好准备。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **robust**（adj.）**强健的；强有力的**。  
> **audience abroad**（名词短语）**海外受众**。

**🔹In the words of the OWI: / “As the long-range directive implies, / one of our chief duties / is to convince people of the world / of the ‘overwhelming power and good faith of this country’.**  
**🔸引用 OWI 的话：「正如长期指令所暗示的，我们的首要任务之一是使世界人民相信这个国家具有『压倒性的力量和诚意』。」**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **long-range directive**（名词短语）**长期指令/指导方针**。  
> **convince... of...**（动词短语）**使某人确信……**。  
> **overwhelming**（adj.）**压倒性的；势不可挡的**。  
> **good faith**（n. [U]）**诚意；信用**。

**🔹By informing peoples of other countries fully / concerning the nature of this country and of its people, / sympathy, trust and friendliness will grow”.**  
**🔸通过向其他国家的人民充分通报这个国家及其人民的本质，同情、信任和友好将会增长」。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **concerning**（prep.）**关于**。  
> **nature**（n. [U]）**本质；特性**（此处非「自然界」）。

**🔹America had a mission.**  
**🔸美国负有一种使命。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **mission**（n. [C]）**使命；天职；任务**。

**🔹Carson summarizes the goals / of the OWI Overseas Branch / as not only to help to win the war, / but also to lay the foundations / of a new order, / led by Americans.9**  
**🔸卡森将 OWI 海外分支的目标总结为：不仅是协助赢得战争，还要为建立一个由美国人领导的新秩序打下基础。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **summarize**（v.）**总结；概述**。  
> **lay the foundations of**（动词短语）**为……奠定基础**。

**🔹7 The structure of the Office of War Information was complicated.**  
**🔸7 战争信息局的结构非常复杂。**

**🔹The director’s office and the Domestic Branch / were located in Washington, / while the communication and news center, / together with the offices of the Overseas Branch, / were mainly located in New York.**  
**🔸局长办公室和国内分支位于华盛顿，而通讯与新闻中心以及海外分支的办公室主要位于纽约。**

**🔹Congressional discussions, / differences of opinion over strategy, / and some personal rivalries / led to shifts in budgets and personnel / in the spring of 1943.**  
**🔸1943 年春季，国会的讨论、战略观点的分歧以及一些个人间的竞争导致了预算和人员的变动。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **congressional**（adj.）**国会的**。  
> **rivalry**（n.）**竞争；对抗**。  
> **personnel**（n. [集合名词]）**人员；职员**。  
> **shift**（n. [C]）**变动**。

**🔹Congress especially was very critical of the Domestic Branch.**  
**🔸国会尤其对国内分支持高度批评态度。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **be critical of**（形容词短语）**对……挑剔/批评的**。

**🔹This branch lost most of its funding, / whereas the Overseas Branch was cut / by only 10% .**  
**🔸该分支失去了大部分资金，而海外分支的预算仅被削减了 10%。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **funding**（n. [U]）**资金；拨款**。  
> **whereas**（conj.）**然而；但是**（正式对比连接词）。

**🔹Regarding personnel employed by the Overseas Branch, / Edward Barrett replaced Sherwood / as the head of the US based Overseas Branch; / and Sherwood, in turn, / became the head of the London post.**  
**🔸关于海外分支雇佣的人员，爱德华·巴雷特接替舍伍德担任总部位于美国的海外分支负责人；而舍伍德转而担任伦敦办事处的负责人。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **in turn**（副词短语）**轮流地；转而；进而**。  
> **post**（n. [C]）**办事处；岗位；哨所**。

**🔹Sherwood had a background in illustrated magazines, / and was interested in human-interest stories and photo-essays; / Barrett had a background in news magazines, / and favored more text / and a more impersonal social science approach.10**  
**🔸舍伍德有画报从业背景，对人情味故事和摄影专题报道感兴趣；巴雷特则有新闻杂志背景，更倾向于使用较多的文字和更客观的社会科学方法。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **human-interest story**（名词短语）**人情味故事；趣味性新闻**。  
> **photo-essay**（n. [C]）**摄影专题；图片文章**（用户原文作「摄影评论」，此处与常见用法一致译为「摄影专题」）。  
> **impersonal**（adj.）**客观的；非个人的**（此处偏「客观」）。  
> **approach**（n. [C]）**方法；路径**。

**🔹8 From spring 1944, / work by the Overseas Branch / was increasingly done by the outposts, / of which London was the most important.**  
**🔸8 从 1944 年春季起，海外分支的工作越来越多地由各前哨办事处承担，其中伦敦办事处最为重要。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **outpost**（n. [C]）**前哨；办事处；边远分部**。  
> **increasingly**（adv.）**越来越多地**。

**🔹In 1944 / a total of approximately 5,000 people / worked for the Overseas Branch, / of whom roughly 1,200 / (about 500 Americans and 700 locals) / worked at the London Outpost.11**  
**🔸1944 年，总共有大约 5,000 人为海外分支工作，其中约有 1,200 人（包括约 500 名美国人和 700 名当地人）在伦敦办事处工作。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **approximately**（adv.）**大概；近乎**。近义词：roughly, about。

**🔹The Americans in London / came from all 48 states / and were “thus fitted to express / a composite of American attitudes.”12**  
**🔸在伦敦的这些美国人来自当时所有的 48 个州，因此「能够表达一种综合性的美国态度」。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **composite**（n. [C]）**合成物；综合体**。  
> **be fitted to**（动词短语）**适合于……**。  
> 背景：1944 年时美国为 48 州，阿拉斯加与夏威夷尚未建州。

**🔹The Netherlands / - and partly its colony, the Dutch East Indies – / were served by London.**  
**🔸荷兰——以及其部分殖民地荷属东印度群岛——由伦敦办事处负责。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **colony**（n. [C]）**殖民地**。  
> **Dutch East Indies**：今印度尼西亚一带。

**🔹London was closer / to the actual theater of war, / and home to many governments in exile.**  
**🔸伦敦离实际战场更近，且是许多流亡政府的所在地。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **theater of war**（名词短语）**战区**。  
> **government in exile**（名词短语）**流亡政府**。

**🔹The London Outpost / was thus in a better position / to address the needs of different countries, / including propaganda clearance requirements.13**  
**🔸因此，伦敦办事处更有利于应对不同国家的需求，包括宣传审查要求。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **in a position to**（形容词短语）**有能力/有机会做某事**。  
> **clearance**（n. [U]）**许可；审查批准**。

**🔹It could also serve / as the ‘ears and eyes’ / for the US based offices.14**  
**🔸它还可以充当美国本土办公室的「耳目」。**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **serve as**（动词短语）**充当；担任；起……作用**。

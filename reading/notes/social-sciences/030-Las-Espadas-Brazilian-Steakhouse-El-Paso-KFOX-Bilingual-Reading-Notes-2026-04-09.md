EL PASO, Texas (KFOX14/CBS4) — A new dining experience is on the horizon for El Paso residents as Las Espadas – Brazilian Steakhouse prepares to open its doors in summer 2026.

The restaurant, which recently launched its official Instagram presence under the handle "lasespadasusa," is already generating buzz despite having only one post.

The restaurant's promotional post, captioned "Almost Here," highlights its upcoming El Paso location and signals that preparations are underway.

It will be located at 6044 Gateway Boulevard East by Exit 24/Geronimo Drive.

While details about the exact opening date remain limited, the announcement has begun to attract attention locally, with early followers eager to see what the new establishment will bring to the city's culinary scene.

Brazilian steakhouses have gained popularity across the United States for their unique blend of hospitality and high-quality meats, and Las Espadas appears poised to bring that experience to the Borderland.

The addition of a salad bar suggests a full-service dining approach catering to a wide range of tastes.

As El Paso continues to expand its food and hospitality offerings, Las Espadas could become a notable addition, especially for diners seeking something beyond traditional steakhouse fare.

More updates are expected as the restaurant moves closer to its anticipated 2026 opening.

### 中英对照概要

**A Brazilian culinary concept arrives in El Paso's dining frontier.** Las Espadas – Brazilian Steakhouse, building on the established success of rodizio-style dining in Mexico, is expanding northward into Texas this summer. The restaurant capitalizes on a growing appetite for interactive, premium-quality dining experiences that blend theatricality with gastronomic substance. Unlike conventional steakhouses that emphasize à la carte ordering, rodizio service orchestrates a choreographed flow of expertly carved meats directly to the table, transforming dining into a participatory spectacle. The East El Paso location at Gateway Boulevard positions the establishment within the city's commercial corridor, signaling strategic confidence in the market. With a comprehensive salad bar complement, Las Espadas addresses contemporary dining preferences for variety and dietary flexibility, while maintaining the Brazilian steakhouse tradition of abundance and hospitality. The venture exemplifies how regional restaurant models scale across borders, translating cultural dining practices into transnational commercial success.

### 3A. 重点词汇解析

#### W - 写作高频词

##### **1. horizon /həˈraɪz(ə)n/**
- **词性** | noun (可数名词)
- **英文释义** | The line at the farthest place that you can see, where the sky seems to touch the land or sea; the range of knowledge, experience, or activity that can be anticipated
- **中文释义** | 地平线；视野；知识或经历的范围；前景
- **语域标注** | 正式/书面/文学/新闻
- **同义词/反义词/常见词组** | Synonyms: vista, outlook, prospect | 常见词组：**on the horizon**（即将到来）、**new horizons**（新视野）、**expand one's horizons**（拓展视野）、**broaden horizons**（开阔眼界）
- **拓展内容（双语）** | "On the horizon"为新闻与商业报道中的常用短语，表示"即将发生或到来"。该短语强调的是未来的事物或机遇。与"in the distance"的区别在于：horizon更多指向明确的前景，而distance则是笼统的远方。"Horizon"作名词为可数名词，复数形式为"horizons"。 | 在美国英语中，"on the horizon"多用于表达积极的前景或机遇。"Expand horizons"为常见搭配，表示"扩大视野或机遇"。名词可数形式，复数"horizons"常见于"new horizons"这类表述中。
- **例句** | **A new dining experience is on the horizon for the residents.** / **一种新的用餐体验正等待着这些居民。** | **The company is always looking for new horizons in international markets.** / **该公司始终在国际市场中寻求新的机遇。**

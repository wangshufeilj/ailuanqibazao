# 西安青年投入数十万元制作「拍肩卡」，以互助精神守护老年人

**来源：** 综合新闻摘要（题图摄影张远 / CFP视觉中国）  
**录入日期：** 2026-04-02  

---

🔸 **西安青年投入数十万元制作"拍肩卡"，以互助精神守护老年人**  
🔹 **A Young Man from Xi'an Invests Hundreds of Thousands of Yuan to Create "Tap-My-Shoulder Cards," Promoting a Spirit of Mutual Aid**

---

🔸 陕西省西安市的赵晨，2014年因母亲受到他人帮助，开始关注身边需要帮助的中老年人，并发起了跨地域互助计划——**"子女代际守护温暖计划"**，倡导异地子女互相照看对方的父母。  
🔹 Zhao Chen, a resident of Xi'an, Shaanxi Province, was moved in 2014 when his mother received help from a stranger. This experience prompted him to pay closer attention to the elderly in need around him, and he launched a cross-regional mutual aid initiative — **the "Intergenerational Warmth Plan"** — encouraging people living away from home to look after each other's parents.

---

🔸 他最初制作了一张写有**"需要帮助时，请轻拍我的肩膀"**的卡片，挂在背包上。此后，他成立了工作室，研发出更实用、便于携带、**内置芯片**的"远程互助拍肩卡"。  
🔹 He initially made a simple card reading **"If you need help, please tap my shoulder,"** and hung it on his backpack. He later founded a studio and developed a more practical, portable version — **a chip-embedded "Remote Mutual Aid Tap-My-Shoulder Card."**

---

🔸 2024年11月，赵晨**自掏腰包投入数十万元**，将"拍肩卡"免费发放给全国各地热心公益的志愿者及中老年群体。目前，响应他号召参与活动的志愿者已**接近50万人**。  
🔹 In November 2024, Zhao Chen **invested hundreds of thousands of yuan of his own savings** to distribute the cards free of charge to volunteers and elderly individuals across the country. To date, nearly **500,000 volunteers** have joined his initiative.

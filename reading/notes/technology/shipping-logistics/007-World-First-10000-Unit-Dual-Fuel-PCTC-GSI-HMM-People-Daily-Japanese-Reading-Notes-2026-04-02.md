> The first vessel in a series of 10,800-CEU LNG dual-fuel Pure Car and Truck Carriers (PCTCs), built by Guangzhou Shipyard International (GSI) — a subsidiary of China State Shipbuilding Corporation (CSSC) — for South Korea's HMM, set sail on March 31 for sea trials. This marks the world's first 10,000-CEU-class dual-fuel car carrier currently under construction.
>
> The vessel measures 230 meters in length, 40 meters in breadth, has a structural draft of 10.5 meters, and a service speed of approximately 19 knots, achieving an optimal balance between high-efficiency transport and stable navigation. Equipped with 14 vehicle decks, it can flexibly accommodate a wide range of vehicle types including electric vehicles, hydrogen-powered vehicles, and heavy trucks, with a maximum single-vessel capacity of 10,800 units. Calculated on the basis of standard 5-meter passenger cars placed end-to-end, the total length would exceed 50 kilometers.
>
> The vessel's propulsion system adopts a dual-fuel configuration running on both fuel oil and LNG, and is fitted with a shaft generator that enables power generation while underway, thereby achieving energy savings and emissions reduction. In terms of environmental performance, it meets the International Maritime Organization (IMO) Tier III NOx emission standards.
>
> A responsible official at GSI explained: "Car carriers are a high-tech, high-value-added vessel type with demanding construction difficulty and stringent technical requirements. First, a large quantity of thin steel plates must be used, and our construction team has overcome the challenge of deformation control in thin-plate fabrication. Second, the vessel features numerous roll-on/roll-off (RoRo) systems with a high level of mechanization. These equipment were previously largely dependent on imports, but are now entirely domestically produced. The successful construction and sea trial of this 10,000-CEU-class carrier demonstrates that GSI has fully established its construction technology for this vessel category and continues to maintain a leading position in this specialized market segment."

**① Sea trial**
- **音标：** /ˈsiː ˌtraɪəl/ *n.*
- **英文释义：** A test of a newly built or repaired ship's performance, conducted at sea before official handover or commercial operation.（新造或修缮船舶在正式交付或投入商业运营前进行的海上性能测试）
- **中文释义：** 海上试航；海试
- **语域：** 专业/技术（海事工程）
- **同义词：** sea test, performance trial, maiden test run（注：与 *maiden voyage* 区分——sea trial 是交付前的性能测试，maiden voyage 是交付后的首次正式航行）
- **拓展：** 常用复数形式 *sea trials*；动词搭配：*conduct / carry out / complete sea trials*；固定短语：*set sail for sea trials*（出海试航）
- **例句：**

**⑥ Pure Car and Truck Carrier (PCTC)**
- **音标：** /pjʊr kɑːr ænd trʌk ˈkæriər/ *n.*
- **英文释义：** A specialized type of roll-on/roll-off vessel designed exclusively to transport automobiles, trucks, and other wheeled cargo across oceans.（专为跨洋运输汽车、卡车及其他轮式货物而设计的专用滚装船型）
- **中文释义：** 纯汽车/卡车运输船；汽车滚装船
- **语域：** 专业/航运
- **相关词：** RoRo ship（滚装船），Car Equivalent Unit（CEU，车辆当量单位）
- **拓展：** *CEU* 为PCTC核心容量单位，1 CEU通常以标准轿车（约5米长）为基准；区分：PCTC vs. RoRo（RoRo泛指所有滚装船，PCTC特指汽车运输型）

  > 随着中国汽车出口的快速增长，全球对汽车滚装船的需求急剧上升，促使各大船厂扩大汽车滚装船订单。

**⑦ Draft (draught)**
- **音标：** /dræft/ *n.*
- **英文释义：** The vertical distance between the waterline and the lowest point of a vessel's hull; determines the minimum water depth required for safe navigation.（船舶吃水线至船底最低点的垂直距离，决定安全航行所需的最小水深）
- **中文释义：** 吃水；吃水深度
- **语域：** 专业/海事
- **拓展：** 英式拼写为 *draught*，美式为 *draft*；*structural draft* 指设计吃水（结构吃水）；区分：*draft* vs. *freeboard*（干舷：水线以上船侧高度）
- **例句：**
  > With a structural **draft** of 10.5 meters, the vessel can call at most major deep-water terminals without significant port restriction.
  > 该船结构吃水为10.5米，可挂靠大多数主要深水码头而不受显著的港口限制。

---

**⑧ Shaft generator**
- **音标：** /ʃæft ˈdʒenəreɪtər/ *n.*
- **英文释义：** A generator mechanically connected to a ship's propeller shaft, capable of producing electricity from the shaft's rotation while the vessel is underway.（与船舶螺旋桨轴机械连接的发电机，在船舶航行时可利用轴的转动发电）
- **中文释义：** 轴带发电机；轴发电机
- **语域：** 专业/机械工程/海事
- **同义词：** shaft power take-off (PTO), PTO generator
- **拓展：** 是绿色船舶节能技术的重要组成部分；与 *waste heat recovery system*（余热回收系统）并列为主流船舶节能手段
- **例句：**
  > The integration of a **shaft generator** allows the ship to meet onboard power demands without running auxiliary engines, yielding measurable fuel savings on long-haul voyages.
  > 轴带发电机的集成使船舶无需运行辅助发动机即可满足船上用电需求，在长途航行中实现了可观的燃油节省。

---

**⑨ Deformation control**
- **音标：** /ˌdiːfɔːrˈmeɪʃən kənˈtroʊl/ *n.*
- **英文释义：** Engineering techniques used to prevent or minimize unwanted warping, bending, or distortion in thin steel panels during welding or fabrication.（在焊接或制造过程中用于防止或减少薄钢板发生不必要翘曲、弯曲或变形的工程技术）
- **中文释义：** 变形控制
- **语域：** 专业/工程/造船
- **拓展：** 薄板（thin plate / thin-gauge steel）焊接变形是造船一大技术难题，PCTC因车甲板大量使用薄钢板（约6-8mm）而挑战尤为突出；相关术语：*thermal stress*（热应力），*buckling*（屈曲/翘曲）
- **例句：**
  > Mastering **deformation control** in thin-plate welding is essential for PCTC construction, as even minor distortions can compromise the structural integrity of vehicle decks.
  > 掌握薄板焊接变形控制技术对汽车滚装船建造至关重要，因为即使是轻微的变形也可能损害车甲板的结构完整性。

---

**⑩ Knot (unit of speed)**
- **音标：** /nɒt/ *n.*
- **英文释义：** A unit of speed equal to one nautical mile per hour (approximately 1.852 km/h), used in maritime and aviation contexts.（等于每小时一海里的速度单位，约合1.852公里/时，用于航海和航空语境）
- **中文释义：** 节（航速单位）
- **语域：** 专业/海事/航空
- **拓展：** 注意：不说 *"knots per hour"*（错误冗余），直接说 *"19 knots"* 即可；1 knot = 1.852 km/h；区分：*knot*（速度）vs. *nautical mile*（距离）
- **例句：**
  > The vessel's service speed of approximately 19 **knots** is optimized to balance fuel efficiency and delivery schedules across major trans-Pacific trade routes.
  > 该船约19节的服务航速经过优化，以在主要跨太平洋航线上平衡燃油效率与交付时间安排。

**⑪ Roll-on/Roll-off (RoRo)**
- **音标：** /ˌroʊl ɒn ˌroʊl ɒf/ *adj. / n.*
- **英文释义（Longman）：** A roll-on roll-off ship is one that vehicles can drive straight on and off.（车辆可以直接驶入和驶出的船型）
- **中文释义：** 滚装（动词/形容词）；滚装船（名词）；即"开上开下"装卸方式
- **语域：** 专业/航运/贸易
- **翻译要点：** 日文原文"ロールオン・ロールオフ（RORO）" 即英文 *Roll-on/Roll-off*，中文译为"滚装"或"滚上滚下"，切忌直译为"卷上卷下"
- **拓展：** 区分 *RoRo*（滚装）vs. *LoLo*（吊装，Lift-on/Lift-off）；PCTC即RoRo船的细分品种

- **翻译要点：** 日文"1万800台積載"中"台"即CEU；中文译为"台位"或"车位容量"，英文统一用"CEU"
- **例句：**
  > With a **10,800-CEU** capacity, the vessel can transport a mix of standard sedans, electric SUVs, and heavy commercial vehicles simultaneously on its 14 dedicated decks.
  > 凭借10,800台位的容量，该船可在其14层专用甲板上同时运输标准轿车、电动SUV和重型商用车等多种车型。

**⑭ Localization（国产化语境）**
- **音标：** /ˌloʊkələˈzeɪʃən/ *n.*
- **英文释义：** In an industrial/supply chain context: the process of developing or sourcing components domestically rather than relying on foreign imports.（在工业/供应链语境中：在国内开发或采购零部件而非依赖外国进口的过程）
- **中文释义：** 国产化；本土化
- **语域：** 经济/工业/政策
- **翻译要点：** 中文"国产化"在英文有多种对应：*localization, domestic production, import substitution, indigenization*；在中国制造业语境中，最贴切的英文表述通常是 *localization of production* 或 *domestic substitution*
- **拓展：** 近义词区分：*localization*（本土化，强调地点）vs. *indigenization*（本地化，强调文化/人员）vs. *nationalization*（国有化，强调所有权，意义不同，切勿混用）
- **例句：**
  > The full **localization** of RoRo equipment represents a significant step in reducing China's dependency on foreign maritime machinery suppliers.
  > 滚装设备的全面国产化是减少中国对外国海事机械供应商依赖的重要一步。

**⑮ Subsidize / Subsidy（延伸：韩国HMM背景）**
- **音标：** /ˈsʌbsɪdaɪz/ *v.* | /ˈsʌbsɪdi/ *n.*
- **英文释义：** To provide financial support, typically from a government, to an organization or industry to reduce costs or sustain operations.（通常指政府向机构或行业提供资金支持，以降低成本或维持运营）
- **中文释义：** 补贴（动词）；补贴金（名词）
- **语域：** 经济/政策/新闻
- **拓展：** *subsidy* 为可数名词，复数 *subsidies*；*subsidize* 不规则变化无，常见搭配：*government subsidy, state subsidy, cross-subsidy*；与文章相关：韩国政府曾向HMM注资逾50亿美元以维持其运营
- **例句：**
  > The South Korean government's decision to **subsidize** HMM with billions of dollars in state funding ensured the survival of the country's flagship carrier during a prolonged industry downturn.
  > 韩国政府向HMM注入数十亿美元国家资金的决定，确保了这家旗舰运营商在行业长期低迷期间得以存续。

**⑰ Overcome**

**㉑ Set sail for**
- **音标：** /set seɪl fɔːr/
- **英文释义：** To begin a voyage toward a particular destination.（开始向特定目的地航行；启航前往）

**㉒ End-to-end**

## 4D. 金句积累

**① 技术突破的标志性表述**

> "The successful construction and sea trial of this 10,000-CEU-class carrier demonstrates that GSI has fully established its construction technology for this vessel category and continues to maintain a leading position in this specialized market segment."
>
> **中文：** 本次万台级汽车运输船的建造及试航成功，证明广船国际已完全确立了该船型的建造技术体系，并在这一细分市场持续保持领先地位。

> 📝 **写作价值：** 适用于科技突破、产品发布、企业里程碑等写作场景；"fully established"与"continue to maintain"双重递进结构，逻辑严密，可直接仿用于论说文。

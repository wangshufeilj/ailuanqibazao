# 文章结构导航 | Article Structure Map

## I. 整体结构 (Overall Structure)

文章采取“由点及面”的结构。首先以作者参观比亚迪加州工厂为切入点，探讨比亚迪在美洲的市场布局；随后扩展至“The Drain”周报栏目，汇总全美及全球范围内能源、环境、政策、气候及地缘政治的最新动态。

The article adopts a "micro-to-macro" structure, starting with a factory tour of BYD in California to discuss its American footprint, then expanding into "The Drain" column to summarize global energy, environment, and policy updates.

   - **加州动态 (California Specifics):** 输油管道争议 (Sable Offshore)、洛杉矶电力局变动。

### 文章一：小米SU7工厂——中国最热门的旅游打卡地

**【英文概要 Summary】**

What began as a manufacturing facility has quietly transformed into one of China's most sought-after tourist destinations. The Xiaomi SU7 factory in Beijing now commands ticket prices of $280 per person, drawing gearheads and curious travelers alike with the spectacle of 700+ robots assembling a complete vehicle every 76 seconds. With tours capped at 20 participants, a 700,000 square-meter floor plan, and immersive test drives on offer, the experience is less "factory visit" and more curated theater. The contrast with Tesla's Gigafactories — where access is tightly restricted to shareholders — underscores both the savvy of Xiaomi's consumer-facing strategy and the remarkable brand evolution of a company once known primarily for budget smartphones.

**【中文概要】**

曾经的制造车间，如今摇身一变成为中国最炙手可热的旅游目的地。北京小米SU7超级工厂每张门票高达280美元，吸引了大批汽车爱好者与游客驻足。工厂占地70万平方米，700余台机器人平均每76秒下线一辆整车，参观人数严格限定在每批20人。游客不仅可以观赏"机器人芭蕾"，更能亲身参与SU7试驾，体验感十足。与特斯拉仅对股东开放参观权限的做法形成鲜明对比，小米此举折射出其以消费者为导向的品牌策略，也见证了这家曾以平价智能手机起家的企业的深度蜕变。

### 文章二：NASA宇航员：太空让我看清了我们"活在谎言中"

**【英文概要 Summary】**

Former NASA astronaut Ronald Garan, who logged 178 days aboard the International Space Station, recounts the disorienting clarity that comes from viewing Earth from orbit — a psychological phenomenon known as the "overview effect." Looking down at the illuminated border between India and Pakistan, Garan found himself confronting the peculiar human habit of inscribing conflict onto a planet that, from space, appears luminous and whole. His central provocation — that humanity has inverted its priorities by placing economy above ecology — challenges us to rethink civilizational order: not "economy, society, planet" but "planet, society, economy."

**【中文概要】**

曾在国际空间站驻留178天的前NASA宇航员罗纳德·加兰，讲述了俯瞰地球时所产生的强烈认知冲击——即心理学界所称的"概览效应"。当他从轨道上望见印巴边境那条蜿蜒的灯火长链，才意识到人类将自身的冲突与分裂铭刻于这颗在太空中看来浑然一体的星球上。他提出的核心质问——人类因将"经济"置于"生态"之上而"活在谎言中"——呼吁我们重新审视文明的优先秩序：不应是"经济—社会—地球"，而应是"地球—社会—经济"。

## 模块四：素材与语料库积累

### 4A. 重点词汇解析

---

#### 🔵 W — 写作高频词（Writing）

---

**1. meticulously** /məˈtɪk.jə.ləs.li/ *adv.*

- **英文释义**：In a way that shows very careful attention to detail; with extreme precision.
- **中文释义**：一丝不苟地；精心地；细致入微地
- **语域**：书面语 / 正式 / 新闻写作
- **同义词**：carefully, painstakingly, scrupulously, rigorously
- **反义词**：carelessly, sloppily, haphazardly
- **拓展**：形容词为 meticulous，名词为 meticulousness。常见搭配：*meticulously planned / crafted / documented / organized*。在学术写作中常用于描述研究方法的严谨性；在新闻写作中用以强调策划的精密程度。
- **例句**：The archaeological site was **meticulously** excavated over three decades, yielding thousands of artifacts that reshaped our understanding of the civilization.
  > 该考古遗址历经三十年的精心发掘，出土数千件文物，从根本上改写了我们对这一文明的认知。

---

**2. exclusive** /ɪkˈskluː.sɪv/ *adj. / n.*

- **英文释义**：(adj.) ① limited to only one person or group; ② high quality and expensive, and therefore not available to everyone; (n.) a news story reported by only one media outlet.
- **中文释义**：①独家的；排他的 ②高档的；限量的；（n.）独家报道
- **语域**：新闻 / 商业 / 日常正式
- **同义词**：sole, restricted, premier, high-end
- **反义词**：inclusive, open, accessible, public
- **拓展**：名词 exclusivity（不可数），常见搭配：*exclusive access / exclusive tour / exclusive deal / mutually exclusive*。注意 *exclusive of* 表示"不含……"（书面用语）。区分：*exclusive* vs. *excusive*（后者不存在，常见拼写错误）。
- **例句**：The tech giant offered **exclusive** early access to its new operating system to a select group of developers worldwide.
  > 这家科技巨头向全球精选的开发者群体提供了其新操作系统的独家抢先体验资格。

---

**3. immersive** /ɪˈmɜːr.sɪv/ *adj.*

- **英文释义**：(LDOCE) Making you feel you are really taking part in the events you are seeing on a screen or hearing about, or are really in a virtual environment.
- **中文释义**：沉浸式的；身临其境的
- **语域**：科技 / 新闻 / 商业营销
- **同义词**：engaging, absorbing, all-encompassing, experiential
- **反义词**：detached, passive, observational
- **拓展**：动词 *immerse*，名词 *immersion*（不可数）。固定搭配：*immersive experience / immersive technology / immersive learning / immersive tour*。"沉浸式"是近年科技与教育领域高频词，尤其在VR/AR语境下大量使用。
- **例句**：The museum's redesigned gallery offers an **immersive** walk-through of ancient Rome, complete with 3D projections and spatial audio.
  > 该博物馆重新设计的展廊提供了一段古罗马的沉浸式穿越体验，配有3D投影与空间音频。

---

**4. state-of-the-art** /ˌsteɪt.əv.ðiˈɑːrt/ *adj.*

- **英文释义**：Using the most modern and recently developed methods, materials, or knowledge.
- **中文释义**：最先进的；处于技术前沿的
- **语域**：新闻 / 科技 / 商业
- **同义词**：cutting-edge, leading-edge, advanced, pioneering
- **反义词**：outdated, obsolete, antiquated
- **拓展**：通常作前置定语，使用连字符；作表语时可不加连字符（*the technology is state of the art*）。常见搭配：*state-of-the-art facility / equipment / technology / design*。
- **例句**：The hospital's new oncology wing is equipped with **state-of-the-art** imaging technology that reduces diagnosis time by 40%.
  > 该医院新建的肿瘤科病区配备了最先进的影像技术，将诊断时间缩短了40%。

---

**5. prestige / prestigious** /prɛˈstiːʒ/ /prɛˈstɪdʒ.əs/ *n. / adj.*

- **英文释义**：(n.) The respect and admiration that someone or something receives, usually because of their success, importance, or wealth; (adj.) admired and respected as one of the best.
- **中文释义**：（n.）声望；威望；（adj.）享有盛名的；令人瞩目的
- **语域**：正式 / 学术 / 新闻
- **同义词**：renown, distinction, eminence; prestigious → renowned, esteemed, eminent
- **反义词**：disgrace, disrepute; disreputable
- **拓展**：*prestigious* 常修饰机构、奖项、职位等（*prestigious university / award / position*）。注意文中 *prestigious location* 略带口语夸张色彩，在正式写作中应搭配更具体的名词。
- **例句**：Winning the Fields Medal is considered one of the most **prestigious** honors in mathematics, awarded every four years to scholars under 40.
  > 荣获菲尔兹奖被视为数学界最崇高的荣誉之一，每四年授予一位40岁以下的学者。

---

**6. accommodate** /əˈkɒm.ə.deɪt/ *v.*

- **英文释义**：① to provide with a place to stay; ② to have or provide enough space for; ③ to consider or include someone's needs, wishes, etc.
- **中文释义**：①提供住宿；②容纳；③迁就；满足（需求）
- **语域**：正式 / 学术 / 商业
- **同义词**：house, contain, hold; cater to, satisfy
- **拓展**：名词 *accommodation*（英式常用复数 accommodations）；注意拼写易错（双 c 双 m）。常见搭配：*accommodate up to / accommodate diverse needs / accommodate a request*。在商业及法律语境中 *accommodate* 常有"满足合理需要"之义（如 reasonable accommodation，美国法律术语）。
- **例句**：The new conference center can **accommodate** up to 3,000 delegates and features simultaneous interpretation in six languages.
  > 新会议中心最多可容纳3000名代表，并配备六种语言的同声传译设施。

---

**7. profound** /prəˈfaʊnd/ *adj.*

- **英文释义**：Having a strong, serious, or far-reaching effect; showing or needing great knowledge or understanding.
- **中文释义**：深刻的；深远的；影响深重的
- **语域**：正式 / 学术 / 书面
- **同义词**：deep, far-reaching, significant, momentous
- **反义词**：superficial, trivial, shallow
- **拓展**：副词 *profoundly*；名词 *profundity*（不可数，深度/深意）。常见搭配：*profound impact / profound change / profound understanding / profound effect on*。在学术写作与演讲稿中极为高频，有时被过度使用，需注意语境匹配。
- **例句**：The discovery of gravitational waves had a **profound** impact on theoretical physics, confirming predictions made a century earlier by Einstein.
  > 引力波的发现对理论物理学产生了深远影响，印证了爱因斯坦一个世纪前的预言。

---

**8. rival** /ˈraɪ.vəl/ *v. / n. / adj.*

- **英文释义**：(v.) to be as good as or almost as good as someone or something else; (n.) a person or thing competing with or equal to another; (adj.) competing.
- **中文释义**：（v.）与……匹敌；（n.）竞争者；（adj.）竞争的
- **语域**：新闻 / 商业 / 正式
- **同义词**：(v.) match, equal, compete with, parallel; (n.) competitor, contender
- **拓展**：名词 *rivalry*（竞争关系，不可数/可数均可）。常见搭配：*rival company / without rival / unrivalled（无与伦比的）*。文中标题 *rivals Tesla's gigafactories* 使用动词形式，表"可与……媲美"。
- **例句**：No other streaming service has yet managed to **rival** the subscriber base that the industry pioneer amassed in just over a decade.
  > 迄今尚无其他流媒体服务能与这一行业先驱在短短十余年间积累的订阅用户规模相媲美。

---

**9. teeming** /ˈtiː.mɪŋ/ *adj.*

- **英文释义**：Full of people, animals, or things that are all moving around; abundantly filled.
- **中文释义**：充满生机的；密布的；熙熙攘攘的
- **语域**：书面 / 文学 / 新闻
- **同义词**：teeming with = bursting with, swarming with, overflowing with
- **拓展**：常见搭配 *teeming with life / teeming with activity / teeming cities*。动词 *teem with* 表"充满"；注意区分 *team*（团队）与 *teem*（充满）的拼写。文中 *an iridescent biosphere teeming with life* 是极佳的书面表达范式。
- **例句**：The coral reef ecosystem, once **teeming with** marine biodiversity, has suffered bleaching events linked to rising ocean temperatures.
  > 这片珊瑚礁生态系统曾经生机勃勃、物种繁盛，如今已因海水升温引发的白化事件而遭受严重破坏。

---

**10. vantage point** /ˈvæn.tɪdʒ ˌpɔɪnt/ *n.*

- **英文释义**：① A position from which you can see or understand something clearly; ② A position that gives you an advantage over others.
- **中文释义**：①有利的观察位置；②（认识事物的）有利角度；制高点
- **语域**：书面 / 正式 / 学术
- **同义词**：viewpoint, standpoint, perspective, vantage
- **拓展**：常与介词 *from* 搭配，*from the vantage point of*（从……的角度来看）。在政治评论与哲学写作中大量使用，引申为"立场"或"认识视角"。文中 *from the vantage point of space* 是原文最具冲击力的短语之一。
- **例句**：From the **vantage point** of economic history, the 2008 financial crisis was not an anomaly but the predictable outcome of decades of deregulation.
  > 从经济史的视角审视，2008年金融危机并非偶然，而是数十年去监管化政策的必然结果。

---

**11. biosphere** /ˈbaɪ.oʊ.sfɪr/ *n.*

- **英文释义**：The layer of the earth that contains all the living things; the part of the Earth's environment where life exists.
- **中文释义**：生物圈
- **语域**：学术 / 科学 / 正式
- **同义词**：ecosphere（生态圈）

- **例句**：The report calls for a fundamental **shift** in global energy policy, moving away from fossil fuel subsidies toward investment in renewable infrastructure.
  > 该报告呼吁全球能源政策进行根本性转变，从化石燃料补贴转向对可再生能源基础设施的投入。

#### 🟠 S — 熟词僻义 / 引申义（Secondary meanings）

---

**34. treat** /triːt/ *n.*

- **英文释义**：① Something especially enjoyable or pleasant that you give someone or that happens to them; ② (British informal) *a treat* = wonderfully, beautifully.
- **中文释义**：①令人愉快的事；享受；好东西；②（英式口语）极好地；妙极了
- **熟词僻义**：一般学习者熟悉 *treat* 作动词"对待/治疗"，但作名词的 *a treat*（一件乐事/享受）和 *a real treat*（真正的享受/极好的体验）在阅读中极为常见，且容易被忽视。文中 *making this a real treat for automotive lovers* 即此用法。
- **例句**：After months of strict budgeting, the family decided to **splurge on a treat** — a weekend getaway to a lakeside resort.
  > 在数月的严格节省之后，这家人决定奢侈一把，犒劳一下自己——去一家湖畔度假村度过一个周末。

**35. handle** /ˈhæn.dəl/ *v.*

- **英文释义**：① To touch, pick up, or hold; ② **to deal with, manage, or control** (a situation, person, or subject matter).
- **中文释义**：①触摸；搬运；②掌控；处理；擅长（某领域）
- **熟词僻义**：文中 *such a handle on the tourism* 中的 *a handle on sth* 是固定搭配，意为"对某事的掌控力/驾驭能力"（*to have a handle on sth* = to understand or control sth effectively）。多数学习者仅熟悉 *handle* 作"把手"或"处理"之义，此搭配较为生僻。
- **例句**：After three years on the job, she finally felt she had a solid **handle on** the complexities of international trade compliance.
  > 从事这份工作三年后，她终于感到自己对国际贸易合规的复杂性有了切实的把握。

**36. spot** /spɒt/ *v.*

- **英文释义**：To see or notice something or someone, especially something that is difficult to see or not obvious.
- **中文释义**：发现；察觉；认出；（意外）看到
- **熟词僻义**：学习者熟悉 *spot* 作名词"地点"，但作动词"发现/看到"时同样高频。文中 *spotted a long snaking line of lights* 中 *spot* 即此义——从太空中"看到/注意到"。此动词用法在侦探小说、新闻报道及日常英语中极为常见。
- **例句**：A wildlife researcher **spotted** what appeared to be a previously undocumented subspecies of freshwater dolphin near a remote tributary of the Amazon.

**37. grab** /ɡræb/ *v.*

- **英文释义**：① To take hold of suddenly; ② **to affect someone strongly; to capture someone's attention or imagination.**
- **中文释义**：①抓住；②深深影响；牢牢抓住（某人的注意力/情感）
- **熟词僻义**：文中 *it appears to have grabbed Garan particularly strongly* 中，*grab* 不是字面的"抓握"，而是"深深触动/影响"之义（*grab sb* = to make a strong impression on sb）。此引申义在口语及新闻写作中常见。
- **例句**：The documentary's opening sequence immediately **grabs** the viewer, plunging them into the chaos of a refugee camp without any preamble.
  > 这部纪录片的开场画面立刻抓住了观众——毫无铺垫地将他们直接带入一座难民营的混乱景象之中。

**38. heavy stuff** *idiom / colloquial expression*

- **英文释义**：(informal) Content, ideas, or subject matter that is serious, emotionally intense, or intellectually challenging.
- **中文释义**：（非正式）沉重的话题；深沉的内容；让人深思的东西
- **熟词僻义**：*heavy* 作形容词通常表"重量大"，但在口语及非正式写作中，*heavy stuff / heavy* 可形容话题的情感分量或思想深度（*that's heavy* = that's serious/intense）。文中 *Heavy stuff* 作为末段独立短句，是作者的情感收尾语。
- **例句**：The memoir covered everything from childhood trauma to existential reckoning — **heavy stuff**, but written with a lightness that made it surprisingly readable.
  > 这部回忆录涵盖了从童年创伤到存在主义反思的一切——都是沉重的话题，但作者以一种轻盈的笔触写就，使得整本书出乎意料地引人入胜。

**39. clock up** *phrasal verb*

- **英文释义**：To reach or achieve a total or a particular amount of something (often time, distance, or achievements accumulated gradually).
- **中文释义**：积累达到（某数量）；累计
- **熟词僻义**：*clock* 通常理解为"时钟"，但 *clock up / clock* 作动词表"积累/达到（总量）"（*He clocked up 178 days in space*），是体育报道、职场描述中的常见表达，学习者易忽视。
- **例句**：By the time she retired, she had **clocked up** nearly 40 years in the diplomatic service, serving in embassies across five continents.
  > 退休之时，她已在外交领域积累了近40年的资历，曾在横跨五大洲的使馆任职。

**40. off-season** /ˈɒf.ˌsiː.zən/ *adj. / n.*

- **英文释义**：(adj./n.) Relating to or occurring in the period of time when business, tourism, or activity is at its lowest level.
- **中文释义**：（adj./n.）淡季的；非旺季的
- **熟词僻义**：*season* 众所周知，但 *off-season* 作复合词的固定用法（旅游、体育、商业语境中均高频）常被忽视。对应词 *peak season（旺季）、high season（旺季）、low season（淡季）*。
- **例句**：The ski resort attracts mountain bikers and hikers during the **off-season**, ensuring year-round revenue and reducing its dependence on snowfall.
  > 这个滑雪胜地在淡季吸引山地自行车爱好者和徒步者，确保全年收入来源，降低对降雪量的依赖。

**41. complimentary vs. complementary**（辨析词组）

- **complimentary** /ˌkɒm.plɪˈmen.tər.i/：免费的；赞美的
- **complementary** /ˌkɒm.plɪˈmen.tər.i/：互补的；相辅相成的
- **熟词僻义**：两词发音完全相同（同音异形异义词 / homophones），是英语写作中最高频的混淆词对之一。区分方法：*complement* 含 *complete* 的词根，表"使完整/互补"；*compliment* 表"赞美/免费赠送"。
- **例句**：The **complementary** nature of digital and print journalism — one providing speed, the other depth — suggests the two formats will coexist rather than compete.
  > 数字新闻与印刷新闻之间互补的特性——前者提供速度，后者提供深度——表明两种形式将共存而非相互竞争。

**42. wholly owned subsidiary**（法律/商业固定搭配）

- **英文释义**：A company whose entire share capital is owned by a parent company, giving the parent full control.
- **中文释义**：全资子公司
- **熟词僻义**：*wholly*（完全地）+ *owned*（拥有的）+ *subsidiary*（子公司）三词组合是公司法与商业新闻中极为重要的固定表达，文中 Garan 以此作比喻（*life-support systems of our planet, as the wholly owned subsidiary of the global economy*），将其从商业语境迁移至生态批评语境，修辞效果极强。
- **例句**：The acquisition will make the startup a **wholly owned subsidiary** of the acquiring conglomerate, though its management team will remain largely intact.
  > 此次收购将使这家初创企业成为收购方集团的全资子公司，但其管理团队将基本保持不变。

#### 🔴 L — 地道表达（Idiomatic / Natural Expressions）

---

**43. too bad** *idiom*

- **英文释义**：(informal) Used to express that a situation is unfortunate; sometimes used sarcastically to show that you don't feel sorry.
- **中文释义**：真可惜；没办法；（讽刺语）活该；那也没辙
- **语域**：非正式 / 口语
- **拓展**：文中 *Too bad, because tickets are almost impossible to obtain* 是新闻写作中口语化介入（direct address）的典型手法，以增加读者代入感。在正式写作中应改为 *Unfortunately, however, tickets are almost impossible to obtain*。
- **例句**："You wanted the window seat? **Too bad** — you checked in last." — The sarcasm in his voice suggested he'd been waiting for this moment.
  > "你想要靠窗的位置？那没办法——你是最后一个登机的。"他语气中的讽刺意味表明他早已等待这一刻。

---

**44. Who knew (that)…** *rhetorical expression*

- **英文释义**：A rhetorical expression expressing surprise at an unexpected fact or development; "It was impossible to predict that…"
- **中文释义**：谁能想到……；谁曾料到……
- **语域**：非正式 / 口语化写作 / 新闻评论
- **拓展**：*Who knew that...* 是英语评论文章和博客中常见的修辞性反问句，用于表达惊讶或反讽。正式写作中对应表达：*Few could have anticipated that... / It was hardly predictable that...*
- **例句**："**Who knew** that a pandemic-era pivot to home cooking would permanently reshape the restaurant industry?" asked the food critic in her year-end review.
  > "谁能想到，疫情期间转向居家烹饪的这一转变，竟会永久地重塑餐饮业格局？"这位美食评论家在她的年终评论中如此发问。

---

**45. Where's our ticket?** *colloquial closing device*

- **英文释义**：An informal expression of desire to also experience something that has just been described enthusiastically; "We want in too!"
- **中文释义**：（轻松口语结尾）我们的票呢？我们也想去！
- **语域**：非正式 / 新闻软文结尾技巧
- **拓展**：这是英语软文写作（feature writing）中常见的"情感收尾法"（affective close），让作者与读者共情，消除媒体的旁观者距离感。类似结构：*Sign us up! / Count us in! / Can we get one?*
- **例句**：*The new deep-sea submersible can carry two passengers to previously unexplored ocean trenches. **Where do we sign up?***
  > 这艘新型深海潜水器可搭载两名乘客前往此前从未探索过的海沟。我们在哪里报名？

---

**46. feel connected to** *colloquial / emotional expression*

- **英文释义**：To experience a sense of emotional, spiritual, or intellectual kinship with someone or something.
- **中文释义**：感到与……有联结；感受到归属感；产生共鸣
- **语域**：口语 / 情感表达 / 日常书面
- **拓展**：*connect* 系列表达在当代英语中极为高频，尤其在心理健康、社会学及媒体叙事语境中：*feel connected / stay connected / a sense of connection / disconnect from*。注意名词 *connection* 兼有"联系/关系"与"情感共鸣"双重含义。
- **例句**：Many readers reported **feeling deeply connected to** the protagonist's sense of alienation, having experienced similar struggles with belonging in a new country.
  > 许多读者表示深深认同主人公那种疏离感，因为他们自己也曾在异乡经历过类似的归属困境。

---

**47. in short** *discourse marker*

- **英文释义**：Used to introduce a brief summary of what has just been said; "to put it briefly."
- **中文释义**：简而言之；总之；换句话说
- **语域**：书面 / 口语 / 正式与非正式均可
- **拓展**：常见话语标记（discourse markers）对比：*in short / in brief / to sum up / in a nutshell（口语化）/ to put it succinctly（正式）*。在写作与演讲中，*in short* 之后应紧跟核心观点，切忌用于引出冗长段落。
- **例句**：The proposal demanded a complete overhaul of the tax code, the elimination of agricultural subsidies, and a restructuring of the national pension system. **In short**, it was politically toxic.
  > 这份提案要求彻底重构税法体系、取消农业补贴并对全国养老金制度进行重组。简而言之，这在政治上是毒药。

---

**48. as you can probably imagine** *hedging / engagement expression*

- **英文释义**：A phrase used to invite the reader to empathize with an experience while acknowledging the limits of their direct knowledge.
- **中文释义**：你大概可以想象；你可想而知
- **语域**：口语化书面 / 新闻特写 / 说服性写作
- **拓展**：此类"读者代入短语"（reader engagement phrases）是新闻软文写作的重要技巧，包括：*as you might expect / as one might imagine / needless to say / it goes without saying*。注意在学术写作中应慎用，以避免显得过于主观。
- **例句**："**As you can probably imagine**, negotiating a trade agreement between 27 sovereign states is rarely a swift or straightforward process," the minister told reporters.
  > "你们大概可以想象，在27个主权国家之间谈判达成一项贸易协议，从来不是一件迅速或简单的事，"这位部长告诉记者。

---

**49. nothing compared to** *comparative idiom*

- **英文释义**：Used to express that something is far inferior to or less significant than something else.
- **中文释义**：与……相比根本不算什么；远不及……；望尘莫及
- **语域**：口语 / 新闻 / 比较性写作
- **拓展**：对应正式写作表达：*pale in comparison to / be no match for / fall far short of*。文中 *Tesla's Gigafactories are nothing compared to this tourist hotspot* 是典型的新媒体写作风格——用口语化对比句制造强烈阅读效果。
- **例句**：The initial funding round raised $10 million — **nothing compared to** the $2 billion the company eventually secured in its pre-IPO round five years later.
  > 初始融资轮融资1000万美元——与该公司五年后在IPO前融资轮最终获得的20亿美元相比，简直不值一提。

---

**50. on a completely elevated level** *informal intensifier phrase*

- **英文释义**：Far superior in quality, scale, or sophistication; on a whole different tier.
- **中文释义**：完全处于更高的层次；不在一个段位上；高出不止一个档次
- **语域**：非正式 / 口语化新媒体写作
- **地道拓展**：类似表达：*on a whole other level / in a league of its own / in a class apart / leagues ahead*。在正式写作中对应：*far superior to / markedly more sophisticated than / of an entirely different magnitude*。
- **例句**：The chef's tasting menu wasn't just better than the competition — it was **on a completely elevated level**, combining molecular techniques with hyper-local sourcing in ways no one had attempted before.
  > 这位主厨的品鉴菜单不仅仅是优于竞争者——它完全处于另一个层次，将分子料理技术与极致本地化食材采购相结合，达到了前所未有的高度。

---

**51. catch up in** / **caught up in** *phrasal verb*

- **英文释义**：To be so involved in an activity or situation that you do not notice other things, often to a negative or excessive degree.
- **中文释义**：陷入；被……所左右；过分沉溺于
- **语域**：口语 / 书面均可
- **拓展**：区分：*catch up with（追上/叙旧）* vs. *be/get caught up in（被卷入/沉溺于）*。后者多含轻微负面意味，暗示当事人忽视了更重要的事物。文中 *too caught up in the wrong things* 即此义。
- **例句**：Many entrepreneurs become so **caught up in** the day-to-day operations of their businesses that they lose sight of the long-term strategic vision.
  > 许多创业者深陷于企业日常运营事务，以至于渐渐迷失了长远的战略眼光。

---

**52. on offer** *British English phrase*

- **英文释义**：Available to be chosen, bought, or used; being presented for consideration.
- **中文释义**：可供选择的；提供的；在售的
- **语域**：书面 / 英式英语 / 正式偏口语化
- **拓展**：*on offer* 为英式英语，美式英语对应 *available / on sale / offered*。文中 *what Xiaomi has on offer* = "小米所提供的（一切）"。在英式商业写作和新闻中极为高频。
- **例句**：The university's counseling center has expanded the mental health services **on offer**, including both in-person and digital therapy options for students.
  > 该大学心理咨询中心已扩大了所提供的心理健康服务，为学生新增了面对面及数字治疗两种选择。

### 4D. 金句积累

---

**金句一（文章二）**

> **原文：** "I saw an iridescent biosphere teeming with life. I didn't see the economy."

> **中文译文：** "我看到的是一个闪耀着七彩光芒、生机盎然的生物圈。我没有看到经济。"

> **写作价值：** 这是一组极具张力的对比句（juxtaposition），以简短的否定句直接回应前一句的壮丽描述，形成强烈的修辞落差。适用于环境议题、经济优先级批评或文明反思类写作的开篇或结尾，可直接引用或化用。

---

**金句二（文章二）**

> **原文：** "Since our human-made systems treat everything, including the very life-support systems of our planet, as the wholly owned subsidiary of the global economy, it's obvious from the vantage point of space that we're living a lie."

> **中文译文：** "由于我们人类所建立的体系将一切——包括地球本身的生命维系系统——都视作全球经济的全资子公司，从太空的视角来看，我们正活在一个谎言之中，这一点显而易见。"

> **写作价值：** 这是一个结构严密的长句，包含了插入语（including the very life-support systems of our planet）、商业隐喻（wholly owned subsidiary）与视角论证（from the vantage point of space）三重修辞手法。适合在论述经济发展与生态保护关系的议论文中作为权威引语使用。

---

**金句三（文章一）**

> **原文：** "The tours are meticulously planned and timed, like a beautifully choreographed ballet — and speaking of ballet, there's the chance to lay your eyes on the dance of over 700 robots."

> **中文译文：** "参观流程经过精心策划与计时，宛如一出精彩绝伦的芭蕾舞表演——说到芭蕾，你还有机会亲眼目睹700余台机器人共同演绎的'机械之舞'。"

> **写作价值：** 这是一个利用隐喻（metaphor）与过渡词的双关技法（"speaking of ballet"兼具承上启下与语意转接功能）的优美段落。将工业场景与艺术美感并置，体现了特写写作（feature writing）中感性化表达技术现象的高级手法，适合作为描写科技场景、展示写作灵活性的范例。

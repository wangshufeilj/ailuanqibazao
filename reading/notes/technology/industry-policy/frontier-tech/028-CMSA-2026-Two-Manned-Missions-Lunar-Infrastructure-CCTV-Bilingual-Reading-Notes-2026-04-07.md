## 正文（中英对照）

🔹 **2026年计划实施2次载人飞行任务 全力推进登月配套设施建设**  
🔸 **Two Manned Spaceflight Missions Planned for 2026 as Construction of Lunar Landing Supporting Facilities Gains Full Momentum**

🔹 据中国载人航天工程办公室消息，2026年，中国载人航天工程将深入贯彻落实“**十五五**”规划部署，在新起点上深化推进**空间站应用与发展**和**载人月球探测**两大任务，努力为加快建设**航天强国**作出更大贡献。  
🔸 According to the China Manned Space Agency (CMSA), in 2026, China's manned space program will thoroughly implement the deployments of the **15th Five-Year Plan**, deepening the two major missions of **space station application and development** and **manned lunar exploration** from a new starting point, striving to contribute more to the accelerated building of a **leading space power**.

🔹 目前，中国空间站在轨运行稳定、效益发挥良好；载人月球探测工程登月阶段任务各项研制建设进展顺利，取得多项阶段性突破。  
🔸 At present, the China Space Station (CSS) is operating stably in orbit with significant benefits being realized; various research and construction tasks for the **manned lunar landing stage** are progressing smoothly, achieving multiple phased breakthroughs.

🔹 进入空间站应用与发展阶段以来，工程全线密切协同，先后圆满完成**6次载人飞行**、4次货运补给、7次飞船返回任务，成功实施首次**应急发射**。  
🔸 Since entering the application and development stage of the space station, all sectors of the program have collaborated closely, successfully completing **six manned missions**, four cargo resupply missions, and seven spacecraft return missions, while also successfully executing the first **emergency launch**.

🔹 6个航天员乘组、18人次在轨长期驻留，累计进行13次航天员出舱和多次应用载荷出舱，开展多次舱外维修任务，刷新航天员**单次出舱活动时长**的世界纪录。  
🔸 Six astronaut crews totaling 18 person-times have completed long-term stays in orbit; a total of 13 extravehicular activities (EVAs) and multiple payload deployments have been conducted, alongside various external maintenance tasks, setting a new world record for the **duration of a single EVA**.

🔹 完成包括**港澳载荷专家**在内的第四批预备航天员选拔、低成本货物运输系统择优并启动研制等工作。  
🔸 The selection of the **fourth batch of reserve astronauts**, including **payload specialists from Hong Kong and Macau**, has been completed, and the competitive selection and development of a low-cost cargo transport system have been initiated.

🔹 目前，中国空间站已在轨部署和实施**267项**科学与应用项目，涉及空间生命科学与人体研究、微重力物理和空间新技术等领域，取得多项国际领先的应用与技术成果。  
🔸 Currently, **267 scientific and application projects** have been deployed and implemented on the CSS, covering fields such as space life sciences, human research, microgravity physics, and new space technologies, yielding numerous world-leading application and technical achievements.

🔹 部分成果已实现转移转化和推广应用，显著推动我国空间科学与应用快速发展。  
🔸 Some of these achievements have already been transferred and applied, significantly driving the rapid development of China's **space science and applications**.

🔹 2026年，计划实施**2次载人飞行任务**、1次货运飞船补给任务。  
🔸 In 2026, the program plans to execute **two manned spaceflight missions** and one cargo resupply mission.

🔹 来自港澳地区的航天员有望最早于今年执行空间站飞行任务，**神舟二十三号**飞行乘组1名航天员将开展**一年期驻留试验**。  
🔸 Astronauts from the Hong Kong and Macau regions are expected to carry out space station missions as early as this year, and one astronaut from the **Shenzhou-23** crew will conduct a **one-year residency experiment**.

🔹 瞄准**2030年前**实现中国人首次登陆月球的目标，载人月球探测工程登月阶段任务各项研制建设工作正在扎实稳步推进。  
🔸 Aiming for the goal of realizing the **first Chinese lunar landing before 2030**, various research and construction efforts for the manned lunar exploration stage are advancing steadily and solidly.

🔹 截至目前，**长征十号**运载火箭、**梦舟**载人飞船、**揽月**月面着陆器等主要飞行产品研制进展顺利。  
🔸 To date, the development of key flight hardware, including the **Long March 10** carrier rocket, the **Mengzhou** manned spacecraft, and the **Lanyue** lunar lander, has been progressing smoothly.

🔹 已陆续完成梦舟载人飞船零高度逃逸、揽月着陆器着陆起飞、长征十号运载火箭系留点火、长征十号运载火箭系统低空演示验证与梦舟载人飞船系统**最大动压逃逸飞行**等大型试验。  
🔸 Major tests have been completed successively, including the zero-altitude escape of the Mengzhou spacecraft, the landing and takeoff of the Lanyue lander, the captive firing of the Long March 10 rocket, and the low-altitude demonstration and **maximum dynamic pressure escape flight** of the integrated rocket and spacecraft systems.

🔹 2026年，将全力推进**文昌航天发射场**登月任务相关配套设施设备建设，以及测控通信、着陆场等地面支持系统各项目建设工作。  
🔸 In 2026, the program will go all out to advance the construction of supporting facilities for the lunar mission at the **Wenchang Spacecraft Launch Site**, as well as the development of ground support systems such as tracking, telemetry, command, and landing sites.

🔹 工程立项实施以来，始终秉持“**和平利用、平等互利、共同发展**”的原则，积极促进载人航天领域国际合作交流。  
🔸 Since its inception, the program has consistently upheld the principles of "**peaceful use, equality, mutual benefit, and common development**," actively promoting international cooperation and exchange in the field of manned spaceflight.

🔹 去年，中巴两国签署了选拔训练航天员合作协议，目前选拔工作进展顺利。  
🔸 Last year, China and Pakistan signed a cooperation agreement on the selection and training of astronauts, and the selection process is currently progressing smoothly.

🔹 根据飞行任务规划安排，后续将有1名**巴基斯坦航天员**以载荷专家身份执行短期飞行任务，在中国空间站开展巴方科学实验等工作。  
🔸 According to the mission schedule, a **Pakistani astronaut** will subsequently perform a short-term flight mission as a payload specialist to conduct scientific experiments on the CSS.

🔹 此外，中国载人航天工程办公室将持续推进与**联合国外空司**合作项目实施。  
🔸 Furthermore, the CMSA will continue to advance the implementation of cooperative projects with the **United Nations Office for Outer Space Affairs (UNOOSA)**.

🔹 中国始终欢迎世界同行参与共享中国载人航天发展成果，携手推动世界航天技术发展，为**和平利用太空、造福全人类**作出新的更大贡献。  
🔸 China consistently welcomes international counterparts to participate in and share the achievements of its manned space development, working together to advance global space technology and make new, greater contributions to the **peaceful use of outer space for the benefit of all mankind**.

## 三、中英文对照概要

**[EN]** China's AI chip market underwent a structural realignment in 2025. According to IDC data reviewed by Reuters, domestic chipmakers collectively captured nearly 41% of China's AI accelerator server market—a figure that would have seemed improbable just two years earlier. Nvidia, though retaining its leadership with a 55% share and approximately 2.2 million cards shipped, has seen its grip on what was once a captive market loosen markedly. Among Chinese vendors, Huawei led by a wide margin, shipping roughly 812,000 AI chips—about half of domestically branded volumes. Alibaba's T-Head ranked second; Baidu's Kunlunxin and Cambricon tied for third at similar shipment levels; Hygon, MetaX, and Iluvatar CoreX accounted for smaller, incremental shares. The catalyst is as much geopolitical as technological: successive rounds of U.S. export controls, combined with central and local government mandates to "buy Chinese," have created both the demand signal and the policy scaffolding that domestic vendors needed to scale. The article is a data-dense snapshot of a market in transition—one where the competitive landscape is being reshaped not purely by silicon performance, but by trade policy and state procurement.

**【中文】** 2025年，中国AI芯片市场经历了一场结构性重塑。据路透社审阅的IDC数据显示，国内芯片厂商合计占据中国AI加速器服务器市场约41%的份额——这一数字在两年前还几乎难以想象。英伟达尽管以约220万张卡的出货量和55%的市占率维持领先地位，但其对这一曾经「囊中之物」的市场的掌控已明显松动。华为在国内厂商中遥遥领先，出货约81.2万颗AI芯片，约占国产品牌出货量的一半；阿里巴巴平头哥位居第二；百度昆仑芯与寒武纪并列第三、出货量接近；海光、沐曦、天数智芯等则占据相对较小的增量份额。这场变局的驱动力既是技术层面的，更是地缘政治层面的：美国出口管制的持续收紧，加之中央和地方政府「优先使用国产」的采购导向，共同为国内厂商规模化发展提供了需求信号和政策支撑。这篇报道是一幅市场转型中的数据快照——竞争格局的重塑，靠的不只是芯片性能，更是贸易政策和国家采购的力量。

> 语域：新闻/商业。  
> 动词变化：claimed, claiming.  
> 拓展内容：**Synonyms**: seize, occupy, acquire. **Antonym**: forfeit, lose. **常见搭配**: claim a share of the market (占据市场份额), claim a victory (夺取胜利). **考试考点**: 在雅思写作中可代替 "take up" 或 "account for" 来描述市场份额的占领，更具动感和竞争意味。
>
> **shrink** (v.): To become or to make something smaller in amount, size, or value. [缩减，萎缩]  
> 语域：通用/经济。  
> 动词变化：shrank (过去式), shrunk (过去分词).  
> 拓展内容：**Synonyms**: contract, diminish, dwindle. **Antonym**: expand, swell. **用法要点**: 常用于描述市场份额 (market share)、利润 (profit) 或经济 (economy) 的缩小。注意其不规则变形，过去式是 shrank 而非 shrinked。

> 拓展内容：**Synonyms**: enterprise, corporation, venture. **辨析**: firm 通常指规模较小的合伙制公司（如律所、会计师事务所），但在新闻中常作为 company 的同义替换词以避免重复。

### W — 写作高频词

---

**1. erode**
- **音标**：/ɪˈroʊd/ *v.*
- **英文释义**：To gradually reduce or destroy something; to wear away by the action of water, wind, or other forces
- **中文释义**：（逐渐）侵蚀；削弱；磨损
- **语域**：新闻/学术/书面
- **同义词**：undermine, wear away, diminish, corrode, chip away at
- **反义词**：strengthen, bolster, reinforce, build up
- **拓展**：
  - 不规则变化：erode / eroded / eroded（规则）
  - 名词形式：**erosion**（不可数，土壤侵蚀；引申义：权威/信心的逐渐丧失）
  - 常见搭配：**erode confidence / erode market share / erode trust / erode the foundation of sth**
  - 写作提示：该词由地质学术语引申为抽象语境中的"逐渐削弱"，在新闻和学术写作中极为高频，尤其用于描述市场份额、权威、公信力等的渐进式衰减，比 "reduce" 更具动态感和过程感。
- **例句**：
  - Decades of corruption have slowly **eroded** public **trust** in the judicial system.（数十年的腐败现象已逐渐侵蚀了公众对司法体系的信任。）

---

**2. dominant / dominance**
- **音标**：/ˈdɑːmɪnənt/ *adj.*；/ˈdɑːmɪnəns/ *n.*
- **英文释义**：Having or exerting control or power over others; most important, powerful, or influential
- **中文释义**：主导的；占支配地位的；**dominance**：主导地位，支配力
- **语域**：新闻/学术/商业书面
- **同义词**：leading, prevailing, paramount, preponderant
- **反义词**：subordinate, minor, peripheral
- **拓展**：
  - 动词形式：**dominate**（v. 主导、支配）
  - 常见搭配：**market dominance / dominant position / dominant player / dominant share**
  - 区分：**dominant**（形容词，表状态）vs. **predominant**（形容词，强调"数量上占多数"或"最普遍的"，如 *a predominant view*）
- **例句**：
  - The company's **dominant position** in the smartphone market has come under increasing scrutiny from antitrust regulators.（该公司在智能手机市场的主导地位正受到反垄断监管机构越来越多的审视。）

---

**3. capture (market share)**
- **音标**：/ˈkæptʃər/ *v.*
- **英文释义**：To succeed in getting control or a share of something, especially in a competitive context
- **中文释义**：占据；夺得；赢得（市场份额）
- **语域**：商业/新闻/书面
- **同义词**：seize, claim, secure, win, garner
- **拓展**：
  - 常见搭配：**capture market share / capture a significant portion / capture the imagination**
  - 注意：**capture** 在商业语境中常作及物动词，后接市场、份额、机会等抽象名词，语感积极主动。
- **例句**：
  - The startup managed to **capture** a **30% share** of the domestic cloud storage market within just three years of its launch.（该初创公司在成立短短三年内便赢得了国内云存储市场30%的份额。）

---

**4. accelerator**
- **音标**：/əkˈseləreɪtər/ *n.*（可数）
- **英文释义**：(1) A device or substance that increases speed or rate of reaction; (2) In computing: a chip or hardware component designed to speed up specific types of computation, especially AI/ML workloads
- **中文释义**：加速器；（计算领域）AI加速芯片/硬件加速器
- **语域**：技术/新闻
- **同义词**（计算语境）：processor, compute unit, co-processor
- **拓展**：
  - 动词：**accelerate**（v. 加速）；名词：**acceleration**（n. 加速；提升）
  - 常见搭配：**AI accelerator / AI accelerator card / hardware accelerator / accelerator chip**
  - 商业用法延伸：**business accelerator**（商业加速器，帮助初创企业的孵化机构）
- **例句**：
  - Investment in **AI accelerator** chips surged globally as companies raced to build out large language model infrastructure.（随着企业竞相构建大语言模型基础设施，对AI加速芯片的全球投资大幅攀升。）

**5. retreat**
- **音标**：/rɪˈtriːt/ *n./v.*
- **英文释义**：(n.) A backward movement; a withdrawal from a position; a decline. (v.) To move backward; to withdraw
- **中文释义**：（名词）撤退；退缩；（商业语境）市占率/影响力的下降 ；（动词）撤退，退让
- **语域**：新闻/书面/军事（引申至商业）
- **同义词**（商业语境）：decline, withdrawal, pullback, setback
- **反义词**：advance, surge, expansion
- **拓展**：
  - 常见搭配：**a significant retreat / strategic retreat / mark a retreat for sb/sth**
  - 写作技巧："mark a significant retreat for..." 是新闻写作中描述市场地位下滑的高级表达，原文即使用 *"that figure marks a significant retreat for the U.S. chipmaker"*
- **例句**：
  - The latest quarterly figures **mark a significant retreat** for the once-unassailable retail giant, whose domestic sales fell by nearly a fifth.（最新季度数据标志着这家曾无人能撼动的零售巨头的明显退步，其国内销售额下滑近两成。）

**6. milestone**
- **音标**：/ˈmaɪlstoʊn/ *n.*（可数）
- **英文释义**：An important event or development that marks a significant stage in a process
- **中文释义**：里程碑；重要节点
- **语域**：新闻/商业/书面
- **同义词**：landmark, watershed, turning point, benchmark
- **拓展**：
  - 常见搭配：**a milestone that underscores... / reach a milestone / mark a milestone / historic milestone**

- **英文释义**：To make something stricter, more restrictive, or harder to bypass; to become more restrictive
- **中文释义**：收紧；加强（管控/限制）；趋严
- **语域**：新闻/政策/书面

**8. underscore**
- **音标**：/ˌʌndərˈskɔːr/ *v./n.*
- **英文释义**：(v.) To emphasize or highlight the importance or truth of something; (n.) An underline character (_)

- **语域**：书面/新闻/学术（正式）
- **同义词**：emphasize, highlight, underline, accentuate, drive home
- **拓展**：
  - 书面语中常见结构：**This underscores the need for... / a milestone that underscores how...**
  - 与 **underline** 区别：两者意思相近，**underscore** 略更正式，且在美式英语中更为普遍；**underline** 在英式中更常用。

- **中文释义**：隐含的；未明言的；心照不宣的
- **语域**：书面/学术/法律/新闻
- **同义词**：implied, unspoken, tacit, unstated
- **反义词**：explicit, express, stated, overt
- **拓展**：

  - 名词形式：**implication**（含义，暗示）；副词：**implicitly**
- **例句**：

**16. successive**

**17. carved out**
- **音标**：/kɑːrvd aʊt/ *phrasal verb*
- **英文释义**：To create or establish something through effort, especially a distinct position or share in a competitive field
- **中文释义**：开辟出；（通过努力）争取到；切割出（市场份额）
- **语域**：新闻/商业（地道表达）
- **同义词**：established, secured, claimed, obtained
- **拓展**：
  - **carve out a niche**（开辟细分市场）；**carve out a presence**（在市场中立足）；**carve out time**（挤出时间）
  - 翻译注意：不可直译为"雕刻出"，需根据语境灵活处理，商业场合通常译为"占据""争得""开辟出"
- **例句**：
  - The company has successfully **carved out a niche** in the luxury sustainable fashion segment, attracting environmentally conscious high-income consumers.（该公司已成功在奢侈可持续时装细分领域开辟出一席之地，吸引了注重环保的高收入消费者。）

**18. fill the void**
- **音标**：/fɪl ðə vɔɪd/ *phrase*
- **英文释义**：To replace something that is missing or has been removed; to step in where there is a gap
- **中文释义**：填补空缺；填补真空
- **语域**：新闻/书面/地道表达
- **同义词**：fill the gap, step into the breach, substitute for
- **拓展**：
  - 原文：*"fill the void left by tightening U.S. export controls"* ——此类"空缺+后定语"结构是英文新闻的固定表达方式
  - **void** 本义"空虚；无效"，在此语境中作"真空/空缺"讲
- **例句**：

**19. emerge as**

- **例句**：

**20. mark a retreat**
- **音标**：/mɑːrk ə rɪˈtriːt/ *phrase*
- **英文释义**：To signify or represent a notable decline or withdrawal from a previously held position
- **中文释义**：标志着（某种）退步/退让/下滑
- **语域**：新闻/书面（正式）
- **拓展**：
  - 原文：*"that figure marks a significant retreat for the U.S. chipmaker"*
  - **mark** 在此作"标志着、意味着"，是新闻写作常见用法（区别于"做标记"的基本义）
  - 类似结构：**mark a turning point / mark a milestone / mark a departure from**
- **例句**：

**21. account for**
- **音标**：/əˈkaʊnt fər/ *phrasal verb*
- **英文释义**：(1) To constitute or make up a proportion; (2) To explain the reason for something
- **中文释义**：（释义1）占（比例/份额）；（释义2）说明……的原因
- **语域**：学术/商业/新闻（通用）
- **拓展**：
  - 原文用法为释义1：*"accounting for 41% of the total market"*

  - 常见搭配：**account for a significant portion / account for the discrepancy / what accounts for the difference**
- **例句（释义1）**：

### 7D. 金句积累

**金句一**
> *"Chinese vendors collectively shipped 1.65 million cards, accounting for 41% of the total market — a milestone that underscores how aggressively domestic players have moved to fill the void left by tightening U.S. export controls."*

**中文翻译：**
中国厂商合计出货165万张卡，占总市场的41%——这是一个里程碑，深刻揭示了国内厂商以何等积极进取的姿态，填补了美国出口管制持续收紧所留下的市场空白。

> ✍️ **写作价值**：该句结构精良，由数据引出结论，以破折号追加评价，最后用定语从句点明因果，是"数据+判断+背景"三层递进的新闻写作范式，可直接仿写用于市场分析类议论文。

---

**金句二**
> *"The gains come as Beijing grows increasingly cautious about dependence on foreign chips, pushing government agencies and companies to adopt domestic alternatives after successive waves of U.S. export controls cut China off from Nvidia's most advanced products."*

**中文翻译：**
上述进展背后，是北京对依赖外国芯片的日益警觉——在美国出口管制一轮接一轮地切断中国获取英伟达最先进产品的渠道之后，政府开始推动政府机构和企业转向国产替代方案。

> ✍️ **写作价值**：这句话展示了如何用一个复合句同时呈现"结果—原因—背景"三层逻辑，尤其 *"cut China off from..."* 是描述"断供/脱钩"的地道新闻表达，极具迁移价值。

---

**金句三**
> *"Nvidia remained the market leader, shipping around 2.2 million cards and holding a 55% share. But that figure marks a significant retreat for the U.S. chipmaker, which held a dominant market share in China's AI chip market."*

**中文翻译：**
英伟达仍是市场领导者，出货约220万张卡，持有55%的份额。但这一数字标志着这家美国芯片制造商的明显退步——它曾在中国AI芯片市场拥有绝对主导地位。

> ✍️ **写作价值**：先扬后抑的叙事结构（先确认现状，再用"But"转折揭示深层含义），是新闻与学术写作中呈现"相对衰退"的经典句式。*"marks a significant retreat"* 可广泛迁移至任何描述地位或影响力下滑的场景。

**3. 写作素材积累 (AI与版权话题):**
*   **论点:** AI crawling without compensation undermines the business model of the internet. (未经补偿的AI抓取破坏了互联网的商业模式。)
*   **论据:** Monopolistic tech giants bundle services (like search and AI scraping) to force compliance from creators. (垄断科技巨头捆绑服务，强迫创作者服从。)
*   **对策:** Regulation and licensing deals are essential for a sustainable digital ecosystem. (监管和许可协议对于可持续的数字生态系统至关重要。)

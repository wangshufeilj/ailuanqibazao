## 逐句精读

🔹 Anthropic is prepping for an IPO that could come as early as 2026, the FT reports.  
🔸 据英国《金融时报》报道，**Anthropic 正在为首次公开募股做准备，这一步骤可能最早在 2026 年进行**。

🔹 It has brought on law firm Wilson Sonsini to help kick off the process, and the company is tackling an internal checklist to prepare it for what could be one of the largest IPOs ever.  
🔸 该公司聘请了律师事务所 Wilson Sonsini 协助启动此流程，并正在逐项落实内部清单，为**可能成为规模最大的 IPO 之一做好准备**。

🔹 The company is also reportedly looking to raise a funding round that could value it at over $300 billion and has also been in talks with investment banks, though it has not chosen an underwriter, the FT reported.  
🔸 据《金融时报》报道，该公司还在寻求进行融资，**估值可能超过 3000 亿美元**，并与投资银行进行了磋商，但尚未选定承销商。

🔹 Anthropic last announced a $13 billion raise in September, giving it a $183 billion valuation.  
🔸 Anthropic 上次于 9 月宣布融资 130 亿美元，当时的估值为**1830 亿美元**。

🔹 Wilson Sonsini has been an adviser to Anthropic since 2022.  
🔸 Wilson Sonsini 自 2022 年以来一直是 Anthropic 的顾问。

🔹 This news comes as OpenAI, valued at $500 billion, is also reportedly testing the waters for an IPO and has started to prep itself for the process, though no listing date has been suggested, Reuters reported.  
🔸 据路透社报道，这一消息发布之际，**估值 5000 亿美元的 OpenAI 也在为 IPO 试水**，并已开始为这一过程做准备，但尚未确定上市日期。

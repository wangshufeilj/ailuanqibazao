<strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">1. impose</strong> /ɪmˈpoʊz/ *v.*

- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：To officially force something, especially a rule, tax, punishment, etc., to be obeyed or paid *(Longman)*
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：（正式）强制推行；征收（税款、惩罚等）
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：正式 / 新闻 / 法律
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">同义词</strong>：levy, enforce, exact, inflict
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">反义词</strong>：lift, remove, abolish, repeal
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">常见词组</strong>：
  - impose tariffs/sanctions/restrictions/fines/penalties
  - impose a ban on sth.
  - impose one's views on sb.（将观点强加于人）
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 名词形式：imposition（征收；强加）
  - 注意区分：impose <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">on/upon</strong> sb.（对某人强制实施）/ impose sth. <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">on</strong> sb.（向某人征收/强加某物）
  - 在贸易语境中，impose tariffs（加征关税）为超高频搭配，在WTO文书、判决书及新闻报道中极常见。
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > The WTO panel ruled that the bloc had unlawfully <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">imposed</strong> countervailing duties on imports from developing countries.
  > 世贸组织专家组裁定，该经济体对来自发展中国家的进口产品非法征收了反补贴税。

<strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">2. sweeping</strong> /ˈswiːpɪŋ/ *adj.*

- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：Affecting many things or people, or making an important difference; broad and wide-ranging *(Longman)*
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：广泛的；大刀阔斧的；影响深远的
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：书面 / 新闻 / 正式
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">同义词</strong>：wide-ranging, extensive, comprehensive, far-reaching, broad
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">反义词</strong>：narrow, limited, targeted, modest
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">常见词组</strong>：

- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：

<strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">3. challenge</strong> /ˈtʃælɪndʒ/ *n. / v.*

- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：*(n.)* Something that tests strength, skill, or ability; *(v., legal)* to question the truth, legality, or right of something; to formally dispute *(Cambridge)*

- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：通用 / 法律 / 新闻

- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">常见词组</strong>：
  - challenge a law / ruling / tariff / decision
  - mount a challenge against sth.（发起挑战）
  - face a legal challenge
  - challenge sb. to do sth.
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 在法律新闻中，challenge 作动词几乎成为"提起诉讼质疑某法律/命令效力"的专用表达，比 "sue over" 更精准地传达法律层面的争议性。
  - 注意 challenging 作形容词意为"具有挑战性的"，与 challenge 本义有所延伸。
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > Hundreds of companies have gone to court to <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">challenge</strong> the legality of the executive orders, arguing they exceed the president's statutory authority.

<strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">4. legality</strong> /lɪˈɡæləti/ *n.* [U]

- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：The fact of being allowed by law; the quality or state of conforming to the law *(Longman AWL)*
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：合法性；依法性
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：法律 / 正式 / 学术
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">同义词</strong>：lawfulness, legitimacy, validity
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">反义词</strong>：illegality, unlawfulness
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">常见词组</strong>：
  - question/challenge/doubt the legality of sth.
  - rule on the legality of sth.（就……合法性作出裁定）
  - the legality of a contract / measure / order
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 词族：legal (adj.) → legality (n.) → legalize (v.) → legalization (n.) → legally (adv.) → illegality (n.)
  - legality 为不可数名词（表抽象概念），但 legalities 作复数时表示"依法须履行的手续/程序"（例：We need to deal with the legalities before signing.）
  - 辨析：legality（是否符合法律规定）vs. legitimacy（是否具有正当性，更广泛，可含道德维度）
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > The court was asked to rule on the <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">legality</strong> of the emergency measures before they could take effect.

<strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">5. authorize</strong> /ˈɔːθəraɪz/ *v.*

- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：To give official permission for something, or to give someone the power to do something *(Longman)*
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：授权；批准；正式许可
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：正式 / 法律 / 行政
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">同义词</strong>：empower, sanction, permit, approve, entitle
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">反义词</strong>：prohibit, forbid, disallow, revoke

  - unauthorized（未经授权的）
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 名词形式：authorization（授权；批准）
  - 本文核心法律争点正是：IEEPA是否 authorizes（授权）总统征关税。最终最高法院认定"does not authorize"（不授权）。

- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：

<strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">6. seek</strong> /siːk/ *v.* （不规则变化：seek – sought – sought）

- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：To try to find or obtain something; to ask for something in a formal context *(Longman)*
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：寻求；谋求；请求（正式）

- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">反义词</strong>：abandon, relinquish
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">常见词组</strong>：

<strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">7. remedy</strong> /ˈremədi/ *n. [C] / v.*

<strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">10. tariff</strong> /ˈtærɪf/ *n.* [C]

- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：A tax on goods coming into or going out of a country *(Longman)*
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：关税；进出口税
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：新闻 / 正式 / 经济
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">同义词</strong>：duty, customs duty, import tax, levy
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">常见词组</strong>：

  - tariff on imports / goods
  - protective / punitive / retaliatory / reciprocal tariff
  - tariff reform / barrier / wall
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：

<strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">11. levy</strong> /ˈlevi/ *n.* [C] / *v.*

- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：（名）税款；征收额；（动）征收（税款、罚款等）
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：正式 / 法律 / 新闻
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">同义词</strong>：tax, duty, charge, impost; *(v.)* impose, charge, collect
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">常见词组</strong>：
  - levy a tax / fine / charge on sth.
  - a levy on imports / goods
  - capital levy（资本税）
  - payroll levy（薪资税）
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 动词不规则变化：levy → levied → levied（第三人称单数：levies）
  - 名词可数（a levy, levies）
  - 本文中 "levies" 指已缴纳的关税款项，为 tariff 的近义替换，是新闻写作中避免重复的常见手法。
  - 重要语感区分：levy 强调"征收"的动作，而 tariff/duty 更强调税款本身的种类。
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：

<strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">16. statute</strong> /ˈstætʃuːt/ *n.* [C/U]

- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：A law passed by a parliament, council, etc., and formally written down; also, a formal rule of an institution *(Longman)*
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：成文法；法规；法令；（机构的）章程
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：法律 / 正式 / 学术
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">同义词</strong>：law, act, legislation, ordinance, enactment
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">反义词</strong>：common law（判例法，与成文法相对）
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">常见词组</strong>：
  - laid down by statute（由法律规定）
  - on the statute book（已成为法律）
  - statute of limitations（诉讼时效/追诉时效）
  - statutory（形容词形式：法定的）
  - statutory rights（法定权利）
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 词源：来自拉丁语 *statutum*（"所设立之规则"），与 state、status 同根
  - 可数（a statute, statutes）；但 "by statute" 通常不加冠词
  - 辨析：statute（成文法，议会立法）vs. regulation（行政法规/细则，行政机关制定）vs. common law（法官造法，判例法）
  - statute law = statutory law（成文法整体）
  - 本文核心论点：IEEPA 作为一部 statute，其条文中并未出现 "tariff" 一词，故不能作为征收关税的法律依据。
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > The court held that the president's action lacked any basis in <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">statute</strong> and therefore constituted an unconstitutional exercise of executive power.
  > 法院认定总统的行为缺乏任何成文法依据，因此构成对行政权力的违宪行使。

---

<strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">17. unprecedented</strong> /ʌnˈpresɪdentɪd/ *adj.*

- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：Never having happened before, or never having happened so much *(Longman AWL)*
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：史无前例的；空前的；前所未有的
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：正式 / 书面 / 新闻 / 学术
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">同义词</strong>：unparalleled, unrivaled, novel, extraordinary, groundbreaking
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">反义词</strong>：routine, typical, ordinary, precedented（罕用）
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">常见词组</strong>：
  - unprecedented in + 领域/时间段
  - on an unprecedented scale（以空前的规模）
  - take the unprecedented step of doing sth.（采取空前举措）
  - unprecedented demand / growth / challenge / ruling
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 词族：precedent (n.) → unprecedented (adj.) → unprecedentedly (adv.)
  - precedent（先例）本身在法律中极为重要：set a precedent（树立先例），follow a precedent（遵循先例），without precedent（无先例可循）
  - BYD 作为首家提起此类诉讼的中国车企，其行动可称 "unprecedented"
  - 注意搭配：不说 ~~very unprecedented~~，应用 absolutely / truly / virtually unprecedented
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > The volume of IEEPA tariff challenges filed by importers was <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">unprecedented</strong> in the history of the U.S. Court of International Trade.
  > 进口商就IEEPA关税提起挑战的诉讼数量，在美国国际贸易法院的历史上是空前的。

<strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">18. enormous</strong> /ɪˈnɔːrməs/ *adj.*

- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：Very large in size or amount *(Longman AWL)*
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：巨大的；庞大的；极其重大的
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：通用 / 正式 / 书面
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">同义词</strong>：huge, immense, vast, colossal, tremendous
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">反义词</strong>：tiny, modest, negligible, minimal
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">常见词组</strong>：
  - an enormous amount/sum/number of
  - enormous stakes / implications / consequences / pressure
  - enormous effort / impact
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 词族：enormous (adj.) → enormously (adv.) → enormity (n.，表"极其严重性"或"滔天大罪"，注意不作"巨大"之意！)
  - ⚠️ 辨析陷阱：enormity 的主要含义是"（罪行、错误的）严重性；滔天大罪"，而非 "enormous size"（巨大体积），后者应用 immensity/enormousness。
  - 本文中 "enormous stakes"（极其重大的利害关系）为贸易法律新闻中的常见表达
  - 语法注意：不与 very 连用，用 absolutely enormous ✓
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > The <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">scale</strong> of the tariff dispute became clear as importers calculated refund claims running into hundreds of millions of dollars.
  > 随着进口商计算出高达数亿美元的退款索赔，关税纠纷所涉金额之巨变得清晰起来。

<strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">19. border tax</strong> *n. phr.*

- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：A tax levied on goods as they cross a national border, used interchangeably with "tariff" or "customs duty" in trade policy contexts *(Trade policy usage)*
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：边境税；关境税（即进口关税的政策性表述）
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：政策 / 新闻 / 正式
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">同义词</strong>：tariff, import duty, customs duty, border levy
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - "border tax" 是贸易政策讨论中的非技术性说法，常见于政客演讲和新闻报道，与 tariff 近义但更具政治色彩
  - 注意与 "carbon border adjustment mechanism"（碳边境调整机制，CBAM）区分，后者为欧盟特定政策术语
  - 翻译时可据语境译为"关税"（经济语境）或"边境税"（政策语境）
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > Critics argued that calling tariffs <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">border taxes</strong> was a rhetorical rebranding designed to obscure their protectionist nature.
  > 批评人士认为，将关税称为"边境税"是一种修辞包装，旨在掩盖其保护主义本质。

---

<strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">20. independent complaint</strong> *n. phr.*

- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：A separate legal action filed by a party to preserve its own legal rights, distinct from broader litigation in which it may also participate *(Legal usage)*
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：独立诉状；自主申诉（为保留自身法律救济权利而单独提起的诉讼）
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：法律
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 本文中 BYD 解释其提起"independent complaint"的原因：参加集体诉讼或等待他人案件结果不足以保障其退税权利，必须自行提起以"protect its ability to be refunded"
  - 翻译要点："independent"在此并非"独立自主"的价值判断，而是"单独的、自成一体的"程序概念，可译"独立诉讼"或"单独申诉"
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > Without filing an <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">independent complaint</strong>, BYD risked being time-barred from seeking a refund even if the broader IEEPA challenges succeeded.
  > 如果不提起独立诉讼，即便更广泛的IEEPA挑战最终胜诉，比亚迪也可能因超过时效而丧失申请退税的权利。

---

<strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">21. commercial vehicles</strong> *n. phr.*

- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：Vehicles used for transporting goods or passengers as part of a business, including trucks, buses, and vans, as distinct from passenger cars *(General usage)*
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：商用车辆（包括卡车、公共汽车、货车等，有别于乘用车）
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：商业 / 行业 / 法律（海关分类）
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">同义词</strong>：commercial motor vehicles, freight vehicles, heavy-duty vehicles
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">反义词</strong>：passenger cars（乘用车）
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">常见词组</strong>：
  - commercial vehicle regulations（商用车法规）
  - light/heavy commercial vehicle（轻/重型商用车）
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 翻译时注意：不同语境下 "commercial vehicle" 涵盖范围不同。在美国海关分类中，buses（公共汽车）与 trucks（卡车）均属 commercial vehicles，可享受不同于乘用车的关税税率，因此本文中BYD在美国运营的商用车业务与其不在美销售乘用车这一事实，共同构成其诉讼的重要背景。
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > Although the automaker does not sell <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">commercial vehicles</strong> in some markets, its extensive fleet of buses and trucks in North America generated significant cross-border trade activity.
  > 尽管该汽车制造商在某些市场不销售商用车，但其在北美拥有的大量公共汽车和卡车车队产生了大量跨境贸易活动。

---

<strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">22. energy storage systems</strong> *n. phr.*

- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：Technologies that store energy for later use, including battery-based systems for grid or industrial applications *(Technical/industry usage)*
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：储能系统（用于电网或工业应用的能源存储技术）
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：行业 / 技术 / 新闻
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">同义词</strong>：battery storage systems, ESS（缩写）, grid storage
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 在中美贸易摩擦语境中，energy storage systems 和 solar panels 均是双方关税博弈的重要产品类别，属于战略性新兴产业
  - 翻译时注意区分：energy storage（储能，泛指）vs. battery energy storage system (BESS)（电池储能系统，具体技术）
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > The tariffs on <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">energy storage systems</strong> and solar panels directly affected BYD's non-automotive business operations in the United States.
  > 针对储能系统和太阳能板征收的关税直接影响了比亚迪在美国的非汽车业务运营。

<strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">23. court documents</strong> *n. phr.*

- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：Official papers filed with or issued by a court, including complaints, motions, orders, and judgments *(Legal usage)*
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：法庭文件；司法文书（包括诉状、动议、法院命令及判决等）
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：法律 / 新闻
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">常见词组</strong>：
  - according to court documents（根据法庭文件）
  - filed at / with the court（向法院提交的文件）
  - court records（法庭记录）
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 新闻写作中，"court documents show" 是引用法律信源的标准句式，相当于"诉讼材料显示"
  - 翻译时注意：court documents ≠ court rulings（法院裁决）；前者包括当事方提交的文件，后者专指法院的裁决
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > The details of BYD's legal arguments were revealed through <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">court documents</strong> unsealed by the U.S. Court of International Trade.
  > 比亚迪法律论点的细节，通过美国国际贸易法院解封的<strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">法庭文件</strong>得以披露。

<strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">24. U.S. soil</strong> *n. phr.*

- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：The territory of the United States; used figuratively to mean "within the United States" *(Idiomatic/formal)*
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：美国本土；美国领土（常用于政治、外交或产业语境）
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：政治 / 新闻 / 正式（带修辞色彩）
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">同义词</strong>：American territory, the United States, domestic (U.S.) land
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - "on U.S. soil" 是英语新闻写作中表达"在美国境内（生产/运营/发生）"的习惯性表达，带有一定政治/民族主义色彩，常出现在讨论外国企业是否应在本国建厂的语境中
  - 类似表达：on Chinese/European/home soil
  - 翻译时，"build cars on U.S. soil"（在美国本土造车）比直译"在美国土地上"更自然
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > Several foreign automakers have responded to tariff pressure by pledging to expand production <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">on U.S. soil</strong>, thereby shielding their products from import duties.
  > 几家外国汽车制造商为应对关税压力，承诺扩大在<strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">美国本土</strong>的生产，从而使其产品免受进口关税影响。

<h4 style="color:#334155;font-weight:600;margin:0.95em 0 0.35em;font-size:1.02em;border-left:4px solid #38bdf8;padding-left:0.55em">S — 熟词僻义 / 引申义</h4>

<strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">25. employ</strong> /ɪmˈplɔɪ/ *v.*

- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">英文释义①</strong>（常见义）：To pay someone to work for you 雇用
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">英文释义②</strong>（僻义）：To use a particular method, skill, or technique; to use a word or phrase *(Longman)* <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">使用（方法/词语/技巧）</strong>
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：①雇用；②使用（语言/手段/方法）——<strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">本文用法为②</strong>
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：正式 / 书面 / 法律 / 学术
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">关键原文句</strong>：
  > "the text of IEEPA <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">does not employ</strong> the word 'tariff' or any term of equivalent meaning"
  > （IEEPA 的条文并未<strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">使用</strong>"关税"一词或任何同等含义的术语）
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - employ 作"使用"义时，是正式书面语，多出现于法律文书和学术写作中，口语一般用 use
  - 类似表达：employ a strategy（采用策略）/ employ a technique（运用技巧）/ employ force（动用武力）
  - 词族：employer（雇主）→ employee（雇员）→ employment（就业）→ unemployment（失业）→ employable（有就业能力的）
  - <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">这是法律写作中的熟词僻义高频考点</strong>：在法律条文解释（statutory interpretation）中，"the statute employs the term X"（该法律使用了X一词）是标准表述
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">例句（义①）</strong>：
  > BYD North America <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">employs</strong> over 750 unionized workers at its electric bus manufacturing plant in Lancaster, California.
  > 比亚迪北美公司在其加利福尼亚州兰开斯特的电动巴士制造工厂<strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">雇用</strong>逾750名工会工人。
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">例句（义②）</strong>：
  > When <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">employing</strong> the doctrine of expressio unius, courts reason that the legislature's decision to name one item implies the exclusion of others.
  > 在<strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">运用</strong>"明示其一即排除其他"原则时，法院认为立法机关列举某一事项的决定意味着排除了其他事项。

---

<strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">26. follow</strong> /ˈfɑːloʊ/ *v.*

- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">英文释义①</strong>（常见义）：To go after or behind someone or something
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">英文释义②</strong>（僻义/引申义）：To happen as a result of something; to come after in time or sequence *(Longman)*
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：①跟随；②（时间/逻辑上）紧随其后；继……之后发生
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">关键原文句</strong>：
  > "The lawsuit…<strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">follows</strong> similar complaints by thousands of global companies"
  > （这一诉讼紧随全球数千家公司的类似申诉<strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">之后</strong>提出）
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：通用 / 正式 / 书面
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - follow 在此表时间上的"跟随发生"，是新闻英语的高频句式："Event A follows Event B"（A紧随B发生），强调先后关系
  - 类似表达：in the wake of, on the heels of（比 follow 更强调迅速跟随）
  - 固定句式：It follows that...（由此可得出……）——逻辑推理中的正式表达
  - as follows（如下所述）——引出列举内容
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > The flood of corporate lawsuits <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">followed</strong> the administration's invocation of emergency powers to impose border taxes without congressional approval.
  > 企业诉讼潮紧随政府援引紧急权力、在未经国会批准的情况下征收边境税之后而来。

---

<strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">27. take one's time</strong> *v. phr.*

- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：To do something slowly or carefully, without hurrying *(Longman)*
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：不急着做；从容处理；慢慢来
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">关键原文句</strong>：
  > "the court was <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">taking its time</strong> given the 'enormous' stakes involved"
  > （鉴于所涉利害关系的"极端重大性"，最高法院正在<strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">从容审慎地</strong>推进）
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：通用 / 口语化→在正式新闻语境中带有轻微委婉色彩
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 本文中 "taking its time" 是一个委婉且意味深长的表达，暗示最高法院审慎、不急于裁决，并非懈怠，而是因利害重大而审慎权衡

<strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">34. does not employ the word '...' or any term of equivalent meaning</strong> *legal phr.*

- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">释义</strong>：A statutory interpretation argument asserting that the text of a law does not contain the disputed term or its functional equivalent
- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：（该法律）条文中未出现"……"一词或任何同等含义的表述——法律解释中的文本主义论点
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：法律 / 正式
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 这是 textualism（文本主义）法律解释方法的经典论式，在美国最高法院辩论中极为常见
  - "term of equivalent meaning"（同等含义的术语）= functional equivalent（功能等同词）
  - 类似表达：the statute is silent on...（该法律对……未作规定）/ the text does not encompass...（条文不涵盖……）
  - 这一论证策略的有效性已在本案中被最高法院采纳——成为2026年最重要的法律语言论据之一
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > The plaintiffs' core argument was that the relevant statute "<strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">does not employ</strong> the word 'confiscate' or <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">any term of equivalent meaning</strong>," foreclosing the agency's expansive reading.
  > 原告的核心论点是：相关法规"<strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">未使用</strong>'没收'一词或<strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">任何同等含义的术语</strong>"，从而排除了行政机构对该法的扩张性解读。

<strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">36. text</strong> /tɛkst/ *n.* [C/U]

- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">英文释义①</strong>（常见义）：The written content of a book, article, etc.（文本；正文）
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">英文释义②</strong>（法律僻义）：The precise wording of a statute, contract, or legal document, as distinguished from its legislative history or purpose *(OED; legal)*
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：①文字；正文；②（法律）条文原文；成文表述——<strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">本文核心用法为②</strong>
- <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：法律 / 学术（义②为法律专业术语）
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">关键原文句</strong>：
  > "the <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">text</strong> of IEEPA does not employ the word 'tariff'"
  > （IEEPA 的<strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">条文原文</strong>并未使用"关税"一词）
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 在法律解释学（statutory interpretation）中，"the text" = 法律条文本身（区别于立法历史、立法目的）
  - textualism（文本主义）vs. purposivism（目的主义）是美国宪法/法律解释的两大主流方法论
  - 本文的核心法律论点正是 textualist argument（文本主义论点）：既然法律文本中根本没有 "tariff" 这个词，总统自然无权据此征税
  - 词族：textual (adj.)→ textually (adv.)→ textbook (n./adj.)
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > The dissent argued that focusing solely on the <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">text</strong> of the statute ignored the broader congressional intent underlying the emergency powers framework.
  > 持异议的法官认为，仅关注法律<strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">条文原文</strong>忽视了紧急权力框架背后更宏观的国会立法意图。

<strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">38. stakes</strong> /steɪks/ *n. pl.*

- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">英文释义</strong>：The things that can be gained or lost in a risky situation; what is at risk *(Longman)*

- <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">关键原文句</strong>：

- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">常见词组</strong>：

  - raise the stakes（提高赌注→使形势更为严峻）

<strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">39. protect one's ability to</strong> *v. phr.*

- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">释义</strong>：To take legal or practical steps ensuring that one retains the right or capacity to do something in the future
- <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">中文释义</strong>：为维护自身……的能力/权利而采取保护性措施
- <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">语域</strong>：法律 / 商业 / 正式
- <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">原文出处</strong>：
  > "BYD said…it had to file an independent complaint to <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">protect its ability</strong> to be refunded"
  > （比亚迪表示……必须提起独立诉讼，以<strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">维护自身获得退款的权利</strong>）
- <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">拓展</strong>：
  - 这一表达揭示了诉讼策略中的重要原则：即便有利可图的判决尚未作出，当事方也须及时采取法律行动，以免因时效（statute of limitations）届满而丧失权利
  - 常见变体：preserve one's right to...（保留……的权利）/ safeguard one's entitlement to...（维护对……的权利主张）
  - 在法律语境中，"protect the ability to seek a remedy" = "preserve standing/the right to claim"
- <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">例句</strong>：
  > Importers were advised to file individual claims promptly in order to <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">protect their ability</strong> to recover duties should the Supreme Court rule in their favor.

<strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">金句① — 法律文本主义论点的经典表述</strong>

> <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">原文</strong>：
> "the text of IEEPA does not employ the word 'tariff' or any term of equivalent meaning."

> <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">高质量中文翻译</strong>：
> IEEPA 的法律条文中，既未使用"关税"一词，也未出现任何与之含义相当的表述。

> <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">写作场景指引</strong>：
> 此句是 <strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">textualism（文本主义）</strong> 法律解释方法的教科书级表述，适用于一切以"法律文本未明确授权"为由挑战行政权力的语境。其核心逻辑结构——"the [statutory text] does not employ the term [X] or any equivalent"——可作为论证行政越权的万能句式，在英文学术写作、法律备考和时评写作中均具极高的迁移价值。

<strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">金句② — 揭示高利害关系下司法审慎的深层逻辑</strong>

> <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">原文</strong>：

> 美国贸易代表贾米森·格里尔上周表示，鉴于此案牵涉的利害关系"极为重大"，最高法院正在审慎推进，不急于作出裁决。

> <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">写作场景指引</strong>：
> 这句话以高层官员之口，间接承认了司法博弈的历史分量。句中 <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">"taking its time"</strong> 与 <strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">"enormous stakes"</strong> 的搭配，既传达了司法机构的权威与审慎，又暗示了行政部门对裁决结果的高度关切。适用于描写重大决策者在关键时刻"以慎重替代仓促"的写作场景，也可用于分析司法独立性与政治压力之间关系的议论文段。

<strong style="color:#7c3aed;font-weight:600">金句③ — 中国企业在美法律维权的里程碑时刻</strong>

> <strong style="color:#2563eb;font-weight:600">原文</strong>：
> "The lawsuit, the first by a Chinese carmaker over U.S. tariffs, follows similar complaints by thousands of global companies with U.S. operations challenging Trump's use of the International Emergency Economic Powers Act (IEEPA) to impose border taxes."

> <strong style="color:#db2777;font-weight:600">高质量中文翻译</strong>：
> 这是首件由中国汽车制造商就美国关税提起的诉讼，继全球数千家在美运营企业挑战特朗普援引《国际紧急经济权力法》征收边境税之后而来。

> <strong style="color:#0891b2;font-weight:600">写作场景指引</strong>：
> 此句以 <strong style="color:#ca8a04;font-weight:600">"the first by [主体] over [议题]"</strong> 的结构强调历史开创性，再以 <strong style="color:#0d9488;font-weight:600">"follows similar [actions] by thousands of..."</strong> 将个案置于宏观浪潮之中，手法老练。此句型适用于一切"开创性举措"的写作场景，无论是描写企业维权、外交突破还是政策先例，均可仿效。核心句式模板：<strong style="color:#c2410c;font-weight:600">[Event A], the first [X] to [do Y], follows similar [actions] by [parties] challenging [Z].</strong>

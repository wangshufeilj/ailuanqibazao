# 模块一：翻译与全文概要

## 英文翻译

**Building High-Quality Datasets to Make Artificial Intelligence Smarter**
*(People's Daily, May 21, 2025, Page 18 — Industry & Economy)*
*By Wang Yunshan*

What is a high-quality dataset? Simply put, it is data that is high-value, high-density, and standardized.

"Data is to large language models what petroleum is to automobiles. Crude oil can only power a vehicle after it has been refined through a series of complex processes into gasoline. Similarly, vast quantities of raw data must be 'refined' into high-quality datasets before they can enable large models to accurately learn data features and patterns, effectively enhancing their adaptability to different scenarios and tasks." Wei Liang, Vice President of the China Academy of Information and Communications Technology (CAICT), told reporters that the quality of a dataset influences AI's "IQ." In the recent training of the DeepSeek series of models, high-quality reasoning datasets were used extensively, underscoring the importance of high-quality data. "The deep integration of large models with vertical industries equally requires the support of high-quality datasets," he added.

Active steps are being taken to build high-quality datasets. The *Three-Year Action Plan for 'Data Factor×' (2024–2026)*, jointly issued by the National Data Administration and 16 other departments, calls for "promoting research institutions, leading enterprises and others to build industry-wide shared data resource libraries and to create high-quality AI large-model training datasets." At the 8th Digital China Construction Summit, the State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission (SASAC) released the first batch of high-quality AI datasets for over ten industries, comprising 30 datasets from central state-owned enterprises (SOEs), covering areas such as AI load-forecasting datasets for power grid dispatch, health diagnostics and fault prediction datasets for nuclear power plant SPV equipment, and datasets for large financial models.

"As open-source foundation models become the prevailing trend, the gaps between various players in computing power and model algorithms are steadily narrowing. The value of data as a production factor is becoming increasingly prominent, and it has become the core arena of AI competition." Hu Wujie, Deputy Director of the Planning and Development Department at SASAC, stated that it is necessary to accelerate the aggregation and sharing of high-quality industry datasets to provide sufficient "nutrients" for the AI sector, continuously optimize training across different scenarios, and promote the deployment of foundation models across all industries.

At present, the development of high-quality datasets still faces significant challenges. Wei Liang noted that, on the one hand, industry-specific large models have diverse data requirements, and different industry departments have varying demands for model scenario data, which increases the complexity of data processing and management. On the other hand, in practice, there are no uniform measurement standards for data that is either built or purchased, and the completeness and accuracy of data from different industries and sources can vary considerably, affecting the training effectiveness and predictive accuracy of large models and leading to wasteful use of training resources.

On April 30, the *Guidelines for Building High-Quality Datasets (Draft for Public Comment)* was released. The National Technical Committee for Data Standardization proposed strengthening standards-led development and categorizing high-quality datasets into three types: "general knowledge datasets," comprising universal knowledge accessible to the general public without professional background, mainly for supporting the deployment of general-purpose models; "industry-general datasets," comprising knowledge common within a specific industry that requires some professional background to understand, mainly for supporting the deployment of industry-specific models; and "industry-specialized datasets," comprising professional knowledge in specific business scenarios that requires a deeper professional background to understand, mainly for supporting business scenario model deployment.

Xia Bing, Deputy Director of the National Data Administration, stated that improving the quality and effectiveness of datasets is the "catalyst" for AI to empower the real economy. In the next step, the National Data Administration will establish an inter-ministerial coordination mechanism with central-local collaboration, advance research on a standards system for high-quality datasets, facilitate the alignment of data, technology, and application scenarios, build a diversified and collaborative data-annotation industry ecosystem, and consolidate the data foundations for AI development.

## 2C. 原文实体注释

### 机构/组织

**① 中国信息通信研究院（CAICT）**
China Academy of Information and Communications Technology，简称"中国信通院"。成立于1957年，是工业和信息化部直属科研事业单位，总部位于北京。长期从事信息通信技术研究、标准制定、测试认证及政策咨询，是国内ICT领域最主要的国家级智库。

**② 国家数据局（National Data Administration, NDA）**
2023年根据《党和国家机构改革方案》组建，是国家层面专门负责数据治理的行政机构。主要职责包括协调推进数据基础制度建设、统筹数据资源整合共享和开发利用、统筹推进数字中国规划与建设等。

**③ 国务院国有资产监督管理委员会（SASAC）**
国务院国有资产监督管理委员会，简称"国资委"，成立于2003年，是国务院直属特设机构。负责对中央管理的国有企业（即央企）履行出资人职责，监督管理国有资产，推动国有经济布局结构调整。

**④ 全国数据标准化技术委员会（SAC/TC609）**
2022年经国家标准化管理委员会批准成立，秘书处设于国家数据局。主要负责数据资源、数据技术、数据流通、数据安全等领域的国家标准制修订工作，对口ISO/IEC等国际标准化组织相关技术委员会。

**⑤ 第八届数字中国建设峰会**
Digital China Summit，是中国规格最高的数字化领域年度峰会，由中央网信办主办，每年在福建省福州市举办（历届峰会以此为主要举办地）。峰会汇聚政府部门、科研机构与科技企业，是中国数字政策发布的重要平台。

### 外国公司/国内企业

**⑥ 深度求索（DeepSeek）**
全称杭州深度求索人工智能基础技术研究有限公司，中国AI企业，2023年7月由知名量化私募幻方量化创立，总部位于浙江省杭州市。专注于大语言模型（LLM）及通用人工智能底层技术研发，2025年初以其低成本、高性能模型在全球AI领域引发广泛关注。

### 政策文件

**⑦ 《"数据要素×"三年行动计划（2024—2026年）》**
2024年1月由国家数据局等17个部门联合印发，旨在通过充分发挥数据要素的"乘数效应"，赋能12个重点领域（工业制造、现代农业、商贸流通、交通运输、金融服务、科技创新、文化旅游、医疗健康、应急管理、气象服务、城市治理、绿色低碳），推动数字经济高质量发展。

# 模块四：素材与语料库积累

## 4A. 重点词汇解析

---

### W — 写作高频词

---

**W1. refine**
- **音标/词性**：/rɪˈfaɪn/ *v.*
- **英文释义**：To improve something by making small changes; also, to remove impurities from a substance through an industrial process.
- **中文释义**：改进，精炼，提纯；（通过反复修改使某事物）臻于完善
- **语域**：正式 / 学术 / 书面
- **同义词**：improve, enhance, perfect, purify, process
- **反义词**：coarsen, degrade, contaminate
- **常见词组**：refine a theory / refine crude oil / a refined approach
- **拓展**：
  - 动词变形规则：refine → refined → refining
  - 名词形式：**refinement**（不可数/可数均可，表"改进之处"时可数）
  - 形容词：**refined**（精炼的；有教养的，如 *a refined taste*）
  - 区分：*refine* 侧重多次迭代改进；*improve* 更泛化；*purify* 侧重去除杂质
  - 本文用法："海量原始数据需要经过'炼化'形成高质量数据集"——原文以"炼化"对应 *refine*，极为精当，是科技翻译中的地道表达
- **例句**：
  > **Refining** raw training data through systematic filtering and annotation is essential before it can be used to **fine-tune** a large language model.
  > 在将原始训练数据用于大型语言模型的微调之前，必须通过系统性筛选和标注对其进行**精炼**。

---

**W2. harness**
- **音标/词性**：/ˈhɑːrnɪs/ *v. / n.*
- **英文释义**：*(v.)* To control and make use of the power or potential of something for a particular purpose; *(n.)* a set of straps for controlling a horse or securing a person.
- **中文释义**：（动）利用，驾驭（力量或资源）；（名）马具；安全带
- **语域**：正式 / 书面 / 新闻
- **同义词**：leverage, exploit, utilize, mobilize
- **反义词**：waste, squander
- **常见词组**：harness the power of data / harness renewable energy / harness potential
- **拓展**：
  - 动词变形规则：harness → harnessed → harnessing（规则变化）
  - 写作亮点词：在商业/科技报道中，*harness* 比 *use* 更具力度，隐含"驾驭难以控制之物"之意
  - 辨析：*exploit* 有时含负面色彩（剥削）；*harness* 中性偏正
- **例句**：
  > Governments and corporations alike are racing to **harness** the power of artificial intelligence to drive productivity gains across every sector of the economy.
  > 政府和企业都在竞相**驾驭**人工智能的力量，以推动各经济领域的生产率提升。

---

**W3. catalyst**
- **音标/词性**：/ˈkætl̩ɪst/ *n.* [C]
- **英文释义**：（1）*chemistry* a substance that speeds up a chemical reaction without itself being permanently changed; （2）something or someone that causes an important change or event to happen.
- **中文释义**：（化学）催化剂；（比喻）促进变革的人或事物
- **语域**：学术 / 新闻 / 正式书面
- **同义词**：stimulus, impetus, spur, trigger
- **反义词**：inhibitor, obstacle, impediment
- **常见词组**：act as a catalyst for / serve as a catalyst / catalyst for change / catalyst for reform
- **拓展**：
  - 衍生词：**catalytic** *adj.* / **catalyze** *v.*（美式拼写）/ **catalyse**（英式拼写）
  - 本文对应原文"数据集的质效提升是人工智能赋能实体经济的'催化剂'"——是典型的书面比喻用法
  - 词源：源自1900年前后，由 *catalysis*（催化作用）类比 *analyst* 构成
- **例句**：
  > The release of standardized, high-quality datasets could serve as a **catalyst** for accelerating the **deployment** of AI applications across China's manufacturing sector.
  > 标准化高质量数据集的发布，可成为加速AI应用在中国制造业落地的**催化剂**。

---

**W4. aggregate**
- **音标/词性**：/ˈæɡrɪɡət/ *n. [C/U] / adj. / v.*
- **英文释义**：*(n.)* a total formed by combining several separate amounts or elements; *(adj.)* total; *(v.)* to combine into a whole.
- **中文释义**：总量，汇总；集合的；汇总，聚合
- **语域**：正式 / 学术 / 经济统计 / 新闻
- **同义词**：total, sum, collective, cumulative
- **反义词**：individual, separate, component
- **常见词组**：aggregate data / aggregate demand / in the aggregate / aggregate output
- **拓展**：
  - 三词性注意：发音变化——名词/形容词：/ˈæɡrɪɡət/；动词：/ˈæɡrɪɡeɪt/
  - 经济学高频词：*aggregate demand / aggregate supply* 是宏观经济学核心术语
  - 数据语境：*aggregate data* 指聚合/汇总数据，与本文"汇聚共享"主题高度相关
- **例句**：
  > The platform is designed to **aggregate** dispersed industry datasets from multiple central enterprises, enabling more effective training of domain-specific AI models.
  > 该平台旨在**汇聚**来自多家央企的分散行业数据集，从而更有效地训练垂直领域AI模型。

---

**W5. benchmark**
- **音标/词性**：/ˈbentʃmɑːrk/ *n. [C] / v.*
- **英文释义**：*(n.)* a standard or point of reference against which things may be compared or assessed; *(v.)* to measure or evaluate something by comparison with a standard.
- **中文释义**：基准，参照标准；对…进行基准测试
- **语域**：正式 / 商业 / 技术 / 新闻
- **同义词**：standard, yardstick, reference point, metric
- **常见词组**：set a benchmark / benchmark against / industry benchmark / performance benchmark
- **拓展**：
  - 在AI语境中，*benchmark* 专指模型性能测试基准（如 MMLU、HELM 等），是技术写作必备词
  - 与本文关联：文章提到"不同行业、不同数据源的数据没有统一衡量标准"——英文表达为 *lack of unified benchmarks* 或 *no consistent benchmarking criteria*
- **例句**：
  > Without clear quality **benchmarks** for training datasets, it becomes extremely difficult to compare the performance of AI models developed by different organizations.
  > 如果缺乏明确的训练数据集质量**基准**，要比较不同机构开发的AI模型性能就极为困难。

---

**W6. proliferate**
- **音标/词性**：/prəˈlɪfəreɪt/ *v.*
- **英文释义**：To increase rapidly in number or spread widely.
- **中文释义**：激增，快速扩散，大量涌现
- **语域**：正式 / 学术 / 新闻
- **同义词**：multiply, surge, burgeon, mushroom
- **反义词**：dwindle, shrink, decline
- **常见词组**：proliferate rapidly / proliferating technologies / proliferation of…
- **拓展**：
  - 名词：**proliferation** *n. [U/C]*（核武器扩散 nuclear proliferation；AI应用激增 proliferation of AI applications）
  - 形容词：**proliferative**（多用于医学，如 *proliferative disorder*）
  - 写作用途：替代 *increase rapidly* 的精确书面选词
- **例句**：
  > As foundation models **proliferate** across industries, the demand for domain-specific, high-quality training data is expected to grow exponentially.
  > 随着基础模型在各行业**大量涌现**，对垂直领域高质量训练数据的需求预计将呈指数级增长。

---

**W7. consolidate**
- **音标/词性**：/kənˈsɒlɪdeɪt/ *v.*
- **英文释义**：To make something stronger or more solid; to combine several things, especially businesses or organizations, into one.
- **中文释义**：巩固，加强；整合，合并
- **语域**：正式 / 商业 / 政策 / 新闻
- **同义词**：strengthen, reinforce, unify, integrate, merge
- **反义词**：fragment, disperse, dissolve
- **常见词组**：consolidate resources / consolidate one's position / consolidate the foundation
- **拓展**：
  - 名词：**consolidation** *n. [U/C]*
  - 本文对应表达："夯实人工智能发展数据根基"——*consolidate the data foundation for AI development*，是政策文件的惯用表达
  - 区分：*consolidate* 含"使稳固"之意，比 *merge* 更广义
- **例句**：
  > The government's goal is to **consolidate** a coherent data governance framework that **underpins** the long-term development of the artificial intelligence industry.
  > 政府的目标是**夯实**一套连贯的数据治理框架，为人工智能产业的长期发展**奠定基础**。

---

**W8. interoperability**
- **音标/词性**：/ˌɪntərˌɒpərəˈbɪlɪti/ *n. [U]*
- **英文释义**：The ability of different systems, organizations, or formats to work together and exchange information effectively.
- **中文释义**：互操作性，互联互通能力
- **语域**：技术 / 学术 / 政策
- **同义词**：compatibility, integration capability, cross-system communication
- **常见词组**：ensure interoperability / interoperability standards / data interoperability
- **拓展**：
  - 形容词：**interoperable** *adj.*
  - 在AI和数字治理领域为超高频术语，尤其与数据共享（data sharing）机制密切相关
  - 本文语境对应："部际联通、央地协同的工作机制"——即推动系统和数据间的互操作性
- **例句**：
  > Ensuring **interoperability** between datasets across government agencies is a prerequisite for building a truly integrated national AI data infrastructure.
  > 确保政府各机构数据集之间的**互操作性**，是构建真正整合的国家AI数据基础设施的先决条件。

---

### R — 阅读高频词

---

**R1. domain-specific**
- **音标/词性**：/dəˈmeɪn spəˈsɪfɪk/ *adj.*（复合修饰语）
- **英文释义**：Relating to or designed for a particular area of activity or knowledge.
- **中文释义**：特定领域的，垂直领域的
- **语域**：技术 / 学术
- **同义词**：sector-specific, industry-specific, specialized, vertical
- **常见词组**：domain-specific model / domain-specific knowledge / domain-specific data
- **拓展**：
  - 与 *general-purpose*（通用的）相对；AI语境中高频出现
  - 对应原文："行业大模型"即 *domain-specific / industry-specific large language models*
- **例句**：
  > **Domain-specific** datasets that incorporate professional terminology and industry logic are far more effective at training AI models for medical diagnosis than **general-purpose** corpora.
  > 融入专业术语和行业逻辑的**垂直领域**数据集，在训练医疗诊断AI模型方面远比**通用**语料库更有效。

---

**R2. fine-tune**
- **音标/词性**：/ˌfaɪnˈtjuːn/ *v.*
- **英文释义**：To make very small and precise adjustments to something to make it work as well as possible; in AI, to further train a pre-trained model on a specific dataset.
- **中文释义**：微调；（AI术语）在预训练模型基础上针对特定任务进行进一步训练
- **语域**：技术 / 学术 / 书面
- **同义词**：calibrate, optimize, adjust, tune
- **常见词组**：fine-tune a model / fine-tuning stage / instruction fine-tuning
- **拓展**：
  - 名词形式：**fine-tuning** *n. [U]*
  - 与原文"指令微调阶段"（instruction fine-tuning）对应
  - 区分：*pre-training*（预训练，大规模通用数据）→ *fine-tuning*（微调，特定任务数据）→ *RLHF*（基于人类反馈的强化学习）
- **例句**：
  > The company used a curated dataset of 500,000 annotated legal documents to **fine-tune** its base model, significantly improving performance on contract review tasks.
  > 该公司使用一个包含50万份标注法律文件的精选数据集对基础模型进行**微调**，大幅提升了合同审查任务的表现。

---

**R3. heterogeneous**
- **音标/词性**：/ˌhetərəˈdʒiːniəs/ *adj.*
- **英文释义**：Consisting of many different kinds of things or people that are not related to each other; diverse in character or content.
- **中文释义**：异质的，多样化的，由不同成分构成的
- **语域**：学术 / 正式 / 统计
- **反义词**：homogeneous（同质的）
- **常见词组**：heterogeneous data / heterogeneous sources / heterogeneous systems
- **拓展**：
  - 名词：**heterogeneity** *n. [U]*
  - 对应原文"不同行业部门对模型场景数据的需求各不相同"——学术英语中精准表达为 *heterogeneous data requirements across sectors*
- **例句**：
  > Managing **heterogeneous** data sources from across multiple industries remains one of the most persistent technical challenges in building large-scale AI training corpora.
  > 管理来自多个行业的**异质性**数据源，仍然是构建大规模AI训练语料库中最为棘手的技术挑战之一。

---

**R4. annotation**
- **音标/词性**：/ˌænəˈteɪʃn/ *n. [C/U]*
- **英文释义**：A note added to a text or diagram giving explanation or comment; in AI/ML, the process of labeling data to train supervised learning models.
- **中文释义**：注释，标注；（AI语境）数据标注
- **语域**：学术 / 技术
- **同义词**：labeling, tagging, markup
- **常见词组**：data annotation / annotation quality / manual annotation / annotation pipeline
- **拓展**：
  - 动词：**annotate** *v.*；**annotator** *n.*（标注员）
  - 本文原文对应："构建多元协同的数据标注产业生态"（data annotation industry ecosystem）
  - 区分：*annotation* 更强调添加说明信息；*labeling* 更口语化，指贴标签
- **例句**：
  > The accuracy of AI models in clinical settings depends heavily on the quality of **annotations** provided by medical professionals during the data preparation phase.
  > AI模型在临床环境中的准确性，在很大程度上取决于数据准备阶段由医疗专业人员提供的**标注**质量。

---

**R5. interministerial / inter-ministerial**
- **音标/词性**：/ˌɪntəˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəl/ *adj.*
- **英文释义**：Involving or between two or more government ministries or departments.
- **中文释义**：跨部门的，多部委联合的
- **语域**：政策 / 政府文件 / 新闻
- **同义词**：cross-departmental, multi-agency, interdepartmental
- **常见词组**：inter-ministerial coordination / inter-ministerial working group / interministerial mechanism
- **拓展**：
  - 对应原文："部际联通、央地协同的工作机制"——*an inter-ministerial coordination mechanism with central-local collaboration*
  - 政策文件翻译必备词汇
- **例句**：
  > The lack of **inter-ministerial** coordination between the education ministry and the technology ministry created overlapping mandates and **inefficiencies** in national AI data governance.
  > 教育部与科技部之间缺乏**跨部门**协调，导致国家AI数据治理中职权重叠、**效率低下**。

---

**R6. open-source**
- **音标/词性**：/ˌoʊpənˈsɔːrs/ *adj. / v.*
- **英文释义**：Denoting software or AI models whose source code, architecture, or weights are made freely available to the public and can be modified and distributed.

- **反义词**：closed-source, proprietary（专有的）
- **常见词组**：open-source model / open-source community / open-source ecosystem / release under open-source license
- **拓展**：

### T — 翻译重要词

---

**T1. 数据要素 → data factor / data as a factor of production**
- **音标/词性**：*n. phrase*
- **英文释义**：Data conceptualized as a fundamental input to economic production, analogous to land, labor, and capital in classical economics.
- **中文释义**：数据要素；将数据作为新型生产要素
- **语域**：政策 / 学术 / 经济
- **拓展**：
  - 这是中国特有的政策术语，英译时需加解释性语言。官方英译倾向于 *data factor* 或 *data as a factor of production*
  - 对应文件："数据要素×三年行动计划"中"×"象征"乘数效应"（multiplicative effect），英译为 *Data Factor Multiplier Effect Action Plan* 或保留符号 *"Data Factor×" Action Plan*
- **例句**：
  > China's economic planners have elevated **data** to the status of a **core factor of production**, placing it alongside land, labor, capital, and technology in national resource allocation frameworks.
  > 中国的经济规划者已将**数据**提升至**核心生产要素**的地位，将其与土地、劳动力、资本和技术并列于国家资源配置框架之中。

---

**T2. 垂直领域 → vertical sector / vertical industry / niche domain**
- **音标/词性**：*n. phrase / adj. phrase*
- **英文释义**：A specific industry or specialized field of business, as opposed to a general or horizontal application.
- **中文释义**：垂直领域，特定行业，细分赛道
- **语域**：商业 / 技术 / 新闻
- **同义词**：specialized sector, industry-specific domain, niche market
- **反义词**：horizontal market, general-purpose domain
- **拓展**：
  - "行业大模型 + 垂直领域深度融合"的标准英译：*deep integration of large models with vertical industries / domain-specific large models*
  - 区分：*vertical* 指深耕某一行业（如医疗AI、金融AI）；*horizontal* 指跨行业的通用能力
- **例句**：
  > **Vertical sector** AI solutions, such as those designed for power grid management or pharmaceutical research, require highly curated, **domain-specific** datasets that general training corpora cannot adequately provide.
  > 针对电网管理或药物研究等**垂直领域**的AI解决方案，需要通用训练语料库无法充分提供的高度精选**专业数据集**。

---

**T3. 赋能 → empower / enable / AI-empower**
- **音标/词性**：/ɪmˈpaʊər/ *v.*
- **英文释义**：To give power, authority, or ability to someone or something; to make something capable of achieving more.
- **中文释义**：赋能，使能够，授权；（产业语境）通过技术手段提升某行业/领域的能力
- **语域**：商业 / 政策 / 正式
- **常见词组**：empower the real economy / AI-empowered industry / empower enterprises with data
- **拓展**：
  - "AI赋能实体经济"的标准英译：*AI to empower the real economy* 或 *AI-driven empowerment of the real economy*
  - 注意："赋能"在中文商业语境中已被过度使用，英译时根据语境可替换为 *enable, enhance, transform, equip*
  - 区分：*empower* 侧重给予主动权和能力；*enable* 侧重提供条件和可能性
- **例句**：
  > High-quality datasets are not merely a technical asset—they **empower** businesses across every sector to unlock the full potential of AI and drive genuine productivity improvements.
  > 高质量数据集不仅仅是一种技术资产——它们**赋能**各行业企业，助其充分释放AI的潜力，推动切实的生产率提升。

---

**T4. 千行百业 → every sector of the economy / across all industries / the full spectrum of industries**
- **音标/词性**：*idiomatic phrase*
- **英文释义**：A Chinese idiomatic expression referring to the entire range of industries and economic sectors.
- **中文释义**：各行各业，国民经济各领域
- **语域**：政策 / 新闻（中文成语，英译需意译）
- **翻译建议**：
  - 正式政策文件：*all industries and sectors of the national economy*
  - 新闻报道：*industries ranging from manufacturing to finance*
  - 简洁版：*across the board / economy-wide*
- **例句**：
  > The government's ambition is for AI to penetrate **every sector of the economy**—from smart agriculture and advanced manufacturing to healthcare, logistics, and financial services.
  > 政府的目标是让AI渗透**国民经济各行各业**——从智慧农业和先进制造业，到医疗健康、物流和金融服务。

---

**T5. 标准引领 → standards-led (development) / standards-guided approach**
- **音标/词性**：*adj. phrase / n. phrase*
- **英文释义**：An approach in which the development or implementation of something is guided and shaped by formal standards.
- **中文释义**：以标准为引领；标准先行
- **语域**：政策 / 技术 / 工业
- **拓展**：
  - 这是中国政策文件中的惯用表达，英文类似搭配包括：*regulation-led, policy-driven, standards-based*
  - 对应原文："将强化标准引领，分三类建设高质量数据集"——*proposing a standards-led development approach, categorizing high-quality datasets into three types*
- **例句**：
  > A **standards-led** approach to data governance ensures that different organizations can contribute to and draw from shared datasets without sacrificing consistency or reliability.
  > 以**标准为引领**的数据治理路径，确保不同机构在共享数据集中的贡献与使用不以牺牲一致性和可靠性为代价。

---

**T6. 征求意见稿 → draft for public comment / exposure draft / consultation draft**
- **音标/词性**：*n. phrase*
- **英文释义**：A preliminary version of a regulation, standard, or policy document released to solicit feedback from the public or industry stakeholders before finalization.
- **中文释义**：征求意见稿，公开征询意见的草案
- **语域**：法律 / 政策 / 正式
- **拓展**：
  - 中国官方通常译为 *draft for public comment*；国际标准化组织语境（ISO/IEC）常用 *committee draft (CD)* 或 *draft international standard (DIS)*
  - 区分：*exposure draft*（更多见于会计准则如 IFRS/FASB）；*consultation paper*（更多见于英国、澳大利亚政策语境）；*green paper*（欧盟政策讨论初稿）
- **例句**：
  > The National Technical Committee released a **draft for public comment** on high-quality dataset construction standards, inviting industry feedback before the guidelines are formally adopted.
  > 全国数据标准化技术委员会就高质量数据集建设标准发布**征求意见稿**，在指南正式采纳前广泛征集行业反馈。

---

**T7. 参差不齐 → vary considerably / uneven / inconsistent / of mixed quality**
- **音标/词性**：*idiomatic adj. phrase*
- **英文释义**：Varying greatly in quality, standard, or level; not uniform.
- **中文释义**：良莠不齐，水平高低不一
- **语域**：书面 / 新闻 / 学术
- **翻译建议**：
  - 学术英文：*highly variable / exhibit significant heterogeneity*
  - 新闻英文：*of mixed quality / uneven in standard*
  - 对应原文："不同行业、不同数据源的数据完整性和准确性可能参差不齐"
- **例句**：
  > The accuracy of publicly available AI training datasets **varies considerably** across domains, with some sectors providing meticulously curated data while others rely on poorly **annotated** or outdated sources.
  > 公开可用的AI训练数据集在不同领域的准确性**参差不齐**，部分行业提供精心整理的数据，而其他行业则依赖质量欠佳或过时的**标注**来源。

---

**T8. 落地应用 → real-world deployment / practical application / operational implementation**
- **音标/词性**：*n. phrase*
- **英文释义**：The process of taking a technology or solution from theory or development into actual, practical use in operational contexts.
- **中文释义**：落地，实际应用，从开发走向使用
- **语域**：商业 / 技术 / 政策
- **拓展**：
  - "落地"是中文科技政策语境中的超高频词，直译为 *land* 不当，应意译
  - 常见英译搭配：*go live, roll out, operationalize, put into practice*
  - 本文语境："推动通用模型落地应用"——*supporting the real-world deployment of general-purpose models*
- **例句**：
  > Bridging the gap between laboratory performance and **real-world deployment** remains the central challenge for AI developers seeking to embed intelligent systems into critical infrastructure.
  > 弥合实验室性能与**实际落地应用**之间的差距，仍是希望将智能系统嵌入关键基础设施的AI开发者面临的核心挑战。

### S — 熟词僻义 / 引申义

**S1. density** *(n.)* — 熟义：密度 → **僻义：（信息/数据）密集程度，含量丰富度**
- **音标/词性**：/ˈdensəti/ *n. [U/C]*
- **英文释义**：（1）*physics* the degree to which something is dense; （2）*information science* the concentration or richness of meaningful information within a unit of data.
- **中文释义**：密度；（信息语境）数据的"含金量"，有效信息密度
- **语域**：物理学 / 信息科学 / 新闻
- **拓展**：
  - 本文原文："高价值、高密度、标准化的数据"——*high-value, high-density, and standardized data*。这里 *high-density* 不指物理密度，而指单位数据中有效信息含量高
  - 衍生词：**dense** *adj.*（密集的；难懂的）；**densely** *adv.*
- **例句**：
  > Unlike raw web-scraped text, curated training corpora are valued for their **information density**—each data point carries a higher concentration of **actionable patterns** and domain-relevant signals.
  > 与从网络爬取的原始文本不同，精心整理的训练语料库因其**信息密度**而受到重视——每个数据点都蕴含更高浓度的**可用模式**和领域相关信号。

**S2. narrow** *(v.)* — 熟义：变窄 → **僻义：（差距）缩小，收窄**
- **音标/词性**：/ˈnæroʊ/ *v. / adj.*
- **英文释义**：*(v.)* to reduce the distance, difference, or gap between things; *(adj.)* having only a small distance from one side to the other.
- **中文释义**：（动）缩小，收窄；（形）窄的，有限的
- **语域**：正式 / 新闻 / 商业
- **常见词组**：narrow the gap / narrow the divide / narrow the lead / narrow down
- **拓展**：
  - 本文对应："各方在算力和模型算法层面的差距正在不断收窄"——*the gaps in computing power and model algorithms are steadily narrowing*
  - *narrow down* 另有"缩小范围（以便筛选）"之义，如 *narrow down the candidates*（缩小候选名单）
- **例句**：
  > As open-source models **narrow the gap** with proprietary counterparts in benchmark performance, competitive advantage increasingly hinges on the **quality and exclusivity** of training data.
  > 随着开源模型在基准测试中与专有模型**差距缩小**，竞争优势越来越取决于训练数据的**质量和独特性**。

**S3. prevailing** *(adj.)* — 熟义：占主导的 → **僻义：（趋势）正在形成的，当前盛行的**
- **音标/词性**：/prɪˈveɪlɪŋ/ *adj.*
- **英文释义**：Existing or most common at a particular time; dominant or widespread.
- **中文释义**：主导的，盛行的，当下占主流的
- **语域**：正式 / 书面 / 学术
- **同义词**：dominant, prevailing, predominant, mainstream, current
- **常见词组**：prevailing trend / prevailing view / prevailing conditions / prevailing winds（气象，字面义）
- **拓展**：
  - 动词：**prevail** *v.*（占上风；流行；劝服 *prevail on/upon sb to do*）
  - 注意区分：*prevailing*（现在分词作形容词，"当前盛行的"）vs *prevalent*（"普遍存在的，广泛流行的"，更强调广泛性）
- **例句**：
  > The **prevailing** consensus among AI researchers is that data quality, rather than sheer model size, will be the **determining factor** in next-generation AI performance.
  > AI研究者中**占主流的**共识是：数据质量而非单纯的模型规模，将成为下一代AI性能的**决定性因素**。

**S4. feature** *(n./v.)* — 熟义：特征 → **僻义（机器学习）：特征（变量）；（动）以…为特色**
- **音标/词性**：/ˈfiːtʃər/ *n. [C] / v.*
- **英文释义**：*(n.)* （1）a distinctive attribute or aspect; （2）*machine learning* an individual measurable property or characteristic of observed data used as input to a model; *(v.)* to include or highlight something as an important element.
- **中文释义**：特征，特性；（机器学习）特征变量；（动）以…为主要内容
- **语域**：通用 / 技术（ML语境）/ 媒体
- **常见词组**：data features / feature extraction / feature engineering / feature patterns
- **拓展**：
  - 本文原文："大模型精准学习数据特征与规律"——*large models accurately learn data features and patterns*
  - **feature engineering**（特征工程）：是传统机器学习的核心步骤，指人工设计输入特征；深度学习出现后，模型可自动学习特征
- **例句**：
  > Advanced **feature extraction** techniques allow AI models to identify subtle **patterns** in medical imaging data that are imperceptible to even highly trained radiologists.
  > 先进的**特征提取**技术使AI模型能够识别医学影像数据中连经验丰富的放射科医生都难以察觉的细微**规律**。

**S5. regime** *(n.)* — 熟义：政权，政体 → **僻义：（法律/技术）监管框架，制度体系**
- **音标/词性**：/reɪˈʒiːm/ *n. [C]*
- **英文释义**：（1）a government, especially an authoritarian one; （2）a system or set of rules, standards, or conditions that govern something.
- **中文释义**：（1）政权，政体；（2）制度，体制，监管框架
- **语域**：政治（含贬义） / 法律（中性） / 学术
- **常见词组**：regulatory regime / data governance regime / sanctions regime / standards regime
- **拓展**：
  - 在国际法、经济法、科技政策语境下，*regime* 完全中性，如：*the data protection regime under GDPR*
  - 写作提示：勿在非政治语境中滥用其政治含义；在法律/政策文章中 *regime* 是高频术语
- **例句**：
  > Establishing a robust **regulatory regime** for AI data governance is critical to ensuring that the rapid deployment of large language models does not outpace the legal safeguards designed to protect data subjects.
  > 为AI数据治理建立健全的**监管体制**，对于确保大语言模型的快速部署不超越为保护数据主体而设计的法律保障至关重要。

**S6. nutrition / nutrient → (引申) 养分，"滋养"数据**

- **英文释义**：A substance that provides essential nourishment for growth and development; metaphorically, inputs that sustain and develop a system.
- **中文释义**：营养素，养分；（比喻）（产业发展的）滋养要素
- **语域**：生物 / 正式 / 比喻用法（新闻/政策）
- **拓展**：
  - 本文"养分"的英译：*nutrients / nourishment*，作比喻时写作亮点词
  - 区分：**nutrition** *n. [U]*（营养，过程/状态）；**nutrient** *n. [C]*（营养物质，具体物质）；**nourishment** *n. [U]*（滋养，偏书面）

  > 正如均衡饮食提供健康成长所需的全面**营养素**，多样且精心整理的训练数据集为AI模型有效泛化提供了所需的**信息滋养**。

**S7. crude** *(adj./n.)* — 熟义：粗糙的 → **僻义：（石油）原油；未加工的，原始的**
- **音标/词性**：/kruːd/ *adj. / n. [U]*
- **英文释义**：*(adj.)* （1）in a natural or raw state; not yet processed or refined; （2）offensive or rude; *(n.)* crude oil.
- **中文释义**：未加工的，原始的；（名）原油；粗鄙的
- **语域**：石油行业 / 工业 / 通用
- **常见词组**：crude oil / crude data / crude estimate / crude method
- **拓展**：
  - 本文比喻核心：*crude oil* → 炼化 → 汽油 = *raw data* → 处理 → 高质量数据集
  - 注意：*crude* 与 *raw* 在"未加工"义上高度接近，但 *crude* 更多用于石油、矿石等工业原料，*raw* 更泛用（raw data, raw materials）
- **例句**：
  > **Crude data** collected from social media platforms, much like **crude oil** extracted from the ground, has immense latent value but requires extensive **processing and refinement** before it can power intelligent applications.
  > 从社交媒体平台采集的**原始数据**，犹如从地下开采的**原油**，蕴藏着巨大的潜在价值，但需要经过大量的**处理与精炼**，才能驱动智能应用。

**S8. integrity** *(n.)* — 熟义：诚信，正直 → **僻义：（数据）完整性，不被篡改的状态**
- **音标/词性**：/ɪnˈteɡrəti/ *n. [U]*
- **英文释义**：（1）the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles; （2）the state of being whole and undivided; in data science, the accuracy and consistency of data over its lifecycle.
- **中文释义**：（1）正直，诚信；（2）完整性；（数据）完整性，一致性
- **语域**：（1）道德 / 通用；（2）技术 / 法律
- **常见词组**：data integrity / structural integrity / academic integrity / maintain integrity
- **拓展**：
  - 本文对应："不同数据源的数据完整性和准确性可能参差不齐"——*data completeness and integrity may vary considerably*
  - 法律语境：*territorial integrity*（领土完整）；*judicial integrity*（司法廉正）
  - 技术三角：**data integrity**（完整性）+ **data accuracy**（准确性）+ **data consistency**（一致性）= 数据质量的核心维度
- **例句**：
  > Ensuring **data integrity** throughout the collection, cleaning, and annotation pipeline is foundational to building AI models that perform reliably in **real-world** medical and legal applications.
  > 在数据采集、清洗和标注的全流程中确保**数据完整性**，是构建能在医疗和法律领域**实际应用**中可靠运行的AI模型的基础。

### L — 地道表达

---

**L1. across the board**
- **音标/词性**：*adv. phrase / adj. phrase*
- **英文释义**：Applying to or affecting all things, people, or situations equally; universally.
- **中文释义**：全面地，全方位地，一概
- **语域**：新闻 / 口语 / 非正式书面
- **同义词**：universally, across all categories, comprehensively, economy-wide
- **常见词组**：across-the-board cuts / across-the-board improvement / apply across the board
- **拓展**：
  - 来源于赛马用语（赛马下注所有名次），现已泛化为"全面，不加区别地"
  - 书面正式替换：*comprehensively / universally / without exception*
- **例句**：
  > The new data quality standards are expected to improve AI model performance **across the board**, benefiting applications from autonomous driving to medical imaging.
  > 新的数据质量标准预计将**全面**提升AI模型性能，惠及从自动驾驶到医学影像的各类应用。

---

**L2. keep pace with**
- **音标/词性**：*v. phrase*
- **英文释义**：To move, develop, or progress at the same speed as something else; to not fall behind.
- **中文释义**：跟上，与…同步发展，不落后于
- **语域**：新闻 / 书面 / 正式
- **同义词**：keep up with / match the pace of / stay abreast of
- **反义词**：fall behind / lag behind / trail
- **常见词组**：keep pace with technological change / keep pace with demand / struggle to keep pace
- **拓展**：
  - 与本文语境："数据集质量提升能否跟上 AI 应用扩张的速度"是全文的隐含命题之一
  - 辨析：*keep pace with* 强调并肩齐进、同步性；*catch up with* 强调曾经落后后的追赶；*stay ahead of* 强调保持领先
- **例句**：
  > China's regulatory framework for data governance is evolving rapidly, but the question remains whether **legislative capacity can keep pace with** the exponential growth of AI deployments in sensitive sectors.
  > 中国的数据治理监管框架正在快速演进，但**立法能力能否跟上**敏感领域AI部署指数级增长的步伐，仍是一个问题。
  > Regulatory frameworks for data governance are frequently criticized for failing to **keep pace with** the exponential speed at which AI capabilities—and the associated risks—are advancing.
  > 数据治理的监管框架频繁受到批评，被认为未能**跟上** AI 能力——及其相关风险——以指数级速度发展的步伐。

---

**L3. be a far cry from**
- **音标/词性**：*idiomatic phrase*
- **英文释义**（朗文）：To be very different from something.
- **中文释义**：与…相去甚远；远非…
- **语域**：新闻 / 书面 / 半正式
- **同义词**：be worlds apart from / bear little resemblance to / pale in comparison to
- **反义词**：be on a par with / be comparable to
- **常见词组**：
  - be a far cry from last year's figures（与去年的数字相去甚远）
  - be a far cry from what was promised（远非当初承诺的那样）
- **拓展**：
  - 该短语源自"呼喊声传不到的遥远距离"这一意象，现已完全固定为习语
  - 注意：主语通常是"新情况/现状"，比较对象用 *from* 引出
  - 辨析：*be far from*（远非，后接形容词或名词，如 *far from perfect*）与 *be a far cry from*（后接名词短语，表示性质差异大）
- **例句**：
  > The thirty centrally released enterprise datasets are a promising start, but they are still **a far cry from** the comprehensive, unified national data infrastructure that AI developers truly need.
  > 首批发布的30项央企数据集是一个良好开端，但与AI开发者真正所需的全面统一的国家数据基础设施相比，仍是**相去甚远**。

---

**L4. strike a balance (between)**
- **音标/词性**：*v. phrase*
- **英文释义**（朗文）：To give the correct amount of importance or attention to two contrasting things or priorities simultaneously.
- **中文释义**：在…之间取得平衡；兼顾两方
- **语域**：正式 / 书面 / 学术 / 政策
- **同义词**：achieve a balance / find a middle ground / reconcile competing interests
- **常见词组**：
  - strike a balance between openness and security（在开放与安全之间取得平衡）
  - strike a balance between innovation and regulation（在创新与监管之间寻求平衡）
- **拓展**：
  - 朗文例句：*It is necessary to strike a balance between the needs of employers and employees.*
  - 高频写作用语，在政策类、学术类和评论类文章中极为常见
  - 近义短语对比：*strike a balance*（积极努力达成平衡）vs *find a balance*（稍偏被动，找到平衡点）vs *maintain a balance*（维持已有的平衡）
- **例句**：
  > Policymakers must **strike a careful balance** between encouraging open sharing of training datasets and protecting commercially sensitive or proprietary data from exploitation.
  > 政策制定者必须在鼓励开放共享训练数据集与保护具有商业敏感性或专有性的数据免遭滥用之间**取得审慎的平衡**。

---

**L5. lay the groundwork (for)**
- **音标/词性**：*v. phrase*
- **英文释义**（朗文）：To prepare carefully for something so that it will be successful; to establish the necessary foundations.
- **中文释义**：为…奠定基础；打好根基
- **语域**：正式 / 书面 / 政策 / 新闻
- **同义词**：lay the foundation(s) for / pave the way for / set the stage for
- **常见词组**：
  - lay the groundwork for reform（为改革奠定基础）
  - lay the groundwork for collaboration（为合作打好基础）
- **拓展**：
  - 与本文语境对应："夯实人工智能发展数据根基"——*lay the groundwork for AI development* / *consolidate the data foundation for AI*
  - 区分：
    - *lay the groundwork for*：强调前期准备性工作
    - *pave the way for*：强调扫清障碍、开辟通道
    - *set the stage for*：强调营造条件与氛围
- **例句**：
  > The release of the draft dataset construction guidelines in April 2025 is intended to **lay the groundwork for** a standards-based, interoperable national data ecosystem that can sustain long-term AI development.
  > 2025年4月发布的数据集建设指南征求意见稿，旨在为构建以标准为基础、具有互操作性的国家数据生态系统**奠定基础**，从而支撑AI的长期发展。

---

**L6. bridge the gap (between)**
- **音标/词性**：*v. phrase*
- **英文释义**（朗文）：To reduce or eliminate the difference between two things; to make two contrasting sides more compatible.
- **中文释义**：弥合差距；消除…之间的鸿沟
- **语域**：正式 / 新闻 / 学术
- **同义词**：close the gap / narrow the divide / reconcile differences
- **常见词组**：
  - bridge the gap between theory and practice（弥合理论与实践之间的差距）
  - bridge the digital divide（弥合数字鸿沟）
  - bridge the gap between supply and demand（弥合供需差距）
- **拓展**：
  - 与本文关联：文章隐含"数据供给与AI需求之间的缺口"——*bridging the gap between data availability and AI training requirements*
  - 搭配区分：*bridge a gap*（弥合某一特定差距）；*close a gap*（关闭/消除差距，更强调最终结果）；*narrow a gap*（缩小差距，未必完全消除）
- **例句**：
  > A coordinated, multi-agency data sharing mechanism could **bridge the gap** between the fragmented datasets currently held by different government departments and the comprehensive corpora that AI training demands.
  > 一套协调一致的多机构数据共享机制，可以**弥合**各政府部门现有分散数据集与AI训练所需完整语料之间的**差距**。

---

**L7. level the playing field**
- **音标/词性**：*v. phrase*
- **英文释义**（朗文）：To make a situation fair for everyone competing, by removing disadvantages that some participants face.
- **中文释义**：创造公平竞争环境；拉平竞争起跑线
- **语域**：商业 / 政策 / 新闻
- **同义词**：create a fair competition environment / ensure equal footing / even the odds
- **常见词组**：
  - level the playing field for SMEs（为中小企业创造公平竞争条件）
  - a level playing field（公平竞争的环境，名词短语）
- **拓展**：
  - 与本文关联：开源模型趋势正在一定程度上"拉平"算力与算法领域的差距，数据质量则成为新的差异化竞争点
  - 该表达源自体育语境（平整的运动场地），现已在商业与政策语境中广泛使用
  - 反向表达：*tilt the playing field*（使竞争向某方倾斜）
- **例句**：
  > Open-sourcing foundation models has done much to **level the playing field** among AI developers, but access to **proprietary, high-quality training data** now represents the new frontier of competitive advantage.
  > 基础模型开源化在很大程度上**拉平了**AI开发者之间的竞争起跑线，但获取**专有高质量训练数据**的能力，如今已成为竞争优势的新前沿。

## 4C. 相关法律问题

本文涉及**数据要素、AI训练数据、国家数据治理、数据标准合规**等多个法律维度，以下6个搜索关键词聚焦中美两国相关法律议题，供备考积累：

---

**① 中国《数据安全法》第21条——数据分类分级保护**

> 搜索关键词：`《数据安全法》第21条 数据分类分级 重要数据目录 2021`

- **法律背景**：《数据安全法》（2021年9月施行）第21条要求国家建立数据分类分级保护制度，按照数据的重要程度以及遭到篡改、破坏、泄露或非法获取、使用的危害程度，将数据分为一般数据、重要数据和核心数据。本文所涉高质量数据集建设，涉及行业数据的分级管理与流通许可问题，与此条款直接相关。

---

**② 中国《个人信息保护法》第13条——处理个人信息的合法性基础（AI训练数据适用）**

> 搜索关键词：`《个人信息保护法》第13条 合法性基础 AI训练数据 授权同意 2021`

- **法律背景**：AI训练数据集中往往包含大量个人信息（文字、图像、语音等）。《个人信息保护法》第13条规定了处理个人信息须具备的合法性依据，包括取得个人同意、履行合同必要、履行法定职责等，直接影响数据集的合规采集与使用。

---

**③ 中国《生成式人工智能服务管理暂行办法》——训练数据合规要求**

> 搜索关键词：`生成式人工智能服务管理暂行办法 第七条 训练数据合规 数据标注规范 2023`

- **法律背景**：2023年8月施行的《生成式人工智能服务管理暂行办法》第7条明确规定：使用具有版权的数据，应取得授权；收集个人信息，应依法取得同意；不得使用含有侵犯知识产权内容的数据训练模型。这是目前中国规范AI训练数据的最直接专项规定。

---

**④ 中国《网络数据安全管理条例》——数据跨境与数据处理者义务**

> 搜索关键词：`《网络数据安全管理条例》2025 施行 数据处理者义务 重要数据出境`

- **法律背景**：该条例于2025年1月1日正式施行，是《数据安全法》《网络安全法》《个人信息保护法》框架下首部行政法规层级的系统性数据安全规范。对数据处理者（含AI开发企业）的合规义务、重要数据识别、跨境传输限制等作出细化规定，直接影响高质量数据集的采集、存储与共享。

---

**⑤ 美国 *Thomson Reuters v. Ross Intelligence*——AI训练数据版权侵权**

> 搜索关键词：`Thomson Reuters v. Ross Intelligence AI training data copyright fair use 2025 Delaware`

- **法律背景**：2025年2月，美国特拉华州联邦法院在该案中作出简易判决，认定被告Ross Intelligence使用汤森路透旗下Westlaw版权材料训练法律AI工具的行为不构成合理使用（fair use），须承担版权侵权责任。这是迄今为止美国AI训练数据版权侵权领域最具里程碑意义的裁判，直接关联本文数据集的知识产权归属问题。

**⑥ 美国 *Kadrey v. Meta Platforms*——生成式AI合理使用抗辩**

## 4D. 金句积累

---

**金句一**

> **原文**："数据之于大模型，就像石油之于汽车。原油只有经过一系列复杂的过程炼化成汽油后，才能供汽车使用。"

> **英译**：Data is to large language models what petroleum is to automobiles. Just as crude oil must undergo a series of complex refining processes before it can power a vehicle, vast quantities of raw data must be "refined" into high-quality datasets before they can enable large models to learn effectively.

> **中文**：数据之于大模型，犹如石油之于汽车。正如原油须经一系列复杂提炼方能驱动车辆，海量原始数据亦须"炼化"为高质量数据集，才能使大模型有效学习。

> **写作价值**：采用经典的**类比论证结构**（A之于B，犹如C之于D），逻辑清晰、形象生动。适用于科技政策类写作中解释抽象概念，或说明"原材料→加工→成品"这一价值转化逻辑。英文对应句式：*A is to B what C is to D*，为写作考试高分句式。

---

**金句二**

> **原文**："随着基础模型开源态势的形成，各方在算力和模型算法层面的差距正在不断收窄，数据要素价值更加凸显，已成为人工智能竞争的核心领域。"

> **英译**：As open-source foundation models become the prevailing trend, the gaps among players in computing power and algorithmic capabilities are steadily narrowing. In this context, the value of data as a factor of production is becoming ever more prominent—it has emerged as the core arena of AI competition.

> **中文**：随着基础模型开源化趋势的形成，各方在算力与算法能力上的差距持续收窄。在此背景下，数据作为生产要素的价值愈发凸显，已成为AI竞争的核心领域。

> **写作价值**：典型的**背景—转折—结论**三段式推进结构，逻辑递进感强。"数据已成为竞争核心领域"这一论断可直接引用于分析数字经济竞争格局的写作中；"差距收窄"（gaps narrowing）→"新变量凸显"的论证范式值得在议论文中仿用。

---

**金句三**

> **原文**："数据集的质效提升是人工智能赋能实体经济的'催化剂'……夯实人工智能发展数据根基。"

> **英译**：Improving the quality and effectiveness of datasets is the "catalyst" for AI to empower the real economy… [The goal is to] consolidate the data foundations upon which artificial intelligence development ultimately rests.

> **中文**：提升数据集的质量与效能，是人工智能赋能实体经济的"催化剂"……夯实人工智能发展赖以依托的数据根基。

> **写作价值**：连续运用两个核心比喻——**"催化剂"**（catalyst，推动变革的触媒）与**"根基"**（foundation，基础性支撑）——在政策类、评论类写作中既传递战略高度，又保持形象可感。此类"工具—目的—基础"的三层表达结构，适用于论述任何技术赋能议题（AI/大数据/绿色能源等）。

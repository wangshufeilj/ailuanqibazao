## 中英文对照概要

**English Summary:**
China has officially launched a three-year action plan (2026–2028) to drive the high-quality development of energy-saving equipment, targeting breakthroughs in key materials, components, and core technologies. The plan, jointly issued by multiple ministries led by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (MIIT), sets internationally competitive benchmarks for the efficiency of electric motors, transformers, and other major industrial equipment. It also calls for the expanded deployment of next-generation technologies—including artificial intelligence—across the energy-saving sector, with a focus on six major equipment categories. Experts view this initiative as a pivotal step toward meeting China's dual carbon goals of peaking carbon emissions before 2030 and achieving carbon neutrality by 2060, while simultaneously reinforcing industrial competitiveness.

**中文概要：**
中国正式推出2026至2028年三年行动计划，致力于推动节能装备高质量发展。该计划由工业和信息化部牵头，多部门联合发布，明确提出在关键材料、零部件及核心技术上实现突破，并要求电机、变压器等主要工业装备的效率达到国际领先水平。计划同时强调将人工智能等新一代信息技术深度融入节能装备领域，聚焦六大重点品类。专家指出，此举是推动工业节能增效、实现"双碳"目标（2030年前碳达峰、2060年前碳中和）的关键举措，同时也是提升中国工业竞争力的战略支撑。

# 模块四：素材与语料库积累

## 4A. 重点词汇解析

---

### W — 写作高频词

---

**① unveil**
/ʌnˈveɪl/ *v.*

- **英文释义**：To make something new known to the public for the first time; to officially announce or reveal a plan, product, or policy.
- **中文释义**：（首次）公开发布、推出、揭晓（计划、政策、产品等）
- **语域**：新闻、正式、书面
- **同义词**：announce, reveal, introduce, launch, disclose
- **反义词**：conceal, withhold, keep secret
- **常见词组**：unveil a plan / policy / strategy / initiative / product
- **拓展**：
  - *unveil* 源自"揭开面纱（veil）"，引申为首次公开展示。与 *announce* 相比，*unveil* 强调某事此前不为人知、带有"揭幕式"意味，常用于政策发布、新品发布等正式场合。
  - 词性变化：unveil (v.) → unveiling (n.) 揭幕/发布（可数）
- **例句**：
  > The government **unveiled a sweeping reform package** aimed at reducing industrial carbon emissions by 30 percent within five years.
  > 政府推出了一项全面改革方案，旨在五年内将工业碳排放减少30%。

---

**② transition**
/trænˈzɪʃən/ *n. / v.*

- **英文释义**：（n.）The process or a period of changing from one state, condition, or form to another; （v.）to undergo such a change.
- **中文释义**：转型、过渡、变迁（名词）；转变（动词）
- **语域**：正式、学术、新闻
- **同义词**：shift, transformation, change, conversion
- **反义词**：stagnation, stability (相对语境)
- **常见词组**：green transition / energy transition / low-carbon transition / period of transition / make the transition from...to...
- **拓展**：
  - 名词：**可数/不可数均可**。"a transition" 指具体的一次转变；"in transition" 指处于过渡状态。
  - 动词用法（较正式）：He will **transition** to his new role next month.
  - 写作中常见搭配：*smooth / gradual / difficult / painful* transition
  - 词性变化：transition (n./v.) → transitional (adj.) 过渡性的
- **例句**：
  > Accelerating the **transition from fossil fuels to renewable energy** is widely regarded as the most urgent challenge of our era.
  > 加速从化石燃料向可再生能源的转型，被普遍认为是我们这个时代最紧迫的挑战。

---

**③ accelerate**
/əkˈseləreɪt/ *v.*

- **英文释义**：To happen or make something happen faster or earlier than expected; to increase in speed or rate.
- **中文释义**：加速、加快（进程、发展等）
- **语域**：正式、新闻、学术
- **同义词**：speed up, hasten, quicken, expedite
- **反义词**：decelerate, slow down, retard
- **常见词组**：accelerate development / growth / reform / the pace of...
- **拓展**：
  - 词性变化：accelerate (v.) → acceleration (n. 不可数/可数) → accelerated (adj.) 加速的
  - 区别：*accelerate* 强调主动推进速度；*expedite* 多用于正式场合，指"加快（手续/程序）"。
  - 不规则变化：无，规则动词。
- **例句**：
  > Breakthroughs in battery technology could significantly **accelerate the adoption of electric vehicles** across emerging markets.
  > 电池技术的突破可能大大加快电动汽车在新兴市场的普及速度。

---

**④ competitiveness**
/kəmˈpetətɪvnəs/ *n. (不可数)*

- **英文释义**：The ability of a company, country, or product to compete successfully with others in quality, price, or efficiency.
- **中文释义**：竞争力（尤指商业、产业或国家层面）
- **语域**：正式、经济、商业、学术
- **同义词**：competitive edge, competitive strength
- **常见词组**：industrial / global / international competitiveness; enhance/boost/strengthen competitiveness
- **拓展**：
  - 词族：compete (v.) → competitive (adj.) → competitiveness (n.) → competitively (adv.) → competitor (n.) → competition (n.)
  - *competitiveness* 是不可数名词，不加冠词"a"。
  - 区别：*competition* 指竞争行为/比赛本身；*competitiveness* 指具备竞争能力的程度。
- **例句**：
  > Investing in green technology is increasingly seen as a way to **enhance long-term industrial competitiveness** rather than merely fulfill environmental obligations.

**② carbon neutrality**

### T — 翻译重要词

---

**① roll out**
/roʊl aʊt/ *phrasal v.*

- **英文释义**：To officially launch or introduce something, especially a plan, policy, product, or service, usually on a large scale.
- **中文释义**：（大规模）推出、正式实施、颁布（计划、政策、产品等）
- **语域**：新闻、商业、政策
- **同义词**：launch, introduce, deploy, unveil, implement
- **常见词组**：roll out a plan / policy / program / initiative; roll out nationally/across the country
- **拓展**：
  - 名词形式：**rollout** (n., 可数) 推出、发布；如 *the rollout of the new policy*
  - 翻译陷阱：*roll out* 不能直译为"滚出"，应译为"推出、颁布、铺开实施"。
  - *roll out* vs. *launch*：前者侧重"分阶段、大规模铺开"；后者侧重"正式启动"。
- **例句**：
  > The government plans to **roll out** a national charging infrastructure scheme for electric vehicles by the end of the decade.
  > 政府计划在本十年末前在全国推出电动汽车充电基础设施建设方案。

**② conditions permit**
/kənˈdɪʃənz pərˈmɪt/ *固定表达*

- **英文释义**：A phrase meaning "when/where circumstances or situations allow"; used to indicate that something will happen if the relevant conditions are favorable.
- **中文释义**：条件允许的情况下（官方文件常见表达，指视各地实际情况而定）
- **语域**：正式、政策文本、法律
- **同义词**：where/when applicable; where circumstances allow; subject to conditions
- **拓展**：
  - 原文：*"encourage regions where conditions permit to introduce supportive policies"* — 翻译应译为"鼓励条件具备的地区出台支持性政策"，而非逐字硬译。
  - *permit* 此处作及物动词：条件"允许"某事，不同于名词 *permit*（许可证）。
  - 词性变化：permit (v./n.) → permissible (adj.) 允许的 → permission (n.) 许可
- **例句**：
  > The framework encourages local governments, **where conditions permit**, to offer tax incentives for companies that invest in energy-saving retrofits.
  > 该框架鼓励地方政府在条件允许的情况下，对投资节能改造的企业给予税收优惠。

**③ supportive policies**
/səˈpɔːrtɪv ˈpɒləsɪz/ *n. (可数复数)*

- **语域**：政策、新闻、正式
- **同义词**：enabling policies, incentive policies, favorable policies, policy incentives
- **常见词组**：introduce / implement / roll out supportive policies; supportive policy framework; policy support
- **拓展（双语）**：
  - *supportive*（朗文）：giving help or encouragement，表"给予支持的"，常修饰 *policy / framework / environment / measures*。
  - 翻译注意：中文官方文件中"支持性政策"或"扶持政策"均对应 *supportive policies*；"配套政策"则对应 *complementary policies* 或 *accompanying measures*。

- **同义词**：decarbonization, clean energy transition, net-zero transition
- **常见词组**：support / accelerate / drive the low-carbon transition; low-carbon transition pathway; low-carbon transition policy
- **拓展（双语）**：
  - 剑桥词典将 *low-carbon* 定义为：*producing or involving the production of less carbon dioxide than is usual*（比通常情况产生更少二氧化碳的）。
  - *low-carbon* 多作定语：*low-carbon economy / technology / infrastructure / development*
  - 区别：*low-carbon transition* 强调"过渡过程"；*decarbonization* 更强调"去碳化的最终目标"。
- **例句**：
  > The **low-carbon transition** is transforming traditional energy-intensive industries, compelling manufacturers to invest in both efficiency upgrades and alternative fuel sources.
  > 低碳转型正在重塑传统高能耗产业，迫使制造商同时投资于效率提升和替代燃料来源。

**⑧ multiple government departments**
/ˈmʌltɪpəl ˈɡʌvərnmənt dɪˈpɑːrtmənts/ *n. phrase*

- **英文释义**：Several distinct ministries or agencies within a government that collaborate on a shared task, policy, or initiative.
- **中文释义**：多个政府部门（多个不同职能的政府机构联合参与某项工作）
- **语域**：政策、行政、新闻
- **常见词组**：jointly issued by multiple government departments; inter-ministerial coordination; cross-departmental cooperation
- **拓展（双语）**：
  - 翻译时注意：中文"多部门联合发文"在英文中常表达为：*"issued jointly by multiple government departments"* 或 *"a multi-ministry initiative"*。
  - 在中国政策语境中，此类联合发文模式体现了"协同治理"（coordinated governance）的行政特征。
- **例句**：
  > The environmental protection framework was coordinated across **multiple government departments**, ensuring that industrial, agricultural, and urban planning sectors all adhered to unified carbon reduction targets.
  > 该环境保护框架由多个政府部门协同推进，确保工业、农业和城市规划等各领域均遵循统一的碳减排目标。

### S — 熟词僻义 / 引申义

---

**① noted** （原形：note）
/noʊt/ *v.* → *noted* /ˈnoʊtɪd/ *adj.*

- **英文释义（熟义）**：(v.) to notice or pay attention to something; to write something down.（注意；记录）
- **英文释义（僻义/引申义）**：(v. 正式) used to indicate that experts, officials, or commentators have formally stated or observed something in a context of authority — *"experts noted that..."*（专家指出……）
- **中文释义（僻义）**：（正式文体）指出、表示、强调（常以 *experts noted / analysts noted* 形式出现）
- **语域**：新闻、学术、正式报道
- **同义词**（此义）：pointed out, observed, remarked, indicated, stated
- **拓展（双语）**：
  - *note* 作及物动词，后接 *that* 从句，是新闻报道中引用专家或官员言论的标准句式：*"Experts **noted** that..."*
  - 区分：*noted* (adj.) 意为"著名的"：a **noted** economist（一位著名经济学家）——此为另一个僻义。
  - 词族：note (n./v.) → noted (adj. 著名的) → notable (adj. 值得注意的) → notably (adv. 尤其是)
- **例句**：
  > Analysts **noted** that the policy shift toward green hydrogen represents a fundamental restructuring of the country's long-term energy strategy.
  > 分析人士指出，向绿氢转型的政策转变代表着该国长期能源战略的根本性重构。

  > He is a **noted** authority on renewable energy policy, having advised three governments on their net-zero transition plans.
  > 他是可再生能源政策领域的知名权威，曾为三届政府的净零转型计划提供咨询。

---

**② leading** （核心词：lead）
/ˈliːdɪŋ/ *adj.*

- **英文释义（熟义）**：(v.-ing form) guiding or directing.（引导、领导的进行时）
- **英文释义（僻义）**：(adj. only before noun) best, most important, or most advanced — *"internationally leading levels"*（处于国际领先水平的）
- **中文释义（僻义）**：领先的、最先进的（作定语，修饰地位或水平）
- **语域**：新闻、商业、政策，正式
- **同义词**：foremost, top, premier, world-class, cutting-edge
- **拓展（双语）**：
  - 朗文明确标注：*leading* 作形容词时，**only before noun**（只作前置定语），不可说 *"the level is leading"*。
  - 常见搭配：*a leading role / leading edge / leading member / internationally leading level*
  - 区别：*leading* 强调"在同类中居于前列"；*cutting-edge* 强调"处于技术最前沿"；*state-of-the-art* 强调"当下最高水平"。
- **例句**：
  > China's photovoltaic manufacturing sector has achieved an **internationally leading** position in both production volume and cost efficiency.
  > 中国光伏制造业在产量和成本效益方面均已达到国际领先水平。

---

**③ driver**
/ˈdraɪvər/ *n. (可数)*

- **英文释义（熟义）**：A person who drives a vehicle.（司机、驾驶员）
- **英文释义（僻义）**：Something that has an important influence on or causes a process, especially economic or industrial development — *"new drivers of green growth"*（增长的驱动力）
- **中文释义（僻义）**：驱动力、推动因素（尤指经济或产业发展的核心动力）
- **语域**：新闻、经济、商业，正式
- **同义词**（此义）：catalyst, engine, force, impetus, spur
- **拓展（双语）**：
  - 朗文商务词典明确列出此义：*"something that has an important influence on other things"*，并举例：*"Population growth is the biggest single driver of atmospheric pollution."*
  - 高频搭配：*driver of growth / key driver / major driver / new drivers / demand driver*
  - 此义在经济类写作中极为常见，是**熟词僻义**的典型考点。
- **例句**：
  > Technological innovation has emerged as the primary **driver** of economic restructuring in countries seeking to reduce their dependence on carbon-intensive industries.
  > 在寻求减少对碳密集型产业依赖的国家中，技术创新已成为经济结构调整的主要驱动力。

**④ upgrade**
/ˈʌpɡreɪd/ *v. / n.*

- **英文释义（熟义）**：(v.) To improve a piece of equipment or software by replacing it with a newer or better version.（升级设备或软件）
- **英文释义（僻义/引申义）**：(v.) To officially raise something to a higher standard, category, or status; (n.) the act or result of making something better.（提升标准/级别/质量；也可用于政策语境中的"提档升级"）
- **中文释义（僻义）**：（政策语境）升级改造、提档升级（不仅限于软件/硬件，还包括工业设备、标准、人员等级别的整体提升）
- **语域**：技术、政策、商业
- **同义词**：improve, enhance, modernize, overhaul
- **反义词**：downgrade, degrade
- **拓展（双语）**：
  - 剑桥词典示例：*"Much of the machinery and equipment is obsolete and **needs to be upgraded**."*（许多机器设备已经过时，需要升级改造。）
  - 名词（可数）：*an upgrade / upgrades*；作名词时常见于"设备/系统upgrade"场景。
  - 不规则变化：无，规则动词。
  - 区别：*upgrade* 强调"向上提升整体水平"；*update* 更侧重"将信息/版本保持最新"。
- **例句**：
  > The plan calls for a comprehensive **upgrade** of legacy industrial boilers, many of which were installed over three decades ago and fall well below current energy efficiency benchmarks.
  > 该计划要求对老旧工业锅炉进行全面升级改造，其中许多锅炉安装于三十余年前，远低于当前的能效标准。

**⑤ reach**
/riːtʃ/ *v.*

- **英文释义（熟义）**：To arrive at a place or destination.（到达）
- **英文释义（僻义）**：To attain or achieve a particular level, standard, or target — *"reach internationally leading levels"*（达到某一水平/标准）
- **中文释义（僻义）**：达到（水平、目标、标准）——与 *achieve* 相比更强调"到达某一刻度/节点"
- **语域**：正式、政策、新闻
- **同义词**（此义）：achieve, attain, arrive at, hit (informal)
- **拓展（双语）**：
  - *reach* 在政策文本中常与量化目标搭配：*reach a target / reach the standard / reach international levels / reach peak emissions*
  - 注意与 *achieve* 的细微差异：*reach* 更侧重"到达某一量化节点（如峰值、标准）"；*achieve* 更侧重"完成某一成就/目标"。
- **例句**：
  > The government expects domestic electric motor manufacturers to **reach** world-class efficiency standards within three years, driven by both policy mandates and market competition.
  > 政府预期，在政策要求和市场竞争的双重推动下，国内电机制造商将在三年内达到世界级效率标准。

**⑥ sector**
/ˈsektər/ *n. (可数)*

- **英文释义（熟义）**：A part of the economy or a particular area of activity.（（经济）部门、行业）
- **英文释义（僻义）**：In geometry, a sector is a "pie slice" of a circle; in military contexts, it means a designated area of operations.（几何中指扇形；军事中指划定区域）
- **中文释义（重点）**：在政策/经济语境中，特指某一特定**行业或领域**（如能源行业、公共部门、制造业领域等）
- **语域**：经济、政策、学术，正式
- **同义词**：industry, field, area, domain, segment
- **常见词组**：public/private sector; energy/manufacturing/financial sector; sector-specific policies
- **拓展（双语）**：
  - 注意：*sector* 与 *industry* 的区别：*sector* 更宏观（可指"公共部门"vs"私营部门"），*industry* 更具体指某一生产性行业（如汽车工业）。
  - 政策文本中常见：*sector-specific（特定领域的）/ cross-sector（跨行业的）*
- **例句**：
  > The energy **sector** accounts for roughly three-quarters of global greenhouse gas emissions, making it the focal point of most decarbonization strategies.
  > 能源行业约占全球温室气体排放量的四分之三，使其成为大多数脱碳战略的核心关注领域。

**⑦ application**
/ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən/ *n. (可数/不可数)*

- **英文释义（熟义①）**：A formal request, especially in writing.（申请，可数）
- **英文释义（熟义②）**：A computer program.（应用程序，可数）

- **语域**：学术、技术、政策，正式
- **同义词**（此义）：implementation, deployment, utilization, adoption
- **拓展（双语）**：
  - 此义为**不可数**：*the application of AI / the application of technology*
  - 与 *implementation* 区别：*application* 侧重"将某种方法或技术用于实际"；*implementation* 侧重"执行或落实某个计划/制度"。

**⑧ advance**
/ədˈvæns/ *v. / n. / adj.*

- **英文释义（熟义）**：(v.) To move forward; (n.) forward movement.（前进；进展）

- **语域**：正式、学术、政策

**① new drivers of green growth**

- **语域**：政策、经济，正式
- **拓展（双语）**：

/riːtʃ ɪnˌtɜːrnæʃənəli ˈliːdɪŋ ˈlevəlz/ *v. phrase*

- **英文释义**：To attain standards or performance benchmarks that are among the best in the world.
- **中文释义**：达到国际领先水平（在全球范围内处于顶尖位置）
- **语域**：政策、新闻，正式
- **拓展（双语）**：

  - 变体：*reach world-class standards / attain internationally advanced levels / achieve global leadership in...*

**③ where conditions permit**
/wer kənˈdɪʃənz pərˈmɪt/ *adverbial clause*

- **英文释义**：In places or situations where the relevant circumstances allow; used to qualify a recommendation or requirement.
- **中文释义**：在条件允许/具备的地方（表示"视情况而定、有条件地"，常用于政策弹性措辞）
- **语域**：政策、行政、正式文书
- **同义词**：where applicable; where feasible; subject to local conditions; as circumstances allow
- **拓展（双语）**：

**④ step up the upgrading of**

- **语域**：政策、新闻，正式
- **拓展（双语）**：

**⑤ high-quality development**
/haɪ ˈkwɒlɪti dɪˈveləpmənt/ *n. phrase (不可数)*

- **中文释义**：高质量发展（以质量、效益、可持续和创新为核心的发展模式，区别于以往的规模扩张式增长）
- **语域**：政策，正式，中国官方话语体系核心词

**⑥ carbon peaking and carbon neutrality goals**
/ˈkɑːrbən ˈpiːkɪŋ ænd ˈkɑːrbən njuːˈtræləti ɡoʊlz/ *n. phrase*

- **英文释义**：China's twin official climate commitments: peaking carbon dioxide emissions before 2030 and achieving carbon neutrality before 2060, collectively known as the "dual carbon goals."
- **中文释义**："双碳"目标——碳达峰目标（2030年前）与碳中和目标（2060年前）的合称，是中国气候政策的核心承诺
- **语域**：政策、外交、新闻，正式
- **常见词组**：achieve / meet / advance the carbon peaking and carbon neutrality goals; in service of the dual carbon goals; dual carbon targets
- **拓展（双语）**：
  - 中国官方英译固定表述为 *"carbon peaking and carbon neutrality goals"*，简称 *"dual carbon goals"*（双碳目标）。该表述首次正式出现于2020年习近平主席在联合国大会上的讲话。
  - 国际语境中的对应表述：*"net-zero by 2060"* 或 *"climate neutrality"*，但中国官方文件统一使用 *carbon neutrality*。

  - 词组区分：*carbon peaking*（碳达峰，强调峰值节点）≠ *carbon neutrality*（碳中和，强调净零平衡状态）
- **例句**：
  > Every major industrial policy issued since 2021 has been explicitly aligned with China's **carbon peaking and carbon neutrality goals**, signaling that green transition is now embedded in the country's core development logic.
  > 2021年以来出台的每一项重大产业政策都明确与"双碳"目标挂钩，表明绿色转型已深植于中国核心发展逻辑之中。

**⑦ aim to**
/eɪm tuː/ *v. phrase*

- **英文释义**：To have as one's objective or purpose; to intend to achieve a specific result.
- **中文释义**：旨在、以……为目标（表达政策意图或规划目的的核心动词短语）
- **语域**：新闻、政策、正式
- **同义词**：seek to, intend to, be designed to, be geared toward, endeavor to
- **常见词组**：aim to achieve / aim to foster / aim to reduce / aim to improve
- **拓展（双语）**：
  - *aim to do sth.* 是政策文件和新闻报道中表达"政策目的"的最高频表达之一，语气积极，比 *intend to* 更具方向性，比 *plan to* 更强调最终效果。
  - 区别：*aim to*（目标导向，强调结果）/ *plan to*（计划导向，强调安排）/ *seek to*（更正式，常用于外交语境）
  - 名词形式：*aim* (n., 可数) — *the aim of the policy is to...*（政策的目标是……）
- **例句**：
  > The revised industrial energy code **aims to** eliminate outdated production capacity and redirect investment toward smart, low-carbon manufacturing solutions.
  > 修订后的工业能耗法规旨在淘汰落后产能，并将投资引导至智能化、低碳化制造解决方案。

**⑧ information and communications devices**
/ˌɪnfərˈmeɪʃən ænd kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənz dɪˈvaɪsɪz/ *n. phrase*

- **英文释义**：Hardware and equipment used for transmitting, receiving, storing, and processing data and communications signals, including servers, routers, base stations, and terminal devices.

- **语域**：技术、工业、政策
- **常见词组**：energy-efficient information and communications devices; ICT equipment; data center energy consumption
- **拓展（双语）**：
  - 缩写：*ICT devices*（Information and Communications Technology devices），国际通用表述。

## 4D. 金句积累

---

**金句 ①**

> **原文：**
> "China will strive to achieve breakthroughs in key materials and components for energy-saving equipment from 2026 to 2028 and reach internationally leading levels in the efficiency of major equipment such as electric motors and transformers."

> **中文翻译：**
> 中国将努力在2026年至2028年间实现节能装备关键材料和零部件的突破，并使电机、变压器等主要设备的效率达到国际领先水平。

> **写作点评：**
> 本句结构清晰、目标量化，以 *strive to achieve breakthroughs in...* 表达奋进姿态，以 *reach internationally leading levels in...* 收尾，展现政策雄心。适用于写作"技术追赶"或"产业升级"类议题。

---

**金句 ②**

> **原文：**
> "Advancing the high-quality development of energy-saving equipment is a key step to improve industrial energy efficiency and achieve China's carbon peaking and carbon neutrality goals, as well as strengthen industrial competitiveness."

> **中文翻译：**
> 推进节能装备高质量发展，是提升工业能效、实现碳达峰碳中和目标、增强产业竞争力的关键一步。

> **写作点评：**
> 此句以 *key step to...* 结构串联多个政策目标，逻辑层次分明，将环境目标（双碳）与经济目标（竞争力）并列呈现，体现"环境与发展可以兼顾"的核心论点，是议论文段落扩展的绝佳模板句式。

---

**金句 ③**

> **原文：**
> "The application of next-generation information technologies, including artificial intelligence, will be expanded in the sector."

> **中文翻译：**
> 新一代信息技术（包括人工智能）在该领域的应用将得到进一步拓展。

> **写作点评：**
> 本句以 *the application of next-generation information technologies, including...* 的结构，将AI等技术的应用场景精准定位于工业节能领域，是科技赋能产业升级的标准表述。适用于写作"技术与传统产业融合""数字化转型"等主题，句型可直接套用。

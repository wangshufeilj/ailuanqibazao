**【标题 / Title】**  
🔹 官方丨**天舟十号**飞行任务标识发布  
🔸 Official Release: Mission Logo for the **Tianzhou-10** Flight Mission  

---

🔹 **中国载人航天工程办公室**正式发布**天舟十号**飞行任务标识。  
🔸 The **China Manned Space Agency (CMSA)** has officially unveiled the mission logo for the **Tianzhou-10** flight mission.  

🔹 **设计理念**  
🔸 **Design Philosophy**  

🔹 标识采用**圆形设计**，寓意任务**圆满成功**与**天地协同**的和谐统一。  
🔸 The logo utilizes a **circular design**, symbolizing the **complete success** of the mission and the **harmonious synergy** between Earth and space operations.  

🔹 两侧**金色渐变线条**如光芒绽放，象征任务蕴含的**蓬勃力量**与**辉煌成就**，寓意中国航天事业的**光辉灿烂**。  
🔸 The **golden gradient lines** on either side resemble blooming rays of light, symbolizing the **vigorous vitality** and **illustrious achievements** of the mission, signifying the **radiant future** of China's aerospace endeavors.  

🔹 中心画面聚焦**天舟货运飞船**沿金色轨迹奔赴**空间站**的场景，直观呈现任务**核心使命**。  
🔸 The central imagery focuses on the **Tianzhou cargo spacecraft** as it traverses a golden trajectory toward the **space station**, intuitively illustrating the **core mission** objectives.  

🔹 视觉中心创新性地将数字**“10”剪影**与工程**官方标识**融合嵌套，传递出中国航天**厚积薄发**、**使命必达**的坚定信念。  
🔸 The visual centerpiece innovatively integrates the silhouette of the **number "10"** with the **official CMSA logo**, conveying the resolute conviction of China's space industry to **accumulate strength for great leaps** and the unwavering commitment to **mission fulfillment**.  

🔹 色彩以**深邃太空蓝**为主色调，象征着航天任务在浩瀚宇宙中的**精准运行**；**红金渐变**交相辉映，象征着航天人的**无畏勇气**与**卓越追求**。  
🔸 The color palette is dominated by **deep space blue**, representing the **precise execution** of missions within the vast cosmos; the interplay of **red and gold gradients** reflects the **dauntless courage** and the **pursuit of excellence** embodied by China's aerospace professionals.
